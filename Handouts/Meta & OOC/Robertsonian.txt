A WIKI dedicated to chronicalizing (it's a word, my good sir) a number of Dungeons and Dragons campaigns over the last two decades.  

(Edit by Blacky; We started playing the 2ed game when it came out, in the summer of 1989.  Stuart and Hagan are both characters from that first campaign that started on 'Second Edition Saturday'.  As such, we have played this game for the last 3 decades, techinically speaking)

It's named after the gentleman that was kind enough and crazy enough to create it.

Calling something such as this a \"WIKI\" is far too mundane a term for such a marvelous repository of marvelousness.

Hence; the Robertsonian.
