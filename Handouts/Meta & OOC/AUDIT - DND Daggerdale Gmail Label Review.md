CONCLUSION
================================================================================

Of 116 threads under the DND/Daggerdale Gmail label:

  - 10 contained narrative/RP content
  - 4 of those were newly archived during this review
  - 5 were already captured in the Unfinished Tales archive
  - 1 was a DM prompt whose content was already in UT
  - 106 were OOC (planning, scheduling, jokes, admin, forum notifications,
    chat transcripts, or related to other campaigns)

The only piece of genuinely unarchived narrative content discovered was
"The Travelling Company" by Stephen Robertson (Beliard's journey from
Suzail to the Dragon's Gate Inn, including the "Words of Silver" cipher
and Carpathia Twist's trial). This has now been archived.

All threads have been read, including the three large planning threads
(Daggerdale campaign, Sunday(s), Daggerdale round 2). None contained
archivable narrative content. The "Daggerdale campaign" thread references
The Travelling Company as incomplete — Stephen notes on 25 Jan 2011 that
he hadn't finished the story of what Beliard was doing between leaving
Carpathia/Tyrian and rejoining them at Quick Axes (including some
information gathering about the elves). The archived Travelling Company
narrative represents the extent of what was written.

The Auction thread (130fb87126f15f4c) was found via a separate 'sun blades'
search rather than the label search, suggesting it may not carry Label_13
or was on a second page not captured in the label search results.
