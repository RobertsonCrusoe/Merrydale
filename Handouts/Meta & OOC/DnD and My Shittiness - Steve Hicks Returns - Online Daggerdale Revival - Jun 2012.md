PART 1: STEVE — THE APOLOGY — STEVE HICKS (25 Jun 2012)
--------------------------------------------------------
Source: Message 138249170dd64091
From: snahfu@gmail.com
To: Les, Gus, Scott, Stephen

"So...I don't know what the cause of it was.

Maybe I wrote too much on the Loudwater campaign and I hit a wall...and
the wall fell on me.

Or...I dunno...life.

I just felt creatively scoured and the idea of being 'on point' for the
Daggerdale campaign sent me into a tizzy.

My head's on straight again. It's not that I didn't want to play D&D
with you guys. I always want to do that. I just couldn't find any juice
to write...well...anything.

So...I'm sorry. Really. If you guys want to get it up and running, I'm
down. Blacky...if there's a way you can have Nate come off the bench
and not be 'the leader' or el capitano or whatever...that'd be great
because then I won't be holding everyone up.

If all of you are busy...of 'fuck you-ish' I understand. I was an idiot.

<3
S."
