A COUPLE CLOSE CALLS, BUT WE PLAYED SLOW AND CAUTIOUS, AND DIDN'T END UP GETTING SO MUCH AS SERIOUSLY HURT AFTER THAT.

WE BASICALLY SPENT THE NIGHT EXPLORING THE WILDERNESS, KILLING ANIMALS, HARVESTING PELTS AND MEAT, AND SELLING IT.  I EVEN EARNED ENOUGH TO BUY A LONG-BOW!
