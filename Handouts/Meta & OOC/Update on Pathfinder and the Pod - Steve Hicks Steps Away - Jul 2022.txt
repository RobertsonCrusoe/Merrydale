PART 1: STEVE STEPS AWAY — STEVE HICKS (8 Jul 2022)
----------------------------------------------------
Source: Message 181deb88b6e1971f
To: Stephen Robertson, Todd Burry, Alex Francom

"Alright guys. I really apologize for this but I need to drop out.

The last nine years have not been a shining moment for me and being
involved in further endeavors along these lines just causes me a good deal
of anxiety.

I really do apologize.

I just need to start salvaging the last nine years and it's not going to
happen when I keep putting myself into situations that is going to involve
me feeling the way I presently feel.

I encourage all you to keep rolling dice however. It's good for the soul.

Cheers;
S."


PART 2: STEPHEN'S REPLY — STEPHEN ROBERTSON (8 Jul 2022)
---------------------------------------------------------
Source: Message 181dee87aa8c16af

"Sir,

I completely understand, and you have my full support. Drawing a line in
the sand against new distractions is a difficult skill to master, and I
think that you're doing the right thing. For my own part, a gaming
commitment was always going to be touch and go for me - aside from a
slightly unpredictable but full work schedule, three young kids, and a full
summer of more couples and families with young kids staying here for weeks
at a time, dealing with everything that needs to be dealt with in this
house is a game of whack-a-mole with 10 holes and 1000 moles waiting in
reserve. Not to mention organizing a celebration of life for my Dad in
August.

So as much as I liked the idea of taking Mungo on the road, I am happy to
let that sit as an idle fancy for now. Really, you're doing me a favour.
Also, having just finished a listen to the audiobook version of Treasure
Island yesterday, my heart is yearning more for Freeport more than Absalom
at the moment.

All the very best of luck slaying the demons that assail you, and I look
forward to the day when we can roll dice again. Until then, I shall remain

Your loyal friend,
S."
