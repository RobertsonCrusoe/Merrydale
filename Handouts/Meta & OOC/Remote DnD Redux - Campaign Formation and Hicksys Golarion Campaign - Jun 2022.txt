THREAD 2: "AN INTRODUCTION..." — STEVE'S DM NARRATIVE (22–23 Jun 2022)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Source: Thread 1818dc6db7d38cc9 (5 messages)

PART 4: STEVE'S OPENING NARRATIVE — STEVE HICKS (22 Jun 2022)
Source: Message 1818dc6db7d38cc9

Steve delivers a lengthy narrative introduction set in "The Silver Spoon"
orphanage in the Puddles district of Absalom. Key NPCs introduced:

- Father Brill: Elderly proprietor of The Silver Spoon. Kind, enthusiastic.
  "I KNOW you have value and I will lift you up beyond your current station."
- Inwo: A gnome bristling with daggers, brutal taskmaster.
  "Oi cunts! You call me Inwo and if'n you call me anyfing else, then I'll
  dull me knives on ya until ya learn."
- Honey: A tired-looking half-elven female, former beauty worn down by life
  in the Puddles.
- Hune: A massive half-orc with a rusted steel tusk and a gouged-out eye.

The narrative establishes the PCs as orphans aged 10-16, with a 12-year
time jump to "current day" Golarion planned after the first session.

Steve: "I'm not a writer...so my apologies in advance."
