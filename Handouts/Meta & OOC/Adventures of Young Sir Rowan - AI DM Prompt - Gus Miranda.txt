DM OPERATIONAL MANDATES

The document includes extensive instructions for the AI DM covering:
- Player agency (never control the PC)
- Scene management (250-word scene limit, then pause)
- NPC dialogue rules (3 sentences max, then pause)
- Social mechanics with disposition tracking
- Mechanical check guidelines (when to roll, when not to)
- Combat turn structure and damage tracking
- World-building mandates (high fantasy, magic woven into daily life)
- A self-check mandate: "Every 3rd response: Am I still following the
  mandates?"
