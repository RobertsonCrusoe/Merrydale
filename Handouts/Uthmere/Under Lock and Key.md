If ever someone needs a lock fixed, a key fashioned, or a pesky device unlocked, Kellen Wren is the gnome to see.  

The story goes that he is actually an orphan who grew up in Telflamm and moved to Uthmere 15 years ago after retiring from a life of stealing from nobility.  He laid low and used his earnings to open up a small lock shop.  He's an expert at picking locks and setting traps and his services are often required by fellow merchants and Hightowers residents.
