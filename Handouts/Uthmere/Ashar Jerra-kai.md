== Tome of Knowledge: Captain Ashar Jerra-kai ==

Captain of the Uthmere city guard commonly known as the \"Cudgels\".  She is a flame genasi who emigrated here almost 12 years ago (1474 DR) when she crossed the Sea of Fallen Stars from Airspur.  She has mastered the chaotic torrent of fire that rages in her soul, thus dulling her szuldar markings to slivers when she is calm and content.  As her emotions become more passionate though, her orange skin grows increasingly bright and the szuldar will flare a fiery red.

At first the Uthmaar harboured a deep prejudice against her but over the weeks and months she earned their trust and admiration.  Described to have a calm and level-headed attitude in addition to her burning self-desire to form a chaotic Uthmere into an ordered, law-abiding one.  She is a battle-hardened warrior who is more than capable as Captain of the Guard.

Today she is very well liked and well known in the city of Uthmere, having taken the time to establish firm relations with nearly every woman - and by extension, family - in the city.  She has an intense hatred for the destructive Shadowmasters who spread anarchy throughout the city she has worked hard to bring a firm order to.  She stops at nothing to hinder their plans and prosecute their members to the fullest extent of Uthmere law.

During the day, Ashar can be found patrolling the market places with an entourage of four Trusted Hand, one Watchful Eye, Arcane Gauntlet, and Torm Gauntlet.  At night she leads the Arrowflight patrol with group of her most trusted men.

Flames seem to burn deeper and more intensely when she draws near them.  They erupt into infernos, sometimes causing small fires if she is angry or in combat.  She can also extinguish them at will, but requires a moment of focus to do so.  The Shadowmasters have come to depend on this as a flaming signal of her approach and leave the area.
