Is an average sized establishment located just outside the Dale Gate.  It's proprietor, Hursk the Brute, retired from his adventuring days and built this tavern.  It's popular with the dock workers and sailors, more for the women than the food or drink.  If things get too roudy, however, Hursk has no issues with making examples of people.
