The cluttered room is lined with tables and shelves covered in jars, urns, and vases containing various powders, liquids and alchemical mixtures.  Behind the central counter on the far side of the room, stands a large cabinet filled with potions of every colour and size.

Most alchemical needs for rituals or trades can be met in this shop.  Adrendia Itheryst is an accomplished alchemist but her stock is limited to the trade of the region.  She has been known to send out for more rare components should the need arise.  She provides the city guard with as much slow-step oil and as many tanglefoot bags as they require.  She earns a handsome living off of Uthmere's criminal element.

Herbalist Itheryst is an elven wizard from the expansive forests of Aglarond.  She was the apprentice of a prominent wizard by the name of Fenixius Blackwood.  Once he died she explored the eastern regions and settled in the quiet port of Uthmere.  She opened her quaint alchemical shop to assist other spell-users, healers, and craftsmen who require certain chemical compounds for their respective trades.
