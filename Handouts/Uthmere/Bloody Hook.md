This hovel is the polar opposite of the Captain's Loft, located in the grittiest part of the docks it is overflowing with unsavoury folk either coming or going by ship.  Criminals, sailors, and ruffians frequent this hole once the sun goes down.  There are no guards and in large lettering scraped into a stained board above the door it says, \"Enter at yer own risk.\"  It is also common knowledge that you eat at the Bloody Hook at your own risk as well.  The going rates for the rooms filled with broken furniture and soiled beds is 5 copper pieces per room per day, with no lock box service.
