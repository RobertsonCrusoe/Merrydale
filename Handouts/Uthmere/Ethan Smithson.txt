== Tome of Knowledge: Ethan Smithson ==

Ethan is known as both the lordlain of commerce and nephew of Bogdan Smithson, better known as the latter.  Ethan is generally in charge of policy regarding trade, finance, and taxes, working closely with the Five Coins merchant guild to ensure a steady economic growth.  He is calm and collected and weighs decisions very patiently, preferring to see the big picture and to date remains an upstanding council member.  Although the Shadowmasters and some of the Five Coins are certainly trying to change that.
