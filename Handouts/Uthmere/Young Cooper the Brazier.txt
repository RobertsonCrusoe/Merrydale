So long as its caste in bronze or copper, anything from utility to artwork can be found in this shop.  It's run by a man named Kenneth Cooper.  

A strong and muscular young man who took over the smithy when his father, Kethel Cooper, passed away four years ago.  The Silverstone brothers nick-named him Cooper the Younger, he is an accomplished tradesman who is proficient in appraising and crafting most things made of copper, tin, and bronze.  He sometimes does alloy mixing for the Lordlain for their minted copper pieces.
