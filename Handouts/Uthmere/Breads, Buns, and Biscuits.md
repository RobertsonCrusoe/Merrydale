This small bakery can be found out the Northgate and is run by Helen and Freya Gosford.  They, like the Andertops (Buy the Dozen), buy grains from local farmers and bake breads and cakes on a smaller scale.  Their bread is fresh and nutritious and their prices are low, keeping them busy day in and day out serving the locals.
