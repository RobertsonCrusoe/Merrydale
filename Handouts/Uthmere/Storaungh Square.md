Located just south of the Hall of Brilliant Justice, this wide open space is filled with benches and trees with a few scattered trade stalls.  

Storaungh was a long-ago hero of Uthmere, a jovial muscled giant of a man whose statue stands in this square.  It depicts him standing up waving a sword, in the saddle of a rearing stallion.  The statue is beloved of children and squirrels, both of whom are constantly climbing it.
