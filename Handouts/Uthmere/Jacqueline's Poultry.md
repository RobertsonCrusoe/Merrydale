Jacqueline's business is more a chicken ranch with a butchery than a shop in and of itself.  Jacqueline Brighton and her two sons, Auden and Elsar own and operate this average-sized chicken ranch.  Their birds are well fed and free-range so the meat is of high quality, they also sell eggs by the dozen.  It can be found east, just passed Baker's Hill (Buy the Dozen).
