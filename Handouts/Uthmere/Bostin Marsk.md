== Tome of Knowledge: Father Bostin Marsk ==

Born: 1424 D.R. (61 years old)

High Master and chief cleric to Torm in the Hall of Brilliant Justice.  He was born and raised in Uthmere and is seen as the moral compass of the city.  He advises Lord Uthlain and lordlain alike.  During important court proceedings take place within the temple he is present and often times the final sentencing rests with him.  When not in court Father Marsk teaches the younger acolytes within the temple, providing a well-rounded education with a heavy focus on the statutes and philosophies of Torm.

In his youth he travelled to Impiltur with Larson Thesk in hopes of helping The Watchtower ward off the demonic malevolence plaguing that land.  These days he primarily keeps himself cloistered within the grounds of the temple.  He is a fair and just man loved by most Uthmaar.  He rarely dabbles in adventure these days and prefers the quiet life of inspirational teaching to the clash of steel.  He is, however, suspicious of Lady Murove and the sway she has gained over the Uthlain and the merchant guild.
