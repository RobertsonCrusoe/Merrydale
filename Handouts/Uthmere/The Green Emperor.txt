Tsu Lao was born into a large caravan that fled the city of Yenching in the province of Chin Tung of the country of Shou Lung in the far east, Kara-Tur.  He is 63 years old and his earliest memories are of the Horselands of Kara-Tur, endless fields and rolling hills covered in tall grass.  He is close friends with both Esther and Haerandra.  His house and herbal shop can be found near the Easting River, south-east of town.  He specializes in various teas and herbal remedies that bolster an individuals mind, body, and reflex.
