== Tome of Knowledge: Mauberg's Oak ==

A huge tree located in the northwestern reach of the Forest of Lethyr, about a hundred miles south-east of Uthmere.  The tree measures 50 feet in diameter at its base and stands over 300 feet tall, tower over the canopy of the forest around it.  Its branches shade a vast area, and its leaves are thick and green all year round, even in the dead of harshest winters.

Althought still living, the tree was long ago hollowed out by the powerful druid Mauberg.  He magically worked the wood to build a massive tower hideaway in the heart of the amazing tree.  Although Mauberg has been dead for over two centuries, his legacy lives on.  He crafted the oak to house a school for young druids and to display the awesome power of nature.  Today, his dream lives on, as the oak still stands as a place for initiates of the Circle to train, but also as a Circle Stronghold protecting the western portion of Lethyr.   The tower features a dozen levels on the inside of the tree and extends below the soil for three more levels.

The school's headmaster, a Master Tomlin, is welcoming of strangers and in fact took in a young Solomon Graywall after his family was slain by a fiend of the abyssal.  If the visitor is trustworthy and extends kindness to the woodland he or she may sleep under the eaves of the oak.  If they are evil or harbour discontent for the natural world then they are driven off and kept miles from the oak itself.

A large colony of treants lives under the oak's canopy.  They provide Tomlin with an early warning system against incoming threats.
