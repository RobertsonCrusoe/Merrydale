This pub is over 50 years old and is built almost entirely out of rubble and broken stone blocks from the original settlement.  The Silverstones lost a bet with a man named Lorn Graywater, one of the Graywater Boys of old.  Whatever the bet may be, you can be sure the dwarves weren't happy about it.  It sits a stone's throw from the North Gate and has frequent customers.  A young woman, Ysera Redsell inherited it from her mother a few years ago.
