Essan and Emily \"Emm\" Hodgins once worked with Mr. Finch and are close friends with Drydan.  They found a niche market and specialized in making harnesses for all sorts of animals.  Be it a wealthy child's pony, an adventurer's stallion, or a farmer's oxen - the two of them can rig up something that can securely tether your animal so it can be put to good use.  Their home is a stone's throw from the Dalegate.
