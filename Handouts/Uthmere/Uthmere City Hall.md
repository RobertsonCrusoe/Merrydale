This is the council hall of the , where the offices of Aydi Zaristian and Wynra Lana-sal can be found.  It's a large four-story building with a library of books on city laws, tax records, and city planning.  Dozens of citizens of all social order frequent the hall with various enquiries and dilemmas, only a few of which the  and their underlings busy themselves with.  The rest are posted on a large bulletin board in the main entrance way called, the \"Work Wall\".  Most postings are odd jobs, heavy labour, guards for private estates, caravan protection, and animal trackers.

It opens at second braze and closes at fourth braze and armed guards are stationed outside its doorway all hours of the day.
