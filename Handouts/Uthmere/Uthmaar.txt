== Tome of Knowledge: Uthmaar ==

A word for those who live in the city.  Citizens of Uthmere enjoy the privileges of a large city without the crime, hovels, or tattercoats of other larger cities like Amn or Telflamm.  However an oppressive nobility is starting to appear and the Uthmaar are not pleased with them or the Uthlain for allowing it.  Most work the docks or some other retail or trade in the various business districts and squares.  Others are just land owners or farmstead operators who are wealthy enough to live within the walls rather than outside with their labourers.  Mostly comprised of humans, a dozen-to-one ratio for any other race can be found here and there.  

There is a prevalent local-racism that breeds distrust with any exotic race that comes to Uth.  \"They're fine in their place, but their place isn't here\" seems to be the dominant attitude toward non-humans.  This of course applies to visitors, travellers, or new faces in Uthmere.  There is a distrust for anyone \"different\" who walks through their gates.
