This smaller inn is the most private and secluded in the city and can be found tucked away in the Small Dock.  It rates are 5 silver pieces per room per day, with an additional 2 silver pieces per day per locked steel strongbox if needed.  It has a discreet entrance through an adjoining warehouse should an individual feel he or she is being followed.  It is run by an man by the name Braskar who has a  \"no-questions-asked / never-heard-of-him\" kind of attitude.
