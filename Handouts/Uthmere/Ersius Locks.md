The party discovered two intricate locking mechanisms in the home of Ersius Reshwind.  They are stone panels carved into the wall beside Ersius' bedroom door and another next to a prison door in the deepest levels of his home.  The party realized that the small glass multi-pronged spheres that they had been finding fit into these sockets perfectly.  Funnelling the coloured lights (red, yellow, blue) into the coloured receptors (green, orange, purple) unlocked the doors.
