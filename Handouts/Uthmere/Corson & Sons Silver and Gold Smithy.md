Corson Paskenfell and his four sons, Jasker, Ferrin, Gerald, and Nestor run the only silver and gold smithy in Uthmere.  They receive shipments of raw ore, minerals, and broken or unused pieces of jewellery that they convert to gold and silver ingots.  These ingots are then sold to the city to be minted into coinage or sent to Polithere's (Of Gems and Jewels) to be crafted into fine jewellery.
