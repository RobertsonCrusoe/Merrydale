== Tome of Knowledge: Red Claw Goblins ==

There are other dangers in the Eastwatch Ruins, but the largest force is that of the goblins.  They control Eastwatch and stick to buildings surrounding the old watch tower.  They dye their right hands with a pulpy dye that stains their skin a deep red, hence their name.  Some of the nosier goblins snoop through the rest of the town looking for excitement and never return, only their screams are heard back at the tower.

They are lead by two larger goblins named Underboss Hazlish and Big Grigbad.  They moved down to the ruins to extend their cruelty to the travellers of the Great Road.  Laying waste those who they catch and scampering off with their plunder.  

The Red Claws number 50 in all and keep a pack of eight wolves.  In the tower itself are Underboss Hazlish, Big Grigbad, 21 goblin minions, six goblin soldiers, two wolves and a bugbear.  In the vicinity are another 12 goblin minions, six wolves, seven goblins, and three bugbears.  The ones outside the tower generally dig in behind the crude walls they've assembled and keep an eye out for the lycanthropes and kruthik that occasionally test their mettle.

The clan of goblins came down from the mountains six weeks ago and upon entering the ruins found themselves quickly at the mercy of the lycanthropes.  A large battle took place during the first few days in hopes of holding off the goblins, but they eventually overwhelmed the lycan and claimed the tower.  The goblins burned their thirty-or-so dead and the lycan buried their five dead to the south-west.  Since then neither side has attempted to engage the other and the goblins try not to provoke them.

Their latest heist was on a caravan lead by Freya Stockins.  It was transporting leather, furs, and dyes from the Daelen as well as a few wolf pups for Drydan Oakenbow.  The goblins butchered the caravan and brought all the goods back to their tower, the wolf pups they keep in their makeshift stables for later training.
