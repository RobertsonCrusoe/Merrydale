Yusif Kline comes from a long line of groomsmen and many of Lord Uthlain's horses come from his stock.  His family left the Dalelands to settle in these wild open lands over a century ago.  They arrived in Uthmere shortly before the Spellplague and set out into the plains to build their family home.  During those terrible years they lost many of their horses the creatures of the Dunwood and retreated to Uthmere.  His grandfather left to defend the city and gave the horses to his father, who did the same when Yusif turned 16.  Both his father and grandfather died to the demonic forces that ceaselessly assaulted the Richan Wall.  Yusif has been raising and breeding horses ever since.
	
The stables can be found east of the city proper in a small region named the Klinelands, given by Richan Uthlain in honour of the Kline family's heroic and selfless actions in defence of Uthmere.

- The Kline Stables sell an assortment of horses suitable for both travel and farm life.
