Jorgos Tapwise comes from a long line of brewers.  His operation specializes in making several kinds of ale and lager that he sells to the inns and taverns in and around Uthmere.  Dark, pale, red, cream, stout, and bitter are a few styles he makes, depending on demand and season.

His brewery is built on a small mesa near Uthmere's Northgate with a dirt road winding down to the main road.  Beneath his brewery are several hundred feet of tunnels and storage rooms used to house his beers during their fermentation.

He has recently started having problems with animals and goblins infiltrating some of the deeper levels.  He's not sure how they are getting in, possibly a nearby cave or tunnel.
