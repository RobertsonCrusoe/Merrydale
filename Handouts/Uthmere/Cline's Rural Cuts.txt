A portly man, now balding with age, is the butcher of Rural Cuts.  His name is Cline Ospar and he has lived in Uthmere for at least a dozen years now.  His shop can be found out the Southgate a little ways down the road.  He has generous cuts with fairly low prices and serves a lot of the farmsteads and Uthmaar of Southgate.
