This weapon-smithy was founded by an ex-Cudgel named Kavern Lesk who apprenticed under Bogdan for a number of years and decided to split the labour by starting his own smithy and focusing on weapons.  He can make, sell, and repair just about any form of weaponry forged of metal.

Lesk was born shortly after the alliance with the Circle dissolved, the city needed protection so he joined the Cudgels when he was old enough and stayed with them for 12 years.  He earned the rank of Watchful Eye and then retired, to live a quieter life, he was good with his hands so he apprenticed under Bogdan.  He and Bogdan worked together for a few more years and Kavern felt like it was time for him to move on.  So he built a weapon smithy, called the Sheath, next to Bogdan's to help him split the work load, eventually he took over the entire city's weaponry needs.

- Kavern Lesk has an assortment of metallic weapons and items for sale.  

- Magical or otherwise rarer forms of weaponry can be requested.
