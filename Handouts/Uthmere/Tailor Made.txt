The Faysworth sisters, Emma and Maria, have been sewing, hemming, and repairing garments for the last 15 years.  After their father died of old age and their brother left to travel the world the farm was left to them.  It has since grown wild and untended, but they have turned the farmhouse into a small tailor shop.  It can be found a mile up the north road.
