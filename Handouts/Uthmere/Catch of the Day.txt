By far the best seafood eatery in town, it is always filled to capacity with hungry dock workers and seamen.  Aden and Lira Moarin run this dining hall as well as The Fishmonger's Wife, they are both excellent chefs, fisherman and story tellers - the epitome of restaurateurs.
