Is owned and operated by the city and is responsible for much of the building materials that have gone into repairing and restoring, Uthmere.  It was the first lumbermill to be established in the area after the spellplague and helped with the reconstruction effort years ago.  It can be found east out the Dalegate near the Dalestream.  From here it receives logs that are sent downriver from the timber camps on the border of the Forest of Lethyr.  The mill operator is a large, burly man by the name of Ryfar Amerdan.  He has some fifty workers who receive, strip, and carve the logs into boards of all shape and size.

Recently their operations had been slowed by nightly raids by the Red Claw Clan, a tribe of goblins who dwelt in the Eastwatch Ruins.  Tesh Scardale took up the bounty and with the help of several other noteworthy individuals, they successfully rid the forest of the menace.  Since then, work has continued at a steady pace with little interruption.
