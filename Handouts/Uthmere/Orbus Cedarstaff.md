== Tome of Knowledge: Orbus Cedarstaff ==

Born: Unknown (at least 80 years old)

An old and troubled man who met Baschrek Tymodes in a local tavern and hired him to retrieve a magical coin from the house of an old friend.  A few days later Baschrek returned to Uthmere and found Orbus' home in the Silverstone Streets and exchanged the key and coin for the promised magic items.

He could barely answer Baschrek and Solomon's questions about the coin and the home of Ersius.  This much he did tell them though:
*The coin is imbued with some a form of domination/controlling magic.
*Orbus recalls very little of who Ersius Reshwind was, or the questionable contents and occupants of Ersius' home.

Once the coin was in Orbus' hands though, his demeanour and appearance changed slightly.  He appeared as if a heavy burden had been lifted slightly.  Solomon demanded answers from about the nature of the coin and presented his own to Orbus.  The wizard merely replied, \"Once you have worked on that as much as I have, perhaps you will have something to say.\"  With the words still hanging in the air he stepped into his home and shut the door.
