This small dive just off of the Dalegate Square is recognized for its excellent alcohol, it serves the finest imported spirits in the city.  It's a squat two-story building that seems almost out of place here in the Waterfront.  Outside are two strongmen who serve as private guards.  

This fine establishment is where the upper-crust of society tend to mingle after hour, they hate coming to the Waterfront but they respect the owner and appreciate his beverages.  An elf named Carric Lythandir owns and runs this bar, he emigrated here 75 years ago from the forests of Aglarond to find his sister, Adrael, who was last seen here before travelling north into Damara.  

Some of his beverages include, but not limited to (prices are for bottles):
* Firewine 7 gp
* Dwarven Mead 10 gp
* Fine Wine (1364 DR 25 gp, 1410 DR 18 gp, 1455 DR 10 gp)
* Elderberry Wine 6 gp
* Sembian Brandy 11 gp
* Basarin Wine 14 gp
* Elven Evermead 10 gp
* Berduskan Dark 5 gp
