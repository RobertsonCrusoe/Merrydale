While not actually a library in the truest sense of the word, this bookshop does allow its users to rent out the upper floors for quiet study.  An hourly fee of 5 cp grants the visitor free reign of the hundreds and thousands of books on almost every subject under the skies of Faerûn.  It is run by a wealthy Impilturian noble named Ursith Monthere.  A tall man in his 50's with silvered brown hair, who spends his time reading and living out his days in his quiet house of knowledge.  He left Impiltur some 20 years ago and brought his wealth and largely inherited book collection with him.  He is on close terms with Richard Uthlain as they are related through some distant marriage.

Monthere also employs two copyists who spend their days transcribing official, religious, or military documents.  Their names are Horrus and Derek.
