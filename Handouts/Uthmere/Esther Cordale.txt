== Tome of Knowledge: Esther Cordale ==

She's a 79-year-old half-elf and proprietor of Esther's Medicine.  Esther left the Circle of Leth after her village was destroyed by the forces of the Demonwood.  She packed up what little she had and moved to the relative safety of Uthmere as it sat behind the newly constructed Richan Wall.

She served in the militia and is an expert of all things natural and medicinal.  Doctor and pharmacist she along with her staff, keeps the Uthmaar more or less healthy.

Between her, Tsu Lao, and Haerandra Wellesley they import a vast quantity of herbs every year and mash them into potions, salves, and elixirs.  She was given a seat in the Five Coins when it was first formed and represents her fellow students of herbalism and alchemy.
