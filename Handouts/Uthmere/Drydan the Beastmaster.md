Drydan Oakenbow is the premier animal trainer in this end of the Dale.  His business is run from three large buildings in Dalegate Square.  Two are stables to keep animals that either require further training or have yet to been purchased.  The remaining building serves as his home and a small general store that sells harnesses, leashes, saddles, repellents, and other animal-related wares.  He has a number of stable workers who help him clean, feed, and take care of the various beasts ranging from common house pets to horses and on occasion an owlbear or even a displacer beast.

His latest shipment of wolf cubs was delayed by goblins raiding the trade roads.  Thankfully, a group of adventurers retrieved the caged wolves and returned them to Drydan for a hefty sum.
