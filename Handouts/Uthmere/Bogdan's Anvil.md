While the building has taken Mr. Smithson's name he rarely performs any work there nowadays.  Instead, he walks around inspecting his labourers work and corrects their technique if needed (usually with a mild scolding).  Any metal work that needs doing that lies outside the realms of art, weaponry, or armour is done here, he is without a doubt one of the best metal workers within one hundred miles.

Bogdan is a gruff old man who has lived in Uthmere for some 60 years.  His family emigrated here from Impiltur when they heard that fortune turned its face toward this small port city once again.  His father was a smith and Bogdan grew up in the trade, he even did a short apprenticeship under Sorrick Silverstone.  Over the years he has become a prominent figure in Uthmere and there are few who have lived there longer than he.

- The Anvil has an assortment of metal armours and items for sale.  

- Armour sizes can be refitted to another size for an additional fee.  

- Magical or otherwise rarer forms of metalwork can be requested.
