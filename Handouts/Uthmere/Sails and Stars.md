This three-story building is covered in a large painting of billowing sails filled by stars (rather than wind) above the front entrance.  Officially an inn but is run by 12 ladies of the night who try to ply their trade off the books.  They are a civil and appealing lot but should they be threatened they are more than capable of defending themselves and are armed at almost all times.  Janessa Rhoarindyl runs the establishment.

Janessa is known as Tallbones due to her 6'0\", slender figure and is a long-time personal friend to Lord Uthlain.  She is the owner of the Stars and Sails. Shortkeg is the nickname of the short, broad-shouldered and burly Tethra Yulmrin.  She is in charge of the bookkeeping for the Sails and is quick with accounts and shrewd in business.

Rates for rooms are 2 silver pieces per room per day, with no lock box service or guards.  The women charge anywhere from 2 silver pieces to five gold pieces per hour per service per attire per - you get the picture.
