This rather large, barn-shaped building is where the major decisions and discussions that affect Uthmere trade and commerce take place.  There are several officials who work directly for Lordlain Smithson, as his offices are located in this building.  

In the large entry room are several benches and charges in rows facing a stage.  On the stage is a long table with six chairs, one for each member on the Five Coins council.  The five are, Haldar Ithrik, Esther Cordale, the brothers Stirgorn and Sorrick Silverstone, and Lady Aymelia Murove.  The sixth chair is for Lordlain Smithson himself, and this is where they have meetings with the city's merchants and labour force when new laws, tax rates, or regulations are being instated or reviewed.

Behind this room is a smaller more private room with a circular table with similar seating arrangements.  This is the council chamber where they discuss trade agreements, the flow of material and goods in and out of the city, and cargo taxes for caravans and trade ships.
