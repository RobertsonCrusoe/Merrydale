Inside the building is a work table with several delicate tools, display cabinets glittering with jewellery, a few purple-coloured leather chairs and a matching leather couch.  

A rich Impilturian couple named Marshal and Jemina Polithere have owned this store for over twenty years now.  They are both a little heavy-set and of average height.  Professionals who know much about jewels, gems, and precious metals, they not only dress the part but are experts in the trade.

- Of Gems and Jewels has an assortment of valuable jewellery for sale.  

- Magical or otherwise rarer forms of jewellery can be requested.
