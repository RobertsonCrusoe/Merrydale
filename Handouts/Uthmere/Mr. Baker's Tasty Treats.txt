Thomas Baker and his wife, Cindy Baker are two of the ten pastry chefs and bread bakers in and around Uthmere.  Their treats are by far the tastiest according to the dozens of hungry Uthmaar who come in to their bakery every day.  The Bakers are a short plump couple who love to bake delicious desserts and see the smiles on all the children who come in to buy their cookies and sweets.
