Terrick Hannabe worked with the Silverstone brothers for a few years before opening his own masonry business.  Specializing instead in brick work rather than stone masonry.  His operation has constructed several of the farmsteads and buildings outside the city walls.  Once the snows melt he has six or seven workers beneath him and they set to work on projects Terrick had arranged over the winter.  The House of Bricks can be found down the road leading from the Southgate.
