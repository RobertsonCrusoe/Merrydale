== Tome of Knowledge: Wynra Lana-sal ==

This female air genasi from Airspur emigrated to Uthmere a number of years ago, she is close friends with Ashar Jerra-kai.  Lordlain Lana-sal is accurately described as having a capricious nature that unsettles and frustrates those not familiar with her way of doing things.  She is also one of the most important members of the council and deals mostly with the social policies that directly affect the lives and morale of the citizenry.  From civil disputes to urban living conditions to festivals, she is critical for maintaining a content populace.
