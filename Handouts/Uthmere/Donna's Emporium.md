Donna Freeport, better known as Donna the Snake, owns and operates this huge bazaar of assorted and eccentric goods both new and old.  If you have it, she's probably seen it and if she doesn't have it, she can and may get her hands on it within a short amount of time.  This is a one-stop shop for any obscure or unusual items.

Her nickname originates from her supposed \"hypnotic stare\" that she uses to coerce those who step foot in her store into relinquishing their goods for mere coppers.  Coupled with her sharp tongue and unearthly beauty, some claim she is a greed-ridden avatar of the old god, Set.  Others think her long black hair and curvy figure keep her victims from focusing on the transaction at hand.
