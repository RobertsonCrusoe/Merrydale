Is a quaint little shop that spews a profound odour into Storaungh Square due to the chemicals and materials used in the tanning process.  It's run by a man named Mr. Finch, he's an expert tradesman with one assistant.  The curious part is that Mr. Finch was blinded by a chemical accident years ago.  His reputation for both speed and excellence has grown roughly in proportion to the spread of rumours surrounding the source of his talent.  Some say he uses dark magic or that his very eyes are somehow enchanted.

- Mr. Finch has an assortment of leather armours and items for sale.  

- Armour sizes can be refitted to another size for an additional fee.  

- Magical or otherwise rarer forms of leatherwork can be requested.
