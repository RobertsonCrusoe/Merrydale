A key given to Baschrek Tymodes by Orbus Cedarstaff used to unlock the warded home of Ersius Reshwind.  It was later returned to Orbus.  An ornate lock can be found outside of Orbus' tower in the Silverstone Streets of Uthmere, it is similar to the one on Ersius' house.  It is believed that the key may open the tower door as well.
