A scroll of thin human leather inscribed with black ink.  It was found on the leader of the cultists aboard the Dawncaller, a Sembian merchant vessel.  Several names are introduced on this scroll and all of them are, according to this, capable of bringing about some kind of catastrophe of epic proportion.  Things of interest on the scroll include:

* the Weaver
* the Moongod
* Qorsuuroth (KOR-soo-roth)
* the black void
* the taint
* \"He is coming.\"  A phrase that was written in flesh by the cultists aboard the Dawncaller and also found in Ersius Reshwind's last journal entry.

(The Five Feathers are currently in possession of this item.)
