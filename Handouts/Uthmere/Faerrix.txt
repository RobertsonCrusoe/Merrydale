== Tome of Knowledge: Faerrix ==

Born: Unknown (at least 80 years old)

This mysterious man came to Uthmere the day after Reginald Uthlain killed himself.  Since that day he has counselled both Richan Uthlain and Richard Uthlain throughout their lives.  Both father and son have leaned heavily upon Faerrix's wisdom and the magnitude of his help can never be over exaggerated.  His attitude is that of a quiet observer, when he speaks it is quick, concise and to the point.  Most Uthmaar see him as a benevolent yet mysterious force, kind but frightening.  He has many names as he does years in this city, such as \"The Observer\" and \"Old Man Indigo\".  Most citizens respect this mysterious figure out of curiosity and fear, he isn't elven but has lived among them for at least 80 years and doesn't look a day over 45.
