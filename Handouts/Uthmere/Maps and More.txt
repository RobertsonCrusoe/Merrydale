A middle-aged woman with black hair sits at a large desk covered in sheets of paper and books.  Her name is Anita Crawford and she is a very skilled cartographer and artist, her geographic and nautical maps of Faerûn are accurate within one mile (on average).  Maps drawn of the eastern regions surrounding the Great Dale are far more detailed and accurate than the ones of western Faerûn.  The Great Dale and Uthmere maps are by far the most accurately detailed, covering shops, major settlements, and geographic features and sometimes even some ancient Nar ruins.

Depending on the quality of map, she will sell her best maps for 10-15 gold pieces, smaller and less detailed maps for 3-5 gold pieces, and general maps with little detail for 5-10 silver pieces.
