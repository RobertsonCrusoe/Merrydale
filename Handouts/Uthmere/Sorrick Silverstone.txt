== Tome of Knowledge: Sorrick Silverstone ==

A dwarf from who came to Uthmere with his brother Stirgorn fifty years ago (1435 DR).  An expert craftsman who hails from the Earthspur Mountains of Impiltur, he is rumoured to be the best armoursmith east of the Moonsea.  He opened up a smithy called the Silverstone Armoury and primarily does work for the city guard and Lord Uthlain.  In addition, he has no problem with fixing or making armour for any passing adventurers, many of whom seek out his expertise.  

He is a member of the Five Coins and represents the city's metalworkers.  His attitude is that of a kind father caring for the city he helped rebuild and breathe life into.  He is well known and liked within the city.
