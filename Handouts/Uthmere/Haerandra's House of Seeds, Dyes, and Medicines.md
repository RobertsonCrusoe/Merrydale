Anything you need from deep within the forests of the Great Dale and other countries surrounding the Sea of Fallen Stars can be found here.  Rituals, medicines, herbs and dyes this as speciality shop has it all.  Haerandra Wellesly has mastered her trade of healing and alchemy and is highly sought after when sicknesses start making their rounds through the city.  Her main competition is Esther Cordale over at Esther's Medicines, they both specialize in restorative potions and salves.  Their potions are similar but very different to the trained eye, slightly different formulae and ingredient ratios lead to a drastic change in potency.
