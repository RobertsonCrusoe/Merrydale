Rumours abound that Brom Sorenson is a cowardly little sneak who enjoys gouging his customers and making as much money as he can.  He is one of the few quality tailors in the region and the only one in Uthmere so he sees a lot of business from his fellow Uthmaar, over whom he holds a monopoly.  His shop can be found in the Civic District on the western side of Storaungh Square.

- Brom has an assortment of clothes, cloth armour, and items for sale.  

- Clothing sizes can be refitted to another size for an additional fee.  

- Magical or otherwise rarer forms of clothing or cloth armour can be requested.
