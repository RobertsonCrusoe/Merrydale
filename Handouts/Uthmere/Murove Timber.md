Lady Murove owns this lumbermill, it's located some 15 miles northwest of Uthmere near the mouth of the Easting River.  They receive the sent down the Easting River from Timberton.  There are several camps along the east and west side of the river that makes sure there are no log jams and that the tall trees of the Dunwood make it safely to the coast.  Senick Felman operates this mill and oversees the wood trade from Timberton.
