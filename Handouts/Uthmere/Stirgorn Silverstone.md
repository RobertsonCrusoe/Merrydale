== Tome of Knowledge: Stirgorn Silverstone ==

A dwarf from who came to Uthmere with his brother Sorrick some fifty years ago (1435 DR).  Diverting from the family trade he decided to become a stone mason and helped rebuild much of the city over the last few decades.  The Uthlain honoured him by naming a district after him.  His shop, \"Stirgorn's Masonry and Stonework\", can be found in the Silverstone Streets near Southgate.

He is a member of the Five Coins and represents his fellow masons.  Stirgorn's demeanour can be described as a blunt and gruff.  He is infamous for his foul temper that simmers beneath the surface that often flares up with the slightest provocation.
