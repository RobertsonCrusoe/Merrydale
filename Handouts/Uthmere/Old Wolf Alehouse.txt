This dive is located south of Uthmere just off the main road.  The owner, Marcus Halibert, serves some nice beverages but doesn't talk very much as he had his tongue cut out by Orcs when he was a child.  Marcus, in the years since, has repaid the damage done to him by mounting goblinoid heads along his walls.  Most Uthmaar are put-off by the spoils of war, so the Alehouse isn't as popular a place as it could be.  It is frequented by the more elderly and road-hard travellers and sell-swords.
