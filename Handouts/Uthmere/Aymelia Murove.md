== Tome of Knowledge: Lady Amymelia Murove ==

Lady Aymelia Murove, granddaughter to Lady Baelma Murove and member of the Sembian shipping fleets owned by the Murove family of Urmlaspyr.  She is a tall, slender, long-haired beauty of sharp wit and sharper tongue.  As the only child born to Lord Staymos and Lady Maestra of Urmlaspyr she became the favoured heiress of the Murove family and inherited her great-grandmother's magic guardians -- rumoured to be six gargoyles of unknown origins, she is most commonly seen with two statues resembling a primate and a devil.

According to the locals, she arrived in Uthmere eight years ago (1477 DR), quickly becoming one of the most wealthy entrepreneurs in the region.

Her family struck a deal with some Impilturian officials and she was given certain privileges to help foster trade and wealth for Impiltur's resurrected frontier city-state, Uthmere. The wealth from the Murove shipping routes that span the coast of the Inner Sea and her numerous properties along the Easting River are easily leveraged over Uthlain, the Lordlain, the Cudgels, and anyone else who gets in the way of her aggressive expansion.  She wields her wealth like a weapon to accomplish her goals done and sway rulings that conflict with her interests.  If Impiltur will not expand into this cash crop of a nation then she will for the sake of Sembia.

One such goal is the establishment of an official class system separating the wealthy from the common folk, supported by Uthmere law.  Given the presence of the Tormish temple and its influence on the populace, she is finding the task to be more arduous than she had anticipated.  While some believe she is trying to carve up the city for herself, many others think she is more or less contained by the Uthlain and the Tormites.

Her lands to the north, near the border of the Dunwood, have been harvesting timber by the acre for a couple of years now, angering the Circle of Leth and druids in the vicinity.  There have been reports that some of her workers have been disappearing in the dead of night.  Her workforce includes many of the fur scrapers, leather workers, and loggers who live up at Timberton.  She also owns a number of farms that surround Uthmere and the bakeries that utilize their crops.  

She is a cold and precise woman who cares little for those she hurts with her economic decisions.  Her upbringing and accumulated wealth gives her an air of superiority; few, if any, enjoy her company.  Aymelia Murove is a member of the Five Coins and has no known ties other than her family in Urmlaspyr, and those she does close business with.
