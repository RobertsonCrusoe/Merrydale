This three-story workshop contains piles of wood lying in stacks and on tables along with partially finished bows and arrows.  Several ready-to-use bows and crossbows hang from the walls with a few more resting on table tops near open jars of oils and paints used for protecting and finishing the carved weaponry.

Hedger Cromwell and his workers have been carving ranged weapons and arrows for the city guard, merchants, and adventurers for some 14 years.  He's a tall, strong blond-haired man who enjoys working long hours in his workshop.  He also goes hunting with Drydan on occasion to test out his wares.

- Hedger has an assortment of ranged weaponry and items for sale.   

- Magical or otherwise rarer forms of bow making or fletching can be requested.
