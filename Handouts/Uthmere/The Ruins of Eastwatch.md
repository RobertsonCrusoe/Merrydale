== Tome of Knowledge: Delving Into Eastwatch ==
These events take place from the 4th of Mirtul through the 8th of Mirtul, spring of 1485 DR.

This particular Tome of Knowledge details the events of a small group of adventurers that happened upon a caravan slaughtered at the hands of goblins, who found that their paths converged in or at least near the Eastwatch Ruins.  They decided to band together and stand as one and ventured off into the Forest of Lethyr.

They numbered seven in total, their ruthless efficiency and cunning matched only by their quiet step and knowledge of the forest.  There was the militant cleric of Oghma, Alba Skylark, seeking to travel further east to the druidic enclave, Yeshelmaar.  Accompanying her was a large draconic sorcerer by the name of Baschrek Tymodes, who had business-arcane to attend to somewhere within the vicinity of Eastwatch.  A Dale man called Tesh Scardale discovered the others while backtracking along a path of destruction left in the wake of the Red Claw Goblins, whom he was hunting for a bounty.  The destroyed caravan was lead by a rough and tumble woman who goes by Freya Stockins, the strongest of her guards, Xabbu, survived the encounter and vowed to save her life - in hopes of receiving his payment.  Lastly, the two distant members of the Third Circle of Leth, Solomon Graywall and Willow Sunnidale, have Circle business to attend to within the walled city of Uthmere.
