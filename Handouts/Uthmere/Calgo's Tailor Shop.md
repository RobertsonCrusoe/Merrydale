A well spoken and sly halfling bard, Calgo Silverstring, can be heard playing his lute when not fashioning exquisite clothing for Uthmere's upper class.  His shop can be located just outside the north gates.  His business tends to favour the wealthy, so he isn't in direct competition with Fine Clothes, as Brom tends to serve the masses instead of the wealthy.  After hours Calgo plays at a couple upper class establishments a few nights a week.
