== Tome of Knowledge: Larson Thesk the Just ==

Born: 1427 D.R. (58 years old)

A lawful man and devout paladin of Torm who serves the church and the city of Uthmere with the utmost faith and loyalty. He is a long-time friend of Bostin Marsk and in their youth they used to adventure together in Impiltur.  He and to a lesser extent, Faerrix and Bostin, decide the edicts and statutes of Uthmere-law.  He is strong willed and tends to favour the most direct route rather than circling the issue with bureaucracy.
