The Captain's Loft is a rich and luxurious inn located near the Hightowers and is by far the most exquisite lodgings outside of the Uthlain's Keep.  The exterior is a grand stone four-story pile of snarling rampart griffins sculpted to hold out flagstaffs and balconies. It has a tiled mansard roof, and its own stables and carriage house located discreetly in the centre of the first city block west of the Loft proper.  

The owner of the Captain's Loft is named Gaelin Orlenti, a retired Sembian ship captain.  He owned and operated a successful trade ship called the Stormbreaker.  He retired from sailing for his own reasons and sold the ship.  Today he is the proud owner of the Captain's Loft.  He's proper, but still respectful to those of lower societal and economic standing than himself.  Being a naval man he has a strict daily schedule and work ethic.

The rates for rooms are 2 gold pieces per room per day, larger suits that can hold 3-4 people are 10 gold pieces per suite per day.  Stable fees are 6 silver pieces per horse per day with an additional fee of 3 silver pieces per horse per day for storage of harnesses.  There is also a fee of 1 gold piece per safe strongbox per day.
