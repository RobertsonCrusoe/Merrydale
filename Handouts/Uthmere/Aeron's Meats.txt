A counter divides the front of the room from the back.  Against the back wall stands a large cutting table and with butcher knives secured above it.  A small food locker, kept cold by blocks of ice, sits in the corner of the room.  The building is reasonably clean for a butcher shop and the aroma of spiced meat fills the air.

Aeron Ghessmeer and his son, Julian, are two of the best butchers in town.  They buy the highest grade meat from the Dalegate Square merchants.  Their prime cuts are always trying to catch up with demand from the wealthier Hightowers residents.  Specializing in every flavour and cut of beef, turkey, poultry, pork, fish, venison, and wild boar.
