This large stone fortress has ornate openwork \"black steel\" gates at the midpoints of the northern and eastern walls.  Walking through the gates would take one to the domed three-story temple to Torm that stands within its courtyard.  Appointments must be made prior to entering the temple-proper if one so wished to speak with the priesthood.  Within the temple is an altar made of polished black stone carved into the likeness of a gigantic upright open-handed gauntlet.  In front of the altar are two magical scales made of what appears to be gold, floating five feet off the ground and two feet away from the altar.

Mounted on the wall is an average-sized leather buckler, written beneath it on a polished brass plate is the following, \"A Trueword Buckler, one of the few remaining since the Time of Troubles and the fall of the Lord of Justice in the harbours of Tantras.  This divine shield was first given to Caydwin Thesk, a devout child orphaned by the crisis in Tantras.  This artifact has been handed down through her family ever since.\"

Children can be found in the temple being taught lessons in basic counting and vocabulary, matters of faith, and a few moral lessons of life.  Clergy, Cudgels, and officials can be found walking these halls from time to time seeking guidance from the Lord of Justice.
