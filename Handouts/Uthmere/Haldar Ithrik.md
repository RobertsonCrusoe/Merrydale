The 34-year-old human fled from Impiltur's demon-crazed lands some two-dozen years ago.  He's a hardworking man who likes to keep to himself and respects others who share his working spirit.  He is well respected in the craftsmen circles and runs a successful Woodworkers Hall outside the city gates.

He is a member of the Five Coins and represents all tradesmen who work or have their work affected by the timber trade.
