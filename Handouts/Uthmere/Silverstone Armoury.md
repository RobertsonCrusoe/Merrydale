Run by Sorrick Silverstone, most of floor is devoted to the work area that contains a bellows, several anvils, a couple forges and dozens of tools.  He has all manner of metallic armours and could possibly craft any sort of linked, scale, or plate armour given the proper materials, accurate measurements, and enough time.

- Sorrick has an assortment of metal armours and items for sale.  

- Armour sizes can be refitted to another size for an additional fee.  

- Magical or otherwise rarer forms of metalwork can be requested.
