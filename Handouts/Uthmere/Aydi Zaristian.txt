== Tome of Knowledge: Aydi Zaristian ==

A rough woman who, as lordlain, directs the operations of the city guard and the general protection of Uthmere and its surrounding lands and assets.  Her father served on the Richan Wall just before the dissolving of the alliance with the Circle of Leth, so she grew up on the front lines and atop the bastion of Uthmere's defense.  She is very proud and demands the sovereignty of Uthmere and the expansion of its borders.  Lord Richard Uthlain strongly agrees with her.
