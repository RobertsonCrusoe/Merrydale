== Tome of Knowledge: Demon Lord Chro'ax ==

Excerpt from \"Tome of Knowledge: Solomon Graywall\"

The hoofed-leg checked its footing and then out of the smoking gateway emerged a fully formed sixteen foot Balor wreathed in flame with huge torn leathery wings and reeking of death. In one hand a menacing blade of unspeakable power and in the other a coiled whip that crackled with flame and embers as it unfurled to the now charred stone floor. Its shoulder armour was draped with skewered people stuck on meat hooks writhing in agony and shouting out blood-curdling screams through the choking smog. It turned its head toward the adventurers with red eyes blazing and barked at them in the foul demonic tongue, hand raised toward Korvax's direction.

The sound that emerged was that of boulders being ground together mixed with the final cry of a dying animal. Korvax shuddered, vomiting at the sound of it with blood seeped from his eyes and ears. After a few moments he dropped to the floor dead, to the horror of the remaining four. Job was visibly shaking even now at the retelling of these events. Corinth was steady enough to recite a powerful shielding spell that enveloped the rest of the group. The Red Wizard was not quite fast enough to save them though, the Balor's flaming whip seized Lorn by the throat and pulled him out before the shield fully formed. The three of them watched in revulsion as Lorn's screams were cut short by having his torso ripped in half. His warm blood was devoured by the demon as if it were poured from a flesh-tankard directly into its mouth.

Finished with the body, the Balor hurled it at the shield and cleared the room in two strides. The demon was upon them, raining colossal blows on the abjurist's shield one after another with all its might. Malekki had tears streaming down his face as he beseeched his lord Helm to intervene. The darkness closed in fast as the magical light was almost completely shrouded by smoke and it appeared to be the end for the Graywater Boys. Corinth grew visibly weaker with each brutal strike against the barrier, collapsing to the floor after one exceedingly savage strike.

== Details ==

A powerful demon lord from an unknown layer of the Abyss.  Its minions are responsible for the creation of the bloodcoins that lead to corruption and the inevitable destruction of the demon-worshipping Narfell of centuries past.  

Solomon Graywall is in possession of one such coin that he inherited from his grandfather, Job, after he narrowly escaped being sacrificed to Chro'ax itself.  Solomon's father was not so lucky and was murdered by the demon lord some twenty years prior.  Failing in its attempt to devour Solomon and retrieve the coin it went on a rampage throughout the countryside, butchering and destroying everything in its path.

Chro'ax is also responsible for the deaths of the Graywater Gang, of which Job was a member.  Which explains their disappearance all those years ago.

It is believed that Ersius Reshwind also possessed a similar coin that he was forced to abandon in his Eastwatch home after the demons he had been imprisoning broke loose (circa ~1385 D.R.).  According to his journal, Ersius had been labouring over the implement for some time, trying to change the coin in some way.  The exact purpose for these changes are currently unknown.  Baschrek Tymodes was sent to retrieve the coin for an old wizard and traded it to him for two magical items.

The Watchtower know of his existence only through painstaking research into ancient Narfell's history.  According to their research, Chro'ax has no explicit groups or followers in Faerûn and the location, name, and exact nature of the plane he resides on has long been a mystery.
