== Tome of Knowledge: Kruthik Nest ==

The Kruthik are the silent killers who patiently dig out their labyrinthine tunnel systems to house their ever growing numbers.  They are another recent addition to the ruins and have been more or less been preoccupied with their tunnelling.

A young hive lord burrowed here from a large ascending cavern over three months ago and has slowly been populating this smaller cave system.  The hive is relatively small and is filled with numerous hatcheries, tunnels, and fungi.  A thick opaque ooze coats the walls and drips from the ceiling, forming pools of organic sludge on the floors.  It is cold and damp and plumes of white and yellow fungus grow where ever the organic spackle is absent.  The resulting smell is horrible, some kind of mixture of rotting meat, excrement, fungus and the organic slimes combine to form a gut retching odour.

They have survived off of various underground vermin for a long time but as their numbers grow they pushed to the surface to find larger prey.  They have hunted and killed every manner of forest life, from rabbits to bears and even a few unlucky goblins who wandered too far into the woods.

They have mostly left the lycans and the goblins alone for now, but are steadily growing in number and will eventually make a push to kill and devour anything else that resides in the ruins. 

The swarm itself is composed of mostly young kruthik and hatchlings who take care of the larva and eggs as well as expansion of their tunnel system.  There are a few adults who guard the hive itself and bring back a few hundred pounds of meat a night from above and below ground.
