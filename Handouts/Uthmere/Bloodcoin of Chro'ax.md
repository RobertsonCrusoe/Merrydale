{{blocktitle|Bloodcoin of Chro'ax|Artifact}}
This fist-sized silver disc is actually a cursed coin crafted by the a demon lord named Chro'ax, it is slowly draining the bearer's soul.
Item slot: Neck
Enhancement: +1 Fortitude, Reflex, and Will<br>(This bonus increases by +1 at levels: 6, 11, 16, 21, 26.)

Properties: During a short rest or an extended rest, you may store one at-will or encounter power in the medallion.  The power must belong to a martial, primal, or arcane class and it must be currently known to either you or an ally within 20 squares.
You can have only one power stored in the medallion at a time
The Bloodcoin of Chro'ax cannot store a power of higher level than the person carrying it. Once the power is used, another power must be stored in the medallion before it can be used again.
If a new power is stored before the old one is used, the old power is lost.
Power (Daily): Standard Action. Use the power stored in the medallion and roll 1d6 to determine the nature of Soul Siphon, if any. The effects last until you take an extended rest, or bolster your strength at a milestone.
Soul Siphon
[1-2] Waves of evil and madness course through your body: your flesh becomes brittle and aged, muscles tighten and ache, your mind loses focus, and you feel a shadow of your former self.<br>(-1 to all defenses)<br>(This increases to -2 for paragon tier and -3 for epic tier)

[3-4] A dark cloud hangs over your memories, causing you to forget skills you have long mastered.<br>(-1 to untrained skill checks and -3 to trained)<br>(This increases to -3/-5 for paragon tier and -5/-7 for epic tier)

[5] During combat, miasmatic tendrils erupt from your body in a burst one centered on your square. The horrific sounds they make, while barely audible, are enough to make anything near you lose its mind. Any creature who enters or starts their turn within the burst is attacked:
Madness Aura (immediate interrupt; free action)
(1/2 level + highest attribute modifier + 1) vs Will;<br>Hit: the target makes a basic attack with a +1 power bonus against you.  The power bonus increases to +2 for paragon tier and +3 for epic tier.<br>Miss: the target is dazed until the end of their next turn.

[6] The waves of madness and agony crash upon your iron will and break.  The pull of the medallion dissipates providing you temporary relief.

You may spend a healing surge to avoid having a portion of your soul ripped violently from your body. Allowing you to use the power within the medallion without rolling to determine the effects of soul siphon.  (The healing surge requirement increases to 2 for paragon tier and 3 for epic tier.

A powerful artifact left over from the fall of the Narfell Empire that once controlled this region of Faerûn.  The device holds potent magics from the beyond, as to where or what that beyond is remains to be discovered.  The device is believed to channel and manifest the powers of an ancient demon named, Chro'ax, who slew an adventuring company that Solomon's grandfather was a part of.  Its purpose is still unclear, its origin unknown, but the strange designs and arcane properties have been found elsewhere in the region:
300px|thumb|left|A bloodcoin of Chro'ax, worn by Solomon Graywall.
[1] A similar medallion was found in Ersius Reshwind's home out in the Eastwatch Ruins, it was discovered by Baschrek Tymodes and it was believed to be used in experiments to control abyssal demons.  It was later turned over to Orbus Cedarstaff.

[2] Designs matching that of the coin were found on antique plates from the height of the Narfell Empire.  They were a part of Lady Aymelia Murove's collection.

[3] Tattoos adorned the backs, arms, and legs of the murderous cultists aboard the Dawncaller - tattoos that bare a stark resemblance to the symbolism of the coin.

[4] Markings on a large, unfathomably complex, obsidian doorway that seals shut a mysterious structure beneath the city of Uthmere are akin to those found on this coin and the one found at the Eastwatch Ruins.

(Solomon Graywall is currently in possession of this item.)
