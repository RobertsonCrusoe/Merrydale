This huge compound is composed of several buildings used for storage facilities, bakeries, and living quarters.  Situated a ways out of the Dalegate on the north side of the road.  It's run by a family of halflings named the Andertops.  They buy a lot of the grains grown by the farmers of the region and bake much of the bread that is sold in the markets and eateries within Uthmere.
