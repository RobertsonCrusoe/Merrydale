This smelly store sells raw fish, frozen fish, and fishing supplies from tackle to rowboats.  They also charge by the barrel-full to clean and descale fish brought into the store.  Aden and Lira Moarin run this shop as well as their fine fishing eatery, Catch of the Day.  Often times Lira will be here, hence the name of the shop, and Aden will be cooking next door.
