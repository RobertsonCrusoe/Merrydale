The Meadhouse is a brewery of mead and similar alcoholic beverages, owned and operated by a man named Garren Timly.  He and his workers have a small apiary built on a nearby mesa with the brewery located down in the valley.  There are a few tunnels and storage rooms dug out of the mesa.  It can be found 3 miles south of Uthmere.
