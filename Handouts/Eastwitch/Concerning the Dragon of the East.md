Translated from the scrolls found in the ruins of the Old Library.

It is said that before the first Men came to the Eastwitch, the Great Wyrm Verithrax ruled the peaks of the Iron Mountains. He was not like the dragons of the south, obsessed with gold and gems. Verithrax craved knowledge.

The legends say he would descend upon the mountain villages not to burn them, but to demand their oldest books and their wisest scholars. For three hundred years, the hoard of Verithrax grew, consisting not of coins, but of scrolls, codices, and tablets from empires that have long since vanished into the mists of time.

When the Dragon finally vanished during the Year of the Shattered Crown, he did not leave his hoard behind. Some say he flew into the sun; others say he burrowed deep into the heart of the world to read in peace. But the rangers of the East still speak of a cave where the air smells of old parchment and the wind sounds like the turning of a thousand pages.
