This place is set on the outskirts of the village, behind many tall thick trees. A path leads up to the stone fence which surrounds it, where a sign with a simple hammer and anvil is displayed. There are two buildings inside the fence; the actual smithy, which is the small southern building, and the Garrison household. The smithy is build of crude-lain stone (laid by the current owners grand father), while the house is a descent timber place of two stories.

Garrison of Eastwitch is the only real competent blacksmith in Eastwitch. He inherited the trade from his father, who has passed away, and is subsequently teaching his two boys and two girls the trade. Teaching them, recently, has slowed down his productivity, but he can turn out some fine products at need. Below are the types of things that he has molds for, and can produce.
