Judge Beorl recently purchased a long sword for Sheriff Hansig Miller. This purchase took three evenings of discussion, and a final price of 60 gold, which, it is reported, was a discount price given for a man of Office. The Sword, named Leirardor (meaning 'cunning ray of merciful death' in Old Imperial, or so it's said) took four full months to forge; it’s blade is of mithral, and the hilt has been fashioned from rare Black Oak from (or so it's said) the Valdenwail Hedge. Two long ribbons of silver material, akin to silk, flow from the pommel, and are said to have bound the locks of a fair warrior maiden from the Archipelago.
