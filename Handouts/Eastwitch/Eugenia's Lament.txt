== Eugenia’s Lament (sometimes known as Woman’s Scorn)<br> ==

<br>
A Warden courted me<br>
Nine months and longer<br>
He fairly won my heart<br>
His heart was stronger<br>
With his axe-blade in his hand<br>
He looked quite clever<br>
And if I was with my love<br>
I’d live forever.<br>
<br>
But where is my love gone<br>
With his eyes like weather <br>
And his strange brown token on<br>
Decked out in wolf’s leather<br>
I’m afraid the darkling Wild<br>
Has robbed me of his beauty<br>
And if I was with my love<br>
I’d do my duty.<br>
<br>
Strange news is come to town<br>
Strange news is carried<br>
Strange news flies up and down<br>
That my love is buried.<br>
I wish his brethren joy<br>
Though they can’t hear me<br>
And may the Wild reward them well<br>
For the slighting of me.<br>
<br>
They don’t remember when<br>
He lay beside me<br>
And he said he’d marry me<br>
And not deny me.<br>
No witness have I none<br>
Save the Wild murky<br>
And may It reward them all<br>
For the slighting of me.<br>
<br>
Her lips grew pale and wan<br>
It made a poor heart wonder<br>
To see the Wildwood yon<br>
Twist her love asunder.<br>
<br>
