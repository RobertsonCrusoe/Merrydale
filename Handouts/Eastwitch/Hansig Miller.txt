Sheriff Hansig Miller is the second eldest son of Gelbrook Miller, patron of the Miller household. Hansig is a clever man of 35 years who had traveled quite a bit in his youth. He is familiar with the ways of treacherous City-Folk, and was thus appointed as Sheriff. This position sees him greeting most travelers that come into town. He inquires into their business, but is usually very friendly. He often asks where they are coming from, as he may know a thing or two about the visitor’s homeland (usually to the surprise of the visitor). Once in a while, as Sheriff, he has to do unpleasant business with crooked merchants or thieves from Argent. Hansig himself is a gaunt man; he is tall and lanky, but his limbs are wiry and strong. He has dark eyes and hair, like man of his line, but a charming smile. He is often found on the back of his horse, a great Beorl steed, Greyhammer, and with his ‘dogs’ Lerethian and Nyelleran (Boughberry Veragos).
