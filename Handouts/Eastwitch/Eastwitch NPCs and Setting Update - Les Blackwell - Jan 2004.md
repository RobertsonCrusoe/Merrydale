HOWDY, LADS;

I JUST THOUGHT I WOULD FILL YOU IN ON SOME CHANGES TO EASTWITCH, BEFORE WE GET ROLLING THIS WEEKEND.

***TIME***

WE ARE USING THE GREGORIAN CALENDAR. THE PRESENT YEAR IS 1752. MANY YEARS AND DATES THAT YOU ALL (ROBERTSON ESPECIALLY) HAVE BEEN CHANGED OR WILL BE. THE ONLY DATES YOU WILL NEED FOR THIS WEEKEND'S ADVENTURE WILL BE SUPPLIED THIS WEEKEND.

***FAMILIES***

THERE ARE 5 MAIN FAMILIES IN EASTWITCH NOW, RATHER THAN SEVERAL.

-EASTWITCH; THIS FAMILY IS LIKELY EXTINCT, THOUGH MEMBERS MAY LIVE UP IN THE BLACK HILLS, IF THE RUMOURS ARE TRUE. DESCENDANTS OF ONE LINE OF THE FAMILY ARE KNOWN BY THE NAME HANNOCH. THE HANNOCH FAMILY IS A BIG FAMILY IN THE CAPITAL CITY, BUT ONLY A FEW BLACK SHEEP LIVE IN EASTWITCH. THESE INCLUDE POL HANNOCH, THE STEWARD OF EASTWITCH GRANGE, AND HIS COUSIN SETH, WHO LIVES ON THE NORTHERN MOOR. BOTH MEN ARE ODD, TO SAY THE LEAST. EASTWITCH GRANGE HAS BEEN OCCUPIED BY POL FOR SOME 20 YEARS AFTER SOME 50 YEARS OF ABANDONMNENT, BUT HE HAS DONE LITTLE TO RESTORE IT TO IT'S FORMER GLORY, AND KEEPS ONLY A SMALL RETINUE. EASTWITCH GRANGE WAS COMPLETED IN THE YEAR 1290.

-MILLER; THIS FAMILY, OF COURSE, OWNS THE MILL ON THE RIVER. THEY ALSO OWN A LARGE HUNTING LODGE, A LARGE FARM AND DO A LARGE AMOUNT OF LOGGING. PATRIARCH IS SAMUEL MILLER

-MASON; THIS FAMILY IS ONE OF THE OLDEST, HAVING COME TO THE AREA HUNDREDS OF YEARS AGO WITH THE EASTWITCH FAMILY TO HELP THEM BUILD THEIR MANSION. TODAY, THEY HAVE MANY INTERESTS, INCLUDING A LARGE HUNTING LODGE, FARMING, AND MASONRY. THEY ARE ALSO THE OWNERS OF THE GREEN GRIZZLY. THE PATRIARCH AND PROPRIETOR OF THE GRIZZLY IS EPHERAM MASON.

-FLETCHER; ANOTHER OLD AND SUCCESFUL FAMILY. THEIR CHEIF INTEREST AND NAMESAKE IS THE MANUFACTURE OF FINE BOWS AND ARROWS WHICH ARE LARGELY EXPORTED. LIKE ALL SUCCESFUL FAMILIES, THEY OWN A LARGE FARM AND HUNTING LODGE. ONE OF THIER MAIN SOURCES OF REVENUE IS 'THE PROVISIONER', THE GENERAL STORE IN THE VILLAGE, WHICH IS RUN BY EPHERAM.

-KAVENAUGH; THIS FAMILY IS NOT AS OLD, BUT IS SUCCESFUL. THIS FAMILY IS THE VERY PICTURE OF THE 'FARMER WHO HAS DONE WELL'. THE FAMILY HOME SITS ATOP A LARGE AND FERTILE FARM, SECOND IN SIZE ONLY TO EASTWITCH GRANGE ACROSS THE RIVER. THE FAMILY OWNS MUCH LAND, AND IT IS SUSPECTED THAT MOST OF THEIR INCOME IS FROM TENANTS. THEY ALSO OWN A HUNTING LODGE ON THE EDGE OF THE WOODS.

***IN TOWN***

THERE ARE FEWER BUSINESSES IN TOWN NOW. THE VILLAGE OF EASTWITCH IS COMPRISED MOSTLY OF A RING OF BUILDINGS AROUND A STAND OF ANCIENT WALNUT TREES. IN THE MIDDLE OF THE WALNUTS IS A WELL, THOUGH MANY BUILDINGS IN THE VILLAGE HAVE THEIR OWN INTERNAL WELL. THERE ARE ABOUT 200 PEOPLE WHO LIVE RIGHT IN THE VILLAGE. THE MAIN BUILDINGS/BUISINESSES INCLUDE THE FOLLOWING;

-THE GREEN GRIZZLY; THIS IS THE LARGEST BUILDING IN EASTWITCH, BEING EVEN LARGER THAN EASTWITCH GRANGE. THIS PLACE IS STILL ESENTIALLY THE SAME, WITH A NEW PROPRIETOR.

-THE PROVISIONER; THIS IS THE ONLY REAL STORE IN TOWN. IT IS OWNED BY THE FLETCHER FAMILY. IT STARTED OFF AS A TRADING POST OF SORTS, THEN AN EXPORTER, AND IS NOW A RATHER WELL APOINTED STORE. THEY STILL BUY FURS AND OTHER RESOURCES FROM THE WOODS, AND DEAL WITH ALL THE IMPORTER/EXPORTERS THAT COME INTO THE VILLAGE.

-THE COURTHOUSE; THIS BUILDING IS MUCH MORE THAN A COURTHOUSE. IT DOES HAVE A LARGE ROOM THAT IS USED FOR THE FEW LEGAL PROCEEDINGS THAT HAPPEN IN EASTWITCH, BUT IT IS MORE OFTEN USED FOR TOWN MEETINGS. THE COURTHOUSE ALSO HAS THE OFFICE OF THE JUDGE (AROD FLETCHER), THE OFFICE OF THE SHERRIF (NATHAN MILLER), THE OFFICE AND HERB GARDEN OF THE DOCTOR (EZEKIEL MILLER), THE SCALES (USED TO SETTLE ANY DISPUTEES OF MEASUREMENT), THE OFFICE OF THE NOTARY (BERN ______), AND A SMALL LIBRARY. THERE IS A MESSAGE BOARD OUT FRONT THAT ANYONE CAN LEAVE NOTES ON AS WELL.

-THE MILL; ONE OF THE OLDEST STRUCTURES IN TOWN (1309), AND ONE OF THE STURDIEST. THIS MILL HAS BEEN THE GREATEST SOURCE OF REVENUE FOR THE MILLER FAMILY FOR 100'S OF YEARS, AND REMAINS SO TODAY. SINCE THE EASTWITCH FAMILY BASICALLY LEFT THE TOWN, AND DID NOT DEMAND A TITHE FROM THE MILL, THE MILLER FAMILY HAS PROSPERED EVEN MORE.

-THE POTTER; AN OLD STONE HUT, NOT VERY LARGE OR NOTABLE, IS THE HOME OF ESTHER POTTER, A WIDOWER AT THE END OF THE FAMILY LINE. SHE HAS A COUPLE YOUNG LADIES THAT SHE HAS TAUGHT HER TRADE TO. THEY EXPORT MUCH POTTERY MADE FROM THE RICH, RED CLAY FOUND AT THE BOTTOM OF THE RIVER.

-THE SMITHY; OWNED BY JUDE OWENS, THE ONLY DECENT BLACK SMITH IN TOWN. THIS IS A PROSPEROUS BUSINESS, AND JUDE HAS TWO APPRENTICES. HE DOESN'T MAKE MUCH ARMOUR, BUT IS A MASTER KNIFE MAKER (MASTERCRAFT KNIVES AVAILABLE).

-EDGEWOOD LEATHER; A SMALL BUSINESS THAT BUYS UP MUCH OF THE ANIMAL LEATHER COMING IN FROM THE WILDS, AND ALSO FROM CATTLE FARMS. THEY PAY LESS THAN THE PROVISIONER FOR PELTS, BUT PAY CASH, RATHER THAN CREDIT. THEY TURN OUT QUALITY LEATHER ARMOUR AND WILDERNESS GEAR (MASTERCRAFT ITEMS AVAILABLE).

-BLACKWALL'S; LAZLO BLACKWALL IS THE BEST CARPENTER IN TOWN, AND HE IS BUSY ENOUGH THAT HE HAS A FEW APPRENTICES. HIS MAIN BUISINESS COMES IN THE FORM OF HIGH QUALITY CARRIAGE ITEMS (CHESTS, CRATES AND SUCH) FOR TRAVEL IN THE BUSH (MASTERWORK CHESTS AND SUCH AVAILABLE).

-EASTWITCH ORDER OF GENTLEMEN; THIS IMPOSING BUILDING IS SITUATED BETWEEN THE GRIZZLY AND THE COURTHOUSE, MAKING A VERY PICTURESQUE AND IMPRESSIVE SCENE. ALMOST NO PEOPLE ARE PERMITED INSIDE, EXCEPT FOR THE FEW MEMBERS. THE CRITERIA FOR MEMBERSHIP IS NOT CLEARLY KNOWN BY NON MEMBERS, BUT OWNING LAND IS CERTAINLY A MUST. KNOWN MEMBERS ARE THE PATRIARCHS OF THE 4 LARGE FAMILIES AND A FEW OTHERS. THEY MEET ABOUT ONCE EVERY OTHER MONTH OR SO, BUT SAY NOTHING OF WHAT BUSINESS THEY CONDUCT.

THERE ARE NUMEROUS OTHER SMALL BUSINESS THAT OPERATE OUT OF HOMES IN THE VILLAGE. SEVARAL OF THE VILLAGE WOMEN HAVE BAKED GOODS IN THEIR WINDOWS, AND MANY MEN OFFER GUIDE SERVICES.

***MISC***

-NO SORCERERS. THIS CLASS IS RESTRICTED, AS IT HAS BECOME PART OF THE EASTWITCH FOUNDATION. IF ANY OF YOU ARE INTERESTED IN MAKING A SORCERER, LET ME KNOW AND MAYBE WE CAN WORK SOMETHING OUT. IN THIS STORY, YOU NEED A CERTAIN GENETIC EQUATION TO GET THIS CLASS

-RELIGION. THERE IS NO RELIGION PER SE IN EASTWITCH, AND FOR THE MOST PART, PEOPLE SEEM TO TAKE AN ACTIVE ROLE IN KEEPING IT THUS. IT IS KNOWN THAT A LARGE STONE CHURCH LAYS IN RUIN ON THE OUTSKIRTS OF TOWN. THERE IS NO ONE ALIVE TODAY THAT REMEMBERS WHEN IT HELD SERVICES. IF ANY KNOW THE NAME OF THE GOD THAT THE CHURCH WAS BUILT FOR, THEY DON'T SPEAK IT PUBLICLY.

-MONSTERS; THE WOODS ARE STILL FULL OF MONSTERS. ORCS, ESPECIALLY, ARE ABUNDANT. THERE IS ONLY ONE TRIBE NOW (TO KEEP THINGS SIMPLE) WHICH IS CALLED THE 'BLOOD CLAW'. THERE ARE NUMEROUS BANDS OF THIS TRIBE SCATTERED THROUGHOUT THE FOREST. THEY ARE RUMOURED TO ORIGINATE AND REPOPULATE IN THE MOUNTAINS DEEP IN THE EASTERN REACHES OF THE WOOD. THE OCCASIONAL TROLL IS STILL HEARD OF, WANDERING ON THE SHADOW MOOR (THE MOOR NORTH OF THE VILLAGE). THERE ARE MORE TALES OF TROLLS THAN BONA FIDE SIGHTINGS, THOUGH. THE SPIDERS OF THE DEEP WOOD ARE STILL A FEARED THREAT. THE MOST COMMON MONSTERS ARE 'DIRE' VERSIONS OF REGULAR ANIMALS. IT IS THESE ANIMALS THAT DRAW THE HUNTERS FROM AROUND THE REALMS EVERY YEAR FOR TROPHY HUNTING.
