This man is the gamekeeper of the Hannoch estate. He is hunter and guide of great ability. He hails from the Argent area, where he was a guide in Argent Wood for many years. He was hired on at Eastwitch Grange fourteen years ago, and has served the family ever since. Kareigh is an able guide, but has been turned down by the Wardens several times for admission into their order (one cannot apply to become a Warden, one is asked to become part of the order). This has made him slightly bitter, and hindered him socially in Eastwitch. He is generally disliked by most, even amongst the House retinue at Eastwitch Grange.
