Perhaps you think that a druid would oppose hunting or cultivating land, but I do not think that is true. Druids make use of nature at nature's expense all the time. Their favorite armor is *hide* armor, which must be made from 

hunted game. They also frequently employ weapons (daggers, spears, scimitars) made of mined and forged metals, which must can only be fashioned at the earth's expense.

All that aside, the people of Eastwitch would value and respect the right druid. A far cry from the despised city folk, druids have much to lend a rural community. A small amount of religion might be tolerated from any character who once fixed the leg of old Hamstead's prized breeding mare. And who could forget when my character brought sheriff Hansig Miller back from death's door by casting cure light wounds on his grizzly maulings. And then there was the time the infant Boughberry twins took fever...
