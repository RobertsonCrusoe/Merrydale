This man is the fifth judge of Eastwitch. Rory is a man of 58 winters. His age shows only in his hair, for he wears silver hair in a braid halfway down his back, and bears a full silver beard. His face is brown and leathery, indicating many long years under the sun. The blood of the Northmen has surfaced in him, as in many of his family; he is 6’6’’, and must weigh over 200 lbs. He is broad in the shoulders and in the breadth of his chest, and his limbs are thick and strong. He is also the current patriarch of the Beorl household, and was a Warden for many long years. Rory is a fair man who is loved by the people of Eastwitch. He is happy to hold this post, though he often takes time away from the village to go hunting with his sons and grandsons. Most days he can be found in the Court.
