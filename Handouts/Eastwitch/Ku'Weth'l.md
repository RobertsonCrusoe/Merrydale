Ku'Weth'l is a Great Spider of the Deep Wood who has left her home to go out into the world to hunt and to learn.  She is 18 years old, making her a Young Adult, and a dangerous sorcerer.  She should be introduced, first, as a Special Encounter, and could turn into a very entertaining nemesis for the PC's.  

She is presently slightly over 10' in diameter, though if completely splayed out, she would be 18' from the tip of one appendage to it's opposite.  On the back of her abdomen, she has bright yellow coloured markings.  These markings resemble a stylized skull of sorts, which is over 1 ft. in size.

Ku'Weth'l will prefer to not jump into the middle of a bunch of dangerous looking PC's and duke it out.  Rather, she will keep herself hidden for as long as possible, picking off opponents with Sleep spells and the like, until there is only one or two opponents left.  She would prefer simply to put them all to sleep, or scare some off with Cause Fear spells, and simply snatch up the last victim, poisoning it and making off clean.  She will not stay in any fight where she has taken more than a third of her hit points in damage.  She always saves an Expeditious Retreat spell to make a clean get-away.

This Spider does not keep any lair for long.  She stays in caves or in dense copses of trees covered in her webs, until she eats enough to molt, and grow larger.  Then she seeks out a new home.  She has not acquired any treasure of note as a result of her frequent moves.
