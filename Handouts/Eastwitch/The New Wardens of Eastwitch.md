A favourite at the Green Grizzly, composed by Bin Fletcher...
----

:Brave Bin Fletcher, Warden of the Dale 
:With a jaunty bit of playin', I'll spin a Warden tale.

:Turik Grimwood, Warden of the Thicket 
:He's always usin' Warden ways to aggravate the wicked.

:Gen'ral Clem MacDiarmid, Warden of the Field 
:He'll strategize a thousand ways to bring his foes to heel.

:Ol' Doc Miller, Warden of the Hamlet 
:He's sewin' up the Wardens' wounds as good as near as dammit.

:Wyatt and his buddy Zieg, Warden Wonder Twins, 
:Wyatt cuts the mustard, and Zieg ain't what he seems.

:Dargar Fiddich, Warden of the Rocks. 
:If you're needin' any wardin', Dargar's got the chops.

:It's ill luck to our enemies, and Fortune to our friends,
:When the goodly folk of Eastwitch call the Wardens for defense!
