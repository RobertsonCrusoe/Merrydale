I estimate that they have almost 90% of the entire Tribe holed up at this point in the cycle, and at this time they are cut off from our hunting, for the most part. We still hunt their providers in the woods, but this becomes more difficult, as they leave only the most cunning and able hunters at large, and in small numbers. They are more difficult to find. After they have had their fill of dark holes and procreation, they swell forth, and the bands go their own ways, wreaking the havoc upon the Woods that we dread. Their population dwindles dramatically at this point, as they struggle against the Woods, us, and themselves, but it provides valuable experience for those who are strong enough to survive.
