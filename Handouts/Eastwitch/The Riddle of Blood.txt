A month after the affair of The Grey Hand
----

== Day One ==

*We head into the hills, finding the walnut grove after 2 miles.
*The grove is dark – the trees form a thick canopy with low-hanging branches. It is about 200 yards in diameter, and little light penetrates it. The trees seem to be younger than the other walnuts in town.
*To the north of the grove, a single walnut tree stands alone. 
*Patel notices something hanging in the tree. Climbing it, he realizes it is a skull – and not the only one. In fact, the tree is full of skulls of all kinds, including a massive draconian skull at the top of the tree. He backs down, quickly.
*We investigate the grove – it, too, was full of skulls. The deeper we penetrated, the more there were. Very creepy.
*Towards the middle of the glade, we see sunlight
*A grave dominates an opening at the center of the grove “Phaelos Eastwitch 1214-1300”
*2 stones flank the carved gravestone, roughly carved, with inscriptions “P3 1190-1279” and “MRRDYTH 1100-1242”
*at the base of each of the flanking stones was a roughly carved spider
*Cause of death?
*Dargar thinks he sees a face, an Eastwitch face, with long, shaggy chops, peek from behind a tree. It is gone almost before he sees it.
*He lets the party know, and Sarla began calling out “Hello? Is anyone there?”
*She insists we return to the lone skull-tree to investigate a bit more, says she sees a path.
*Dargar notices it too.
*We follow it a ways, to a major path, which heads North.  We follow it for ¾ of a day, then camp.

==Day Two ==

*Shortly after midnight, we heard a voice “Hey long-face, hey sad-lookin’ lady...”
*Accuses the woman of being a liar, since Claudius hadn’t mentioned having a wife
*He doesn’t like having us here, says we’re not invited, and tells us to leave.
*Sarla may come if she wants – she can meet him up the road at his cabin
*A ways up the path, the moonlight shows the figure of a man step out onto the path and walk north
*We decide to follow, and after 4 hours, come to a cabin, old, run-down, with dirty windows. Sarla enters.
*Patel sneaks through the clearing up to the door and listens – the man is revealed to be Hezzekiah
*Sarla asks about the book, if Hezzekiah still has his letters… “Large and black, Codifix is the first word of the title. She knows about it – Obadiah has it, a days journey on up the road. But you’d better send your friends back – he ain’t civil like me. Only after does she mention her ‘husband’ – “What happened to him? You know what I mean...”
*“You ain’t cryin’ real tears…”
*Patel moves back, and she emerges, and says we can meet her on the road in two days.
*Bin accuses her immediately – insists on not letting her go. He doesn’t want things to turn out the way they did with Claudius
*Dargar insists we leave, Patel agrees. We scoot.
*We march through the day, by evening we make it to the Galadron road and camp

== Day Three ==

*No events

== Day Four ==

*No events
*Until the evening, we feel someone watching
*Bin calls out, to no one in particular, that we won’t leave till she comes back or we get some evidence that she’s not coming back.

== Day Five ==

*We awaken to find a book in the middle of the camp “How did that get there?”
*It’s large, somewhat bloodstained, and written in what Patel says is Thorass.
*He struggles through the title – “The Journal of Claudius Vermilion”
*The last page of text seems to be torn out, with blank pages at the end
*We return to town safely, and Bin confesses all to the judge. Dargar and Patel are reluctant to talk, but Dargar confirms Bin’s story. 200 gp is paid out. Bin returns his to the town.
