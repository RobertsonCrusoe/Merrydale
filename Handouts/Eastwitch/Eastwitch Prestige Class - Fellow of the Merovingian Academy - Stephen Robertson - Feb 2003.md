Eastwitch Prestige Class

Fellow of the Merovingian Academy


Requirements

Alignment:            Any Lawful or Neutral

Skills:
  Concentration                       4 ranks
  Spellcraft                          8 ranks
  Knowledge (arcana)                  8 ranks
  Knowledge (other)                   4 ranks

  Sense Motive                        2 ranks OR
  Diplomacy                           2 ranks

  Scry                                4 ranks OR
  Alchemy                             4 ranks


- Feats
  o Alertness
  o Education
  o Extend Spell
- Ability to cast 3rd-level spells
- Sponsorship by a member of the Harpers, approval of the High Harpers

Stats:

Hit Die:   d4
Skill Points:           4 + Int bonus
Simple weapon Proficiency
Spells as existing class
Lvl 1 - "Harper Knowledge" (as Bardic Knowledge), "Oghma's Insight" (Skill Focus on one Knowledge Skill)

Lvl 2 - "Arcane theory" (Skill Focus feat on either Spellcraft or Knowledge (Arcana))

Lvl 3 - "Extend Spell" (Extend spell at same spell level slot on Cha + 1 spells/day)
Lvl 4 - "Exchew Materials" (as feat in Forgotten Realms)
Lvl 5 - "Mystra's Grace" (+2 insight bonus on saves vs. magic effects)

Eastwitch Conversion

Requirements:
- Some kind of working agreement with the Wardens instead of Harper Approval

Abilities:
- Harper Knowledge = Eastwitch Lore
- Oghma's Insight = Research Specialist
- Mystra's Grace = maybe a form of the Wardens' "attunement"?
