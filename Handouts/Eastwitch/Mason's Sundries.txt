This building was build of stone that was brought a great distance down the Wen, from a granite quarry. It is one of the finest examples of architecture in the village, with a ceilling held up by flying butresses, and stone arches without flaws.

This fine stone building is crammed full of merchandise throughout the year. It is the best place in town to purchase most manufactured goods that are imported by merchants who come to town. The proprietor of this shoppe is Julia Mason, the young matriarch of the family. Two of her daughters, Syleste and Sheaalre, can be found here on most days helping with the business. Merchants who come often go to Mason’s first, to sell their goods wholesale to Julia, in hopes that they might unload their entire cargo at once. Julia does buy just about everything from the merchants who come here, and has a small order-business with one particular Red Shield merchant who comes at least 4 times/year.
