The naive boy performer. Nothing seems to faze him and the world is there for his personal enjoyment. He is too young, to un-wise to realize what else exists in the world. But there is something more to him too: Why does his father never speak to him of his dead mother? Why does the old wizard look at him so? He may save the world, but he probably would never realize it. The truth is that the town needs people like him to help them forget the cruel world that they live in.
