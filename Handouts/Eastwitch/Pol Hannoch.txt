Heir to the family patriarchy, Pol Hannoch is a very grounded and wise man. He is 39 years old, and very handsome. He has a rugged build, and does not have the soft hands of most nobles. Indeed, he enjoys the outdoors, and can often me seen working with the family retinue. He enjoys such labours as cutting wood, skinning animals, building and repairing buildings and furniture, and so on. His father frowns upon his rustic way of life, but there is little that old Orbul can do, as he is very far away in the Capital. Indeed, most of the people in Eastwitch, including those at Eastwitch Grange, think of Pol as the head of the family, rather than Orbul. Pol is still single, which makes him the most eligible bachelor in Eastwitch. He has had to escape the marital agendas of is family on more than one occasion, and many think he will one day marry within Eastwitch. It is said of him, in the Capital, that he is too much like the savages that live in that town, and that he will surely interbreed with them, as is their manner. Pol can often be found at the Green Grizzly when he is not in the forest or working on the estate. 

It should be noted that Pol is not his father's favorite son. The patriarch thinks he spends too much time in Eastwitch, when he should be back in the Capital, learning to manage the family. Pol does not make any secret of this, either. He can often be heard in the Green Grizzly making light of his father's complaints, and how his younger brother, Sage, has never even been to Eastwitch, and is being groomed to take over the family.
