Howdy, Boys;

I have seen Hicksey campaigns come and go and go, and I will be honest and
tell you that I am not optimistic about this ever getting very far off the
ground.  The email RIFTS campaign that I ran was probably the most effort
so little story that I ever invested, but it was quite fun.

I would like to volunteer my voice in this story, however.  I already have
two characters for this Archendale Campaign of Hicksey's, which are near
and dear to me.  And I will make no more DND characters until one of my
current characters either accomplishes one of his goals, or dies.  Too many
characters with so many unresolved issues.  It's like I have a half dozen
Shelley St.George's in the Realms.  Faak.

Anyway, best of luck to our newest addition to the DND DM position, and I
hope we all have a little fun.

I have to say, though, that as far as DND campaigns over the 'net goes,
there is probably no better setting than Waterdeep, with an intrigue type
plot.  Don't get me wrong; we are not going to play Blacky's Big Bad
Beholder Adventure over the net.  I just think that is a great place to
have one of those adventures.

By the way; my characters include;

1.  William Moorestone, Revered Messenger of Torm (a really pious
crusader).

2.  Odin Tallhallow, Sheriff from High Dale.

There ya have it.

Hi Lo Hickso.

Here's the skinny on my character proposal.

Tomdril Jon Hollowbough.  Young Tojo to his elders.  Level 1/1 Neutral
Good Rogue/Ranger - he will be legendary among halflings as soon as he
gets his ranger career moving!

13	STR
19	DEX
15	CON
16	INT
14	WIS
13	CHA

(first roll the WotC Character Generator came up with.)

I'm not sure what the technicalities of Sheriff a la Odin Tallhallow, but
my idea for Tojo is that he's a rogue from Mistledale who is running a
"problem-solving" business in Archendale.  He hires himself out to anyone
looking for an item, a person in the area, information as a private
investigator.

He's been working for the last couple of years with a grizzled old
half-elf Ranger named Delgren Quarterman.  The two met in Mistledale when
Delgren hired the young halfling to keep an eye on the back entrance to a
kobold cave for a week to make sure that their Orc priest didn't leave
while Delgren was scouting the area.  Thanks to Tojo, Delgren was able to
defeat the Orc priest, and the two of them managed to pick off most of the
kobolds in the process.

Since then, Tojo's been training as a ranger (he's now level 1/1) and
working with Delgren on a few other projects.  Lately, Delgren's been
pretty much retired, and he's retired in Archendale.  Tojo's been selling
the private eye angle, and handles all the cases himself, relying on
Delgren for advice and non-dangerous information gathering (a la Bruce
Wayne in Batman Beyond).

I'd love it if you made Delgren an NPC, but if you don't want to, I won't
bother rolling him up.

I know absolutely squat about Archendale, and don't have any source
material around (yet - looks like a whole lot of the 2nd edition catalogue
is available for download.  I'm gonna get a pack of it tomorrow at work,
once I have a better connection.)

I'll give you some more detailed information tomorrow.

Stephen
