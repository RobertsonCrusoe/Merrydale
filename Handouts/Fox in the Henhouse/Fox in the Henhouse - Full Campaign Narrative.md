The Story so far
A Crusader of Torm, Reverend Messenger William Moorstone and his advisor Malerix of the Moonshaes, a follower of the Holy Triumvirate (Torm, Tyr, Illmater) have been sent by leaders of the Holy Triumvirate to deliver a message to High Battle Chaplain Gordon Stakaria of Tempus in the city of Archenbridge.
The Elders of the Triumvirate feel that a great battle is coming. The unabated growth of humanoid hordes in the Storm Horn Mountains and the Goblin Marches is reaching critical levels. It seems that they are more focused, more organized. It is unlikely that they have stumbled upon this themselves, and perhaps a leader has risen to the task to unite the hordes. The Elders feel that in the upcoming battle, the assistance of Tempus would be wise. With this they sent one of their most inexperienced, but most promising young crusaders, William Moorstone, to deliver their request for assistance from Stakaria, a man that the God of Battles surely respects. That young man, with his appointed advisor Malerix carries the hopes of the Sword Coast, and the Dales.
