Simon Gerrod Pettiman is the son of Baron Gerome Pettiman and Lady Sarah Nonesuch, and elder brother of Jeremy Puck Pettiman. Simon Gerrod is a dour and unimaginative man in his early thirties. Silent and friendless, uninspired and uninspiring, he acts as his father’s steward, cook, handyman and formerly coachman – though the family’s last horses were sold off six years ago. His days are spent in the upkeep of the estate, and his evenings listening in nodding agreement to his father’s never-ending tirades. Given the benefit of an education, it is possible that Simon Gerrod would be dissatisfied with his position. Not having had one, he is not.
