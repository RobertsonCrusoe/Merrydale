Consul Jack Spinner, (Master of the Royal Company of Silversmiths), is the consummate self-made-man-of-humble-origins. He managed to claw his way into power through the careful manipulation of Company politics, achieving the rank of Master at the young age of 44. Though the Patron of the Company has always been St. Dunstan, Spinner is a Faithful follower of Ulric. The faith of Ulric in the Republic, as you are likely aware, is associated more with the ‘New Finance’ than the traditional portfolio that we are familiar with in our country. Spinner was instrumental in the creation of the Bank of Waldheim, and it is rumoured that he is privately underwriting a number of financial projects, the details of which, I am ashamed to own, baffle me. Spinner was granted the title of Baron three years ago by the King, but he was stripped of this title earlier this year for his participation in one of his fiscal plots. He will likely thrive without the title, though; Spinner is rumoured to be the richest man in the Republic, and perhaps all of Avilund.
