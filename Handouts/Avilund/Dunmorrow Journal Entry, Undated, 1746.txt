(The entry, made at some point between November of 1746 and January of 1747, is undated and quite scribbly)

I will brook no more Exonerations of St. Jack Ass, or Flatulence of St. Orifice. I must find an end to this incarceration of darkness. The Bishop was right about the Scion. He is mad!

(Much scribbling and nonsense follows for several pages. At one point, a list of 245 Saints follows, though some are obviously falsely named, such as St. Jack Ass, who is mentioned here for the second time.)
