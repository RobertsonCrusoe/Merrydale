== Avilund Templates ==

Templates display text in a certain format, and are called by using { rather than [

*Saints pages
**include the tag <nowiki>name of saint goes here</nowiki> at the beginning of the page to include the saintly border, and to automatically create a Category associated with that saint.

*Artifacts
**Artifact pages should follow the format <nowiki>{{{artifact|1|2|3|4|5|6|7|8}}</nowiki>, where the text represented from 1 to 8 follows the format outlined in the Encyclopaedae Reliquiae Arcanum.
