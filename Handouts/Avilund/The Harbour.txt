South of Methwold and east of Sever's Cross, across Severn Harbour, lies the district known only, and appropriately, as The Harbour. This is precisely what its name implies, holding the majority of the city's current docks, shipyards and warehouses. It is a largely industrial neighbourhood with little to offer outside of the nautical professions and associated trades (and even then the brothels and taverns of Sever's Cross are preferred by the sailors themselves.) Aside from this, The Harbour is also home to the city's main fish market.
