The Marshes was built, appropriately, over drained marshland on the banks of the River Nith just to the east of Chapelton and Pondluck. The Marshes is a district that remains as poor as Chapelton and Pondluck, but, unlike those two, is rife with mass unemployment. The soft ground of The Marshes prevents the construction of buildings of any great size, so the factories and workhouses have not come here. This is perhaps the most impoverished district in the city.
