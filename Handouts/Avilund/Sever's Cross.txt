The district of Sever's Cross, located east of Lord's and north of The Fleet, is the main commercial and trades district of the city. Sever's Cross is both residential and commercial, densely housing many of the city's more prosperous tradesmen and shops in large five and six storey buildings. A reasonably clean, safe and neatly maintained district, the old town of Sever's Cross is where most of the city's middle and upper classes come to shop, dine and seek entertainment in its theatres, clubs, bordellos and taverns. Sever's Cross also houses most of the city's markets, except for livestock (located, appropriately, in the district of Hog's Market) and fish (located at The Harbour).
