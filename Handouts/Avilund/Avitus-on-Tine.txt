Avitus-on-Tine is one of 43 parishes within the County of Norweald. The parish is unique in that it has four churches; the ancient St. Avitus-on-Tine temple (a large and crumbling ruin), St. Sebald’s in the Field (an empty though sound structure), St. Ulric’s (a small chapel in moderate ruin), and St. Cardigan’s (Avitus-on-Tine), a small but reasonably well maintained structure, and the only church still in use. A priest from Sebald-upon-Tine comes once or twice a month to hold a brief service.
