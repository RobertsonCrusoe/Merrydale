Consul Benjamin Whitehall, (Master of the Royal Company of Haberdashers), in his third year of Consulship, is a Man of the City. He is the head of his Livery Company, has served several terms in the House of Commons, served in the Council of Aldermen, has twice been mayor, and holds a great deal of sway with the Freemen of the City and the other Livery Companies. Though in our country we would look down on such a trade as a ‘haberdasher’, this trade guild is quite powerful in the City, and it is mostly through his seniority in this guild that he has made his achievements. Whitehall is also a Faithful follower of St. Severinius, which also explains his success. We shall find him a useful ally when the Conclave is assembled.
