Today, or rather, tonight, I have emerged from the Hedge, not two days, but several, since the Gate. I scarcely noticed that I had emerged, as it is a moonless and overcast night, and light is but a memory to me again. I have a few scratches from the Hedge, and my mind is on fire half the time, and dreamy the other half. Now, with a hint of breeze in my hair, I again feel like I have command of my senses, though I have not these past several days. I feel like I have just woken up from a very long bout of sleep walking through a nightmare.
