Located on the south bank of the River Nith, across the ferry from Landside and The Spit, Highborough is a city that grew up independently in its own right. It continues this independence today, being formally apart from Avitus. With its smaller suburb of Farmwalk, Highborough is actually little more than a middling market town serving the majority of the farmers on the south bank of the river.  It is also the start of the Lysander Road, and as such, houses a great many inns.
