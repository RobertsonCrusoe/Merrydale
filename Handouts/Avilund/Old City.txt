right|500pxAlso known as Elderton, the Old City is located on Saints' Island at the confluence of the rivers Wen and Nith at Calder's Basin. This was the original site of the Elder City of Avitus. The oldest part of the city, the island is home to Imperial Square where the Cathedral of Saint Avitus, the Monastery of Saint Avitus, the Blue Palace of the Scion and the Palace of Saint Severinius line its sides. This has always been the traditional power centre of the city. For most of its history, the city was confined to the islands themselves, with nothing but farmland and brush on the mainland.
