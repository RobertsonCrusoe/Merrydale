Saint Muriel was the sister of Saint Avitus. She was brutally raped and murdered by her eldest brother, Gaul. Her murder was initially attributed, wrongly, to Avitus, and it was for this crime that he was banished from his tribe. She died at the age of 12.

Muriel's following is small and almost exclusively female. She is seen as the protector of rape victims, victims of incest, and victims of similar abuse. She is also seen as the patron of children, especially those who've died prematurely; She is also seen as patron of all murder victims. Families of those who have been murdered, or those who have lost a child at a young age often pray to Muriel for her assistance in seeing them safe to the Judgement.

The Canon of Saint Muriel has no societies or knightly orders. However, there are known to be well over two dozen convents dedicated to Muriel. The largest, and wealthiest is located in the city of Lysander, although similar convents can be found within the cities of Avitus and Havilah, as well as in smaller cities and towns. By its very nature, the convents of Muriel are most often located in populated areas.

It is also rumoured that one of the convents of Saint Muriel is a training ground for a secret society of nuns who make it their life's mission to hunt down murderers, rapists, and those who have committed incest. It is said that these nuns, who have taken vows of chastity, poverty and obedience, are the most lethal assassins in the Empire, and that they operate with the blessing (and some say direction) of the Scion.
