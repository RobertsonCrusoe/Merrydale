Like Saint Gideon, Saint Pitt's Town was an independent town that was annexed into the larger city over time. Unlike Saint Gideon, Saint Pitt's did not maintain its independent feel. Today, Saint Pitt's is a collection of stockyards, abandoned buildings, impoverished monasteries, and military barracks. Saint Pitt's only asset is that this is where some of the crusading orders gather, within easy distance of the necessary supply shops, but far enough away from the sinful temptations that may befall the young recruits. Saint Pitt's however, is a transient town. A way station between the provinces and Lysander for these crusaders.
