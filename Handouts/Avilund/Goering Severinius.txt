The present King of Waldheim is Goering Severinius. Goering is the patriarch of the Severinius family, and is as firm a picture of Old Blood as one would expect in a king. He is 61 years old, and is the picture of health and vitality. He spends most of his time in the City, though his throne is technically in Stonesaddle. Though he is difficult to approach on account of his retinue and guards, the man is only somewhat aloof in his dealings with people, and he does keep appointments with people outside the gentry.
