Located east of Severn Harbour, the neighbourhoods of Waverleigh, Chapelton and Pondluck are, perhaps, indistinguishable from one another. They are poor, dirty, industrial districts which house most of the city's working poor. These neighbourhoods are home to the multitude masses of the city, and are punctuated by the black smoke of the factory fires. While it is not unusual for the more affluent citizens of the city to look down with scorn at these poor neighbourhoods, those with any bit of sense recognise that this is the necessary industrial engine that drives the city.
