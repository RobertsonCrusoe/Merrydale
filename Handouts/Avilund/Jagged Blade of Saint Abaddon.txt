From Sir Geofrey Golfschalk's Encyclopaedae Reliquiae Arcanum, 1723 edition

The library's copy of the Enc. Rel. Arc. is damaged. Scoundrels have torn out several pages from this volume, including the entry for the Jagged Blade of Saint Abbadon. Lord's University asks for your patience and understanding while the library attempts to track down an undamaged copy of this publication.

~ Lord's Library, July 19th, 1726
