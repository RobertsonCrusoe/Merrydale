Consul Lucius Ulmar, (Count Ulmar) is in his first year of Consulship. He is another member of the Old Blood, a Noble of no small standing, and a Brother of the Pilgrims of Vander. That he was admitted into the Pentarchy, past the veto power of Proconsul Falconer indicates that these two powerful families are again allies. This suggests a certain degree of unification and coordination on the part of the Families of the Old Blood, likely in response to the coming of the Pentanum. Their timing is impeccable.
