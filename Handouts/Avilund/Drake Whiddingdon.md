Consul Drake Whiddingdon, (Earl of St. Troy-on-the-Moor), in his second year of Consulship, is a lowly-placed noble from an obscure county, but he is a crafty politician, with years of service in the Senate, the Council of Aldermen and the House of Lords. He controls a good number of the Livery Companies (the City of Waldheim equivalent of trade guilds) of the City through political guile alone, and has been actively increasing the size of the Republican Army for the last year.
