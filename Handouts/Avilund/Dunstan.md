Saint Dunstan was the eldest of Severinius' two sons by Ida. He was an Angelspawn and a fierce warrior by all accounts, as well as a skilled craftsman with both iron and wood. Dunstan never succeeded his father in the leadership of the tribe. That honour fell to his younger brother, Sebald. For his part, Dunstan was the tribal champion and blacksmith, as well as a gifted carver and carpenter. The feats of combat prowess, the tales of battles and of physical tests found within his canon are many. As are the tales and legends of the dozens upon dozens of magical items, relics and artefacts he is said to have created.

Dunstan is seen as a patron of craftsmen, athletes, hard work, and soldiers. His is a crusading and missionary sect, often successfully combining technical innovation with proven tactical methods. Saint Dunstan boasts of a fairly large number of monasteries, clerical societies, and knightly orders, and has a well organised, devout and wealthy following.

Dunstan's followers are unique in the faith in that they have often fully supported the advancement and procurement of knowledge. His monasteries have, in the past, served as scriptoriums and laboratories, as well as workshops and factories for the creation of all manner of tools, weapons and implements. Dunstan's monks are credited (though with some heavy doubts by some scholars) with the invention of gunpowder, among other things.

Unlike most other crusading canons, Dunstan's knights take full advantage of the technological and scientific advances and advantages available in our modern era. While a knight of Saint Avitus might still take to the field mounted and in full armour at the head of a cavalry charge, Dunstan's crusaders are more often found sensibly dressed and armed with firearms and light weapons. Although, the true value of Dunstan's knight in the modern day lies not with his prowess with a blade or even with his accuracy with a musket, but, rather, in his skill with artillery of all types.

Despite the mostly military nature of Dunstan's Canon, he is still a patron of innovation. For this, his followers are always very well regarded and received in most of the world's institutions of learning. However, it was for this reason that Saint Dunstan's canon suffered the heaviest during The Cleansing.
