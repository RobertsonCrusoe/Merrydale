thumb|Mark of Issilith Dalael

Youngest brother to Quevin. Married to Fea Eltorchul.

He has had the longest-running open relationship with a Noble Family of any of his brothers, despite his young age. He married into the Eltorchul family 12 years ago, and today has two children: 11 year-old Julia and 10 year-old Quevin. Issilith is known to be the business man of the Dalael. It was he who introduced Quellar to the Dezlentyr Family many years ago, long after he had already initiated trade dealings with that House. 

Issilith is the easiest of the Dalael to find, as he is always about on business. He goes from one Noble Villa to the next, and from many businesses in the City, conducting the bulk of the Company’s business. According to Agamemnon, Issilith is the most law-abiding of the three brothers, and may actually crave having a legitimate business on the surface. Perhaps becoming a father will do that to the rest of them.
