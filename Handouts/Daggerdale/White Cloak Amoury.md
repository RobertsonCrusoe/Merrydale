Found on the 3rd floor of White Cloak Tower, this large room has a thick metal door which only Lumina and the Company Quartermaster have keys for.

The armoury is furnished with armour and weapon racks, wooden and of spartan appearance, along with numerous wall-hanging shelves and chests of a similar theme. Many are empty. There are several hooks of thick iron embedded in the walls, mostly for holding vast quantities of rope and other \"hangables\". A large, ornate bookshelf stands out from the other furniture, and contain only vials and flasks full of curious liquids.

In, on, and around these objects are the following equipment:

*10 spare wooden chests, stacked and piled
*10 spare backpacks, hung and orderly
*15 spare belt pouches, hung and orderly
*10 spare bedrolls, piled in a chest
*25 spare waterskins, hung and orderly
*5 sets of well-made clothing, unisex, folded in a chest
*5 prepared adventurer kits, carefully placed in a chest

*5 crowbars, blackened iron, piled in a chest
*5 hammers, blackened iron, piled in the same chest
*5 grappling hook heads, blackened iron, piled in the same chest
*30 pitons, blackened iron, bound in groups of 5, piled by the same chest
*25 sections of hempen rope, 50 ft each, hung along the walls
*5 prepared climbing kits, carefully placed in a chest

*50 candles, packaged safely in a chest
*240 pints of oil in a 30 gallon barrel, in a dirty corner
*30 copper flasks, 1 pint capacity, stacked neatly on shelves
*5 lanterns, burns 6 hours on 1 pint of oil, hung individually along the walls
*50 torches, wrapped in straw and packed in chests
*10 sunrods, wrapped in cloth and carefully arranged on a shelf
*5 sets of flint and steel, in a chest

*5 daggers, well-made, hung from a rack
*2 longswords, well-made, on a separate rack
*3 maces, well-made, on a separate rack
*300 arrows, standard make, separated into bundles of 30, cloth-wrapped, piled neatly
*200 quarrels, standard make, separated into bundles of 20, cloth-wrapped, piled neatly

*5 horns, bleached with leather straps, hung from a hook
*1 lute, plain and untuned, leaning against the bookshelf

Specifically on the ornate bookshelf, sitting on folded pieces of cloth:

*2 gravespawn potions, purple and ill-smelling
*2 potions of healing, deep cyan and bitter
*2 lesser potions of invisibilty, mostly clear with an oily feel
*2 potions of life shield, lemony with a shade of grey
*2 potions of regeneration, russet with a coppery tinge
*2 potions of resistance (necrotic), thick like dark, dark honey

A solemn desk leans against the wall. Sitting beside it is a chest, stained with a red finish and gilded in silver. Inside are six pale sacks filled with 500 gold pieces each. The chest is secured by an ornate gold lock featuring two slots. It is Gondsman in origin, and bears the motifs and iconography of the sect. 

Two keys open the chest: One is kept inside of the lantern hanging behind the desk, dangling off the hinged door on a small piece of wire. The second is contained within the desk itself, the means to access it being a third key in the quartermaster's possession. All 3 keys are thin rectangular tabs of metal 2 1/2 inches long, with square bites missing at several intervals along three of their four edges. Each one appears to be made of quality steel, with the one kept in the lantern bearing light discolouration from the heat.
