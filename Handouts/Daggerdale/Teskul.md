Suspicious fellow that seemed to be Thagdul’s right-hand-man. As it turns out, he was also romancing Dragon. He spent a great deal of time tailing the Company of the Red Herring.

In 1372, Teskul hired Grimwald's Rangers to pursue the parties who departed North and East.

*Status: At large
