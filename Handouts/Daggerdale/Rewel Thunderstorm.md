Formerly a ranking Dawn Priest in the service of Lathander under High Dawnlord Cassimar in Shadowdale, Thuderstorm broke with Cassimar's congregation and joined the Eastern Storm during the crusade that purged Dagger Falls in 1372. Though he has reconciled with Cassimar, Thunderstorm remains in Dagger Falls as High Dawnlord of the newly established Lathanderite congregation in that town.
