Jasper's Shopping and Crafting - Post-Battle Tenday, Mirtul 1483 DR
by Gus Miranda (as Jasper), Stephen Robertson (as Lucan), Les Blackwell (DM)
Email thread: "Jasper's shopping and crafting", 15 Jun - 1 Jul 2025

Day-by-day tenday plans for Jasper and Lucan following the Battle of Thistle Ridge on Greengrass/Mirtul 1, 1483 DR. Includes Jasper's spell learning, crafting, and shopping, plus Lucan's information-gathering rounds in Dagger Falls. Les asks Todd and Scott to provide similar breakdowns for their characters.

---

GUS (15 Jun 2025, 10:49 PM) — initial shopping/crafting list:

Over the course of the next few days, this is what Jasper would like to do:

He will split his time between Thistle Ridge, the lab in the Tower and the Limestone Court.

In between I'm sure I will have conversations with Nemia and Alexandus at Thistle Ridge, and Contessa Calix and Masra at the Limestone Court.

I also want to talk to Fiona again, because I should probably fix that, and I don't think I left that well.

In fact, I will invite her to come with me to the Limestone Court and meet the Contessa and the fey brides, in order to make up for it.

Spell Learning from Mari:
- Cantrips: Message, Needle Darts
- Level 1: Enfeeble, Helpful Steps, Lock, Mending, Necromancer's Generosity
- Level 2: Cleanse Air, Create Food, Darkvision, Deafness, Dispel Magic, Environmental Endurance, Peaceful Rest, Radiant Field, Resist Energy, Translate, Water Breathing

Shopping at Meagre's:
- Sell Accursed Staff (250gp) and Beast Staff (340gp)
- Purchase Divination Staff (60gp) and Bag of Holding (400gp)
- Jasper does not like the aesthetic of carrying a staff: requests it be a crystal ball with an eyeball in the centre, like how his Staff of Water was made into a canteen

Scroll purchases (2nd Level): Blur, Gecko Grip, Illusory Creature, Knock, Mirror Image, Mist, Umbral Extraction, Web

Scroll purchases (3rd Level): Bind Undead, Blindness, Clairaudience, Cozy Cabin, Enthrall, Fireball, Ghostly Weapon, Gravity Well, Haste, Hypnotize, Impending Doom, Lightning Bolt, Mind of Menace, Paralyze, Phantom Prison, Rouse Skeletons, Safe Passage, Shared Invisibility, Slow, Wall of Shadow, Wall of Thorns

Crafting: Repair the Skeleton Key; Craft Wondrous Figurine (Onyx Dog); Scribe scrolls (Thunderstrike, Enlarge, Final Sacrifice, Pest Form, See the Unseen, Summon Elemental)

GUS (29 Jun 2025, 11:28 PM) — revised day-by-day tenday:

- Greengrass: Assault on Thistle Ridge at night
- Mirtul 1: Battle of Thistle Ridge, banners unfurled; attends to Coventina's body with Mari; Mari teaches Jasper new spells
- Mirtul 2: Various personages arrive, Council of War; talks with Nemia, Alexandus and Lucan
- Mirtul 3: Travel to Dagger Falls; returns Coventina's remains to her family; returns stolen caravan supplies to Sanduskan; "Traumatises Fiona"; stays with his brother and family, NOT at the Embassy
- Mirtul 4: Visits Meagre; invites Fiona to Limestone Court; tells Lucan that Mortimer will stay with him (Lucan can recall Jasper via the familiar); LEAVES DAGGER FALLS, PLANNING NEVER TO RETURN
- Mirtul 5: At the Tower — repairs Skeleton Key, crafts Onyx Dog Figurine
- Mirtul 6: Travels to Limestone Court via Greenwood, collecting Fiona
- Mirtul 7-9: Hiding from the world, scribing scrolls, spending time with Masra among the fey
- Mirtul 10: Returns to Greenwood Farm and the Tower

Revised spell learning from Mari (Arcana +11, Spell Prodigy, Magical Shorthand — 60 minutes total):
- 1st Level (DC15): Enfeeble, Helpful Steps, Mending
- 2nd Level (DC18): Darkvision, Dispel Magic, Radiant Field

Revised crafting (Crafting +12):
- Repair the Skeleton Key (DC20)
- Wondrous Figurine of an Onyx Dog (DC14)
- Scrolls: Invisibility x4, Dispel Magic x4, Radiant Field x4

Purchases: Sell Accursed Staff and Beast Staff, buy Bag of Holding; various scrolls and alchemicals
