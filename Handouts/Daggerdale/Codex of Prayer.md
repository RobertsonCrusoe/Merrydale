This is a book that was referenced in Abandon Hope, All Ye Who Enter Here.  It contains an elaborate ritual to return the souls of those that have fallen prey to the ritual known as Bel's Bargain.

Nathan Gale sent a message to the  asking if they knew about the book.  Brother Neddle actually sent an amanuensis of the relevant rituals from this book to Magistrate Invictus in order to help the free the victims of the Sons of Bel.
