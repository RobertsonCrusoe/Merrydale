The remains of Gaebril Tremalkin were originally interred alongside his brother-in-arms, Maturin Horgath. A month after the battle with the dragon, the demon and the Sharrans, it was revealed that part of Tremalkin’s remains were missing. Tremalkin’s right hand had not burned entirely and, in fact, had turned to what appeared to be stone.  It was discovered a month later that Tremalkin’s right hand had been taken from the monastery.  Attempts at scrying the location of the hand revealed that it was east of the monastery and no doubt, being taken deeper into the desert of Anauroch.  No attempt was made to retrieve the hand of Gaebril Tremalkin, but after the desecration, the remains of Gaebril Tremalkin and the remains of Maturin Horgath were moved to the secured vault that had been created by Loremaster Kennyth Thames and the unidentified Gondsman he had with him for assistance.}}
