Jeska gathers the team.. including Merry, tired as we are.. on the night of
the attack after the meeting with the SSL.

"We have won this ridiculous competition... our status in this town is now
greatly improved. I care not for a meeting of shareholders. We have
contracts and Mr Ferguson.. I have no desire for personal contact, we will
honor our contract but possibly not to his expectation."
Jeska smiles...
"Instead, by word of mouth, I would like a gathering of everyone in the
city at the scene of the attack. Chrysalis will drive the repair, and win
the hearts of the Dalesfolk.. I will speak to them.``
Jeska smiles...
"As an aside.. we now own a town.. or 5/6th of it... we should leverage
that with all businesses in town.. we gain credit for the promise of
buildings and land... the wee child with chickens is a genius negotiator..
we learn from her."
Jeska does not smile...
"Without support of the commoners and shopkeepers we fail, no matter how
successful we are at cleansing Thistle Ridge. We cannot rely on the rich
alone to sponsor our effort. Go out.. make deals."

With that she bows to each of you in turn.. "We are Chrysalis.. a new birth
awaits".

and what to do about Ferguson.
