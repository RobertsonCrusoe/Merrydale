A letter from Quevin Dalael to Kendal Thorne.

Mr. Thorne;

Please find, enclosed, the head of your missing friend, Mr. Rhork. This fine fellow put up a splendid fight before my men took him and the girl, killed all the fools at the Caravan House, and burned the confounded place to the ground.

You should know that Mr. Rhork had a very loose tongue towards the end of my ‘debriefing’ session with him, and that he may have let slip some details he should not have. His commitment to your organization, it would seem, was not above that of his commitment to his own bodily integrity, and now I have a very tidy list of the people who work for you.

And what else have I? Why, your little doll, Mhairne is now residing with me and my folk, quite happily at the moment. You shall find her pendant in that messy box I have sent. While she is not currently in a state of violation, I can assure you that this will well change, if you do not do as I shall ask here.

Please come to my home as soon as you can, to discuss the ugly matter of your men attacking my Kin. I should like to talk over the terms and boundaries of our businesses, so that I will not have to spend valuable resources on retaliating against your hostilities.  If you are occupied, or otherwise indisposed, and can not make it to such a meeting in the immediate future, please send a trusted representative. Or send Steele.

Thank you.

Mhairne and I will be expecting your reply.

— Q.D.}}
