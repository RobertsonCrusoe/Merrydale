Letter to Malveau Greenleaf - Jasper Offers Employment
by Gus Miranda (as Jasper Hawkford)
Email thread: "Letter to Malveau Greenleaf", 19-27 Jan 2025

An in-character letter from Jasper Hawkford to Malveau Greenleaf, sent via Brother Vessuvian. Jasper offers employment as a scout and ranger for Company Chrysalis, to patrol the perimeter beyond the Sunblade Foresters and train the young soldiers in surviving Cormanthor and the Fey. Written after the party has slain Karn and retaken Thistle Ridge Tower.

---

A letter sent by Jasper Hawkford via Brother Vessuvian.

To Malveau Greenleaf,

I write on behalf of Company Chrysalis with an offer of immediate employment. We are in need of your particular skills and expertise.

We have secured the region of Greenwood Farm, which is presently being revived and strengthened. As well, we have pushed further east, and have slain the bugbear Karn and retaken Thistle Ridge Tower, securing control of the land separating Greenwood and the Tower in the process.

However, as we proceed with our efforts to reclaim Thistle Ridge Farm in the East, we are concerned about the growing encroachment of Cormanthor and the Fey on the lands we have liberated from the goblins around Greenwood Farm in the West.

No one knows this area of the Dale as well as you do. No one knows the threat of the Fey as well as you do.

We would engage your services as scout and ranger, keeping eyes on the perimeter beyond where the Sunblade Foresters patrol, and bringing news to us of any growing or potential threats that lay within these lands so that we may deal with them before the settlers at Greenwood are imperilled.

Secondly, we would be honoured if you would pass your knowledge onwards to the young Foresters who patrol the borders for us now. Give them the skills and knowledge you possess, so that they may stand a better chance of surviving Cormanthor and any encounter with the Fey.

You have seen the deeds of which we are capable. We are advancing with purpose, and will succeed in liberating Thistle Ridge Farm. Please help us ensure that that success is permanent.

If you are amenable to discussing this offer, please meet us at Greenwood Farm at your earliest convenience.

Yours,
Jasper Hawkford of Company Chrysalis
