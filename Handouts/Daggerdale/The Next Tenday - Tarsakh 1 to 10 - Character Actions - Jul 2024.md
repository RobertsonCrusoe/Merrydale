*Tarsakh 1 to 10*

After we have done our shareholder's meeting, and over the course of this
tenday, Jasper is going to do the following:

• Pay a visit to Sanduskan Vale and exposition dump about what we have
found, leaving out the information we agreed not to include in our
intelligence report.  (Deception of -1, if you want to drag some extra
information out of him)

• I want to pay a visit to Nemia to make sure she is sticking around, and
attempt to convince her to work more closely with us.  By the end of the
tenday I hope we can zero in on what we need to cast the ritual again and
learn more about the Alavaxi girl.  I VERY MUCH would like for her to teach
me to perform this ritual.  If she doesn't, I'm still going to pay very
close attention the next time we perform it, as well as remembering from
last time, to see if I can learn to perform it myself.

• I'm going to visit Alex my BFF and try to convince him to stick around as
well, and join us in direct employment.  I think he would be a good
addition to our Sunblade Company, as not only a good advisor for them, but
also to fling fireballs around if needed.

• I'm going to spend quite a bit of time in the library, looking up
whatever I can find about Sylvanus worship, the Fey and the Feywild ( I
speak Fey), Cormathnor, goblins, hobgoblins, bugbears, Maglubiyet,
myconids, Chesenta, Unther, Sembia, gargoyles, purple worms, Netheril, the
Shadovar, Randal Morn, Shadowdale, the flora and fauna of the Dales,
Hillsfar, and halfling cuisine.  (I have Library Lore +9.)

I am going to the library twice a day.  I go in and the first thing I do is
request to be given access to the Historial Wing.  When that is denied, I
go and check out a handful of books on the topics above. As I leave, I stop
and ask if I can access the Historical Wing.  When that is denied, I go
away and read, and come back in the afternoon.  As I arrive, I ask for
permission to access the Historical Wing. When that is denied, I return the
books I borrowed in the morning, and borrow another handful, from my list
of topics above.  As I leave, I once again ask for access to the Historical
Wing.

The next day, I go in and ask for permission to access the Historical Wing,
etc.  Repeat the same as before. I do this every single day.  Maybe after
the 20th time I've asked for permission, Evil Bad Guy Invictus will ban me
from the library, as I expect an Evil Bad Guy to do, or maybe he'll agree
to speak with me.

I also go see if anyone has borrowed the books that I returned the day
before.  I do this every time, to see if there is the same person borrowing
the same books.  Over the course of the tenday I would've borrowed
somewhere north of 50 books on a wide assortment of unrelated topics.  I am
genuinely reading the books and  interested in their contents, but I am
also looking to see if someone is following me.

*(I am also purposefully NOT returning "A Theory on the Hauntings of
Thistle Ridge". Or at least, I'll have a copy made before we return it.  I
am curious, though: shouldn't this book be from the Historical Wing?  I
thought these sorts of things were banned. )*

• I am going to seek out Magistrate Winters' tomb in the mausoleum, and see
how easy it would be to break into it.  Is it just a wooden casket on a
shelf, or is he behind a stone wall?  I'm hoping to be able to snag a piece
of bone or clothing. Right now, I'm just looking, but I can turn myself
into a rat or similar for up to 30 minutes, and I have Mortimer.

• I am going to collect my items from the respective merchants that I had
ordered previously*:*

1.  My *Wand of Pocket Library* from Meagre.  So long as I use this during

2. My *Scroll of Everlight* from Meagre.  Once I have this scroll I will

3. My scroll folio.

For Jeska, in this first tenday, she is aloof, even for her.
She spends much of the first day in silence, sitting, rolling the blue
wraps around her wrists again and again...while staring at the doll wearing
them.

The next morning she sits in the lower chamber eyes closed, legs crossed
(in an almost impossible way).. for an hour.
She nods to no-one.. then Jeska smiles.

She takes off her chain and sets it on a table, picks up the poisoned
dagger on the same table and leaves the embassy. No words spoken.

The blue wraps are on her wrists...

In the days that follow she is Jeska, but if you are up late at night you
will see her on the balcony.. weaving through rhythmic forms in a strange
dance...
That she is concentrating is clear.. at times pleased.. at times
frustrated.. you cannot tell why her mood changes...

If awake, and watching on the sixth night she nods to no-one.. and Jeska
smiles.
She jumps from standing to the second level, grabbing the railing and
pulling herself up with ease over the rail.
She effortlessly climbs to the top of the highest pillar... then just
drops.. she seems to glide to the lowest level.. rolls, and darts across
the room.
Was a hand touching the pillar.. a foot?

Merry and the Night Guards were watching.. even if you were preoccupied and
were not.
They feel assured and the next day they seem to find their tasks less
daunting.. achieved with confidence and ease.....
