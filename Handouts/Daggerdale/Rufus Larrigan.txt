Originally from Mistledale, Rufus Larrigan, the \"Touched Priest\" of Selûne was a rodentine lycanthrope and Harper. 

Status: Rufus was killed in Neverwinter by Malkor Githeas.

== Significant Documents ==
* Rufus' notes on the theft of the Heart of the Earth.
* Entries from Rufus' Journal, recovered in Neverwinter
* Rufus' Will
