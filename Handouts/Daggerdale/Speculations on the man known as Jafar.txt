Was Jafar a Zhentarim agent from the very beginning?

*After the first raid on the Dalael Safehouse in Northward, Jafar offered to take possession of the collection of scrolls and journals that we had taken. He admitted to taking them to his friend Feldon Azadand (of Company Drurginz) to be identified, and stashes them at the HQ of WOMP for safekeeping.
*Jafar rented a room from Garth soon after the CRH met Thagdul. At the next meeting with Thagdul, he and Jafar purported to know each other from “dealings in the South” (Jafar was masquerading as a Calishite at that time).
*After the heist, Jafar was particularly insistent that we take the books (minus the scroll and Draconomicon) to Serpentil’s, where we find Quevin and soon thereafter lose the books to him.
*Jafar gave Quevin the impression that he was working on some project at the meeting. He claimed “I’m working on it!” Quevin’s mysterious rejoinder was “You’d better be.”
*The books were surrendered to Magistrate Thuman, (who, in all fairness, was later murdered by Jafar). Thuman was in the employ of Ersgul, and at the seizure was in the company of both Yarkul and Quevin.
*The seizure of the books by Thumin also resulted in Thagdul gaining leverage over the CRH.
*The encounter with the three men occurred at the same time that the houses of Tomas, Ellyth and Esperel were broken into. Garth’s house is not touched – Garth’s house was also Jafar’s house.
*Jafar reveals after Garth’s encounter with Dally that he is \"familiar\" with him from the Watchful Order.
*While the rest of the party focuses on dealings with the Reprobates, Jafar researched beholders.
*Jafar was not accounted for in the fight that occurred with the Reprobates the night the Heart of the Earth was stolen.
