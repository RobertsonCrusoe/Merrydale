One of many Harper ballads about the Liberation of Dagger Falls and the Heroes who brought the Zhentarim to their knees in 1372.
----

:\"In 1372 we took a little trip,
:Across the desert Anauroch, to take away the whips,
:We had the hero Dorian, and Maturin the Grey,
:With Sparrow and a Dwarven Dread
:The Fat Man led the way

:\"We lost our guns but the Zhents they kept a'comin'
:There wasn’t quite as many as there was a while ago
:With one heroic strike, we sent the Zhents a'runnin'
:Across the mighty desert to the nine hells far below.

:\"Ol' Sparrow said we could take them by surprise
:If he killed their mighty wizard while the sleep was in his eyes
:The Harpers helped to destroy the snakes with wings
:And we cleared the Town of Dagger Falls of all the evil things.

:\"We lost our guns but the Zhents they kept a'comin'
:There wasn’t quite as many as there was a while ago
:With one heroic strike, we sent the Zhents a'runnin'
:Across the mighty desert to the nine hells far below.

:\"We freed the slaves and returned them to their towns
:But the slabs were off for Anauroch b'fore the snows came down
:Shar had sent a paladin, with Garrik it made five
:To free the sands of Anarauch and leave no Zhents alive

:\"We lost our guns but the Zhents they kept a'comin'
:There wasn’t quite as many as there was a while ago
:With one heroic strike, we sent the Zhents a'runnin'
:Across the mighty desert to the nine hells far below.

:“O we fought through the guards,
:We fought through the gen'rals,
:We fought all the leaders of each mighty Zhent outpost
:We fought so hard that the Zhents'll not forget us
:The Five brave fighters who came on them in the snow.\"
