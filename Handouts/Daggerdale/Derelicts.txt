The victims of the Sons of Bel and the vile ritual known as Bel's Bargain, these people have their souls taken from their bodies.  Derelicts then become the mindless slaves of the villain that made them.  These 'husks' walk around muttering things quietly to themselves, often in the Infernal Tongue.

The very nature of these soulless bodies is such an anathema to the living that they cause significant psychic damage to those that get too close.

The ritual is reversable, however.  There is an elaborate ritual found in the Codex of Prayer that can reverse this bargain, provided the body is still 'alive', and that the Soul Key that contains the soul of the victim is available.

In late autumn of 1481 DR, in the secret sub-basement of the Yellow Abbey, many high-ranking clergy of the Church caried out this prayer, and returned the souls of five victims to their bodies.
