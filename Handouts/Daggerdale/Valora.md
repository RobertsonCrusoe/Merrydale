Valora is the eldest daughter of the master alchemist, Arcadium.  She is about 30 years old, and is an attractive, curvy brunette who does an excellent job of running the business portion of this establishment (a portion which her father is mostly incompetent at).  Valora is known to be an uncommonly good judge of character, and some whisper that she is some sort of mind-reading witch.

It is rumoured that she will hire adventurers to gather rare or dangerous components in the wilds, if she thinks they are capable.  She operates outside of the bounty system, however, and is very discrete about her business.
