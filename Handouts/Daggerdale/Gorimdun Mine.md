This is an old mine that has had centuries of excavation.  It was originally a back-door to Tethyamar.  This entrance was lost nearly 1000 years ago, and since that time it has been serched for by many groups and organizations.  The Zhents actually excavated and found the gates to Tethyamar in 1371, but kept it a close secret.  These gates were discovered again by Deadnettle sometime in the 1478 or 1479.  She set up a colony of Trollspawn, using a Troll Queen and the stolen eggs of the black dragon, Kruhwahll to create a very resilient race of Black Trolls.

The White Cloaks found this lair, destroyed the trolls, and drove off Deadnettle.  They discovered the doors, and kept the discovery secret for only a little while.  Lumina Whitegate revealed the existence of the doors at the Situation Report on the 1st of Kythorn.

As of late summer, 1480 DR, the Tethyamar League is preparing for an expedtion to the doors to verify their authenticity.  This could change the politics of the region tremendously.
