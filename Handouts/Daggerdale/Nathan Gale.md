At thirty-two years of age Gale is the oldest member of the White Cloaks Bravo Team, and one of the oldest in the entire company, Gale is a member of a special branch of the clergy of Candle Keep.  He was sent to Daggerdale on a secret errand from the Keep, and has been in contact with the , in particular reporting to and getting information from Brother Neddle.

In the final battle in the corn-fields surrounding Dwimmerholme Farm, Gale received an assuredly fatal blow; a blast of arcane energy delivered by the tiefling warlock, Vetis.  He was only saved by the Belial Prayerbook which he was carrying in his vest.  The book absorbed the blast and saved his life.
