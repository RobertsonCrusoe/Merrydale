Haskar Hunabar, the elder, is the patriarch of the Waterdhavian noble house of Hunabar. It is known to the PCs that he and his family are werewolves, and worshippers of the divine patron of lycanthropes, Muir'Quaggoth. However, this would seem to be a secret well-kept among Waterdeep's upper classes.
