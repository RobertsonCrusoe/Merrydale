DDC – 1481 – 3: Situation Report for Administrator Reks, 29 Mirtul, 1481 
Being a note delivered by runner from Base Camp Silver to Loedegan Close, from Captain Iolus Warburton 
GFD Notes: 
Here we read the first real account of how difficult things are in Tethyamar. Iolus explains the difficulties to the Administrator, and effectively tells him to staff up, whatever the cost. 

This note was graciously provided by the Administrator, who seemed over-delighted to show it to me. I think this is the first legitimate order given by Iolus in his new capacity as captain to Reks, and it legitimized the Administrator. 
-GFD 
Reks; 
Paper is difficult to come by at Base Camp Silver, so I'll keep this short. 
We have just been delivered from the black maze of the depths after more than 30 days of wandering lost in the dangerous Lower Halls. The size of Tethyamar is incomprehensible to me thus far. 

I report that Bollinger, Durandan, Creed and Guggenheim have all returned relatively unharmed with me, though we needed rescuing by the very dwarves we are there to help. We will tie up some loose ends here, and return to the Close in about 3 days. 

We will need to redouble our efforts in Tethyamar; please arrange a tournament presently to select a new team. I am sure Troia will have some candidates up at the Castle. Be sure to talk to her before you declare the tournament. I'll say it again; talk to Troia first, and get her help. 

And start figuring out how we're going to pay for all of this. We have nothing to share out from our first delve into Tethyamar, and although that might change as we start to make progress, this endeavour is going to cost us more than it gains for several months, I expect. 

I'll be expecting a full report from you in 3 days. Prepare yourself accordingly. 
-Captain Warburton
