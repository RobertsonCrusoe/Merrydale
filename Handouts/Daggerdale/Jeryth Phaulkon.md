Jeryth Phaulkon is the last living member of the trading family established in Waterdeep by her father, Lord Bronson Phaulkon. She is also an experienced ranger and the Chosen of Mielikki.

Jeryth left Waterdeep in early Mirtul of 1372 to harry the Zhentarim in the East. By the spring of 1373, she was conducting a guerilla war against the Zhent forces in Dekanter. Together with members of the Eastern Storm, Phaulkon liberated Dekanter.

After the events of 1371-1372, and the departure of Khelben Arunsun from the organization, Jeryth Phaulkon is also probably the most senior Harper left alive.
