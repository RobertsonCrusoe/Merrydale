A note sent to Alen Reks from Iolus Warburton.

==Eleint 19, 1481 The Year of the Grinning Halfling==

Administrator Reks, I hope this missive finds you in good health and high in spirits.

The task of attaining Thorbardson's Throne has been an interesting, if not challenging one. We find ourselves in a situation in which the denizens of Tethyamar are no longer content with simply defending themselves against our scouting parties and cleansing of the ancient dwarven home. They are now pushing back at our defensive lines; some of which are displaying surprisingly impressive tactics.

I believe that in time; we will prove successful in our endeavour but assistance will be necessary.

I ask of you now to contact all active members of the White Cloaks and inform them that their presence is required in Tethyamar immediately. I will leave it to you to arrange missives to all of our active members save two exclusions that I will share later. All members are to gather at Provisioner's Hall as quickly as possible and once everyone is accounted for and prepared, they are to make way for Tethyamar.

The first exclusion to my order is Alystair Dretch. Alystair is to be the eyes and ears in the 'dale. Trust is something difficult to come by in these times and although Alystair and I do not always agree upon courses of action, I trust him completely. He can and should be expected to put Amaunator's will and the greater good first and foremost and I would advise you to expect such when dealing with him in the future.

The second exclusion to my order is you. Where Alystair will watch the 'dale, you will be the eyes and ears in Dagger Falls and Castle Radiance. From my understanding, only Vanion and myself are aware of your unique skill set. I am also assuming that Durandal is also aware although I have not discussed it with him, I believe that since the three of you arrived at the Castle around the same time, he is also aware. While I am in Tethyamar, you are now the acting Captain of the White Cloaks. I mentioned trust before, Alen and I will do so again. I trust you to make decisions that will benefit the 'Dale first and foremost, the White Cloaks second to that and your own well-being the third. I am trusting the White Cloaks to you. Something that good, brave men and women have bled for and died for. Much of what is wrong with Daggerdale is now tied up in administrative process and bureaucracy. The White Cloaks have freedom to work outside of that and will be as effective as you are creative and courageous. Whatever was said between you and I in Loedegan’s Close was simply an opportunity to bring you fully over to my side. I harbour no ill will towards you and I will not blame you if you harbour any ill will towards me. I expect you to be as patient and smart as you are clever and swift, Alen. Take care of the White Cloaks as if they were your own because as of this very moment, they are your own.

I require you to form a new team. It might very well be that you will be sending these individuals to Tethyamar as well should you receive further word from me.

The structure will remain roughly the same as it had been.

Prior to a mission being given to your new recruits, you will select one to be an acting sergeant. Should that individual be assigned acting sergeant and be deemed as doing an acceptable role for five or more missions they will be offered a promotion to full time sergeant.

The coin and treasure gathered from each mission will be collected at the end of each mission and an amount will be settled upon in the value of gold coins.

This overall amount will be broken into ten shares that will be divided as follows.

*4 shares goes to the White Cloaks
*1 share goes to the church of Amaunator
*1 share goes to each member of that mission

Should more than five members be sent on a mission, each additional member above five will have their share come out of the White Cloak's shares.

Should less than five be sent on a mission, the additional shares not claimed will go to the Church of Amaunator.

Should a member perish while on a mission; that individual's share shall go to a party of their choice prior to going on the mission.

Should a permanent and not an ACTING sergeant be present on a mission, that sergeant shall receive the church's share and that individual may do whatever they wish with that second share.

I expect the White Cloaks to have at least one quarter of its members to be devout and learned in the ways of Amaunator. It is through His light alone that we have dispelled the looming shadows for as long as we have. This company was founded on Amaunatori principles and it will continue to display these traits as it will serve the company as well as you in dealings with Castle Radiance and other members of the higher clergy.

One fifth of magical or enchanted items recovered or claimed by a White Cloak mission will go to the White Cloaks armoury. Those on the mission will be granted first selection of enchanted items available with the remaining one fifth given to the White Cloak's armoury. I will leave it to your discretion to decide upon how this armoury is used.

Loedegan’s Close will also begin accepting children that were orphaned by the war. You will want a cook as well as someone to see to the children so that you are still free to run things. This is important, Alen. These children are alone. They need guidance and security. The White Cloaks can provide both. I will leave it to your good judgement to decide how many children to take into the Close.

I feel a shadow growing over me, Alen. I feel as if we have only seen a glimpse of what awaits us in Tethyamar and I fear I have chosen an incorrect course. I will burn bright. I will put my faith in the Lord On High and trust that the White Cloaks will prove successful here. I put my faith in you to see to the success of the White Cloaks outside of Tethyamar.

Be the light in the darkness, Alen.

Yours in the light;

— Captain Iolus Warburton
Servant of Amaunator
