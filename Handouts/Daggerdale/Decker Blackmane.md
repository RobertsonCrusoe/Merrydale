The Mayor of Tesh Valley Farm. This man is 51  years old, and married the daughter of Argent Whitegate (Aestis, died 1478).  The Blackmanes have long been worshipers of Chauntea and Torm, and it was the grandfather of Decker who designed and built this settlement.  He is openly hostile to the Whitegate Family, and his many children have tried to infiltrate the power structure of the Dale, mostly unsuccessful.  Decker was aloof with the White Cloaks, knowing that they take their orders from Lumina.
