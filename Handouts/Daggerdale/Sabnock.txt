A goblin/tiefling in the service of the Sons of Bel, with the rank of Magus this hexer/warlock was devious, slippery and was able to turn the battlefield into a quagmire.  He was killed outside of the lodge that he had taken over and corrupted, by the White Cloaks Bravo Team.  Before his destruction, he was able to create 4 Derelicts, the fate of which caused great debate among the neophyte team.

Sabnock had a large team of hobgoblins and bugbears under his command.
