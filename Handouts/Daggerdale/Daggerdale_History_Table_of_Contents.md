Prefatory Matter

I. Foreword from an Itinerant Scholar – Grafton Fitzdecker … p. 8
A spirited introductory letter from the compiler of the present volume, wherein he recounts the curious provenance of the documents enclosed, his dealings with the eccentric elf Steele, and his heartfelt devotion to one Sarah Estelmer. Readers are warned and heartened in equal measure.

II. Addendum to the Foreword – Grafton Fitzdecker … p. 10
A charming interlude detailing a rustic conversation overheard in the Old Skull Inn of Shadowdale, wherein the aged locals give their spirited and uncensored opinions on Daggerdale and its turbulent character.

Historical Summaries

III. Chapter Three – Foreword and Summary – Grafton Fitzdecker … p. 12
An account of the antecedents to the principal events herein chronicled, with particular attention paid to the interplay of Zhentarim, drow, Harpers, and beholder-kin during the latter half of the 14th century DR.

Primary Documents: 1371–1372 DR

IV. Dalael Correspondence – Mirtul 1371 – Quelar Dalael … p. 14
A clandestine letter from the surface-aligned drow house Dalael, revealing early alliances with the Zhentarim and the beholder Xulla, with portents of future entanglements.

V. Remarks on the Company of the Red Herring – Capt. Jalthor Murlyn … p. 15
A retrospective account from a Dock Ward constable of Waterdeep, detailing the unlikely origins and rise of a group of adventurers whose errant dealings would culminate in momentous events far from the City of Splendours.

VI. Decisions at Phaulkonmere – 30 Tarsakh 1372 – Agamemnon, trans. by Steele … p. 16

An assembly of concerned parties in the villa Phaulkonmere, including adventurers, Harpers, and mystics, wherein grave decisions are made in the face of Zhentarim ascendance in Daggerdale.

The Daggerdale Expedition

VII. The Daggerdale Expedition – Luminous Whitegate, submitted to the Yellow Abbey (1439 DR) … p. 19

A celebrated and stirring account of the perilous overland journey from Waterdeep to the heart of the Dales undertaken in 1372 DR, whereby a band of heroes sought to deliver Daggerdale from Zhentish tyranny. A narrative marked by suffering, lycanthropy, desert ordeals, and spiritual crisis, culminating in battle and legend.

-   Departure – Preparations and early trials on the road east from Loudwater.

-   Meeting Grandfather – A dire encounter with a mighty werelion that alters the fates of the company.

-   St. Maturin’s Journal – An anguished reflection on affliction and faith by the paladin Maturin Horgath.

-   Infection – The descent of the company into madness and bloodshed as the lycanthropic curse takes hold.

-   The Wanderer and the Quests – A redemptive meeting with a servant of Ilmater, and the divine missions each hero receives.

-   Delivery into the Dales – Passage through Anauroch into the Dales, with hardships mounting.

-   First Battle of Anathar’s Dell – Eleint 5 – A calamitous encounter between paladins and the forces of darkness.

-   The Second Battle at Anathar’s Dell – Eleint 7 – A larger confrontation in the wake of betrayal, revealing the sheer strength of Zhentish forces.

-   Preparations for War – Eleint 8–10 – A moment of brief respite in Shadowdale as the survivors regroup and gather intelligence.

-   The Tyrant’s Mine – Eleint 10–12 – A daring assault upon the third and most feared of the slave mines.

-   Exodus from Chillholm – Eleint 13–21 – The liberation of Chillholm and harrowing retreat across perilous terrain.

-   Exodus from Daggerfalls & Arcane Assassins – Eleint 24–26 – The heroes' efforts to dismantle arcane tyranny in the town.

-   Brotherhood Tower and the Company of the Eastern Storm – Eleint 27 – A victorious convergence of factions long divided.

-   St. Maturin’s Letter to Dalak Quickaxes – Eleint 29 – A reflective epistle from a man sanctified by suffering.

-   The Temple of Cyric – Eleint 30 – A final assault on a bastion of evil and desecration.

-   Departure – Harvestide – The company's final farewell to the field of battle.

-   The Road West – Journey home, tempered by sorrow and triumph.

-   Addas Babar / Bhaerlith / Olomaa – Vignettes from outposts of the desert.

-   Maturin and the Lion – A symbolic reckoning with past and self.

-   The Story Ends and Continues – A coda to the Daggerdale Expedition and a prelude to the years to come.

Supplementary Documents and Reflections

VIII. Notes on the Ocular Alliance in Dagger Falls – 1373 DR
Report by Mnestheus Melshimber, with files from Jeryth Phaulkon; submitted to Twilight Hall, Berdusk, 10 Mirtul, 1373 … p. 50

A meticulous and unsettling intelligence report concerning the formation of an unholy alliance between aberrant forces (including Beholders), rogue Cyricist clergy, and Zhentarim operatives. Compiled by a scion of House Melshimber and corroborated by a Phaulkon, this dossier lays the groundwork for understanding the entrenchment of cultic malignancy in the heart of Daggerdale.

IX. Chapter Four – Foreword and Summary – Grafton Fitzdecker … p. 55
A concise interpretive guide to the transitions in Daggerdale between 1372 and 1398 DR, including the strange ascension of Maturin, the spread of the Luminous Doctrine, and the first stirrings of what would become the Kingdom of Tethyamar.

Lives and Events, 1398–1479 DR

X. Life of the White Saint – 1398 DR – Anon. (likely a disciple of Maturin) … p. 56

An early hagiography of Maturin Horgath, now revered as the White Saint, detailing his humble farm origins, prophetic visions, and acts of miraculous endurance. Includes sub-sections:

-   Liberty Hill – The founding of a peaceable settlement in the hills.

-   The Farm – Accounts of communal life and divine visions.

-   The Paladins’ Grave – A pilgrimage site and site of resurrection lore.

XI. The Wedding of Lanya Forester and Perspicuus Whitegate – 1455 DR – DDC archive … p. 59

A celebratory notice from the township of Liberty Hill, notable for the union of two prominent families: the saint’s heirs and the founders of Castle Radiance.

XII. Reports from Alystair Dretch to Captain Whitegate – 1479 DR – A. Dretch … p. 62

The first of a series of dispatches from a trusted scout in the field, chronicling emerging tensions and dark whispers regarding a place called the Dark Garden. Begins a sequence of increasingly urgent and metaphysical reports from the years immediately preceding the Battle of the Saint.

-   Ritual of Founding – A ceremonial rite of troubling implication.
