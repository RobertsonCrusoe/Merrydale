The White Cloaks were gifts from Zenith Lord Argent Whitegate to the group of adventurers that would name themselves after the gift.

These cloaks shimmer and reflect light in a conspicuous and dazzling fashion when worn inside out, and otherwise are pristine white linen with the holy symbol of Amaunator prominently displayed in embroidery when worn right-side out.

== Game Statistics ==

*Level 2, +1

*Item Slot: Neck
*Enhancement: +1 Fortitude, Reflex, Will

*Power (Daily):Minor Action.
Gain resist 5 to all damage until the start of your next turn.
