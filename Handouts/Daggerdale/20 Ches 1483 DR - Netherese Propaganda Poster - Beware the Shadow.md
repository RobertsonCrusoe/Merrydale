Netherese Propaganda Poster #3 - "Beware the Shadow"
DM Handout (Les Blackwell)
First appeared in Dagger Falls: 20 Ches 1483 DR
Email: "Poster Found Around Daggerdale starting on Ches 20", 31 Jan 2024

---

Caption text:

"Beware the Shadow. Servants of the Shadow can be anywhere! If you see something suspicious, tell a Magistrate!"

---

Notes: This is one of a series of propaganda posters found around Daggerdale starting on Ches 20, 1483 DR. Les noted that the poster image was AI-generated (BingAI) and had random letters on some words, but the intended text is as above. The posters are likely produced by the Amaunatori or the Magistrate's office. The irony — given Jasper's suspicion that devils hide "in the cold light of day" among the Amaunatori themselves — is not lost on the investigators.
