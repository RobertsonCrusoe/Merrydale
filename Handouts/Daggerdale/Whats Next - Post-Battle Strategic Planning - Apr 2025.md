Lucan will be happy to ghostwrite Jeska's letter, but I don't think Stephen will have time to put all this together until the end of this week. I'm still a little concerned about some assumptions that Jeska is making:

The most critical point is that Ovetchkin's report states the ~200 Torn Cheek goblins (with trolls and siege engine) are coming from the swamp specifically "to help the Thistle Ridge goblins retake the Tower". It also reports "some hundreds" of Yellow Tooth goblins already present and concentrated in Thistle Ridge town itself. The report doesn't state the Torn Cheeks are drawn from the town's garrison. Thus, the attacking force seems to be external reinforcements for the siege, not necessarily depleting the existing forces within the town. I don't see any reason not to assume that the town itself is going to remain heavily defended by the Yellow Tooth clan during the Tower assault. Jeska's assumption that the town will be significantly weakened isn't supported by what we've been told (although Farmountain said that this idea was "likely correct." If so, we have another thing to consider. If "help the TR goblins retake the Tower" means that the YT are going to be marching on the tower in addition to the TC from the swamp, then this is going to be a two-pronged assault with upwards of 400 goblins... And while Farmountain did say "any number of goblins," he might not have been thinking about a full assault from two directions.

Secondly, I'm not sure, but I think Jeska might be misinterpreting the identity of "the witch." I haven't been able to sit down to engage in character with this, but when she said "Going after the Contessa in the mine is risky as there is a band of 100 Goblins somewhere between Thistle Ridge and her..". I didn't follow. The only Contessa in a mine (so far as I recall), is ours. Ovetchkin's report explicitly states the Torn Cheek warband was assembled by "a witch in the swamp," - whom we can assume without too much difficulty is Contessa Palus. If she's sending 200 goblins and 40 Gut Rippers out of the Sorrow Bog, she's the one who is going to be exposed. This was the basis of Lucan's initial plan to neuter the advancing force by burning the siege engines en route to the tower. I thought this would eliminate any danger that the goblins could take the tower back from the Sun Blades, and that while that skirmish was happening, Chrysalis could make a foray into the swamp to take on Palus while she was undefended.

A third point that hasn't come up concerns Contessa Fluvia - we learned from Jasper's spritely paramour that Fluvia inhabits (or inhabited) a waterfall east of Thistle Ridge. I don't think we have time to find/save/recruit her for the current crisis, but we need to remember that we have a lead on her.

All this being said, Lucan will follow the Governess's lead if she wants to go the route she is laying out. I'm game for a raid on Thistle Ridge, but I can't help but feel it's premature.
