This is the second in a series of missives smuggled out of Dagger Falls by an unnamed Harper agent during the Zhent Occupation of 1372. The letter is dated 21 Eleint, 1372 DR.

Friends,

Good tidings! The bulk of the Fist has left Daggerdale. I confess that I did not personally see them leave, but I did see the remainder of the 2nd and 4th Companies ride in two nights ago, and the 3rd Company did not go on patrol as usual.

It is morning, and I can find no evidence of any of the Fist Companies normally present, except the 5th and the 9th. The 9th is here still, in either disguise or in decommission, as they do not wear the Fist uniforms.

Also, the 2nd Garrison marched in with all the slaves from that mine last night. The slaves are being held in the Eastern Bailey, and are being treated in an uncharacteristically kind manner.

Congratulations on whatever it is you have done. I remain on active duty.}}
