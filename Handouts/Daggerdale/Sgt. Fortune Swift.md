This is Tuugan's uncle.  He is the leader of the nuanced militia at Tesh Valley Farm.  The militia in this farm has always been the best of any of the farms; there are more of them than any other farm, and they stay on active duty 1 week in 4 throughout the year, adding another 40 men to the active defence of this farm.  These are mostly mounted, and protect the large cattle population.
