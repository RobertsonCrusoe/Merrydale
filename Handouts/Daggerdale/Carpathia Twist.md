Carpathia Twist was raised in a tiny village in the unclaimed lands to the south and west. Without the protection of a permanent army, these lands have become a haven for small clans of all sorts of monstrous folk. Carpathia has seen tremendous suffering at the hands of The Hunt of Malar, whose predations repeatedly drove her family from their home on several occasions, until some twelve years ago, her little village was finally put to the torch and most of its inhabitants, including all of her kin, decimated.

She is a small lithe woman of undetermined age. She carries no weapon, but at her hip hangs the skull of some toothed avian creature. In the eye sockets are two short forked branches of ironwood.

Whether travelling or at rest, she looks sunken and defeated. A lifetime of anguish can be seen in her eyes. Each morning, she visibly shakes herself into motion for yet another day of an existence she seems to flinch from.

During this awakening, she mumbles prayers which, should one listen closely, are not words of worship, but rather curses and threats. She often clutches at something hidden beneath her shawl, shaking her fist slowly to the sky, her hand clenched so tighly her nails cut her skin. Tears run openly down her cheeks during these outbursts.

This suffering has drawn Tyrian to Carpathia. It was he who learned her story to share with the rest of you.

His telling of her tale ends with \"I know not why she travels here, she seems to have no will to live, she is beaten... but...\" Tyrian breaks off, but then his eyes set in a sudden determination, before he continues with an added softness, \"by Illmater, if she can be healed, I will see it done\".
