DDC – 1480 – 1: Report from Report from Alystair Dretch to Cpt. Whitegate 
Greengrass, 1480 DR Captain Whitegate, 
It would seem the situation with the Dark Garden is growing beyond Tuugan and I. 
This report is not detailed, as we have just begun to receive reports and rumors of attacks at many farms in the region. Tesh Valley, Charity Hills, Liberty Hill, Mystral's Grove, Lost Stead, Gribbons and Crossed Blades have all sent reports of attacks. The perpetrators are mostly of Orcish origin, but there have been rumors of Lycanthropes as well. It is my suspicion that the Dark Garden holds the ultimate leadership, but that is as I said, only a suspicion. I believe it may be time for the Whitecloaks to increase their presence here in Dagger Falls, there are simply too many leads for Tuugan and I to follow on our own. 

We depart shortly for Crossed Blades. The roads are soon to be full of merchants, travellers and most pointedly Pilgrims on their way to pay their respects to our Saint. As Aegus Peregrinus, I must ensure the roads are safe to travel. Thus we may not tarry here to continue to gather intelligence. 
Yours in Faith, Alystair
