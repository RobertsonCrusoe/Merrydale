# Tethyamar League
# Source: The Sieges Perilous wiki (backup dated 2010-02-21)

An organization of Dwarves seeking the remnants of their ancient, hereditary kingdom, Tethymar.  The site of Castle Radiance was thought to be a Tethyamar ruin, but turned out otherwise.  These dwarves pay well for information, and often have jobs on the Sun Wheel.
