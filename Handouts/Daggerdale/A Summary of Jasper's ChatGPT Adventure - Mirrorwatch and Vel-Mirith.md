A Summary of Jasper's ChatGPT Adventure - Mirrorwatch and Vel-Mirith
by Gus Miranda (via ChatGPT as DM)
Email thread: "A summary of Jasper's ChatGPT adventure", 24 May 2025

A three-act narrative summary of a solo prequel adventure Gus ran for teenage Jasper in Shadowdale, with ChatGPT as DM. Jasper investigates a planar disturbance near Mirrorwatch, encounters Mirror Shades, and traps a reflection-bound entity called Vel-Mirith. No replies in the thread. Note: This is AI-generated adventure content, not canonical campaign narrative, though Gus shared it with the group as Jasper backstory development.

---

ACT I: The Flickering Path

Jasper investigated a disturbance near the ruined observatory known as Mirrorwatch. Along the way, he encountered a disoriented scholar fleeing from Mirror Shades — predatory reflections born from a breach in the veil between the Material Plane and the Mirror Realm. Using Invisibility and strategic Figment illusions, Jasper evaded the Shades, rescued the victim, and identified the planar disturbance.

ACT II: The Rift and the Name

The traveler, Tavin, revealed he had glimpsed something beyond — and heard the name Vel-Mirith, a reflection-bound entity of thought and inversion. Jasper sealed the planar rift using Illusory Object, cutting off Vel-Mirith's gaze, and guided Tavin to the safety of the Mirrorwatch ruins.

With his expertise in Arcana and Occultism, Jasper identified Vel-Mirith as a sentient resonance — a being that lives through reflection and memory, growing stronger the more it is seen or recognized. He learned that Mirrorwatch's breach was accidental: a failed ritual meant to trap Vel-Mirith, not summon it.

ACT III: The Ritual Reversed

With Tavin's assistance, Jasper reentered the shattered observatory, used Darkness to blind the reflections temporarily, and discovered the silver hand mirror used in the original containment. Together, they completed the unfinished ritual.

At the exact moment the Darkness spell ended, Jasper used the silver mirror to reflect the standing mirror back on itself. The ritual succeeded: Vel-Mirith was drawn inward, trapped in a recursive gaze of its own infinite reflection.

Aftermath

Jasper wrapped and secured the Mirror of Vel-Mirith, a newly empowered — and dangerously resonant — relic. Tavin agreed to help obscure the true events to prevent future breaches. The tower was left behind, sealed and warded.

But Vel-Mirith still lingers... unseen, but not forgotten.

Key lore points (ChatGPT-generated, not canonical):
- Mirrorwatch is a ruined observatory near Shadowdale
- Mirror Shades are predatory reflections from the Mirror Realm
- Vel-Mirith is a sentient resonance entity that grows stronger when observed
- The Mirror of Vel-Mirith is a relic containing the trapped entity
- Tavin is a scholar/traveler NPC
