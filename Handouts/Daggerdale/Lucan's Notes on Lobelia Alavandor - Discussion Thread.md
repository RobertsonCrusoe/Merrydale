Lucan's Notebook - Lobelia Alavandor Deductions

- Evidence Item 2: Blaze Whitegate's testimony (during discussion re: my own superior brown eyes). Confirmed Thistle Ridge "Alavandors," including Andarl, uniformly possessed blue eyes.

- Deduction 1: The congruence is undeniable, almost offensively obvious once pointed out. Lobelia's Grey Eye = Palus (Swamp Hag mother). Lobelia's Blue Eye = Andarl line (Paternal line eye color). Perfect match. Ergo, Lobelia is hagspawn. (No evidence to the contrary other than Brother V's assurance that heterochromia is sometimes natural)

Lucan sighs.

- Hypothesis: Lobelia = Palus's daughter. Placed via changeling swap at birth into the Alavandor household. Andarl/Susserah unaware. Explains the eyes, the unnatural talents noted by Tergus (hag blood), and Dorian's eventual fate.
