A duplicitous and self-serving Waterdhavian merchant (and for those in the know, fence) whose services were often employed by Company Drurginz and Company Aldebarran. He is the owner of a pawn shop named \"Waukeen's Wares\" as well as a warehouse near the Thirsty Throat Tavern.

He was a known contact of Esperel, and seems to be implicated in the fate of the Heart of the Earth.
