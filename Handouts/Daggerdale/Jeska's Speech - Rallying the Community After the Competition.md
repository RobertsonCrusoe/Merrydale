Jeska's Speech - Rallying the Community After the Competition
by Scott Mclaren (as Jeska Monarch)
Email thread: "Jeska's speech", 24 Jul 2024

An earlier in-character speech by Jeska, delivered to the community of Dagger Falls after the Thistle Ridge Competition brought "evil and grief" to the town. Jeska introduces herself, proposes a community-wide cleanup effort (burning the worm's corpse, filling the hole, planting a garden), offers pay for workers, calls for the disbanding of the Redjacks, and bans lethal fights. Different from the May 2025 speech confronting the Daughter of Shadows.

---

I am Jeska Monarch, assumed ambassador to the embassy here in your town. I am not that. I am in my culture just a young elf, a mere 270 years of age sent here by my own superiors for reasons I do not understand.

I have formed a team, "Chrysalis" as you are aware... and we have won the right through competition to cleanse Thistle Ridge that resides there.

However, this competition has brought evil and grief to this town.
I do not know you, or this land, but I know this was an attack by our enemies.

We face a dire foe, and only as a community can we prevail.

Shall we forge onward?
Yes.
We will burn out the corpse of the worm! We will fill the hole with rock, sand and mortar. We will plant a garden!
Every man woman and child who works to this end will be paid 1sp per day.
All required supplies will be compensated at double normal value.

This cost will be generously donated by the personal accounts of the organizers of the event. There will be no tax increase due to this expenditure.

I have 2 more unrelated facts to note.
1. The Redjacks are to be disbanded, or at minimum, evicted from the Dales.
2. No lethal fights will be permitted. I encourage you to fight, hone your skills, but no killing. I made the same decree in the competition, and I make it now. I will personally deal with any breach of this decree.

That said, legend speaks of the stalwart demeanor of Dalefolk... shall we work together? Forget the past, rally when impossible and forge ahead together!

---

Key lore points:
- Jeska identifies herself as Jeska Monarch, 270 years old, nominally ambassador to the embassy
- She admits she was sent by her superiors for reasons she doesn't understand
- Chrysalis won the Thistle Ridge Competition
- A worm (purple worm?) was killed during the competition, leaving a corpse to be burned and a hole to be filled
- Community workers to be paid 1sp/day; supplies compensated at double normal value
- Costs funded from personal accounts of the event organisers — no tax increase
- Jeska demands the Redjacks be disbanded or evicted from the Dales
- Jeska bans lethal fighting — same decree as during the competition
