Esperel the moon elf is a cousin to Ellyth and nephew to Gilthanas. He is also a distant relation of Lotharil.

Status: Esperel disappeared following an unfriendly encounter with Nero and Steele at the Copper Cup. Harsh words led to drawn blades, and Esperel jumped out a window (which he had purposely positioned himself close to) and escaped, despite the best efforts of Nero and Steele. Rooken intelligence later revealed that, of the elf's known hideouts and safehouses, all had been packed up and deserted. He has not been seen anywhere in the City (in any of his old guises, at any rate) since this attack.
