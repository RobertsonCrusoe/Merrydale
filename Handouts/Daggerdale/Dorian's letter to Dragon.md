# Dorian's letter to Dragon
# Source: The Sieges Perilous wiki (backup dated 2010-02-21)

A letter from Dorian to Dragon, sent around 21 Eleint, 1372 in Dagger Falls
----

{{script}}
Dragon my love,

I think our little show at the Old Skull went off well. However, you should have sealed the deal before you let me go. Your searing black eyes have left me absolutely mesmerized. I fear that having had the beautiful honour of meeting my match (and better) has left me reconsidering my allegiances.

I cannot in good conscience bring myself to do harm to one as gorgeous as yourself. No, alas, my heart is yours. My insides ‘burn’ with desire to see your radiant countenance, but just one more time.

My dear, sweet, beautiful Dragon, it is for my love of you that I betray my side in this terrible war to bring you this one piece of news:

Thagdul knows about the doors in the mine, and its contents. As I write this, he plots and positions to overthrow you and your father for control of Daggerdale.

If you do not trust me, remember that my companions and I are well-connected with Thagdul and his Waterdeep agents.

Protect yourself and your interests, my darling.

With deepest love,
{{sig|Dorian of Shadowdale}}
