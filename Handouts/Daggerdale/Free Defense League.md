# Free Defense League
# Source: The Sieges Perilous wiki (backup dated 2010-02-21)

This is a group of adventurers who are lead by a man named Bargle.  They have been adventuring in the area for over 2 years and have earned great respect and notoriety.  Bargle now leases the Winter Tower from the Castle for 75 gold/month, and keeps private rooms for all the adventurers in his organization.
