Ser Rowan Grumbold Visits the Embassy - Character Introduction
by Todd Burry (as Ser Rowan Grumbold)
Email thread: "Ser Rowan Grumbold visits the embassy", 20 Jan 2024

Todd's introduction of his second character, Ser Rowan Grumbold, a portly former Purple Dragon who turns up at the Embassy demanding entry and offering his mercenary services to Company Chrysalis. Written in Rowan's distinctive Cockney-inflected voice.

---

Outside the embassy, the air crackles with the tension of a brewing kerfuffle, as guards clash with a man at the door.

"What d'ya mean I cahn't go in!? I'm a man o' honor, I am, and I deserves an audience!" the man bellows.

A muffled retort comes from behind the door.

"Don't fink for a second I don't know 'ow you Redjacks operate, tryna rig this 'ere competition in your favor! Redjacks? More like blackguards, I'll 'ave ya know!" he roars, his voice a cannonade of indignation.

The one-sided row escalates, its volume swelling, until you approach the door. There, you're met by the sight of a portly figure, disheveled yet imposing, clad in chainmail with a bright orange tabard stretched over his expansive form. His face, a portrait of fury painted in shades of crimson, suddenly softens upon spotting you.

"Ah, my liege. Well met," he mutters, mopping the sweat from his brow with a meaty hand, straightening his tabard, and offering an awkward, yet earnest, bow.

"Ser Rowan Grumbold, at your service," he introduces himself, his voice a curious melody of gruffness and warmth.

Despite his height being unremarkable, his presence is monumental, a testament to his ample girth and muscle. A collection of formidable weapons of war are strapped to him, as casually borne as if they were mere trinkets.

"I was takin' a gander at your lot durin' the ceremony. A sturdy and serene bunch, you are, but I clocked that you're a company of four, whilst the others boast five. And that got me chinwaggin' to meself. I says, 'Ser Rowan, I reckon this crew could use a bit of 'elp.'"

With that, Rowan strides in, his gaze sweeping over the foyer with wide-eyed wonder. Upon espying a table laden with victuals, he makes a beeline for it, snatching an apple and sinking his teeth into it with gusto.

"Like I said, the name's Ser Rowan, formerly of the Purple Dragons. I can offer you a fair shake on my mercenary services: below the guild rates if you throw in room and board. Might even shave a bit more off if I get a nibble of the spoils."

He catches your scrutinizing glance and a shadow of insecurity flickers across his robust frame.

"Look, how's about I throw meself into this scuffle you've got tomorrer, on the 'ouse? Give ya a chance to eyeball my wares before you sign on the dotted line. Try before you buy, as they say?"

And with that proposition, Ser Rowan Grumbold, the "former Purple Dragon" with a heart as vast as his waistline, pledges his service to your cause.
