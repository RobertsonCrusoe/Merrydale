Notes hastily jotted in Lucan's notebook after the arrival of
Invictus's bookmobile.Lucan's Notebook - Lobelia Alavandor Deductions

   -

   *Subject:* Lobelia A. (supposed daughter of Andarl A. / Susserah C.).
   Born 1428 DR. Key Anomaly: Heterochromia - *One Grey Eye, One Blue Eye*.
   Noted by Tergus C. as exceptional talent, unusual wisdom/discipline.
   -

   *Evidence Item 1:* Folklore text discovered – "On the heterochromia of
   hagspawn." Specifics: Swamp hag spawn = 1 grey eye (hag mother) + 1 eye
   matching father's color. *(Source reliability: Old text, but details
   suspiciously precise).*
   -

   *Evidence Item 2:* Blaze Whitegate's testimony (during discussion re: my
   own superior brown eyes). Confirmed Thistle Ridge "Alavandors," including
   Andarl, uniformly possessed *blue eyes*.
   -

   *Deduction 1:* The congruence is undeniable, almost offensively obvious
   once pointed out. Lobelia's *Grey Eye = Palus* (Swamp Hag mother).
   Lobelia's *Blue Eye = Andarl line* (Paternal line eye color). *Perfect
   match*. Ergo, Lobelia is hagspawn. (No evidence to the contrary other
   than Brother V's assurance that heterochromia is sometimes natural)

*Jesra *pulls at Lucan's ear, giggling, a sound like tiny bells): Grey like
swamp mist, and blue like… the sky in summer? Or perhaps after a winter
frost? Or the eyes of a man foolish enough to tumble with a hag? Hee hee!
Mortals leave their colours everywhere!

Lucan shoos her off his shoulder, annoyed: "Stop that you! And don't read
over my shoulder."

   -

   *Evidence Item 3 (Folklore):* Male hagspawn "almost always destroyed" by
   mother hag.
   -

   *Evidence Item 4 (Genealogy/Séance):* Lobelia's potential twin brother,
   Dorian A. (b. 1428). Killed age 11 (1439 DR) in sacrifice orchestrated by
   Palus (as "Ancient Mother").

*Jesra:* (Claps her tiny hands and flits to Lucan's other shoulder) Oh,
clever hag! Letting the little stag grow antlers before the hunt! More
sport in it that way, perhaps? Or maybe just tidying her messy gardening
later rather than sooner. A broken branch spoils the symmetry, yes?

*Lucan sighs.*

   -

   *Deduction 2:* Dorian's eventual elimination by Palus fits the folklore
   pattern regarding male offspring, albeit delayed. Palus tidied up the loose
   end.
   -

   *Hypothesis:* Lobelia = Palus's daughter. Placed via *changeling swap*
   at birth into the Alavandor household. Andarl/Susserah unaware. Explains
   the eyes, the unnatural talents noted by Tergus (hag blood), and Dorian's
   eventual fate.

*Jesra:* A perfect fit! Like a stolen skin! Or a cuckoo's egg in a
sparrow's nest! Such neat mortal patterns. Did your big book say what
happens when the egg hatches with the wrong feathers?
*Lucan twitches his shoulder to throw Jera off balance.*

   -

   *Implication:* Palus infiltrated the core families *decades* before the
   Tragedy, placing her own offspring within. Deepens the conspiracy
   significantly. Confirms her long-term planning and personal investment.
   -

   *Note to Self:* Father's identity? Could Palus have seduced Andarl? Or
   any blue-eyed fool? Irrelevant to Lobelia's hagspawn status, but perhaps
   useful later. Focus on the infiltration.
   -

   *Note to Self 2:* Inform companions. Ensure they grasp the significance
   beyond just 'odd eyes'. This confirms Palus's methods and timeline. Hag
   blood runs strong, it seems.

*Jesra:* Strong, yes! And tricky! Like river currents. Looks smooth on top,
but pulls you under if you step wrong. A little bit of hag, a little bit of
mortal man… makes for an interesting flavour, doesn't it? Like sweet
berries mixed with bog water! Are you *sure* you know which part is which,
Stone Tree?

*(Satisfied, Lucan nods to himself, no longer paying any attention to
Jesra. Another piece locked into place. The full picture becomes clearer
with every deduction...)*
