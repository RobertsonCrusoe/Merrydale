Argent is the younger brother of Luminous Whitegate, and the highest official in the Church of Amaunator, which also makes him the highest official in the government of Daggerdale and Dagger Falls.  His title is that of Zenith Lord, and it was he who dispensed with any secular title of office for the dale or the town, assimilating those offices under that of Zenith Lord.
