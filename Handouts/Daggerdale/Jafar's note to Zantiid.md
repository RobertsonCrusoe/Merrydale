''A damning note found in the wreckage of the Irlingstar Villa after the defeat of the forces of Company {{catlink|Aldebarran}}. If any uncertainty had remained in the minds of the heroes that opposed the Ocular Alliance about Jafar's treachery, this note proved it beyond any doubts.''

{{script}}
Master [[Zantiid]],

Descriptions and information on my meddlesome ‘companions’

====I. The Neverwinter Team====
;*[[Beljuril of Azuth]] (Harper): An extremely powerful priest, mage and monk of Azuth. His power far surpasses his 16 years. His strength is magic that protects him, such as '''invisible mail''', '''sanctuary''', '''prayer''' and '''stone fist'''. The best way to neutralize him is to strip him of these spells with '''dispel magic''', etc. and/or to hinder his spellcasting abilities by not allowing him the time to rest and memorize, or by casting '''feeblemind''' and so on. Furthermore, as an Azuthian, he is obsessed with collecting as much magic as possible. He can be taken care of by luring him into a trap using a Spellbook or magic item as bait.

;*[[Maevri|May of Rashemen]]: This unparalleled beauty is an enchantress and witch from that far-off land. I know not too much of her, but from what I gather she is of no consequence in battle, but extremely dangerous if her charms are used to persuade information or services from your allies. Neutralize her abilities to charm and enchant and you have neutralized her.

;*[[Fazil]] the Gnome: Nothing but a headache and nuisance. The runt is an illusionist. Simply advise your allies to be alert for such illusions if they see a gnome.

;*[[Oliver Windemchester]]: This is an odd fellow with an uncanny ability to succeed where others fail. I have little information on him and can offer no more advice than using a stout blade to cleave through him.

;*[[Oberon of Lundeth]]: An inconsequential boy of little power and ability. His background is interesting, but I doubt that such a common thief could pose a problem. He does have a big mouth and should perhaps be silenced.

;*[[Rufus of Selune]] (Harper): The most dangerous of the group going to Neverwinter. This werecreature can only be harmed with enchanted weapons and the Art. Silver and mundane have no effect. The best way to dispose of him is to learn his “True” name and thus bend him to you service. Beyond that you should beware his Selunite powers, particularly his abilities to conjure up a '''moonblade''' and to cast his '''hold''' spells.

====II. The Dagger Falls Expedition====
This is probably the most dangerous of all the opponents facing you and your allies. They have “righteousness” on their side and are incorruptible. It sickens me.

;*[[Mourn Raventree II|Mourn “Raven” Raventree]] of Mielikki (Harper): Raven is the leader (and only member of the Company) of the group heading to Daggerdale in search of Yarkul. He is a hard-headed and determined devotee of all that is good and just. His skill as a tracker is impressive in the wild, as is his skill with the blade. However, his greatest weakness is his lack of self-defense. While he can cleave many opponents with but a single blow from his sword, he lacks armour and protection that would otherwise save him from a stout blade to the back.

;*Brother [[Maturin Horgath]] ‘The Grey’ of Ilmater: A paladin of the Crying God. Need I say more? He is an unsurpassed healer, but an inept fighter. The best way to defeat him is to use his Paladin’s Code of Honour against him.

;*[[Boregar|Boregar the Warrior]] (Harper): I know nothing of this man, save that he is a follower of Mielikki, a Harper, and a Dalesman.

;*[[Hanibal Felthian|Hanibal of Harrowdale]]: A Wild Mage. Beware. His greatest strength and weakness is that he can cast spells. You are equally assured that he will kill himself and his companions as often as he will kill your minions. Worry not about him, but beware. Just watch him cast his spells and wait for him to destroy himself.

;*[[Corrina]] the Gnome: Exactly the same as Fazil. (In fact, the world will thank you if you can exterminate such nuisances).

;*[[Sparrow]] of Mystra: A priest and thief or something. I know nothing of him other than he was ordered by the Church of Mystra to capture Yarkul for crimes against the Lady of Mysteries.

The group heading to Daggerdale will never make it to their destination. They intend to cross Anauroch on their own! The Desert will do your job for you. If not you can exterminate them by using their righteousness against them. A damsel in distress as bait to a well-placed ambush ought to take care of them.

====III. The Waterdeep Team====

There is no one here of any note. The [[Zeke|old pirate]] and the [[Brandel|young boy]] will last not a day in my company. [[Rychel]] is dangerous, but so far an excellent ally, even though he doesn’t realize he is helping us. He still believes that I am under the employ of a mage here in Waterdeep that opposes both the Zhents and the Harpers. Both [[Garth]] and [[Esperel]] can be taken care of by using their own greed against them.

Note that Esperel has a hideout in the [[Church of Azuth]], and another in Dockward. They may be trapped. (I have enclosed a map).

I believe that is all. I await further instructions, but we shall proceed to destroy the Drow’s mercantile efforts and funnel some of that wealth to you and [[Thagdul]].

PS. You may start referring to me by my real Zhentish name of [[Soltan]]. There is no more point in carrying on my Calishite charade.
