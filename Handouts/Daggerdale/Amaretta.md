The demonic succubus who was Yarkul's concubine in Dagger Falls. According to information gleaned from the interrogation of Morgul, the Cyricist priest who was interrogated and executed soon after the purge of the evil faiths from Dagger Falls, Amaretta was one of the earliest minions of Iyachtu Xvim in the area.
