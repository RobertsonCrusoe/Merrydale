PART 2: GUS ON PATHBUILDER 2E — GUS MIRANDA (20 Aug 2025)
----------------------------------------------------------
Source: Message 198ca858d6249d91

"It was a fun weekend as always. I wouldn't worry too much about Jeska's
chase with Loki. I think we're all looking forward to our Shadowfell
side-quest.

The site I'm using to manage Jasper now is called Pathbuilder 2E:
https://pathbuilder2e.com/

It's free, and fast. The paid version costs about US$6 and you own it
forever. However, paying for it only gives you access to the optional
rules, familiars and animal companions, and some magic items and other
spells. I'd say around 95% of the content is free, and unless you're a
spell caster, I wouldn't worry about it.

The only thing that we use that is behind the payment is the ability score
improvements. Standard is getting them all at Level 5, but we do it that
you get 1 per level. But that's not a big deal.

And the one thing it sucks for is inventory management. Figure out your
equipment on Foundry or with a pencil and paper. It might be easier if you
don't have a mountain of scrolls and wands and potions like I do, but I
just deal with my inventory on Foundry and don't bother in Pathbuilder.

Best feature is that you can build your character up to 20th level ahead of
time if you want, so you can play around with alternate versions. For
someone like you Scun, who is a master of finding synergy, I think this
would be very useful to you."
