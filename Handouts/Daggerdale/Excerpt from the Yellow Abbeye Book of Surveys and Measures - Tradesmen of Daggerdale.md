Excerpt from the Yellow Abbeye Book of Surveys and Measures – 1482 Edition – Tradesmen of Daggerdale

It being critical to the good governance of the settlements of Daggerdale, and for a fulsome understanding of the productive output of the same, a census of most skilled trades in the dale has been completed and illustrated below.

The census has been separated into Dagger Falls and the Rest of the Dale, though future accountings should be separated for Charity and Tethyamar.

A = Apprentice   J = Journeyman   M = Master (and might include Grand Masters)

CHRYSALIS HIRING AVAILABILITY – TARSAKH/MIRTUL 1483 DR

For the months of Tarsakh and Mirtul, the stream of people flooding the Embassy looking to join Chrysalis equates to the following hiring availability:

From the Tradesmen of Daggerdale table, in the Dagger Falls listing:
  - 1/4 of the apprentices could be hired (rounded down)
