# Maddrick Thunderstorm
# Source: Compiled from Sieges Perilous wiki (backup dated 2010-02-21), White Cloaks narratives, and DDC 1480 Pedigrees

Born 1430 DR. Son of Karrigan Thunderstorm (1400-1476) and Radiance Whitegate (1409-1439). Brother of General Dauntless Thunderstorm, Warden Thunderstorm, Laurel Thunderstorm, and Maddread Thunderstorm. No wives or children recorded.

Magistrate of Crossed Blades Farm (as of 1479). Stationed at the farm for five years (c. 1474 onward). Has a good rapport with the citizens but has "always had a bit of a struggle here with the 'new faith' because the locals here have always had so much investment in Chauntea, and continue to do so." Described as "a typical Magistrate; kind, disciplined, unwavering in his duties."

Active in the field during the White Cloaks' early operations (summer 1479). Dispatched a carrier pigeon to Castle Radiance after Sun Blades spotted a large werewolf leaping over the farm walls. Later appeared at a Shadowfell cave camp "dressed now in chain armour rather than his pristine yellow robes," serving alongside Knight Commander Pauldron and a unit of Sun Blades under ecclesiastic command. He reported that a platoon of the 4th Company (Foresters) under Sgt. Leedo Forester had intercepted and destroyed a roaming party of Krinth and taken a Shadowfell goblinoid prisoner.

Later in the summer, intervened in a debate at Crossed Blades between Sgt. Randall Eversmoore (who wanted the pilgrims to wait for a Sun Dragoon escort) and a large host of pilgrims who wished to continue their journey. Sided with the pilgrims: "They have a divine calling, and none can delay them now if they follow that calling. They will be delivered safely to the tomb they seek, I am assured."

Note: Several wiki sources (Thunderstorm Family, Church of Amaunator, Journey to Provisioner's Hall) attribute the role of Dean of the Yellow Abbey and Sunlord to "Maddrick." However, the Whitegate-Thunderstorm Pedigrees (DDC 1480) distinguish between two brothers: Maddrick (b. 1430, Magistrate of Crossed Blades Farm) and Maddread (b. 1433, Magistrate and Dean of the Yellow Abbey). The pedigree is the more precise source for distinguishing similarly-named family members. The Dean is most likely Maddread, not Maddrick. See also: Maddread Thunderstorm.
