# Farms
# Source: The Sieges Perilous wiki (backup dated 2010-02-21)

In the last 100 years, with the expansion of Cormanthor, and the ever-increasing numbers of orcs and other threats in the Dale, the farms of Daggerdale have consolidated into small fortress-towns in their own right.  All have some sort of wall; some have wooden palisades, while the older and more successful farms have stone walls and towers.

These communities typically have 10-20 families living in them, with a standing guard.  The farms are expected to provide for their own standing defense, and many do so by hiring a number of Sun Blades from Dagger Falls.  The larger farms typically have a company of Sun Blades garrisoned, which number 32.  Smaller farms might have a single unit, which numbers 1 sergeant and 7 soldiers.

These communities all have at least a shrine to Amaunator, though most have churches, many of these significant stone buildings that are defensible structures.  All of the farms have a cleric of Amaunator as a permanent resident.
