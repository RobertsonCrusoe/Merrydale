Dagger Falls Garrison Under the Zhentarim — Commander Tren Noemfor
Source: garrison.htm, from DND/_Wiki/check online and delete/ folder
The pre-Concordat military organisation of Dagger Falls under Zhentarim occupation. This is the garrison that the Sun Blades replaced after the Liberation.

---

The Dagger Falls Garrison is under the command of Commander (formerly Constable) Tren Noemfor (IV epaulets). The Garrison consists of ten companies of twenty men, plus a captain (III epaulets) and 2 sergeants (II epaulets) each.

1st Garrison
  Captain Denethor, Randal Morn's captor
  Posting: Stationed at the Tower

2nd Garrison
  Captain Innisworth
  Posting: Stationed at Rainhill Mine

3rd Garrison
  Captain Aegis, rumoured to be a half-ogre. Both sergeants are his sons
  Posting: Stationed at Chillholm Mine
  Status: Routed and destroyed

4th Garrison
  This company is exclusively made up of crazy Cyricists under Captain Braehl.
  Posting: Stationed at the Dagger Hills Mine
  Status: Treacherously slain at the mine

5th through 8th Garrisons
  Posting: Stationed in Dagger Falls

9th Garrison (cavalry)
  Captain Vargul, an aged veteran of this area's wars.
  Posting: Patrols the East Road

10th Garrison (cavalry)
  Posting: Patrols the South Road

---
Notes:
- Total garrison strength: ~230 men (10 companies x 20 men + 10 captains + 20 sergeants + Commander Noemfor)
- Rank structure: Commander (IV epaulets) > Captain (III epaulets) > Sergeant (II epaulets)
- Two companies destroyed before/during the Liberation: 3rd (routed) and 4th (treacherously slain)
- Two cavalry companies (9th and 10th) patrolled the main roads
- Compare with the post-Liberation Sun Blades organisation under the 1482 Concordat
