This was the name given to an entity - or group of entities - that roamed the streets of Waterdeep in Mirtul of 1371, killing several magic users. To the characters' knowledge, the entity was never identified or brought to justice. At the time, anti-magic sentiment among the Waterdhavian populace had been increasing.

Garth followed up on this with Stuart two years later. Stuart led Garth to believe that the Magebane killings were unfortunately (in the eyes of the Entropists) over.
