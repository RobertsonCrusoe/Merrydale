Information collected by Loremaster Kennyth Thames while studying in The Monastery of Sundered Faiths in 1381 DR, The Year of the Starving

thumb

Not to be confused with the secretive group that splintered off of the Harpers in 1370 DR due to the encouragement of Khelben Arunsun.

The Moonstars I make reference to are an even more secretive group that perhaps inspired Arunsun to evoke their name centuries after they first appeared.  I speculate that considering the age of Arunsun, he might have been a member of the Moonstars that were involved with The Spire.

All I can gather is that the Moonstars were quite secretive, extremely selective in their membership and were used in situations that required extreme secrecy and skill.  The only reference I can find regarding a mission they were given involves the Towers Tyrannous.  A generally foul and dangerous ruin far to the east in the lands of Thay.  Whatever mission they had there was clearly one of no small amount of danger.}}
