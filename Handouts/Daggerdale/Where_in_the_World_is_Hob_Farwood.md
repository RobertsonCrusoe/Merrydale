Where in the world is Hob Farwood? 

When Raven and Rufus arrived back in Waterdeep on the 29th of Ches, they returned without Hob.  Apparently, Hob was captured by slavers in Neverwinter.  He was shipped north to Luskan, then east to Mirabar, then south along the Long Road, and from Amphail, no one knows.  They traveled with a former friend of the Road Mage, a person of similar vocation by the name of Agamemnon.  In Amphail, Agamemnon went east towards Loudwater to look for signs of the passage of the caravan.  . 

After a couple of weeks of information gathering, nothing of the caravan was learned in the City of Splendours.  It either slipped in unnoticed (as would most caravans bearing slaves), or it never came this way. 

The answer would come a couple of days before Greengrass, when Agamemnon would return to Waterdeep.  Agamemnon returned without Hobb as well, but with his companion, Boregar.   This mage turned up first in the house of Azuth and met with Beljuril.  Agamemnon learned that Beljuril was a former friend of the Road Mage, and in turn befriended the priest of Azuth.  Agamemnon was then off to locate another friend of his that had apparently just recently arrived in the city. 

On the same day that Agamemnon returned to the City, Jeryth Phaulkon (presumed dead for a duration of three years) returned, with the mark of Meiliki's Chosen.  Jeryth explained that she had survived the ambush that claimed the life of her mother, by the will of Meiliki.  She then spent three years hunting gnolls all the way from the Westwood to the High Forest.  She was devastated to learn of the deaths of her sister and father, and seemed to blame herself for much of the situation. 

Lady Phaulkon bears the company no ill will for moving into her father's house, but rather takes it as a sign from Meiliki.  She claims that you lot were thrust into her path to help avenge her father's death. 

Jeryth Phaulkon is an attractive woman about 35 years old.  She is rough and rugged looking, but still strangely attractive.  She has long, mostly unkempt blonde hair with an ivory streak along the crown (the mark of Meiliki's chosen).  She has green eyes that flash with vitality and energy and her medium height body is healthy and strong, her frame muscular and fit.  She wears rugged furs and frontier clothing.  She keeps a bow strapped over one shoulder and a pair of scimitars at her sides.  Despite her appearance, she speaks like a cultured Waterdhavian noblewoman. 

When the adventure begins again, it is the last day of Tarsakh, and Agamemnon and Lady Phaulkon have arranged to meet with as many members of the company as possible, to discuss what course of action should be taken that spring. 

It is still cold as the Eighth Layer out, and the festivities of the Greengrass Festival are likely to be cancelled.  Snow was falling lightly that day, and great chunks of ice were still flowing down the coast from the Sea of Moving Ice to the north.
