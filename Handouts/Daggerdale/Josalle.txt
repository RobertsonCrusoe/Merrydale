This was a slave found in the lair of Tuumig on The Old Man's Tooth.  She was brought back to Castle Radiance, but her entry into the Castle was challenged by the clerks at the South Gate, which nearly came to blows between Corran and a pair of Paladins of the .

==Description==

The woman before you is a pitiable thing.  She is naked except for one small bit of deer hide which does not cover anything vital on her.  Her long black hair is a tangled mess that more resembles the nest of a large bird, it is so tangled and matted with straw, small sticks and other foulness.

She is so covered in scrapes and bruises that she appears nearly black and blue all over, and she has only 1 eye left, and it can barely see out of the black and purple swelling that occludes it.  Her other eye socket has a large shiny piece of fool’s gold that has been refined to be roughly circular, and the presence of this gleaming bit of rock in her otherwise dark and abused face strikes you as something of a cruel gift.  She says not a word, but stays curled up in a ball in the corner in a pile of vile straw, sticks and waste that smells worse than the inside of an orc’s belly.
