This dossier was compiled with the input of knowledgeable folk such as Jeryth Phaulkon, Egan, Kennyth Thames, and over a dozen others. Much is speculation, but much is fact.

\"We must be ever vigilant against the Zhent; they are a snake that will grow a new head faster than the troll grows its foul claws; they are a hive of stirges that will nest in any vacant tree; they are leeches in the shallowest and clearest of waters, who remain, somehow, unseen.\"
:- Jeryth Phaulkon

“Is there a Harper standing in the Light for every Zhent lurking in the Shadows? Were it only so, I would rejoice. Alas, our fight is drawing near its end, and others must be brave enough to pick up where we will leave off. The Zhent are numerous, and Shadow has been their armour.”
:- Agamemnon

“The Shadows conceal things worse than the Zhent. Things that were here long before the Zhent, and things that will survive the Zhent. All of us; Waterdhavian, Man and Dwarf, Elf and Beast, Zhent and Harper alike, we are all a vile and temporary currency to the Drow. The Drow threaten us all, and worse; they have plans in place which focus not upon our short and hurried lives, but on those of our great, great, great grandchildren.”
:- Egan, Council Speaker for  of the City

“What could be worse than the Drow?”
:- Filiare, Proprietor of the Inn of the Dripping Dagger
