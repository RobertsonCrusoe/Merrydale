A group of monks from Candlekeep who are here to document the unearthing of the many ancient Netherese sites and ruins, and to catalogue the many artifacts being brought to the Castle.

The head monk is a man named Brother Darred, and aged man who is knowledgeable and kind.  He is happy to discuss any topic he is knowledgeable about, and gives professional advice for free.

One oath that visitors to the Castle take is to bring all artifacts and magic items found in the field to Brother Darred and his monks to be identified and catalogued.  They pay the owner of the item 5 gold or more for this.  They will also purchase any item that the owner doesn't want or need, if the offer is made by the owner.

The Candle Keep Emissaries stock pile a vast quantity of artifacts and magic that they auction off twice each year.
