13 Tarsakh, 1483 DR – Discussion with Jenwendle of Hillsfar
In the broken tower that has become the lair of the bugbear Karn and his small band of goblins, you managed to save a man from being sent to his death in a cage from a shattered balcony.  After the chaos of your storming of the tower had subsided, you tended to the frail form of the man, who you learned to be one Jendwendle, a merchant from Hillsfar.  The man has been a captive of the Yellow Tooth, and specifically, of Karn's, since last fall, and is emaciated, and his pathetic frame bears the gruesome evidence of a protracted and cruel captivity.  He is missing most of his fingers and toes, one ear, one eye and other bits of flesh besides.  His mind is likewise rent, and he is for the most part absent and despondent, but can be brought to respond to some lines of questioning.  He tells you the following bits of information in his broken fashion; 
"There were three of them, bugbears, and the worst of them is the mother, Bollla.  She's the cruelest, she's the cruelest.  All fear Bolla, all save Charrah." 
"My wife was Morganna…a priestess of Tymora…Tymora abandoned her when they cut her throat, and put her into the orb.  The terrible terrible orb.  Charrah was there.  Charrah did it.  You met my Morganna, my poor unlucky Morganna…" 
"From Hillsfar we came, from Hillsfar to join the company.  The captain was fair, but they cut his throat, put him into the terrible terrible orb.  Charrah was there.  Charrah did it.  You'll meet the captain, he's still screaming…still screaming…"
"From Hillsfar we came, to Voonlar.  No more shield, no.  Just Druginz and the captain.  He seemed lost, didn’t know how to seem.  No more shield, just Druginz.  But now we are all done."
"Karn plays with his toys below.  Many go down, only Karn comes up." 
It is difficult to get anything more coherent than that out of him.  He will need months or more of rehabilitation.
