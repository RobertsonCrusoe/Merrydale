[PC Attribution: Maxim Jos (Stephen Robertson)]
An outlander from Unther. He was masquerading as a dragon shaman from Damara, later discovered to be a Dragon Cultist and priest of Tiamat.

After the events of 1372 in Neverwinter, Wyrmtongue made good on his vow to get even with Fazil for having chosen to rescue a chest of treasure over Wyrmtongue's life, and, later, compounding his parsimony by arguing that under the terms of the Red Herring Contract, Wyrmtongue was not eligible for a share of the take from the defeat of Malkor Githeas. Soon afterwards, Wyrmtonuge stole the gnome's money and the amulet of clairaudience, and was last suspected to be hotfooting it back to his lich-boss in Yartar.
