This is a partial map of the Shadowfell from the Sharspire to a cave in the Dagger Hills, which the White Cloaks uncovered.  This is a working-map; it was being drafted by the Shadar-Kai explorers whom the White Cloaks ambushed and killed.

This map is written in Netherese, and with magical inks which make it illegible to anyone who does not have a complete understanding of both Netherese and the Shadowfell.  However, it is clear that the explorers making this map were looking for, a back door into Daggerdale.

This map is presently in the maproom of White Cloak Tower.
