Myth Drannor is concerned with the expansion of the Empire of Netheril, and as such has had an embassy in the Castle for many years now.  They keep a sizeable retinue of elven archers and mages in the Castle, and also have a small group of adventurers who take bounties and gather intelligence.

This Embassy is found on the middle wall, between the outer bailey and middle bailey, and is accessed from the middle bailey.  It is, like the other towers, a fortified and defensible building that has been built for function, not beauty.

The entrance to the tower suggests that the inhabitants would have it otherwise, if they could.  The entire ground floor wall of the tower is covered in rich banners hanging vertically, and an elven-style portico has been added to the front, made out of finely carved oak from Cormanthor, and covered in canopies of rich elven fabric.  A pair of elven centurions are always standing, statue-still on either side of the portico.

The leader of this Embassy is a Gold Elf named Ryeliarlen Miratar, a noble of some small standing in the House Miratar, but a capable diplomat and cunning leader in war.
