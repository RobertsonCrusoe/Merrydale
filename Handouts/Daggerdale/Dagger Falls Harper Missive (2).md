This is the second in a series of missives smuggled out of Dagger Falls by an unnamed Harper agent during the Zhent Occupation of 1372. The letter is dated 9 Eleint, 1372 DR.

Friends,

The word in Dagger Falls is that the Elves of Cormanthor have marched upon the mine in the Dagger Hills and razed it to the ground.

It is said that Captain Braehl and the entire 4th Garrison are slain, and that the elves subsequently marched all the slaves into a pit and burnt them alive.

This seems unlikely to me (the elf-portion of this story, at any rate), but by all accounts the Dagger Hills mine is closed.
Please confirm what you can.

Also, today there was open fighting in the streets between the local Xvimites – the residents of the Brotherhood Tower (a knightly order of Xvimites) – and a bunch of Cyricists.

Before the 6th Fist Company broke them up (their knees and arms included), I did hear one of the priests of Cyric yell “Your silly order is but a thumb on Cyric’s Hand.” This priest subsequently lost his head, and the knight completed his rebuttal by saying “Your treacherous god has not had all of us killed.”

I suppose this is related to the battle of Anathar’s Dell, but I am unsure.

It is exceedingly tense here right now.}}
