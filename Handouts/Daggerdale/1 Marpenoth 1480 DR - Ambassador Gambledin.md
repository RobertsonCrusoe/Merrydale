Gambledin is the voice of dwarven interests in the Dalelands at Castle Radiance. Until the re-establishment of the Kingdom of Tethyamar, Gambledin served as the Curator of the Tethyamar League, a consortium of dwarven researchers, explorers, and their merchant and aristocratic patrons, which had been seeking traces of the ancient dwarven kingdom. He acted as an intermediary between the League and the castle administration, co-ordinating the establishment and collection of bounties of interest to the League. With the rediscovery of the kingdom by the , and following the relief of the Siege of Lastsun, the dwarven presence in the Dalelands became a significant political interest, and Gambledin's office and responsibilities have increased accordingly. On 1 Marpenoth, 1480 DR, Curator Gambledin was officially given the office of 'Ambassador of Tethyamar' by the Council of Silver representing the Kingdom of Tethyamar.

Gambledin still keeps his office in Tethyamar Tower up at Castle Radiance, and continues to post Bounties on behalf of the dwarves. He is a much busier person these days, with the tremendous amount of activity going on in Tethyamar.
