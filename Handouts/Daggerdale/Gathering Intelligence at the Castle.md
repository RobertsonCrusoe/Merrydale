Banitt talked to Zenith Lord Whitegate, and discovered the following;

:The creatures sighted on the road by Tesh Valley Farm are troubling; we have never known the Netherese to use demons before.  We thought they learned from their mistakes in the past.  If it is true that there are demons up there, that are must represent something of strategic importance.

Corran spoke to Captain Rainyerd, who also had some information;

:I was in town only a few days ago, and there were mumblings in the barracks about things flying overhead at night.  Captain Lucius of the 1st company seems to have put stock in the rumours; he has the Warsmith working on new lights to mount on the towers of the town walls, and presently has scores of lantern-bearers going around at night probing the black skies.

:If you go to town, you’ll notice a new company training; the Sun Blade 9th company will be an all-archer company of at least 4 platoons.  They started training the officers in the winter, but they’ll need those new conscripts soon to start training them.  I hear your chum’s brother, Alaric Swift is going to be one of the sergeants, and might even make captain.

With this little intelligence, the party set off to Town.
