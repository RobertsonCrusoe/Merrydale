This is a work that was begun in the winter of 1480, and was compiled in the summer of 1481.  It consists of dozens of large books which contain itemized inventories of every farm and holding under the protection of Dagger Dale and the primacy of the Church.
==Inspiration==  
There was a project like this carried out in England in the 11th Century, called the [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Domesday_Book Domesday Book].
