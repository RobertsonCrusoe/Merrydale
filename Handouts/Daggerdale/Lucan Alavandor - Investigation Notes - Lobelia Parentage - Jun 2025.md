Unless I am missing something, which is very likely...

Lucan needs to have a closer rethink and look at Lobelia's parentage.  From the case files:

Deduction 2: Dorian's eventual elimination by Palus fits the folklore pattern regarding male offspring, albeit delayed. Palus tidied up the loose end.
Hypothesis: Lobelia = Palus's daughter. Placed via changeling swap at birth into the Alavandor household. Andarl/Susserah unaware. Explains the eyes, the unnatural talents noted by Tergus (hag blood), and Dorian's eventual fate.

We know twins were born to Susserah Clarandal in 1428: Lobelia and Dorian, and Dorian died in the 'hunting accident' of 1439, aged 11. Is it assumed 'Lobelia' died in 1441, aged 13, during the tragedy? But she could have been taken away by Palus and still be around right now as a deadly monk/assassin.

If Andarl was the natural father of the twins, and Susserah was the natural mother of the twins, and Lobelia was 'swapped out' without the parents noticing, what happened to the real baby Lobelia?  What do Hags do with those children? Just killed/sacrificed as part of the process?

And so what was Lobelia's role in all this?  It was her younger sister Lilac that suffered and died in the Tragedy of '41, and now lingers as a haunt. But Lobelia seems to be significant, and we do not know what role she played, or what role she was meant to play.

And we still don't really know why.

My best theory now is this:  I'm thinking that Palus and Loki came into an alliance a long time ago, when the Alavandors and Clarandals first arrived. Palus was rewarded for her help with Shadow powers, but they had a falling out. The hunting accident of '39 was blamed on 'The Ancient Mother', so maybe this was a direct attack by Palus on Loki after years of cold war between them.  In return Loki retaliated with the events of '41 wiping out Thistle Ridge. It took a long time, but Palus eventually recovered her strength/power and has able to hit the Durginz Caravan and re-take Thistle Ridge. In retaliation for that, Loki (and her conspirators in Dagger Falls) set up this ridiculous contest to have us go and fuck up Thistle Ridge. So we, Chrysalis (and by extension the Dagger Falls Adjudicating Committee) have been working for Loki the entire time.  We've dealt a blow to Palus and her plans to retake Thistle Ridge, massively furthering Loki's plans in the Dale, and in the process Loki was able to take the orb and the Durginz Captain.

But this leaves me with two big questions:

1. Why was Charrah in league with 'Loki' in that last fight?
2. If Loki and Palus are allies, and not enemies, then what the fuck is actually going on?
