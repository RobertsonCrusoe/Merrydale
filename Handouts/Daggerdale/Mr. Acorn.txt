An old retired and nearly crippled warrior from a distant land, Mr. Acorn is every inch the bitter old campaigner with an ‘in my day’ for every situation.  He is not a diligent butler, or a diplomatic host.  He does what it takes to keep the men fed, the place tolerably tidy, and the fires lit.  He has little time for much socializing, but does like to listen to tales of battles when people are eating in the dining hall, and when he is alone with someone, he might talk about his career as a soldier in Chessenta, and about his hopeless struggle with the new ‘visitors’ to Faerûn, from Abeir.
