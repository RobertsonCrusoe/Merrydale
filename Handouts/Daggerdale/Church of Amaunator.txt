# Church of Amaunator
# Source: The Sieges Perilous wiki (backup dated 2010-02-21)

The Church of Amaunator in Daggerdale is the state religion.  The head of the church, Zenith Lord Argent Whitegate, is also highest ranking official in the dale.

Nearly 100% of people in Daggerdale give Amauanator some level of worship, with over 65% being nearly monotheistic and devout in their worship of the Sun God.  Other good faiths are legally permitted in the dale, but the only other god that is worshiped by any mentionable number of followers is Chauntea.

The church is organized, from the top to bottom, thusly;

• Zenith Lord
• Sun Lord
• Magistrate
• Priest
• Clerk

The current Zenith Lord is Argent Whitegate.  Argent is a capable and popular head of the church and of the dale as a political region.

Sun Lords

There are 3 Sun Lords, and they spend most of their time doing administrative work in Dagger Falls.  Each of these men has a portfolio and a team of Magistrates, Preists and Clerks beneath him.  

Current Sun Lords 
• Blaze Whitegate - Administration of the Town of Dagger Falls (Bishop of Dagger Falls)
• Maddrick Thunderstorm - Administrator of the Yellow Abbey (Dean)
• Silas Archer - Administrator of the Dale (Bishop of Daggerdale)

Magistrates

Magistrates are responsible for most of the day-to-day decision making in the Church and the State.  Some are actual judges, but many hold bureaucratic positions with specific portfolios, such as finance, defense, resources, diplomacy, and so on.  Additionally, there is one Magistrate stationed at each farm in the dale, and these men are jacks-of-all-trades when it comes to governance; they are knowledgable in matters of church, defense, trade, justice, and so on.  There are currently 24 Magistrates in the Dale, most of whom are men.

Magistrates earn 30 gold/tenday.

Priests

Priests are the most common clergy member in teh church, owing mostly to the long duration of service required before one can be considered for ordination as a Magistrate.  All priests serve at least 12 years before they are eligible for promotion, but most serve twice that long.  Preists carry out most of the day-to-day gruntwork for the church.  Of course, they conduct the many observances throughout the day, but most have jobs besides simply tending to their flock.  These jobs include such things as notaries, accountants, librarians, messengers, teachers and all manner of work involving information, law, or literature. Many also have, and enjoy, physical jobs as masons, jointers, painters, and so on.

Priests earn 10 gold/tenday, which is held in trust for them and paid twice/year, or when they leave the service.

Clerks

Clerks are priests in training, and are usually found at the Yellow Abbey.  These are mostly young men between the ages of 14-24, who are being educated at the Abbey.  Education for these young men (and women) is at least 6 years long, and is styled after the rigid monastic orders of the earlier centuries.  There is much emphasis placed on poverty, discipline, sobriety, chastity, and humility.

Some men come to the service of Amaunator later in life, and it is possible for men like this to spend less time as a clerk.

In the last two years of education, clerks are given municipal duties for which their name derives; the work in the various offices of the church and government, and learn the workings of bereaucracy.

Clerks are paid nothing beyond lodging and board.
