The Commander of the Silver Guard and a loyal member of Clan Deep Anvil, and dedicated follower of Clangeddin Silverbeard. Dunkirk is a little too serious even for other career military dwarves. He is a veteran of a dozen campaigns, and spear-headed the assault on the the headquarters position of the Netherese during the siege of Fort Lastsun. he is said to have driven off the general of the Netherese army himself.
