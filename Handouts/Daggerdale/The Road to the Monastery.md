* The “leader” of The Mercykillers in Waterdeep has been dubbed \"The Sphinx\". Being described as “a bit of this and a bit of that...” Might be a mage, might be a warrior, might be something else altogether.
*There was speculation that Tyriol’s father is the leader of the Mercykillers. That speculation was confirmed by Maeghuen Grimbold; Tyriol’s mother.
*Radha Leveaux has been having sporadic dreams involving shadows, hunger, and a desperate craving/seeking of “something” (I believe only Radha knows this so RP accordingly)
*Volund perma-frosted a tower room at Kohr-Murron stables before the party left while he slept.
*The party managed to destroy Warklegnaw’s Span and in doing so delayed the army of The Hand of Doom (Once believed to be called the the army of The Red Hand) until quite possibly the next spring.
*The party is currently travelling to the Monastery of Sundered Faith, accompanying a dwarven priest of Ohgma by the name of Hrogan Goringrimm. Hrogan has informed the party that the monastery has a repository of information there created by a Chosen of Ohgma, Kennyth Thames and if there is any place that might have information about the Towers Tyrannous which was the only location mentioned by Lady Saharel in relation to the Horns of Eltab
*Zamomel Stass
*It was discovered that the monastery (upon entering through a little known “backdoor”) had been assaulted by the Hand of Doom.
*Upon interrogating an orc that survived the initial contact with the party, the Raid Chieftain that is leading the raid on the monastery is “Rahn’ohk” and that the warchief of the warband that IS the Hand of Doom is known as Huu’rom ak Murul, which translates to Huu’rom the Blue in orcish. Seemingly, the warchief is simply referred to most often as “The Blue”
*Leosferis Duo’din – an Eladrin Warrior - rumoured to be a high ranking lieutenant in the Mercykillers, one time friend of Maeghuen Grimbold and long time companion of “The Sphinx” tracked down the party and perished in the battle alongside his fomorian companion by the name of Q’horg.
*Upon concluding the first probing strike in what appeared to be a forge well beneath the monastery, the party had saved five priests and one woman from torture and certain death.
