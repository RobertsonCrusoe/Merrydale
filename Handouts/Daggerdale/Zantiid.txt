This purple-eyed beholder is thought to be the motivating force behind Company Drurginz in the Summer of ’71. It is thought that this beholder (first seen by Ellyth in the headquarters of the Company of the Red Herrings in early summer) is an ally of Thagdul, possibly his boss. This same beholder was spotted in Undermountain at the Dark Market by Esperel, where it convinced the elf that he owed it a favour. Additionally, the mark of this beholder, discovered somehow by Quellar Dalael, was found on a note to Malkor in Neverwinter.

It is suspected to be an ally of Manshoon.
