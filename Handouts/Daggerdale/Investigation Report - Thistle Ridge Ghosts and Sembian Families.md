Alvund’s wives all shared the name “Maeliticus”
Claudius’s first children in 1408 were twins, Tergus and Susserah
Susserah gave birth to twins by Andarl Alavandor in 1428, Dorian and Lobelia | The prevalence of twins may predate Thistle Ridge (the Maeliticus and Sakurth sisters may have been twins)
