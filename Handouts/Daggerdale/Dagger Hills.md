The hills that make up the eastern border of Daggerdale have always been full of trouble.  They used to be infested with wererats and goblin-kind, but in the last century, Cormanthor has grown over the hills at an unnatural rate, covering them with huge majestic oak trees, which have attracted new lycanthropes.  Today, the hills are dominated by clans of werewolves, for the most part.

Some clans of note have been the Silver Tail Clan, which came to an end in the spring of 1479 DR, and the Hollow Hills Clan, which was able to drive off the remnants of Silver Tail after the defeat that they suffered at the hands of the creatures that attacked them from the Shadowfell.  The leader of the Silver Tails was a huge male named Braxus, who had long kept up a treaty with the farmers at Crossed Blades Farm.

Laid atop this political map of small clans of werewolves, is a larger polity belonging to werebears and other larger, more dangerous creatures of the area.  One werebear, known only as The Old Man in the Hill held dominion over the Silver Tails, and now has dominion over the Hollow Hills Clan.  

The Dagger Hills conceal a couple of old landmarks in their monster-ruled woods, which include Castle Daggerdale, and the Dagger Hills Mine, upon which so much fate pivoted.
