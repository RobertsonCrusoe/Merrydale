This bloodstained note, addressed to Lord Mourngrym Amcathra waqs the first of a series of missives smuggled out of Dagger Falls by an unnamed Harper agent during the Zhent Occupation of 1372.

Lord Amcathra,

This is all I have learned about the military in Dagger falls:
*Yarkul is the head of everything, military included
*Two branches: the Garrison and the Fist

THE GARRISON
*Led by Commander (formerly Constable) Tren Noemfor (IV epaulets).

There are 10 companies of 20 men, plus a captain (III) and 2 sergeants (II) each.
*1st Garrison is led by Captain Denethor, Randal Morn’s captor. They are always at the Tower.
*2nd Garrison, led by Captain Innisworth. It is stationed at the First mine (Rainhill).
*3rd Garrison is stationed at the Second mine (Chillholm). It is led by Captain Aegis, rumoured to be a half-ogre. Both sergeants are his sons.
*4th Garrison is led by Captain Braehl. It is stationed at the Third mine in the Dagger Hills. This company is exclusively made up of crazy Cyricists.
*5th through 8th Garrisons are stationed in town.
*9th Garrison is a cavalry company. It patrols the East Road. It is led by Captain Vargul, an aged veteran of this area’s wars.
*10th Garrison is also a cavalry company. It patrols the South Road.

THE FIST

25 Companies, mostly of 20 men each.

1st Company
*Led by Commander Harrington (IV), also the Commander of the Fist Mercenary Company itself. It is stationed at the Tower.

2nd Company - Garlunk’s Roughriders
*Mostly half-orcs led by the Villain of Scardale. It patrols the East Road.
*9 survivors
3rd Company - Blacktooth’s Hussars
*Highly seasoned riders, mostly half-orcs. This company patrols the South Road.
4th Company - Sandalwood’s Roughriders
* is known as the Murderer of Mirabar. He is a nasty half-elf with infallible aim
5th Company - Gumbottom’s Guard
*A unit comprised of 10 hill giants. They are stationed at the gates to the Eastern Bailey
6th Company - Klangedon’s Hammers
*A terror squad of ogres dispatched for specific duties
7th Company - Gadbout’s Fangs
*The Captain Gadbout is known to have killed 2 sheriffs in the Dales. Another ‘specialist’ squad, mostly of orcs.
8th Company - Dunvegan’s Corsairs
*All the finest archers in the Fist are in this company, stationed in the tower.
9th Company - (Cyricists)
*Stationed at the third mine
10th Company - (former Red Plumes)
*Stationed at the first mine.
11th Company (former Purple Dragons)
*Stationed at the second mine.
12th Company
*A group of ogre scounts (?!) led by the relentless Yin-Xu. This company is known to have done a great deal of damage to The Circle, and is hunting the Wanderer
13th to 20th Companies
*Stationed in Anauroch
21st to 25th Companies
*In training at the Citadel of the Raven
26th Company
*Became William Cormaeril’s Hearth Guard

OTHER CONSIDERATIONS
*The Naug-Adar are Yarkul’s mage-lackeys. They command companies of the Fist and Garrison and are individually responsible for assigning ‘priests’ to their company. I believe there are at least ten of them.
*. This is the militant wing of the Church of Iyachtu Xvim. It is led by the enigmatic Baron Drake, and I believe they are outside the chain of command in Dagger Falls.}}
