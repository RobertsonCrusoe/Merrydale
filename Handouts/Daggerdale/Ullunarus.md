Marshall Ullunaris is the official who does most of the governing at the Embassy.  He gives the day-to-day orders and keeps the place running.  He couldn't do this without his Steward.

The Marshall is a stern man who adheres to protocol in the strictest fashion.  He was chosen for this position exactly because of this.  He is actually a follower of Amuanator as well as Corellon, and fits right in with the bureaucratic nature of the Castle.

He is Eladrin, and is rumoured to think his race superior to the wood- and wild-elves of the region.
