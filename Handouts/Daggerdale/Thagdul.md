Mystery continues to surround this man. Thagdul had the Company of the Red Herring promise favour to him, as they stole a cargo that belonged to him (8 blades of wicked-forging). They repaid this by running dangerous yet profitable missions for him, against Co. Ersgul. When the Company started to fill their ranks with more scrupulous, and questioning members, Thagdul explained, in confidence, that he was actually a Harper, deep under cover as a Zhent. The identity of Thagdul he stole from a Zhent mage he killed back in Zhentil Keep. He explained that his cover had to be convincing, and he thus used some bona-fide Zhents in his organization, but that he had conscripted help from virtuous heroes, with the long-view in his mind’s eye. Just as other Harpers began to confront the Heroes, Thagdul left The City for a mission in the Eastern Heartlands, and has not been heard from since.
