# Thunderstorm Family
# Source: The Sieges Perilous wiki (backup dated 2010-02-21)

The Thunderstorm family saw a bit of a decline after the Whitegate Epiphany, and the rise of Amaunator and the Whitegate Family.  The son of Rewel Thunderstorm, Karrigan, married into the Whitegate Family in 1425, though, and changed thier fortunes.  Radiance Whitegate was always thought to be the favoured child of her father, Loedegan Whitegate, and she did indeed have a lovely estate given to her as a wedding gift at Foothills Farm, where she began the prolific line of the Thunderstorm family.  

The union of this marriage produced Dauntless Thunderstorm and a large number of other family members that have multiplied to a couple dozen members in the current generation.  Many of the Thunderstom family still live up at the farm, but the more ambitious have sought out service in the Church and several have distinguished themselves in military service.

Some members of the Whitegate Family resent the Thunderstorm side of the family, whom they percieve as ambitous and scheming.  The Thunderstorms have produced 3 Sunlords, the most notable of which is Maddrick.  Maddrick is a Sunlord at the cathedral in Dagger Falls, and also holds the seat of the Dean of The Yellow Abbey, a position he does not relish.  He is thought to have aspriations for Blaze Whitegate's chair as Bishop of Dagger Falls, a position that is answerable only to the Zenith Lord.  

Many of the present-day members of the family are in active service in the Sun Blades, though none have become members of the Order of the Lambent Shield.
