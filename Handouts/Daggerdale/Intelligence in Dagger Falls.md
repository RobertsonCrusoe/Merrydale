A collection of facts and rumours uncovered in Dagger Falls on the 4th and 5th of Mirtul.

==The Word on the Street==

*There are many rumours about the Black Saint and the Black Hand of Gaebril Tremalkin floating around.  Some say the Hand is here in Dagger Falls.  Some say it doesn’t exist.

*The troubles on the farms this year are because of a stepped-up aggression by Netheril.  Everyone knows it.  There is a large elven host gathering in the Elven Court right now, getting ready to come to our aid.

*The lycanthrope problem down at Crossed Blades has gotten worse; there is some sort of war for territory in the Hills, and the Netherese are able to direct one clan of the monsters, somehow.  A permanent fort is being built at the site of some cave, or right on top of it maybe

*Most of the farms have been in to do some trading in the last week, except for Tesh Valley.  They were supposed to have had a good winter, so I don’t know what could be delaying them.  Apparently, a platoon of Sun Dragoons has been sent up there to see what’s going on.

*The carrier pigeon method of communication has broken down.  No one really knows what is happening to the birds.  Likewise, no one really knows what is happening at the farms and forts in the heartland.  The only good news has come from farms that have actually sent people to town recently for supplies and to report.  I’m worried about those places that don’t send anyone.

*The reason for training a special company of archers is because of an increase in flying agents of Netheril.  Many have been seen over the skies of the farms and forts, and even over the city.  We are hoping to destroy the threat from the skies, but don’t quite know how we can cover all that space.

== Magistrate Hoetmer's Account ==
*We doubt that the Dark Garden is going to use the same tactics this year as they did on Dwimmerholme. We will be looking too closely for such breaches in our security and our faith. That doesn’t mean we won’t be vigilant, mind you. No, we think they are switching to a new plan of increased armed conflict in our heartland, while the army of Netheril draws our troops into the mountains and the plains. We are even considering the idea that the posturing of the armies is to actually draw military resources away from our heartland. This is why the enormous bounty has been placed upon the Dark Garden; we need help countering them. We really can’t spare the troops.

*The Dark Garden’s strength seems to be in finding dangerous allies or pawns to do their work. They have used werewolves, gnolls, orcs by the tribe, and can even use normal animals by some magic. We think they probably have enough of these allies from last year, and maybe years previous, to keep up a tempo of conflict that will make us react; we have more men in all the farms and more road patrols than ever because of them. However, to find them this year, we will be looking for areas of increased activity using new foes. We think they have been away all winter recruiting, and we are waiting for some of our scouts to report back on any strange movements of heretofore unknown monsters in our area. That is where we think we may find them.

*We are sure that they actually established three covens last year. Well, Dwimmerholme would have been the third. We looked at the dates and the calendar of the stars, and realized that they probably established the third ritual a month before the events at Dwimmerholme. The night of the Dwimmerholme revelation was on the full moon of Harvestide, but it was only the second of three rituals for the Founding. We speculate that a very important coven was being established that same night, and that the spiritual leader of the Dark Garden was at that meeting with the Black Hand, completing the Founding there.

*We have an Oghman working with us now who has been looking over reports and doing her own divine consultations, and she suggests that there were three periods last summer when suspected Dark Garden activities all resembled that before the 2nd founding ritual at Dwimmerholme. Each of these three periods ended on a new moon. We therefore suspect that we missed 2 Foundings.

*We have been implementing a system of counter intelligence to deal with the Dark Garden here in town. We have been planting marked information which we have been then looking to resurface. Thus far, it has turned up nothing. As a result, we think that the Dark Garden is actually not working out of, or using their assets here in town like they used to. With the increase in hostilities in the heartland, we thus concluded that the Dark Garden is behind that.

*Our worry is that the Dark Garden was successful in creating a coven here in town. Their increased activities away from town might serve two functions; to draw military resources away from the Netherese front, or to draw our attention from their coven or covens. We don’t think the former is true; they must know that we will not let the western front fall in any event; we would never regain Castle Radiance if we were to loose it. If they have indeed planted a coven here in town, they are being damned thorough about keeping it hidden. We have not a single lead to follow in this matter.

*The Black Hand of Saint Gaebril is real. We are sure that the Dark Garden has it, and that it turns up at the third Ritual of Founding. This information is divinely appointed and beyond reproach. The Black Hand is not here in Dagger Falls, but it is in the Dale, somewhere. If we can work backwards, perhaps we can beat this threat; we must take the bait of attacking the allies and pawns of the Dark Garden, and find them through these lackeys. Then, we can find their lair, and the Black Hand, and put an end to this threat.
