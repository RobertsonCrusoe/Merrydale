The name of this leader of the coven outside of Dwimmerholme Farm was first heard from the tongue of Doghan, who dropped the name in an elaborate lie that he told, trying to save himself.
 
Xaphan also gave up this name, and purchased his freedom with his cooperation.
 
It is supposed that Seir might be a Devil of some sort, but no one is sure.
