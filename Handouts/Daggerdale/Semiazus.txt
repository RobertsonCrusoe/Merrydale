The Familiar of Vetis, and the constant foil of Bravo Team, Semiazus was cunning and slippery.  He is known to have left a trail of rotting animals to lead a dire bear into the party.  In combat, he flew around nearly invisible, poisoning his enemies before flying off invisible again.

Ultimately, he was destroyed at the ambush outside of Dwimmerholme Farm.  It seemed as though he changed his fighting strategy when his master, Vetis, was on the ropes.  He threw himself foolishly in the way of Vetis' foes, and was destroyed.

A later accounting of the appearance of Semiazus, by the captured Dannus Weaver jr. revealed that the imp was purchased with one of the souls of the captured caravan workers.
