== The Burial ==

On the 27th of Mitul, 1479 Dane Grimwald was buried in the graveyard of Castle Radiance with all the other adventurers and mercenaries who perish while in the service of Castle Radiance and Amaunator.

Dane was buried with Corran Altair's heirloom broadsword of Torm, with the quiet prayer that Torm grants the man the guidance and peace he could not find while alive.

Corran retrieved Dane's white cloak and white hooded tabard that Dane seemed to always wear and vowed to wear from that point forward, hoping he could display the same manner of reckless courage that Dane was known for.

Bargle Battlemaster attended the brief service, and maintained a distant and stoney expression as Dane's friends said what few good things they had to say about thier fallen comrade.  Afterwards he grumbled a bit, and was heard to say to himself \"If only he lived another couple months to learn some patience and teamwork...one more dead...\" he shook his head and walked off.
