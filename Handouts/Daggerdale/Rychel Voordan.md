[PC Attribution: Rychel Voordan (Scott McLaren)]
A former jongleur, and a stalker without equal, Rychel Voordan is the best urban tracker in Waterdeep (some say the Realms). He is the sworn enemy the cult of Ghaunadar, and was a sometime comrade of Harkas Kormallis.
