A warlock who had both a pact with, and can trace his blood line to, the Devil known as Bel.

He was the head, or Belial of a coven outside of Dwimmerholme Farm.  He was destroyed in Marpenoth of 1481 DR by White Cloaks Bravo Team after he emerged from a portal/circle, outside which the heroes were laying in ambush.
