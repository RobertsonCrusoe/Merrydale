Flamerule 3, 1435 
The gentle embrace of Thistle Ridge has welcomed me home once more, and I find great joy in the company of my family. The village is abuzz with the laughter and energy of the many children born during my absences. My heart swells with pride and contentment as I spend time with my beloved Senthia and our ever-growing clan of cousins, nieces, and nephews. 

One child in particular has captured my attention during this visitmy niece, Lobelia Alavandor. Though only eight years old, she exhibits a remarkable aptitude for the new martial tradition taught at Fathers school. The Alavandor family, closely tied to the governance and education of Thistle Ridge, has always been known for their intellect and leadership. Yet, Lobelias prowess in combat is a surprising and extraordinary departure from this norm. 

Lobelias different-colored eyes, one grey and the other blue, have always marked her as unique. From an early age, it was clear she was touched for greatness. Her discipline, wisdom, and strength are unparalleled, even among those many years her senior. As I watch her train, I am struck by her natural ability and the ease with which she masters complex techniques. 

The new fighting style, which I once viewed with skepticism, seems almost to have been created for someone like Lobelia. Her movements are fluid and precise, her strikes powerful and controlled. She embodies the very essence of the art, blending the physical with the mental in a way I have rarely seen. 

Despite my own achievements and rank within the Sun Blades, I find myself in awe of this young girl. Her potential is boundless, and I am certain she will accomplish great things in the years to come. Perhaps it is the land itself, the fertile soil of Thistle Ridge, that nurtures such exceptional talent. Or perhaps it is simply the convergence of the right lineage, the right time, and the right spirit. 

As I enjoy this brief respite from my duties, I am reminded of the importance of nurturing and guiding the next generation. Lobelias gifts must be honed and cherished, for she is a beacon of what our people can achieve. I will speak with Father about ensuring she receives the best possible training and support, for her future is not just a personal triumph but a symbol of hope and strength for all of Thistle Ridge. 

In the days to come, I shall savor every moment spent with my family and take solace in the knowledge that the legacy of our village is in capable hands. The future is bright, and with children like Lobelia, I am confident that Thistle Ridge will continue to thrive and prosper. 

Journal of Tergus Clarandal
