This Drow was rumoured to run Company Dalael’s more risky business endeavours, and was also rumoured to have a short fuse for such a long-lived race. Quellar had the good sense and fortune to marry into the Dezlentyr Family, the first Family to dilute the human bloodlines of the Nobility when Arlos married and elven maid 40 years ago. Though she is now dead (many rumours of her death abound), Lyrethian sired twins, Corin and Corinna, who live on. Corinna is openly married to Quellar Dalael, who was first a business associate of the family. This marriage is publicly known, as are their two children, 3 year-old Lyrethian, and 2 year-old Istorvir (ages as of 1372). House Dezlentyr is a wealthy Family, and Quellar is much-loved by them, especially by Arlos and Corin, whom he is rumoured to have taken on ‘exotic trips’ which last 5 months and don’t go by boat or coach. He was much like his wife, Corinna; he craves adventure and danger, and is seemingly fearless.

He was not active in Waterdeep during 1371, having been on a journey through the Underdark after Corinna went missing early that year.

Status: Deceased. Quellar was killed in a confrontation with Garth Shanks, Beljuril and the Jack Dashers in 1373.
