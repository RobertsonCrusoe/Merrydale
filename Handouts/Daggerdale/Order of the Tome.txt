This is a semi-secretive organization based out of Candlekeep, in the service of both Ohgma and Denier.  It is suspected that the agenda of this organization coincides with the celestial agendas of the deities associated with it, though this isn't perfectly clear.

The origins of the Order are unclear, and most of the records concerning it are in the deepest libraries of Candlekeep.  Some historians in Waterdeep have obscure references to the Order from the early 11th century DR, in reference to their helping Ahghairon become the first lord of Waterdeep.  However, there are some compelling arguments that this Order may in fact be much, much older.  Certainly, the crest of Candlekeep itself appears to be a stylized version of a known iteration of the symbol of Thoth.  The many known forays of this organization into the Mulhorandi region, especially in opposition to the Red Wizards of Thay suggest a vested interest in the region.  Rumours of vast amounts of correspondence between the keep and Skuld in the late 14th century, just before the Spellplague also point to a Mulhorandi connection.  Finally, it is suspected that Nestrius Adamanth was being influenced by the Order when he established the The Watchtower, and that this organization is actually a militant arm of the Order of the Tome.

Whatever relationship existed between Skuld and the Order of the Tome before the Spellplague is certainly extinct, along with that city.  Currently, it would seem that Candlekeep is the nerve-centre for this organization, and that it has agents working in many important parts of the Realms of the 15th century.

It is important to note that this organization does not follow the hierarchy of any one clergy or faith; it is a religiously pluralistic organization that is not beholden to any one power, either spiritual or temporal.  As such, it is not a formal arm of the political machine of Candlekeep, but rather something more lateral and nebulous.

==Daggerdale==

*The only member-in-residence of this order in Daggerdale is Brother Darred.  He is primarily a diplomat in service for the Emissaries from Candlekeep, but also fills the role of chief intelligence officer for this region. 

*The woman known as Sara Estelmer spent much time in Daggerdale between 1479-1481.  Her role was as something of a diplomat from the Font of Knowledge in Waterdeep, though she managed to gain access to the Historic Wing of the Yellow Abbey through her relationship with Magistrate Phineas Hoetmer.  Though her access could be interpreted as a happy unintended consequence of her love for the old Magistrate, there are some very high-placed people who would interpret her love as a means to an end.  

*Nathan Gale is a member-afield currently serving in the area.  He receives orders from Brother Darred directly.
