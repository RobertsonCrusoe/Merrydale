This hall is one of the three buildings that form the triangle of the impressive Inner Bailey, the others being Loedgan’s Hall and the Fortress Cathedral which soars above the very peak of the mountain upon which the Castle rests.
   
Madigan’s Hall is a barracks for the Sun Blades and the Knights of the Castle.  It can accommodate as many as 400 soldiers if needed, though only half that many are typically garrisoned here.  It is a 3-story stone building with 3 corresponding tiers of battlements on the outer wall.  It is a thick and imposing building designed first and foremost for the defence of the Inner Bailey and the Fortress Cathedral adjacent to it.
