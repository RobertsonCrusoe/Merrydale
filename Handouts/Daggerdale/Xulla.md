This green-eyed beholder was suspected to have been the force behind Company Ersgul, and was known to Quevin Dalael. However, Dalael intelligence noted that Xulla often had Company Dalael run missions counter to Company Ersgul. Xulla was spotted in the Wavesilver Villa by Esperel, and tried to recover the beholder staff stolen from Yarkul. Xulla was also present at the Phaulkon Cargo heist by the Drow, in the sewers. At that time, Xulla and Quevin exchanged harsh words before parting. It was Xulla who turned Rychel to stone.

Quevin later provided intelligence suggesting that Xulla was sponsoring Company Aldebarran, though his cousin Y'morth suggested that direction was provided not through the company's director, Kendal Thorne, but through another.
