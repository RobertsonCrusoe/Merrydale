The forum has been set up with several categories:
Plot Narrative & PC Actions This is the most important category for ongoing play. Normally the first post would be by the DM to initiate an encounter, and players would be expected to contribute their characters' observable actions only.

PC Background & Reflections This is an optional category for journals, letters, reports, or other documents composed by the characters, as well as descriptions of their heritage and background. It can also be used to record thoughts, internal monologues, dreams, etc., that would not normally be accessible to other characters during the action.

Daggerdale Gazeteer This is a category for the DM to provide material for the setting.
Table Talk This category is for meta-game discussion. Keep it civil.
Rules Discussion 
 A category in which to debate the finer points of the game rules, and discuss interpretations thereof.

Software Discussion This category is earmarked for any discussion of the forum software itself. Other relevant discussions may include discussions of RPTools, Wizards software, Macros, wiki, etc.

Forum Protocol  This is a category for posting protocol for the forums and discussing ways of streamlining the distributed online gaming experience.
