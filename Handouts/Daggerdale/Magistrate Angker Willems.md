Ankger Willems replaced Hoetmer as Magistrate at Dwimmerholme Farm in the late autumn of 1479, after the Dark Garden affair.  Willems is especially pious and aggressive, and his style of leadership was not well recieved at the farm.  In the winter of 1480, he was replaced by Magistrate Renfrew.

Willems now serves as an attendant to the Bishop of Dagger Falls.  Ironically, it was he who lead the ritual in late Marpenoth of 1481 that returned the souls to the victims of the Sons of Bel from Dwimmerholme.
