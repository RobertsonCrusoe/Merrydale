A letter sent from Alen Reks to Mayor Darius Winters, on the 5th of Marpenoth, 1481 DR.  Delivered, unopened, by Sgt. Hennigan Paros.

To whom it may concern;

First and foremost, your discretion in any and all things involving this letter and those who deliver it is mandatory. The contents of this letter are for your eyes and the eyes of my men, period. Depending on who it is delivering this letter to you, you may or may not be aware of the situation.

The White Cloaks have arrived to assist you in your time of need. They are investigating the disappearances of your townsfolk, and the rumours of slavers and kidnappers that follow in the wake of such absences.

Being a particularly intelligent person, you surely understand why it is imperative that the presence of my White Cloaks in Dwimmerholme remains strictly unspoken of. Of course, I do not need to explain that, were any locals in league with the slavers to become aware of their presence, trouble would no doubt follow, followed in turn by any and all leads promptly drying up.

The White Cloaks are a mercenary company first and foremost. I'm sure you see where this is going. I will not put a price on our virtuous efforts -- in such tragic times, to do such a thing would be nothing short of crass and insensitive. I will, however, pre-emptively thank you profusely for your offer of generousity in the form of whatever donation you see fit to award my team and company for their efforts at locating your lost townsfolk and expunging the immoral individuals menacing your blessed community.

A company cannot stay aloft on high morals alone, and I am pleased to hear that you understand and agree wholeheartedly with this sentiment.

My Sergeant and Quartermaster will be ready to discuss the matter at your discretion.

Who could put a price on the safety of your lost townsfolk?

Walk in the Light.

Administrator Reks}}
