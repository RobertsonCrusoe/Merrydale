450px|rightThese nasty shadow-gargoyles were the agents used to terrorize the village that Hruun of Thunder hails from, in the distant Storm Peaks.  They are nasty predators who scatter their foes using the dark magic of the Voice, and then swoop down to pick up victims whom they later drop from whatever height they can achieve. 

600px|right|thumb|These enormous monsters lead packs of Kir Lanan, and are able to make psychic assaults of their prey, dividing them up and immobilizing them.
