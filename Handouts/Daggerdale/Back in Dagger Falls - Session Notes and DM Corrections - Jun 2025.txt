PART 1: SESSION RECAP — STEPHEN ROBERTSON (16 Jun 2025)
-------------------------------------------------------
Source: Message 1977918cf5ec3932

Fellas, I wanted to get this all written down before we lost any threads,
but I crashed last night after the session. I would appreciate it if you
could fill in any blanks. I don't remember what Ser Rowan got up to at all.

At Thistle Ridge

Mirtul 2

- Jasper tells Lucan that Madigara Dale is Invictus's spy then storms off
- Alexandus Ward confirms that Lucan's description of the orb taken by the Shadovar is consistent with what he knows of the Slave Orbs of the Imperiosis Orbis. Moreover, the fact that the orb was not glowing is an indication that the orb had been charged. If you look at the orb with the bands on the outside, it will look like a symbol of Shar. Jasper muses aloud that it might be because, actually, now that he comes to think of it, Coventina was a spellcaster. Why didn't Lucan ever ask him about that?
- Boxfray is given his orders
   - survey the area, identify all entrances to the sewers
   - protect work crews that will be sent for four-hour shifts of clearing underbrush, rubble, and scavenging useful building materials
   - organize a rotation of troops from the temporary garrison at TR Tower to Thistle Ridge - 2 days on, 2 days off
   - Boxfray asks to set up a Foehammer Tabernacle in the old TR Garrison
   - Madigara Dale impugns Coventina's honour but is shut down by Forge Farmountain. She goes on to expand haughtily on Chrysalis's legal obligations regarding the recovery of Thistle Ridge.
   - Loot shared out
- Jasper gives Nemia her orders:
   - Stick around and look for spooky stuff
   - Lucan eavesdrops, but she discovers him
   - She says don't mix up the Imperiosus Orbis and the Tenebrius Ostium - very different. Go talk to Vessuvian if you want an expert.

Mirtul 3

- Back to Dagger Falls
   - We pass Denelius Ormanth and his colleagues, who have mustered their caravans at Gribbon's Farm and with the roads clear are now heading out. He Thanks Jeska and inquires about setting up a factory in TR on his return at the end of the trading season. He makes no mention of the reward he offered for clearing the roads (which is fair enough, since the deadline he gave us was Tarsakh 20)
   - News of Chrysalis's return precedes them and they arrive back in Dagger Falls to great acclaim
   - Lucan missed Greengrass, damnit.
- Back to the Embassy
   - Jeska gives the makeup supplies to Fiona, who tells us they are locally sourced. She is happy to see us. Jasper traumatizes her by telling her she probably won't be abducted, corrupted, and murdered. He's pretty sure, at least. 90%.
   - Merry gives us his congratulations and sitrep (don't remember any details)
   - Lucan composes some correspondence
   - Jasper returns Coventina's body and gives our story to Lilly and her father Renley. Lilly is beside herself, and tells Jasper she can't understand how he could have fucked up this badly.
   - Renley basically blames Jasper and tells him sternly that he'll be hearing from his agents.
   - Jeska composes a letter to Godwin Ferguson to be sent via Charity House. We give Ambassador Whitmore an early summary of our official report. He warns us that he is probably the only one on the Adjudicating Committee without a hidden agenda. Funny that.
   - Sanduskin gets his mail, a year late. Tells us that we should talk to Factor Belwintle if we want to know more about the Drurginz caravans. Gives us mate's rates on scroll supplies - 50% off list price

Mirtul 4

- In Dagger Falls
   - Jasper does a bit of shopping and then sets off back to his date with Nemia. Mari accompanies him.
   - Lucan visits Ondarl's imports and learns (after a charitable donation to the Spice Importer's Pension Fund) that the beauty items that we recovered from Charrah were purchased by someone fitting the description of Coventina Cassimar on the night of the Mayor's Ball. Lucan offers an additional gold piece to keep the gossip pump primed.
   - Lucan visits Tamuk, the Chessentan rug merchant, and asks about the Alavaxi. The family name is uncommon so far as he can tell. The kukri we found is definitely made by Xerxes, and is "a young lady's weapon." He does not seem to have any knowledge of the real Alavandors or the Old Patriarch, as he does not truck with slavers like they do down in Luthcheq. It turns out that some of the rugs in Charrah's Chambres were originally intended for his shop, which he established shortly before the Drurginz Caravan went missing. He laments the takeover of the Shield Trading Company by the Drurginz syndicate, shaking his head at the decline in quality of service. Lucan realizes that Jenwendle's line "From Hillsfar we came, to Voonlar. No more shield, no. Just Drurginz and the captain. He seemed lost, didn't know how to seem" was an allusion to this takeover and may hold more clues than we thought.
   - Invictus barges into the Embassy as Lucan and Jeska are enjoying their evening meal. He asks for a one-on-one meeting with Jeska, who comes clean with him. Lucan eavesdrops. Invictus takes Jeska's story as proof of Coventina's corruption.
   - Invictus reveals the theft of the Slave Orb sometime in the past year. No suspects or ideas about motive. Sloppy management, thinks Lucan, if not downright incompetence. Jeska says, "Let's not tell Cassimar the truth." Invictus agrees.
   - Lucan visits Brother Vessuvian after dinner and reads him in on certain details in the journal of Aisling Maeliticus. Vessuvian is uncomfortable talking about shadow artifacts in the Yellow Abbey, so Lucan invites him to go out on the town.
   - At Quick Axes, Vessuvian gets treated to a less cloistered experience than he is used to. Lucan asks him to investigate the "coven" and the details of the Tenebrius Ostium activation in Daerlun, laying out the various Daerlunian connections. Vessuvian wants to know more, but soon his attention wanders.
