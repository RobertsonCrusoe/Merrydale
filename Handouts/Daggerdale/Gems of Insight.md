==The Wonderbringer Warforge==

After making a number of inquiries around the Castle, Iolus is able to locate Bollinger's residence at the Wonderbringer Warforge above Fort Luminous. He frowned a moment as he brushed momentarily at a wrinkle in his tabard with one hand before reaching out firmly as well as loudly knocking on Bollinger's door.

\"Ahem\" .. sounds of scuffling.. then a crash .. \"oops!\"

Footsteps to the door and it opens a scraggly bearded man stands before you..

\"yerse, ow cin a be asstant\" He frowns, snaps his fingers *snap snap*, his eyes brighten and he spits a small piece of metal into his hand.

\"ahem, yes, how can i be of assistance? Warburton isn't it?\"

Iolus was momentarily surprised that this strange, and unseemly looking man was the one that Altair had spoken so promisingly about. He was also surprised that Bollinger knew who he was.

His brow was furrowed in confusion and concern as he looked at the small piece of metal that the man has spit into the palm of his hand before he shook his head slightly and tried to rein in his thoughts.

\"It's been a long journey here. Might I come in?\"

Altair had warned him that he would do well to control the flow of the conversation with Bollinger, otherwise he might be here for days. Also, it certainly would benefit Iolus if the conversation they were about to have were more private in nature.

The little man looks Iolus up and down... as if thinking about the request…

His fingers snap rapidly *snap snap snap*

\"Might you? well I should think that it is wholly possible that you might.\"

He doesn't move from the doorway however.

\"Will you come in? Now there is the more pertinent question! *snap snap* Yes perhaps in truth the real question one should ask upon being received at a threshold.\"

He seems to be looking right past Iolus now as if thinking through the complexities of social grace in the realms.

\"Goodday to you sir -- one would say - Will I come in?\" 

He murmurs on.

\"A threshold is a dangerous thing to pass\" *snap snap* \"to question whether or not it is safe to cross one SHOULD ask Will I come in?.\" 

He then frowns as if puzzled *SNAP* his fingers almost boom…

\"But to cross any threshold is folly without invitation, so mayhap it be that it is not pertinent to ask WILL I come in?, as one surely Will not without an invitation, and yet one surely will with an invitation…\" he looks to be digging his mind free of a thousand tons of muck...

\"So the correct phrasing would be to ask - CAN I come in?, alas unless disfigured or barred in some way anyone CAN step through a door, and being capable is not evidence that an invitation has been granted.\" His brow furrows again.

*snap snap, snap snap* his fingers seem to push his mind...

\"So that only leaves one option, one must ask leave to enter... one would say.. - MAY I come in, or in the common, MIGHT I come in?, yes that would do just fine.\" He smiles to himself seeming pleased with his logic.

His eyes come back into focus and he jumps as if startled at seeing Iolus before him.

\"How can I be of assistance? Warburton isn't it?\"

==Warburton Agog==

He had heard monstrous tales as a child, told by other children to scare one another...tales of horror and tales of madness to make him huddle under his blankets at night and pray that everything would be alright.

The act of simply listening to Bollinger was enough to make him think he was staring into a pit of madness. He had stopped trying to meet Bollinger's eyes and instead, he found himself focusing on the odd little man's snapping fingers. A small muscle at the corner of his eye, twitching each time he heard a snap. His jaw was set, his teeth pressed firmly together as he reminded himself that dealing with this man might very well be necessary in order to safeguard his White Cloaks against the devious plans of Deadnettle and the Dark Garden.

He had to clear his throat, an attempt to disguise his growl of irritation before offering Bollinger a strained smile.

Best to jump straight in and hope I come out alive; he told himself.

\"Indeed; Warburton. We were going to meet inside to discuss the project I have for you?\"

==Bollinger==

Bollinger’s fingers close together... but they remain locked.. there is no snap..

\"We were? Well of course we were, and you are late, I am sure, or I am early\" Bollinger’s eyebrows move as if trying to distance themselves from each other... you realize this is confusion...and an attempt to cover it..

\"Well since it would seem that you are in a hurry I must be early, please forgive my intrusion.. and for Gond’s sakes man step inside and close the door.\"

He turns and takes to steps into his dwelling.. then his hand raises by his ear.. and *SNAP*. One could almost imagine there was something magical in this motion of tiny appendages. Iolus's ears ring for a second and he is sure he can hear the sound echo through the chambers.

\"Of Course!\" Bollinger exclaims.. \"The White Cloak, Warburton, General indeed of the Dragon Company!\" 

He wheels around... \"You would be wanting to have an item crafted yes?\" His eyes sparkle...

\"Some form of vision so true I have had... and I expect you wish the like?\"

Dark eyes blinked at the loud and unusual sound emanating from the odd man's fingers and bounced around his chambers. 

==Warburton’s Request==

Warburton did indeed close the door behind him as bid and he waited for Bollinger's chattering to end before he continued.

He pulled out a simple bag and placed it on a nearby flat surface before speaking. 

\"There are five gemstones in there. I am told it is worth roughly four hundred and forty suns. Consider that an initial investment for what I require. I will be forthcoming and state that I am not entirely sure you...or...anyone really can create what I seek but Altair's and Harbaugh's tales of you has led me to your door.\"

He moved from where he was standing by the closed door and took a seat on a crude but sturdy stool.

\"It is a troubling time in the dale. We are against a foe that can easily pass as you or I...or anyone for that matter. I am seeking for a number of devices or constructs that individuals can use to ensure that they are indeed who they say they are. I believe that being secretive about this, and using so manner of device to mark our flesh in a subtle and hidden fashion, we can ensure that a small group of individuals can be trusted without question.\"

He paused a moment to make sure he had Bollinger's attention before continuing.

\"I believe that if you created some small devices that could mark our flesh with a small coloured dot, possibly a sun, or to venerate your own religion, a gear. We would mark our flesh with these devices at the beginning of each day. The devices would need to be...tied to one another in some fashion so that were I to mark my flesh with a blue mark, others using similar devices would also receive a blue mark when they use it. This would have to be done without communication between the users. This would have to be all handled by the device. Over the course of days and weeks, those marks would form a latticework or mosaic of sorts. Those bearing the same marks...those people can be trusted. I apologize for being unclear...I am afraid I do not really know what I am asking for. I am also unclear as to the cost but I commit to you that whatever the cost, I will ensure that you are properly compensated for it.\"

Bollinger blinks (rapidly). \"You... you truly seek to pay me? For shame Warburton, although I do not heed to your authority as a sergeant, and although I do not accompany the Whitcloaks in the field I still consider myself among you.\"

The absence of snapping is staggering... the man speaks from his heart...

\"With that said... I will need these...\" ... he collects the gemstones 

Snap snap

\"I believe I can connect stones of such value to the notion of your goal... in a way... in fact... I have readied an incantation of similar taste\"

He study's the gems in his hand.

\"I can... connect... these gems…\"

His hand closes over the gems and then reopens, whereupon they have become one of larger size.

== Gems of Insight ==

\"Find similar ones and they can be connected as well\"

His hand closes and reopens… and the original five are present once again.

\"It is not exactly what you have requested... but give them only to those you trust.\"

He hands the gems to Iolus. 

\"if one linked to a gem is of Good Alignment, and speaks their true name, any linked gem will match it's colour\"

He murmurs into his hand..

You see all five gems change from red to green before your eyes...

\"Go now... the White Cloaks time is at hand.\"

He opens the door again and offers the five gems...

Once again.. the absence of snapping fingers during this exchange rolls like a thunderhead of silence...

==Warburton Tests the Merchandise==

It wasn't quite what he had asked for but he supposed that he really didn't know what he was actually asking for. He was well aware of the fact that with Bollinger being the creator of these gems, it was possible that Bollinger was manipulating him. It was possible that Bollinger was a Shadovar agent.

Iolus' brown eyes narrowed as he put Bollinger under scrutiny for what seemed like a lengthy period but was only a few heartbeats. In the end, Iolus was sure that he needed to worry more about Bollinger growing a second head and trying to eat out his heart before he needed to worry about Bollinger being Shadovar.

Iolus placed three of the gems in a pouch, held the fourth in his left hand and offered Bollinger the fifth.

\"I trust you Bollinger. Right now, I trust you because I need to trust you. If I find out you have misled me on this, and in time...I find out everything. If you turn out to be tied to the Shadovar, I will find you and I will scour the flesh from your bones with fire and light.\"

Bollinger’s eyes bulge from his head... apparently the threat was unexpected..

\"Ahem, well… um indeed...\" He pauses, swallows twice and you think he may faint...

But then his eyes sparkle and *snap snap* his fingers return him to his usual (if still unsettled) state.

He steps off to the, brushes some papers off a desk and raises a small golden feather in his hand. He then sticks the feather in his cap.

\"Mr. Warburton, I can guarantee I do not mislead. I confound and confuse, but never mislead. That said\" He taps the feather. \"A little fire and light immunity never hurts does it.\" He winks at you. 

*Snap*

\"Also, you only have the five gems. Do not waste one on such as me. I truly have nothing to hide, and rarely am able to find what it was I thought I was hiding.\" 

He thinks over what he said, scratches behind his ear and nods his approval of his own verbal riposte. 

\"Should you find more gems, as I said, they can be connected. The more similar they are the further range they possess. These are linked to about 2 miles, if anyone who is of good alignment speaks their true name to a gem, all gems within range will change colour. I believe that concludes our business.\" He walks to the door and opens it.

*Snap Snap* 

\"Oh, and by the way, If the name spoken is not the persons true name, no matter alignment, all gems in range will go clear. If one of evil alignment speaks their true name, all will go black.\"

*snap*

\"I hope I have been of assistance, but my time is running short. It is lucky I was early in coming to this meeting because brother Carwinkle is expecting me and I would hate to be late, that would force him to be early, and he always forgets things if he shows up before precisely when he aught.\"

Iolus did not seem to be diverted from his original path. He kept his hand out, offering the gem stone to Bollinger and fixed the man with an intractable gaze. 

\"Take the gem, Bollinger. Say your name and then return the gem.\"

Bollinger simply sighs.

\"Fine... but once I do that the gem is connected to me. it won't work for another\"

He looks Iolus square, Bollinger isn't a proud man at all... Iolus's threat seems to only make him weary.

\"Are you sure you want to waste one? I mean really Warburton. I infused them... do you really think that were I one of the manipulative evils you seek I could not manipulate a gem of my own creation to change as I will it?\"

==Warburton Relents==

A muscle at the corner of his eye twitched as he clenched his teeth. He did not believe he was a proud man. A proud man would not have negotiated with a dragon. A proud man would not have given up his ancestral home. A proud man would not apologize to this odd, bothersome, but talented man.

His nostrils flared as he took in a deep breath and let loose the anger that was brewing inside of him. He turned his head just enough to break eye contact with Bollinger and then lowered his head and let loose a tired sounding breath.

\"I do not know, Bollinger. I do not know anything anymore and I do not know who to trust. \"

He let out another breath and looked up to meet Bollinger's eyes once more.

\"I will trust you Bollinger. As I said before, I will trust you because I need to.\"

Clearing his throat awkwardly, he slipped the two remaining stones into his pouch and turned.

\"Be well Bollinger and...well...my thanks. Keep your eyes bright and sharp.\"

Bollinger smiles. Then his face grows sombre, and his eyes grow earnest.

\"Warburton, this I vow. Should you ever find reason to end that trust, I will be here and will freely submit to your Fire and Light, and woe be to him who stands in your path to bring me my ruin.\" 

He nods to you. \"Journey safe young man\"
