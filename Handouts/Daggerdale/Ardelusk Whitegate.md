# Ardelusk Whitegate †
# Source: The Sieges Perilous wiki (backup dated 2010-02-21)

The first Whitegate in the area, Ardelusk was a slave in the Zhent West Hill Mine.  He is thought to have been born around 1340 DR, but the date is uncertain.  He died in the ruins that would be turned into Castle Radiance in 1399.
