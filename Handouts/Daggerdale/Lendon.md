Magistrate Lendon worked with Harkas Kormallis for 2 decades. He was always trying to keep Cormallis in line, and kept him out of prison when he did cross the line. He had some straight dealings with the Warders of Waterdeep. He has returned to the story, after the recent murder of Harkas Cormallis in his own Villa. He has kept Vader and Richter from taking any action against the Heroes, and has kept them out of the affairs at Irlingstar Villa since the battle. 

After the Irlingstar Villa affair, Garth approached Lendon about procuring some prison space in Dunvolt Tower to assist in the round up of the Zhents. He has been quite accommodating of any request made of him since the death of Cormallis, and the toppling of Company Aldebarran.
