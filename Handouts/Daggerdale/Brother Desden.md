Newer brother with the Emissaries.

===Excerpt from Lord's Auction===

Desden is new to the Emissaries, and his specialty is in arcane writings; scrolls, spellbooks and the like. There are dozens of these on the block today, and he will be auctioning them off with his brand of fast wit and slicing humour. At last year’s auction, he was able to drive up prices of some items through clever insults of some of the bidders; a feat that had the rest of the brethren scratching their heads, and earned him a spot on the block today.
