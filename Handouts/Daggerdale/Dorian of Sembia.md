The half-elf known as Dorian of Sembia has been called \"The Greatest Swordsman in the Realms\". This may be true. <ref>Possibly true, that is, up until 1481 DR and the discovery of one Dannilov Ebonhawke - a Cormyrean noble said to be as good with his sword as he is with the ladies...and he's as pretty darn good with the ladies.</ref><ref>The proper way for Dicklove Ebonchicken to claim the title is to seek Dorian out and defeat him in a proper duel.  I hear that rumours of his demise have been greatly exaggerated. Feel free to head north and turn down the road towards Castle Daggerdale.  Your fate awaits you there.</ref>

When Dorian first encountered the Daggerdale Expedition, which was later to become the Company of the Eastern Storm, he purported to be a druid named Tristan. In actuality, Dorian is a Sembian half-elf (though often mistaken for human) of loose character, and is on the run from the authorities in the City of Waterdeep, where he escaped from prison after being charged with the murder of an elf named Gilthanas.

*Paramour of the half-elven wizard, Dragon.
*Count Dorian of Daggerdale!
**Dorian holds the deed to Castle Daggerdale and surrounding lands. His plan was to retire there, rebuild the castle and set up an Explorer's Society, Museum, and Menagerie as his retirement. Basically, he wanted to use his vast fortune to fund adventurers and explorers, charging 10% interest on the investment. His castle was also to serve as a museum, zoo, and as a meeting point for all manner of adventurers. The best laid plans...
