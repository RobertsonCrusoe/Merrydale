Sherriff Grimwald was a former corrupt Sheriff from the Dalelands. He was a skilled swordsman and mage, and was armed with the Repeating Gond Pistola.

* high-level wizard/thief, maybe fighter
* squinty steely eyes
* thick silver moustache, short silver hair, leathery skin
* wears black riding boots, grey breeches and black riding leather chaps
* grey chemise, black jerkin, beige longcoat
* longsword on his left, holstered pistol on his right

Status: Deceased. Brought to Justice.
