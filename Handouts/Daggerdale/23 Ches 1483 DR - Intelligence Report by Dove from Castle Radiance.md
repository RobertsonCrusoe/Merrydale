Intelligence Report — Ches 23, 1483 DR, by Dove from Castle Radiance
For General Thunderstorm, Bishop Whitegate and Mayor Cassimar

Plains — Two nearly-battalion-strength units manoeuvring WNW 30mi and WSW 35mi from the Rockline. Bearing MLGNTs banners. Some low-intensity skirmishes with the 3rd Co suggests these are green battalions.

Greyhame Mtn — Scouts report possible tribe of gargoyles have taken roost here. First known sightings of such creatures in these parts. Very suspicious.

Maw of Gruumish — Low-intensity skirmishes with probing parties of orcs for the first time in two years, which is three years earlier than expected. Not completely uncommon but the timing with other events seems conspicuous.

Theory — As the contest to take back Thistle Ridge continues, pressure on our Western borders mounts, necessitating the deployment of resources there, rather than in the East. MLGNT certainly involved, possibly coordinating all. Suspect LM active in the dale again.

Suggestions — Redouble the search for spies in Town. Redouble (if possible) the efforts of the Rides and Foresters to seek out agents of LM. End the contest and recon Thistle Ridge in force while we still can. Sell the rights to the holdings to the highest bidder afterward.
