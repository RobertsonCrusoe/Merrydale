Mourn Raventree is the eldest living member of House Raventree, and a surviving member of The Three Old Conspirators. He has ceded the patriarchy to his son Nandos, and rarely leaves the precincts of the Raventree Villa. He was greatly saddened by the death of his namesake, his great-grandson Raven in 1372.
