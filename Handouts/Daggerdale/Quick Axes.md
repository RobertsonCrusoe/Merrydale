Known as the Red Rock Tavern for most of the 14th Century, this establishment was taken on by the brothers Balak and Dalak Quickaxe after the Harvestide Liberation in 1372 DR. Since then, Master Balak has succeeded in the way that only a dwarf can in making Quick Axes a bulwark of stability and comfort against the rapid changes that have swept through the rest of Dagger Falls.
