27 Flamerule, 1479 DR 
GFD Notes: 
This is an extraordinary letter, as it was written more than a year before the trouble of the Sons of Bel appeared in the Dale. This letter was sent to Dunland (son of the mayor of Dwimmerholme), accompanied by the book "Belial Prayerbook". When the White Cloaks came to the settlement, they checked in on Dunland, whom Mr. Dretch had some dealings with in the past, and remained suspicious of. 

Dunland said he felt compelled to give the book to Gale, who was the only White Cloak that was able to read it; it is written in Modern Netherese and interspersed with Infernal. Of course, this book would prove to hold the key (quite literally in the terrible closing act of this chapter) to solving many problems started by the Sons of Bel, but I'm not sure Dunland did Gale good service in the end 
-GFD 

Master Dunland; 
I am not sure why I am sending you this, but I feel you will need it more than I. Maybe not right away, but you will have cause to use it, or to put it to use sometime in the future. It is a diabolical looking thing, I know, and the title is a little frightening. But, rest assured this is a work of disciplined academia. This is not a magical book in any way, but one that explains a terrible magic, and some other foul things besides. 

Put it aside, and let none know that you have it. You will know when it is time to put it to use. Until we meet again, 
Phin Hoetmer. 
27 Flamerule, 1479 DR.
