The plan for Waterdeep

Run an enchanter/diviner that works for the Rooken – a Waterdeep noble named Aramis Fell – handsome but unassuming, a cat’s paw in the background. Maybe 10^(th) level wizard?

Need to make Waterdeep uncomfortable for the drow

1)  Create local demagogues that turn the public against the drow infiltrators

2)  Turn the nobles:

    a.  Wavesilver, Phaulkon, Dulbrawan Anteos

        i.  Vengeance, racism, natural justice

    b.  Anteos, Dezlentyr, Eltorchul

        i.  Jealousy, competition, ostracism

    c.  Umbrusk, Hunabar

        i.  Natural antipathy

3)  Turn the public via a publishing endeavour

    a.  Pamphlets

    b.  Waterdeep Trumpet

Target: ambitious young nobles, houses looking to make a mark, perhaps younger sons looking to prove themselves

Figure out why the vampires were after Garth – were they sent?
