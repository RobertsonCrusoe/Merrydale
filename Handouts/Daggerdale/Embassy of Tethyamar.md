Located in Castle Radiance in Tethyamar Tower, this organization represents the dwarves from 13 clans that have come to Daggerdale to recover the Kingdom of Tethyamar.  

There are a couple dozen dwarves that stay at the Embassy to carry out official business with the dalesmen.  They are headed by Ambassador Gambledin, who has been at the Castle for nearly 7 years, first as the 'Curator' of the Tethyamar League, and now as the Ambassador to the Dalesmen.
 
==Before Rediscovery - The Tethyamar League==
Once one of the great dwarven empires centuries ago, Tethyamar is believed to have been abandoned by it's people when it became uninhabitable due to a number of threats from the Underdark. 

After the Spellplague, ruins believed to be Tethyamar in origin were discovered at a couple of sites in the Desertsmouth Mountains, after various earthquakes and rock slides revealed them. As well, just as the receding sands of Anauroch released their grip on many Netherese ruins, so too did they reveal passages into the mountains that had been buried for centuries. 

All of this has drawn a group of dwarves to the area who call themselves the Tethyamar League. These dwarves have been very cooperative with Castle Radiance, and even lease a tower on a long-term basis there now. The League is actively pursuing leads and rumours about any possible ruins, and pays well for information. They have also been known to offer bounties when a lead is discovered. 

The site of Castle Radiance was thought to be a Tethyamar ruin, but turned out otherwise. These dwarves pay well for information, and often have jobs on the Sun Wheel.

==Rediscovery - The Embassy of Tethyamar==
The East Gate of Tethyamar was located by the White Cloaks in the summer of 1480. This revelation was delivered by the White Cloaks to the Situation Report in Loedegan's Hall a week later. By the close of the summer that year, dwarves from all over Faerun have been migrating to the region.

The Tethyamar League was officially renamed and invested as the Embassy of Tethyamar on the 1st of Marpenoth, 180 DR.
