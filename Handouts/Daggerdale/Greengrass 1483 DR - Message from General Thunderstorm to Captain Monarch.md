Greengrass, 1483 DR — Message from General Dauntless Thunderstorm to Captain Jeska Monarch

[Source: Daggerdale - All Chapter 7 Content - june 11 revised.docx, extracted 2026-04-09. By Carrier Dove. Reply to Jeska's 29 Tarsakh request for aid.]

By Carrier Dove.

Captain Monarch;

2 platoons of the 18th Company are currently making for Greenwood Farm and should arrive well before the sun goes down. They are being led by Captain Renley Rawthorn himself. 1st platoon will stay a tenday, at our cost.

2nd platoon is lead by Lt. Williams Waterborne and will stay with you three tenday at your cost, per the Provisions of the Common Defense. I am authorizing 2nd platoon to stay as long as a year, if you desire, and will consider re-commissioning this platoon as the 7th Company 5th Platoon Bright Spears, if both you and the 7th Company Captain desire it, during Muster next year.

Captain Garrick Greenfields of the 10th Company will reinforce the southern flank of the Tower with a full platoon, and has been given tactical command to decide how to deploy his men in your battle against the Torn Cheek. This Ride will be supplied at our cost, per the Provisions.

Cpt. Greenfields also has a train with the requested alchemical supplies, supplied by our armoury in Towne, which we donate to the cause.

May the sun find your blades held high on the morrow,

Gen. D. Thunderstorm.
