DDC – 1481 – 10: Letter from Administrator Reks to White Cloak Sgt. Hennigan Paros 
Marpenoth 14 
GFD Notes: 
This is a direct copy of the letter, which was provided to my by Reks. 
-GFD 

To the esteemed Hennigan Paros, 
Congratulations once again on being elected acting Sergeant for this mission! You're a man of action, and you're willing to make the hard decisions that no one else seems to be capable of - worry not over broken eggs and all that noise. In a conflict as muddied and twisted as this, lines will, regrettably, be crossed time and time again by those of us who stand firm against the machinations of the unjust. Just be sure to remember what they say about he who fights monsters, Mr. Paros. 

But on to more mundane matters! Denelius Ormanth's caravan will be leaving Dagger Falls at first light on the 16th, less than 2 days from now. Guess where he's heading? Charity Hills, of course! I'd like you to head out with Mr. Ormanth. He'll be stopping in Liberty Hills, and arrive in Charity by the 17th, or so I've heard. 

Of course, that would be the quiet way. Feel free to ride out for Charity Hills at first morning's light. I honestly don't care how you get there, as long as you get there without informing anyone untoward about it, understand? 

When you arrive at your destination, find Medea Rowan. She's a Priestess of Sharess (oh la la), and loves learning new things, so get in there and try to squeeze some information out of her. As politely as possible, of course, she is a lady after all. She tends to favour information over coin, so make sure you don't tell her anything that may compromise your presence in Charity Hills... we can't trust anyone these days, it would seem. 

Lastly, keep an eye on Gale. He'll no doubt want to run off and attend to his own business. I say let him. He knows more than he's leading on about all this nonsense, but a lead is a lead, so humour him a bit. Try not to butt heads, though. You are his immediate superior, after all. Just keep a sharp eye on his sneaking. 
I expect you to hold them in line. Don't disappoint me now! 

Forever Yours, Administrator Reks
