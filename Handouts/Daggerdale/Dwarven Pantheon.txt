In post Spell-Plague Faerun, Moradin is the 'greater god' of the Dwarves, or \"The All-Father\".

His wife, Berronar Truesilver would be considered a 'god'.

All good dwarves worship The All-Father, and all good female dwarves worship Berronar as well.

However, dwarven worship is further divided into four other denominations, all tied to one of Moradin's exarchs;
