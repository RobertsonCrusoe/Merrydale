22 Ches -- Tournament Round 2 - The River -- Chrysalis v. Redjacks

1. River Plunder
    - Objective: Be the first to get all of your team to the top of your 15' platform
    - Secondary Objective: Get all the treasure to the top of the platform with your team
    - Rules:
        - Must get to the top by creating a stair case out of 6 boxes, with your team's colour on top of the box
        - All players start in your starting zone
        - 2 players may cross the river
        - Enemy boxes may be moved/interacted with
        - Boxes must be moved and oriented correctly;
            - Boxes are Bulk 15
                - You can carry Str + 5 bulk w/o penalty
                - You can carry Str +10 bulk as Encumbered (Clumsy 1, -10 speed)
                - You (and others) can drag or push a box on the ground at the following speeds
        - In order to rotate the boxes to the proper side, a player must take a 2-action manoeuvre, and pass the following checks;
            - Int Check DC 12 (or suitable Lore)
            - Str Check DC 15

2. Law of the River
    - Objective: Be the first to get all your prisoners to the top of your 15' platform
    - Secondary Objective: Get the chest of gold to your 15' platform
    - Rules;
        - 3 of your players start in cages
        - 2 of your players start in your starting zone
        - 1 of your players only may cross to the enemy side
        - There are 4 keys hidden under 6 boxes
        - Each key can open a single lock on a cage
            - There is one key that is a 'master' key, as Lucan has learned.

3. Longshoreman's Travail
    - Objective: Be the first to get all three of your chests across the river by any means
    - Loot!  You will get all the gold inside each chest you got across the river by the end of the match
    - 1 of your players must start on the enemy side on the platform opposite their starting position
    - Enemy chests can be moved and interacted with
