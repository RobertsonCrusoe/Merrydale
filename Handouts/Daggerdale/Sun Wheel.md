This is a large stone wheel, something akin to a crown-and-anchor wheel.  It is 12' tall, and has 12 pegs around it's face like a clock.  In the middle of the face are 6 more pegs which form a second ring.

The wheel is carved like a blazing sun, and the stone stand in which it is positioned has been ornately carved to resemble a pair of paladins holding the same lance at oposite ends; the wheel itself rotates around the thick lance, and each paladin raises a sword in his other hand towards the peak of wheel.  Despite the obvious weight of the stone wheel, it spins with little effort.  This is truly a marvel of the masonry skills of the dwarves that built it.  

The outer ring usually has 6 or more plaques with bounty details on them.  The wheel is easily rotated by hand so that all the plaques can be easily read, and they are all easy to remove.
