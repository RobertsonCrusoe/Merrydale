Magistrate Thuman was Yarkul’s principle ally in the ranks of the Magistry. He came to a bad end in 1372, after the raid on Wavesilver Villa.  After the Company of the Red Herring cleaned out this Villa of Zhents and other monsters and villains, Thuman stepped in to oversee the ‘stabilization and security’ of the property and goods of the Villa (there was a huge amount of stolen loot in this Villa). Jafar returned to the Villa later, and incinerated Thuman and over 20 members of the Watch with a fireball. 

There is an ongoing investigation into the events at the Wavesilver Family Villa, being conducted by Magistrate Richter.

*Status: Burned to a crisp
