[PC Attribution: Dalak Quickaxe (Todd Burry)]
A dwarf with an axe and a bad temper, Dalak is a dwarven battle-rager, and brother to Balak. Dalak had once been a cobbler of note among his people, and none could mend a boot more quickly or sturdily than he. He was grievously injured at the Battle of Freedom Hill, and has retired from adventuring.
