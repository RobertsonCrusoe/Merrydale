Jasper's ChatGPT Adventures - AI as Dungeon Master
by Gus Miranda, Stephen Robertson, Les Blackwell
Email thread: "Jasper's ChatGPT adventures", 25-29 May 2025

Gus describes running solo Level 0 prequel adventures for teenage Jasper in Shadowdale, with ChatGPT as DM. The thread evolves into a broader discussion about AI limitations — context windows, hallucinations, and reliability — with contributions from Stephen (technical explanation) and Les (workplace hallucination example). Stephen also shares an entertaining ChatGPT hallucination about an artifact called the "Imperiosus Orbis" when it was asked to mine Les's campaign notes.

---

GUS (25 May 2025, 11:07 PM):

I know this is probably one of the worst uses of AI, but I am having a tremendous amount of fun with a Level 0 Jasper's teenage choose-your-own-adventures in Shadowdale, DM'ed for me by ChatGPT:

[Shares opening scene of a ChatGPT-generated adventure: Jasper wakes in his loft above the Hawkford farmhouse, spell parchment and ink-stained fingers, to the shrill voice of neighbour Maerla Tanglebrook complaining about a scarecrow that has wandered into the Hawkford turnip patch wearing Uncle Vernon's hat.]

GUS (26 May 2025, 10:02 AM):

Well, so far Jasper's prequel adventures have seen him tame a mischievous animated scarecrow, rescue a child from a rampaging earth elemental, find a missing goose for a cranky old witch, evade a murderous bandit in some ruins and (after I asked the robot to raise the stakes slightly) get sent by Elminster to the Feywild in order to prevent some ancient horror from breaching realities and destroying all of Faerun.

All in a day's work for a fledgling hedge wizard.

---

GUS (29 May 2025, 2:55 PM):

A question for those of you that are smarter than me and use AI regularly in your professional capacities:

[Describes the core problem: as his ChatGPT adventure progresses, the quality degrades. ChatGPT makes critical continuity errors — locations change, quest objectives shift, rules are misapplied. When confronted, it claims full access to Pathfinder 2E and Forgotten Realms materials but still makes errors. Gus notes this should concern people relying on AI for serious professional work.]

---

STEPHEN (29 May 2025, 5:29 PM):

[Provides technical explanation of context windows: ChatGPT ~128k tokens, Claude ~200k, Gemini 2.5 ~1 million. Explains that even within those limits, attention to earlier content weakens. Recommends: keep individual conversations short, work in small chunks, use executive summaries to prime new conversations. Notes: "I'm the memory, and the AI is just a tool (an amazing tool)."]

[Had Gemini compare responses from different models on Gus's question — all noted the D&D scenario as a great stress test for LLMs.]

Key lore points:
- Jasper grew up on the Hawkford farmhouse in Shadowdale
- Maerla Tanglebrook is a neighbour (bread-baker, gossipmonger)
- Uncle Vernon is a family member
- Teenage Jasper was a "fledgling hedge wizard"
- Elminster exists in this setting (Gus's ChatGPT adventure sent Jasper to the Feywild on Elminster's orders)
- Stephen coined "Blacky's Big Book of Beholders, Boregarisms, and Bewilderment" as a nickname for the campaign notes
