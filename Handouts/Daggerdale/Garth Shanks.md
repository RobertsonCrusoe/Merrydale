:-Garth Shanks

A young man from the streets of Dock Ward in Waterdeep, this rogue's nondescript features belie an uncanny aptitude for disguise, and a burning determination that has brought him from the alleyways of his youth to a position of power in the shadowy corridors of the City's criminal underworld.
