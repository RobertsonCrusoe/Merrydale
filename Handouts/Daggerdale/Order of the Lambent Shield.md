# Order of the Lambent Shield
# Source: The Sieges Perilous wiki (backup dated 2010-02-21)

An extremely fanatical brotherhood of Knights of Amaunator, founded in 1419 DR by Luminous Whitegate.  The Order draws most of it's soldiers from the ranks of the Sun Blades, but some other men from places beyond Daggerdale have bolstered it's ranks in the last 20 years, as it has gained tremendous notoriety among worshipers of Amaunator far and wide.

Ranks and Pay

• Supplicant - no pay

• Knight - 1 share of war prizes

• Knight Commander - 2 shares of war prizes

• Master Knight - 4 shares of war prizes

• Grand Master - 8 shares of war prizes

Structure

• Unit - Any number of Supplicants, 4 Knights

• Ride - 3 Units, 1 Knight Commander (13+)

• Brotherhood - 3 Rides, 1 Master Knight (40+)

• Chapter - 3 Brotherhoods, Grand Master (121+)

There are currently over 160 members in this Order, with a full complement of 121 plus supplicants.  The usual deployment is 1 unit, often accompanying a Company of Sun Blades, though not always.

The three Brotherhoods are always named after the Master Knight that founded them.  They include the following;

• Master Knight Grendel's Brotherhood

• Master Knight Sansum's Brotherhood

• Master Knight Derfel's Brotherhood

All Brotherhoods are based out of the Fortress Cathedral, but sometimes entire Brotherhoods are deployed at the same time, as is presently the case with Sansum's Brotherhood, which is garrisoned at Fort Sunset indeterminately.

Religious Structure

The head of the Order is the Zenith Lord of Daggerdale, Zenith Lord Whitegate.  Ostensibly, he gives orders to the Grand Master, Luminous Whitegate, but it is well known that Luminous is the more experienced warrior of the two brothers.  Luminous is devoted to protocol however, and undertakes no actions without the consent of his younger brother.

Like the Sun Blades, there are chaplains assigned to the Order; usually one chaplain per Brotherhood.  These chaplains are experienced and battle hardened.  They hold no authority over the knights they are attached to, however, and answer to the Master Knight of the Brotherhood or the Grand master.

Weapons and Tactics

The weapons that the Order makes use of are as individual as each knight.  Most of these men come to the Order with a long and storied military career, and rather than teaching a strict dogma of warfare to the Knights, they are free to use whatever weapons and tactics they already know.  This is the major departure from the strict rules and tactics of the Sun Blades, but it serves this elite force well.

All Knights are required to be proficient fighting from horseback, but aside from that requirement, all other tactics are based on the unit and the men (and some women) who compose it.  Some are full of Knights in plate armour, shields and longswords, as one might expect.  Others are full of axemen or spearmen, and yet others mix their weapons completely, and look more like small adventuring parties.
