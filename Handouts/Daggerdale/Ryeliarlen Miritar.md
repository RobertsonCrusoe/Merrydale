This noble eladrin from the House Miritar is the ambassador from Myth Drannor.  He is an experience war veteran, and skilled diplomat.  He is at the Castle to keep track of the doings of the Empire of Netheril.

He is young for a diplomat, but he has seen a great deal of combat over the last 100 years, and he has all the polished training and education that his powerful family could give him.  It is no secret that this posting at Castle Radiance was not his first choice; he longed for a posting at another elven embassy, or perhaps Waterdeep or Westgate where there are decent libraries and contact with the rest of the world.  It is rumoured that he feels isolated up in the mountains, and is looking for a way out.
