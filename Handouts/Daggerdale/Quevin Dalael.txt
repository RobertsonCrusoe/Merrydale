Firstboy Dalael has supplanted himself in the Anteos Family, through no small treachery. He has sired a child with Ranaya, the wife of the Family’s Patriarch, Dulbrawan. The child is now 5 years old, and is named Millathi. With the death of the Anteos Patriarch, Dulbrawan, the Anteos estate falls to the boy, whom no one in the noble community will challenge as rightful heir. Though the family is managed by Ranaya and two of Dulbrawan’s brothers, Quevin has some degree of control within the family.

He masquerades as a normal elf, a close friend and business partner of the family, and is known to live at the villa. He and his brothers all go by their given names at Noble functions, masquerading as elves of surface-origin. They are quite well known among many families, and accepted as essential parts of the Family Retinues, or more.

Quevin is a servant of the obscure drow god Vhaeraun. He bears no arms, but rarely travels outside the Villa these days, and then only with large escort.
