PART 1: SCOTT MCLAREN (12 May 2025)
-----------------------------------
Source: Gmail thread 196c6e126be96065
From: scunny1973@gmail.com

(Tabletalk: Getting ahead of ourselves... but if Blacky concededs his last
Goblin - almost dead with Jeska and Sir Rowan about to act... we'll go with
this..)

Jeska looks up to see a grim, yet bleary eyed Jasper approach with Sharra's
head... she meets his gaze with a look of.. pride? shame? regret?
"Do with this as you must.. I want her eye" Jasper says, handing her the
head.
Jasper hands her the head and moves to sit on the edge of the pit.
Jeska see's Lucan move toward his friend and lay his hand on his shoulder.

She ignores them and moves to Mari and Sir Rowan.
Jeska Smiles
"Well fuck me, a Dragon.. for real.. Loki?"
She sees their eyes.. "Yes I figured.. and she took the Orb.. I do not
believe Coventina powered it, I saw she was already dead hanging from the
chain.. but she took another slave.. possibly one with Magic that can power
the Orb.. but only Lucan saw anything real..be cautious..she may still be
here." she pauses, then her eyes alight.

"Nonetheless, we have work to do... the two of you must get to our
rear-guard in the basement, heal as you can, we will deal with the cages
later.. get tapestries to the 4 second level balconies and unfurl on my
signal".. she hands them her last healing potion (lesser).
 "As for me, It appears I have yet another speech to make." She holds up
the head of Charra.

Turning away she looks again to the boys.
"Lads, time to focus! Grief is a game that waits forever! While your vision
is still strong (True seeing & Detect Magic).. search these rooms and find
what secrets lay hidden yet! Jasper, you will find your dagger can work
equally well on the living.. Lucan Draw your sword!" her voice softens
"Stay by his side".

With that Jeska makes for the third floor balcony overlooking the building
front.

(More to come.. I need another speech, and have some ideas of my own).
