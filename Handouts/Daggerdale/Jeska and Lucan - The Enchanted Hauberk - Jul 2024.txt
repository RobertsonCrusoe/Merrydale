PART 1: STEPHEN ROBERTSON (24 Jul 2024)
-----------------------------------
Source: Gmail thread 190e6b9da834ff77
From: robertsoncrusoe@gmail.com

The Governess "held up a small shirt of mail. It was close-woven of many
rings, as supple almost as linen, cold as ice, and harder than steel. It
shone like moonlit silver, and was studded with white gems. With it was a
belt of pearl and crystal."

With a frosty gaze fixed upon Lucan, Jeska says, "This hauberk, entrusted
to my care by those to whom I must one day render account, possesses a
value that transcends mere coin. To me, it has become a burdensome shackle,
ill-suited for the journey upon which I now embark. Yet, for one such as
you, it may yet fulfill a nobler destiny, perchance safeguarding your life
many times over. It would be an honour of great measure if you would don it
as a champion of Chrysalis. In exchange, I ask only that you relinquish the
accursed poison-steeped bladet that we wrested from the vile clutches of
the shadow-goblin Mek. Its very existence is a blight upon the virtuous, a
mockery of all that is just. I cannot abide its presence amongst us. What
say you, Lucan? Will you accept this mantle and forsake the dark artifact?"
His eyes goggling, Lucan blurts out, "Tyr's tits, you bet!" He placed the
serpent dagger on the table and reached for the mail shirt, then paused.
Clearing his throat, he looks Jeska squarely in the eyes, his usual jocular
tone replaced by an uncharacteristic gravity.

"Milady Governess, I am deeply honoured by your trust. I will don this
hauberk as a proud member of Company Chrysalis and the Embassy, fully aware
that I am merely its steward for a time, and that it hails from elven hands
to which it must eventually return. I vow to wield it solely in our
company's cause, and should the day come that I depart, I will relinquish
it in as pristine a condition as it was bestowed upon me."

Jeska smiles.

"Dear Lucan, sweet lad. I have worn this vestige for more than 5 of your
current life spans.
It has served me well and I truly hope it serves you.
It is yours, a simple tool to aid where best it fits.
For me, that time is done.
If one day a hundred years from now, I choose to collect it, it will be for
the memory of your deeds wearing it.. nostalgia and all"

Jeska winks at Lucan

"My sword, Eclose, to me is precious, a true family heirloom.. and if you
remember... I once offered it to you. True Alavandor blood... there is
greatness in you."

She turns and exits the room.
