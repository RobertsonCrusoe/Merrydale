"So, as Jasper is an expert craftsman and has Magical Crafting, he can make
whatever items we already have.

For example, since I already have a Minor Healing Potion, which is a Level
1 Item, all I have to do is spend 1 hour and 1gp worth of ingredients to
learn the formula for this item, and then I can start making them for us.
We don't lose the original potion in the process.

But this includes other items we already have as well.

So any items, potions, etc that we have that are Level 3 and below, if you
lend them to me one at a time for an hour at the end of each day, I can
extract the formula to how to reproduce it, so we can make additional ones
in the future.

Going back to the Healing Potion, once I have the Level 1 (Minor) formula
in my book, I can also start making the Level 3 (Lesser) version as well.

Having formulas is not required, but it does make it faster and cheaper for
me to make these things (hours and days, rather than days and weeks) during
our downtime.

So the next time we camp, anything you have that is Level 3 or under, pass
it over please."
