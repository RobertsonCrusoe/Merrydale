**The White Cloaks**

**HQ:** Loedegan Close (\#28 on the DF Map), other properties

**Leadership**: Captain Iolus Warburton (Paladin of Amaunator, usually at the Castle), Administrator Alen Reks (Usually at the Close), Lady Blackmane (Steward of the Close)

**Team Composition:**

1. Tarra Hilltop \- Team Leader \- Priestess of Amaunator, Retired Sunblade  
2. Alexandus Ward \- Cormyrean War Wizard  
3. Zelia Oakbough \- Ranger, good with a bow, from Lostead  
4. Tyrell Dale \- Retired Sunblade, sword, board and spear  
5. Jannus Dale \- Retired Sunblade, sword, board and spear

**Notes:**

Dutton Delimbry (of Bolt & Button & Dutton in the Old Market) crafted 5 magic cloaks for the new members, and was not happy about the deal in the end.

**The Redjacks**

**HQ:**  Redjack Lodge (\#31 on the DF Map), other properties

**Leadership:** Hassle Redwater (Usually at the Castle), Crasster Redwater (son of Hassle, runs the Lodge)

**Team Composition:**

1. Crasster Redwater \- Accomplished 'soldier' (rumoured to be a brutal assassin)  

2. Garradin "Buddy" Gorehammer \- Dwarven Battlerager, recently kicked out of Tethyamar  
3. Livinia Leng \- Monk from some far off land, very mysterious  
4. Zallia Redwater \- Sister of Crasster, returned from 10 years of wandering, brought Livinia & Grimaldus with her \- assumed to be some sort of witch  

5. Grimaldus \- A red-scaled dragonborn that arrived with Zallia.  Wears a chain shirt and carries a huge shield.  There are usually motes of fire floating around him, and he's quite scary.

**Notes:** 

The Redjacks bought a crate of potions and alchemical mixtures from The Phylactery, as well as a mountain of supplies to make some of their own.

**The Free Defense League**

**HQ:** FDL Tower in Castle Radiance, *Working out of a rented warehouse north of the Eastgate Customs Court (near The Arena)*

**Leadership:** Bargle Battlemaster (usually at the Castle), Zultim (runs the Competition Party)

**Team Composition:**

1. Glozbe al Archaemenid Druzir al Calim Zultim yn Zultima ibn Calim ("Zultim") \- team leader \- an accomplished archaeologist from Calimshan and years of experience in eastern Netheril  
2. Swelter \- Dwarven inventor from Tethyamar  
3. Faravyr Narn \- Half Elven Alchemist  
4. Nemia Ul-Nishaadrhi, Ghost Hunter \- Thaumaturge from Vaasa  
5. Ser Reginald Boxfray \- Paladin of Tempus from Battledale

**Notes:** The team is training very hard inside the warehouse every day, with Ser Reginald leading the drills.  They have not been out and about at all.

**The Standing Stone League**

**HQ:** Standing Stone Factory in the Eastern Bailey

**Leadership:** Beliarge Belwintle, Factor of the Standing Stone League, Velemore Emphar \- Team leader

**Team Composition:**

1. Velemore Emphar \- Wizard from the Moonsea  
2. Horus Hardradda \- Caravan Master from Hillsfar (good with a blade)  
3. Bree Bridlestone \- Dwarven priestess of Barronar Truesilver  
4. Entwin Olavander \- Half Elven, wears leather armour and 4 daggers  
5. Madelaine Meroviskyr \- Human female ranger from Harrowdale

**Notes:** Haven't purchased anything that anyone knows of.  Horus is out and about quite a bit, and is easy to find.  He's been enjoying the festivities with vim.
