This is a note intercepted by Mule Company in the early summer of 1379 DR.  It is written in old Netherese, and was deciphered by Ambassador Miritar much later.

Prince Melegaunt;

Our work at Vaer’Lith Katahr is approaching its goal.  

We received your reinforcements yesterday, as well as the much needed supplies and research materials.   Your version of the Ritual of Command did indeed work on the orbs, and I report that I am presently able to command the constructs in the dungeon level of the citadel, though I have not been able to unlock the other properties of the orbs which you suggested may lay dormant.

Yesterday the first train of goods was sent back to the Sharspire, and it should arrive about six days after you read this.  It contains the relics we suspected would be found in the basement.  I have retained none of the choicest bits, sending off everything for proper cataloguing and study by minds more learned in this meticulous aspect of our business.

Curator Loki Mouvranesh
