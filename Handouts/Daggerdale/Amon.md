Amon of Chessenta is a follower of Entropy.

Amon is tall, at 6’1”, and more talkative than his constant companion, Szuth. He wears black starmetal bracers, carries 2 scimitars and wears leather armour.

Status: Active in the Waterdeep neighbourhood of Little Chessenta
