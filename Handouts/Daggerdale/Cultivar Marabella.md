Marabella is the highest ranking cleric of Chauntea at the farm. She is a handsome woman of about 40 years of age, though she looks much younger, whose family has always lived in the area for as many generations as she can name. She is a busy woman, with a large base of parishioners who remain devoted to the Goddess of the Harvest. She has a good relationship with the Dawnlord, but she has many reservations about the growing power of the Church of Amaunator in the dale, and in her community in particular. She has 6 young women in her clergy who are training as clerics.
