The Spire Forge is a very busy smithy in which just about any type of weapon, and many types of armour and tools can be made and purchased.  The proprietor of the place is a man named Grenville, who is a Cormyrean by birth, and who worked as an armourer for the Purple Dragons for 20 years before uprooting and coming to Castle Radiance.  Lord Whitegate is known to credit much of the survival and heroic deeds of the men of the Castle to the fine weapons that Grenville and his apprentices have produced over the last 10 years.

Any simple and military melee weapon can be purchased here for the same price as the PHB.  However, Grenville has a few specialties; the following items are usually available at the Spire Forge as well;

*Longswords, Great Swords, Bastard Swords +1 are all available for 400-450 gp.
*Scale and Plate mail armour +1 are available for 300-400 gp.
