ANALYSIS AND SPECULATION: The primary obstacle to this theory was the matter of Charrah's parentage. How could Lobelia, who carried the blue-eye heritage of the usurper Andarl, mother a child whose second eye is black from a Shadovar? The provided texts on hagspawn lore do not describe second-generation breeding. However, given the profane and magical nature of their bloodline, I propose the following principle: the hag's eye-trait is passed down, unblemished, through the maternal line.

This principle resolves the contradiction perfectly:

1. Palus, the full-blooded Swamp Hag, passes her signature grey eye trait to her daughter, Lobelia.

2. Lobelia, in turn, passes on her own maternal inheritance - that same grey eye - to her own daughter, Charrah.

3. Charrah's second eye is inherited from her father, the Shadovar, which accounts for her black eye.

The lineage, as told by the eyes, is a perfect match. This theory provides the most logical explanation for the observed facts and reveals the full scope of the conspiracy. The command structure we now face is a monstrous family dynasty: Palus the matriarch, her daughter Loki/Lobelia the Shadovar commander, and her granddaughter Charrah the goblin general.

CONCLUSION: The evidence strongly suggests that Lobelia Alavandor was Palus's long-term asset, a sleeper agent who has now matured into a position of significant power as Loki Mouvranesh. This was the true, monstrous outcome of the "Consummation" rituals practiced by the usurpers of my family's name.
