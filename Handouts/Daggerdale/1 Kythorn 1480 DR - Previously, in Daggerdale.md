==14th Century Adventures==
===1372 DR, The Year of the Wild Magic ===
The Daggerdale of 1479 was shaped largely by the events of 1372, when the Company of the Eastern Storm and an assortment of other heroes did a thorough job of scouring the Dale of the evil cults of Cyric, Bane and Iyachtu Xvim. These events have been well documented and can be found here.

*The Road to Dekanter - Greengrass to Early Kythorn
*Across the Sand Sea - Kythorn to Eleint
*Into the Dales - Eleint
*The Liberation of Dagger Falls - Eleasias to Harvestide
*Return to Anauroch - Marpenoth to Year's End
*A Winter's Tale - Early 1373

A number of speculative conclusions as to the underlying politics of this series of events are contained in a Harper report compiled in 1373.
