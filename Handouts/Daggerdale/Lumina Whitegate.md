Lumina is the grand daughter of Zenith Sunlord Argent Whitegate.  She is a low-ranking member of the clergy of Amaunator, and a founding member of the White Cloaks.  Lumina is a very tough, fit young woman who spent many years working in her family's mine.  She only recently joined the clergy, and though she is a devout worshiper of Amaunator, she quietly rejects some of the strict dogma and bureaucratic nuances of the church in Daggerdale.  As such, she has formed an adventuring company, the White Cloaks, to do her god's work in her own way.
