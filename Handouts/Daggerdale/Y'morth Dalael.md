Y'morth Dalael, also known by his surface moniker of Dally, is a cousin to Quevin Dalael. He is a notable mage and, in his surface guise, a known member of the Watchful Order of Magists and Protectors. Dally is known to carry a staff capped with a golden sphinx.

He is a very good friend of Thesp Eltorchul and also an instructor at the Eltorchul Academy.

He has maintained a neutral position in all the conflicts between Company Dalael and its enemies.
