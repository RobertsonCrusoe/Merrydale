''A letter sent from the [[Zhentarim]] wizard [[Malkor]] to his lackey, [[Gilth'zan]]''
----



{{script}}
Gilth'zan:

The last of the [[Malthoon's High Road Shippers|Malthoon Company]] will be heading south in the next tenday. They will number more than twenty, so have the appropriate forces available.

Please send a report about the process of our little pets in [[Thurl’Dan]]. I would like to see them causing more havoc for us, to take some attention away from our orc “allies”.

If our pets in Thurl’Dan require more coaxing, please send word to me. I have fashioned the appropriate scrolls.

Re: the [[Harper]]:

[[Atemnon]] tells me that the Harper [[Road Mage]] is on his way to your encampment with a handful of thugs. I don’t know what he has planned, but I will find out. Please take him alive. The others are hired muscle, and can be exterminated.

Be warned that amongst them is a [[Rufus|lycanthrope of an exotic breed]]. This one should be slain immediately!
