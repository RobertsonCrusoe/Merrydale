A drinking song composed by Maturin Horgath during the long night of his fall from grace.
----

==First Verse==
:O, the year was 1372 (I wish I'd never tried it now)
:And Anauroch's sun might've been the shade
:To the scummiest brew that's ever been made...

==Chorus==
:Tyr damn us all! I was told
:This ale was worth its weight in gold.
:We'd feel no pain, shed no tears
:But it's a foolish man who shows no fear
:At a glass of Dalak's homemade beer.

==Verses==
:Dalak Quickaxe brought forth the jug (I wish I'd never tried it now)
:For three brave men, all warriors who
:Would taste for him the gut shakin' brew.

[chorus]

:Dorian was the first to drink (I wish I’d never tried it now]
:The brew brought forth such chokes and gags,
:Like a lion had ripped his throat to rags.

[chorus]

:Boregar next, belly hard and vast (I wish I’d never tried it now)
:He was seven foot tall, and eight foot wide
:He growled “Pass that jug or I'1l tan your hide!”

[chorus]

:He swallowed down then spewed it up (I wish I'd never tried it now)
:Dalak was smashed with a gutful of dregs
:And his breath set fire to both me legs.

[chorus]

:A Paladin drunk is a Paladin not (I wish I'd never tried it now)
:But Ilmater's away, and I’m pretty sure
:There ain't a hangover I can't endure.

[chorus]

:So here I lie with me twenty-third beer (I wish I'd never tried it now)
:The Gutshaker's great, but soon it'll be
:Grandfather, Ilmater, the desert and me.
