Just a Quick One Post Session from Jasper - Coventina's Death and Aftermath
by Gus Miranda (as Jasper) and Todd Burry (as Mari)
Email thread: "Just a quick one, post session from Jasper", 11-12 May 2025

Extended post-session discussion between Gus and Todd about the devastating session where Coventina Cassimar was killed by Loki Mourvanesh's trap. Includes Jasper's post-battle actions, his analysis of Loki's trap, his failed rescue attempt, and a rich character debate between Jasper's pacifist/sympathetic worldview and Mari's hardline pragmatism. Todd reveals Mari's Golda Meir inspiration.

---

GUS (11 May 2025, 2:38 AM):

If you're writing a narrative, after Jasper cut off Charrah's head he tossed it to Jeska and told her to nail it to the parapet. I think you missed that when you got disconnected.

Then he will grab an edge to drop down into the pit, and collect the remains of Coventina in his cloak with Masra's and his Unseen Servant's help.

If needed, I'll cast jump to leap back out of the cell with the remains, or pick the lock and get out with Rum Tum, or wait for Ser Rowan to bust us out.

I'll then look through that 2nd storey room for blankets etc to wrap her up as best as I can, using my Medicine (and Necromancy) skills to try to preserve the body as much as possible for transport back to Dagger Falls.

Whatever the rest of the party is doing, or if there is a fight continuing Jasper doesn't care. This is what he will do.

Also: I had a pretty good guess who that Shadovar was when I saw her face to face. It also helped that I realized she cast a 5th level summons which makes her immensely overpowered for us, and also when I realized Charrah was actually Charrah.

2+2+2=Loki. Jasper's a smart guy, and he's been hunting her since he got to Daggerdale.

Her taking the orb was another big clue for Jasper. I already have a theory that Palus/Charrah had split from the Shadovar and were holding Thistle Ridge and the orbs against the wishes of the Shadovar. What Jasper saw here was Loki set a trap for us that had us kill Charrah for her and then she stole the orb. Loki waited until Charrah was dead, before she took what she came for and bugged out.

Now Palus will blame us for killing her granddaughter, and Mayor Cassimar and the rest of them will blame us for not saving Coventina. Who's going to believe she was a Sharan spy now? What they saw was a love-sick girl run away from home to chase after Jasper and get killed in Thistle Ridge.

The death of Coventina may be enough for the Committee to pull the contract from us. Never underestimate how upset the public and government gets when a pretty young rich girl comes to harm.

The consolation? The only thing I can think of is that there never was a deal possible. We were never walking out of that floor without a fight. They spent too long setting up that trap. And Blacky Loki was never going to let Coventina live.

On Coventina's rescue plan:

1. Lucan told me Coventina was dangling from the ceiling over a pit. I cast haste, ran into range and tried to disbelieve the illusion (failed)

2. Lucan tells me "Coventina is not there" so I take that to mean there is no Coventina hanging from the ceiling. I get peppered with arrows and nearly die. So I turn invisible.

However, what the plan was, had I seen Coventina, I was going to have Masra cast Tangle Vine to hold her in place, and Jasper was going to cast Jump to go up to where she was. Then try to undo the chains, and use Telekinetic Manoeuvre (basically The Force) to fall clear of the pit.

But that would've taken 3 rounds, and the chain would've been released killing us both.

So went Invisible to go around and try to gain control of the chain so I could release her myself, while Masra used Tangle Vines to pull her to safety.

Two problems: first, a FUCKING DRAGON kept Masra separated on the other side of the room, and the dragon stood exactly where Masra needed to be to cast her spell. And second, I expected the chain to be anchored to the wall guarded by a goblin, and NOT by the most powerful antagonist we've ever seen.

When Jeska put up a rope, that was actually a good call, but the second Coventina touched it, Loki was going to let go of the chain.

So that was that. Coventina was dead before we even got there and we never stood a chance of rescuing her.

We got well and truly fucked.
