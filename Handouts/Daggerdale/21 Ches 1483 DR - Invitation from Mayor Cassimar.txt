Ches 21, 1484 DR - Invitation from Mayor Cassimar
DM Handout (Les Blackwell)
Received by messenger at the Myth Drannor Embassy, late morning Ches 21, 1484 DR.

---

My Dearest Jeska,

I trust this letter finds you in the bloom of good health and spirits. It is with great delight and anticipation that I extend to you a cordial invitation to a gathering of mirth and splendor, to be held within the comfort of my humble abode three nights hence.

In contemplating this forthcoming celebration, my thoughts have often turned to you, dear Jeska, as a cherished beacon of grace and charm within our esteemed town. Your presence, like a gentle zephyr amid a verdant garden, is regarded as a treasure among our circle of acquaintances.

It is my earnest hope that you shall honour me by gracing the soiree with your esteemed presence. Pray, bring with you any friends whose company you hold dear, that they too may partake in the myriad delights that shall be laid before us.

The evening promises to be one of enchantment and camaraderie, where laughter shall mingle with the strains of melodious music, and where the flickering glow of candlelight shall cast its soft enchantment upon all in attendance. All a beauty for every sense.

Anticipating the pleasure of your company, dear Jeska, I remain, with warm regards,

Yours affectionately,

LC.

---
Note: "LC" = Lilly Cassimar, Mayor of Dagger Falls. The invitation is for a soiree "three nights hence" — i.e. Ches 24, 1484 DR. The accompanying image depicts a red-haired woman (Coventina?) with a magnifying glass and letter-writing implements.
