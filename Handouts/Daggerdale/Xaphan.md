Xaphan is a ranking member in the Sons of Bel.  He had the rank of Magus, and served under the Belial, Vetis.  Xaphan is a cunning summoner and illusionist, and is more than a little quick with his tongue.  Despite being captured by the White Cloaks Bravo Team, he managed to negotiate for his life and freedom, by selling out his superior, and foiling a major plot to abduct dozens of villagers from Dwimmerholme Farm.

He remains at-large, though he did have his hands cut off by Hennigan Paros.
