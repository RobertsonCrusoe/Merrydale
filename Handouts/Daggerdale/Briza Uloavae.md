Briza Uloavae, a powerful female Drow is the only survivor of a Family from Menzoberranzan, and has come to the surface with a small army of mercenaries. They did not come to the surface by way of Undermountain, and indeed had to travel by boat for some distance, to get to the City. They actively sought out the Dalael, of whom they apparently learned in Baldur’s Gate. 

Briza's faction has made contact with the Dalael, and, based on their apparent cooperation during the Irlingstar Villa Affair, appears to have formed an alliance with the Vhaeraunites.
