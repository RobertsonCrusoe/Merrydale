A known lycanthrope, Halam is the Patriarch of the Waterdhavian noble house of Umbrusk and a former associate of Yarkul’s.

In Mirtul of 1373, Halam made contact with Gaebril Tremalkin. A devout Selûnite, Halam was uneasy dealing with the Paladin of Shar, but had been told in a vision from his goddess to do so. Halam's news was that the lycanthrope known as Egan, a former ally of Thagdul's (at least by Umbrusk's reckoning), has devoted himself to fighting the Cult of Muir Quaiggoth establishing itself in Waterdeep.
