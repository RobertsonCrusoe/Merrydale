Thanks to the actions of Saint Maturin, Daggerdale is regarded as sacred land by the Church of Ilmater.  It has been a long time since the church's influence has been felt there, and Reynard has dispatched Tyrian Grey to the north as a symbolic reminder to the  of the history of the dale.  Reynard does not approve of the Whitegates' tactics despite the friendliness of Amaunator and Ilmater. However, Daggerdale is very far from the Noble Heart's traditional power base and it is unlikely that the Whitegates would even care about Tyrian's presence.
