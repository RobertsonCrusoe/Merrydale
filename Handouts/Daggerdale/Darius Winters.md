Mayor of Dwimmerholme Farm after his election in the winter of 1480 DR.  He was formerly a Sun Blade with the rank of Captain in the 4th Company.  He is retired and pursuing an unlikely political career in the small village at Dwimmerholme.

==Excerpt from Abandon Hope, All Ye Who Enter Here==
  
  
The mayor is likewise young; perhaps 28 or 29, and is a recently retired Sun Blade Forester who spent 10 years in Cormanthor fighting the enemies of the dale.  He is stout and well-muscled, and has many scars on his arms and head; this is a veteran of many skirmishes.  He has the characteristic features of a Chondathan; brown hair, blue eyes, fair skin.  He is quiet in demeanour for the most part, and when he does speak, it is in short succinct sentences.  He does not seem to be the ideal candidate for a mayor, but here he sits.

This leaves you in the room with the hard-looking ex-military mayor.  He has been holding his breath along with his words until the magistrate leaves.  “He means well, he really does” explains Winters.  “He’s the third magistrate this farm has had in three years.  The people loved Hoetmer.  They hated Willems.  He came down too hard on the Chaunteans after the Dark Garden business.  They actually had him removed, somehow...” he drifts off for a second.  “Well, now the people have Renfrew and I.  Both of us are new, and we are dealing with a terrible tragedy.  I for one will welcome any help, and offer you what aid is mine to give.”  He pauses for a moment.
 
“I have put together a small fund to help you out, if you’ll have it.  I know that soldiers don’t work for free; it is expensive business, soldiering men.  And we need to get our people back.  We’ve put together nearly 300 gold.  We can give you half of that for heading out there, and giving us your word that you will do your damndest to find those people. If you find any alive, we’ll give you the rest.  Sound fair?”
