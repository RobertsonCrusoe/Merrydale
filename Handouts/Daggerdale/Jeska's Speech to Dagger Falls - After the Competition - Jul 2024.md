If we gather the community this is Jeska's speech.

---

I am Jeska Monarch, assumed ambassador to the embassy here in your town. I
am not that. I am in my culture just a young elf, a mere 270 years of age
sent here by my own superiors for reasons I do not understand.

I have formed a team, "Chrysalis" as you are aware... and we have won the
right through competition to cleanse Thistle Ridge that resides there.

However, this competition has brought evil and grief to this town.
I do not know you, or this land, but I know this was an attack by our
enemies.

We face a dire foe, and only as a community can we prevail.

Shall we forge onward?
Yes.
We will burn out the corpse of the worm! We will fill the hole with
rock,sand and mortar. We will plant a garden!
Every man woman and child who works to this end will be paid 1sp per day.
All required supplies will be compensated at double normal value.

This cost will be generously donated by the personal accounts of the
organizers of the event. There will be no tax increase due to this
expenditure.

I have 2 more unrelated facts to note.
1. The Redjacks are to be disbanded, or at minimum, evicted from the Dales.
2. No lethal fights will be permitted. I encourage you to fight, hone your
skills, but no killing. I made the same decree in the competition, and I
make it now. I Will personally deal with any breach of this decree.

That said, legend speaks of the stalwart demeanor of Dalefolk.. shall we
work together? Forget the past, rally when impossible and forge ahead
together!
