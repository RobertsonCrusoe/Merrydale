For Blacky, but cc'd to the rest so you know what Jasper's doing this week, now that we have had this session and cleared some things up from last time.


OBJECTIVES:

• Most of the speculation we have done over email.  Lucan and I are trying to work out the details of the mystery.  While he seems to be more focused on the nature of what happened to the families, I am more concerned about the role Mouvranesh and the Netherese forces seem to be playing in all this.  There is a larger objective here than whatever happened to the Sembian families, and that is my focus.

• On top of that, I am curious about liberating the little girls from the state they find themselves in.  Jasper's interest in necromancy is to cure it or end it, not to harness it (necessarily.). He doesn't want to see the girls destroyed, which is precisely what will happen if the Aumanatori get their influence into Thistle Ridge. There is a reason why I want to favour halflings and dwarves, if possible, in our efforts to rebuild Thistle Ridge, and keep the Amaunatori as far away as possible.  If we need the aid or influence  of a god or church, other than Yondalla, then let it be Chauntea and not Aumanator.

• Jasper is anxious to liberate the prisoners being held by the goblins. With each day that passes over this tenday he gets more and more anxious and insistent upon it. At some point the other party members make it clear that it would be suicidal to attempt a rescue before securing Thistle Ridge Tower at the least.  But he remains determined to save those people.

• In terms of the practical reclamation of Thistle Ridge, Jasper is useless.  He doesn't know who you'd  need to field a crew to do this task. Probably with his intelligence and some reading he could figure it out, but he has almost no interest at all in the actual reclamation of Thistle Ridge and building this town, for commerce or otherwise.  His interest is larger than that.

"You're talking about coppers and carpenters!  I'm talking about infernal bargains and trans-planar plots with catastrophic implications!"

His plan is:

1. Get rid of goblins so we can reach the necessary locations to gather information
1a. Liberate the slaves
2. Gather information about Shades and Shadowfell and the threat looming over this Dale and beyond

3. Liberate the girls from their curse, as well as any other victims suffering in undeath
4. Find out what Mouvranesh's plan is for Daggerdale
5. Stop her, then return home to Shadowdale

Settling down to administer Thistle Ridge is not a part of it.  He still hasn't really comprehended that he is part-owner of this whole endeavour. When he joined, he literally signed a piece of paper Jeska put in front of him without reading it.  At one point Lucan might sit him down to try to con him out of his share, or Mari might sit him down and explain the whole thing to him, but he'll come to understand.  For now, when the topic turns to business he just tunes it all out.  (But as a player, I'm happy to help and don't tune it out.  It's just not Jasper's thing.)


INTELLIGENCE GATHERING:

• We have done pretty much all the conversations I needed to do.  There may be the odd question for Sanduskan Vale or others that I'll send off when I remember them.  Most of the speculation, Lucan and I have been bouncing off each other.

• As mentioned, I was reading as much as I could about Sylvanus.  Jasper grew up in Shadowdale where the two main faiths were Chauntea and Lathander, same as Daggerdale, but his family always leaned more towards the Chauntean worship, and has maintained some distrust for these Amaunatori.  In Shadowdale they still cling on to worship of the Lathander aspect of Amaunator.  Despite being a wizard, Jasper doesn't worship Mystra or any of the magic gods.  If he is hard-pressed to declare a religion, he would point to Chauntea, as a good hedge wizard, just because that's the faith of his farmer upbringing.  However, he has become fascinated with learning all he can about Sylvanus, and the interplay between him and Chauntea.  With the revelation during the ritual about "The Mother of the Ancient Faith" he will start reading more about Chauntean worship in Netheril, as well as just books on Chauntea - the older the better.

• As Nemia, Lucan and I were doing the ritual, I was making a point to remember the steps taken to perform the ritual and asking about and writing down the components Nemia was going to use along the way.  Once the ritual is done, he will write as much of it down, with the help of Lucan. This is his second time casting the ritual, and while I don't expect to learn how to do it myself just yet, I want to at least identify what the ritual is and work towards eventually learning to do it.

• There is quite a bit I want to know about the devils that plagued this land just 2 years prior, and why no one seems to still be concerned that they may still be around, but that's something I am hesitant to delve into right now.  The time will come for that later, and I have enough on my plate.  I do believe this whole thing is infernal in nature.

I'll have more questions, etc when I see the handouts that are coming. Especially the White Cloaks intelligence report.

I am concerned about the Purple Worm attack and the Gargoyle Raid.  We now know that the Purple Worm was lured to Dagger Falls (why attack the arena, and not the centre of town?  I guess they expected most people of note would be at the arena, but the centre of town would've been more devastating.). But what about the gargoyles?  What was that about?


CRAFTING

• I am an Expert Craftsman, and my Magical Crafting is +11. Critical success does not matter as I am not spending extra days crafting.   If you want to roll, the DC's are:

Pyrite Rat (level 2): DC20
Masquerade Scarf (level 2): DC20
Everlight Crystal (level 1): DC15
Shining Ammunition (level 1): DC15
Lightbringer Spellguns (level 2): DC20

I am opting to spend the gold to complete each one in 1 day.  I have the formulas for all of these items.  Let me know if I fail rolls as I might change what I craft.  Especially if there is a critical failure.

• Day 1: Pyrite Mortimer Figurine (16gp worth of materials) - Jasper has the Pyrite Rat formula, so it takes him just a day to craft.  I added a little flavour by making it an attempt at re-summoning  Mortimer.  (In my own Jasper storyline, when he became 3rd level, he gained the 2nd Level spell Final Sacrifice.  Jasper's first attempt at casting it resulted in his complete destruction of Mortimer's Prime Material essence.  Unfortunately, he is unable to resummon Mortimer from wherever he ended up getting blasted to.  Mechanically, this is because Jasper lost the Familiar feat as a result of a small respec, but he will regain the feat (and Mortimer) at 4th Level.  In the meantime, he has trapped a small essence of Mortimer in the Pyrite Rat, which allows him to summon this monstrous version for 1 hour per day.)

• Day 2: Jasper takes up knitting, and from this point on, he is always knitting as soon as he sits down somewhere.  At the end of the day, he presents Lucan with a Masquerade Scarf.  The scarf is in Chrysalis green, with the company emblem and the initials "L. A." embroidered on it.

• Day 3: More knitting, and Jasper creates a second Masquerade Scarf, which he presents to Mari. This is identical to Lucan's, except with the initials "M. T."

• Day 4: More knitting, and Jasper creates a third Masquerade Scarf for Ser Rowan.  This is in Chrysalis green, but it is embroidered with the Purple Dragon emblem alongside the company's, and it bears the initials "Ser R. G."

• Day 5: more knitting, and a fourth Masquerade Scarf, this one for Jeska.  This one is in Chrysalis green, with the company emblem, but has no initials on it.

• Day 6: More knitting, and a final Masquerade Scarf for himself. This one, like all the others in Chrysalis green, with the company emblem embroidered on one end, but instead of Jasper's initials there is an arcane symbol.

• Day  7:  Jasper creates an Everlight Crystal and learns the formula.  Not a difficult item to make since it is just casting a spell on a gem, and the items I had previously purchased and prepared.

• Day 8: Jasper creates some Shining Ammunition arrowheads (x4), which he gives to Lucan.

• Day 9: Jasper creates his Lightbringer Spellguns (x4).

• Day 10: no crafting, in case I failed any crafting rolls above, to be able to try again.

Total cost: 192gp.
