|...being the Last Will and Testament of Mourn Raventree, enacted in sorrow in 1372 DR.

I, Mourn Raventree, the Second of that name, Noble of the City of Waterdeep, being of sound mind and body, hereby set down the details of my Last Will and Testament.

#All property belonging to me and/or in my possession is to be returned to the hands of my father, Surakh Raventree, to be disposed of as he sees fit. If said Surakh Raventree is deceased at the time of my passing, then the property in question is to pass to my younger brother, Jameen Raventree. If he is deceased, then the property passes to whichever Raventree of closest blood relation is still alive.
#An exception to Section 1. The blade Ratbiter, imbued with magical properties and belonging to me is to be returned to Morgunn Lathkule, noble of Waterdeep. If said Morgunn Lathkule is deceased, then ownership of the magical sword is to pass to the Lathkule Family in general, with the provision that it will be put to good use fulfilling its purpose.
#Upon my death, my lifeless body is to be buried along with my ancestors in the Raventree Family crypt. The service and consecration is to be performed by a priest of Mielikki in the traditional fashion, with additional support from the Church of Sune under the direction of my cousin Naiomee Helmfast, noble of Waterdeep and Priestess of Sune.
#Any and all money in my possession at the time of my death is to be donated to the Church of Mielikki.

Failure to comply with any of the bequests set out in this docuemnt will invoke the wrath of Mielikki, Lady of the Forests, and violates Waterdeep Law.

— Mourn Raventree
