[PC Attribution: Gregor Kahsick (Les Blackwell)]
A dedicated surgeon and inspiring professor, Doctor Gregor Kahsick is the founder and Dean of the Waterdeep School of Anatomy and Medicine.  Though sometimes feared and often despised by those he seeks to aid, this erudite man of science brandishes the torch of knowledge against the superstitions that have made him an outcast in his own land.

*Familiar is a flying cat named Aggis
*Kahsick is known as a Necromancer to a select few, but is otherwise respected by the city's populace. He had some dealings with Thagdul, but these seemed to be as a consultant, or mercenary. 
*At one point he cast a powerful degenerative-necromancy spell on Esperel, and threatened Beljuril’s life, for a break-in that occurred in his School
*He later made a written apology to Beljuril for his churlishness
