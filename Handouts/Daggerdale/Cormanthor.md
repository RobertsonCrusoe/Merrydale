The forest of Cormanthor has been growing at an unnatural pace since the Spellplague.  In only 100 years, it has reclaimed nearly all of Daggerdale; lands that took men nearly 300 years to clear and cultivate have been completely overgrown by trees that have the appearance of a centuries-old forest, though most are less than a few decades old.

The growing forest is home to all manner of enemies of the dale, including bands of orcs and other goblinoids, lycanthropes, bandits of many races (including humans), and all sorts of fantastic monsters.  The forest is a beautiful, but very dangerous place.

There is a slowly-growing number of Green and Moon Elves moving into regions of the forest that their ancestors once inhabited before men cut back the forest.  These returned inhabitants argue that the forest is just returning to it's natural state, and though they are on good diplomatic terms with the men of the dales, they certainly aren't displeased about the accelerated regrowth of their forest-home.
