Maturin Horgath was interred initially in the Hearthstone alongside Gaebril Tremalkin. Shortly after the desecration of Gaebril Tremalkin’s remains, Maturin Horgath was interred in the secured vault beneath the temple.  A number of Ilmatari made the journey to the Monastery to look upon the glowing body of Maturin and in 1397 DR, Mautrin Horgath was proclaimed a saint by the Ilmatari faith.}}
