A letter sent by Ellyth to the

Reprobates:

Lucien is intent on slaying my colleagues and myself. After recent events I have sworn off all violence towards Reprobate members.  However, I will still defend myself against attacks against my person. As you are aware, the best defence is a strong offence.

Therefore:

If you cannot persuade Lucien to abandon his jihad against the Red Herring, we will have no choice but to end his threat to us through use of deadly force.

I urge you to convince Lucien to abandon his foolhardy obsession. If so I promise you that I will continue peaceful and amiable relations with the Reprobates. If you cannot convince him, please refrain from preventing us doing as we must.

Yours in good faith,

— Ellyth 100px}}
