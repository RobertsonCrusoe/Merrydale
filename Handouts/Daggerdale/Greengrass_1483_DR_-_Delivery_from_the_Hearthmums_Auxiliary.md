Greengrass,1483 DR—A Delivery from The Hearthmums’ Auxiliary

A large crate arrives, swathed in spring-green ribbons. Inside, folded with care atop a bundle of bright quilted banners, is a letter addressed to Mrs. Mari Thickwickle, penned in a neat, floral hand. The seal reads: Mrs. Dotty Bramblebadge, First Ladle of the Dagger Falls Hearthmums’ Auxiliary.

  Mum,

  Me and the ladies have been hard at work on these, and are right sorry we didn’t get them to you sooner, but it sounds like we’re just in time anyhows, as there’s no end of buzz and other noises about what you’re about now and how close you might be to some major “objective” or some such. I know you’ll be safe, and prudent, and let your friends fight on a full belly and a full heart, and we’re proud to share your stature, Mum.

  From the corners of the Dale and the corners of town, us Hearthmums everywhere got together to make these banners in your colours and heraldry, and we have filled them with every suitable prayer that can be spared this time of year—begging your pardon for all Yondalla requires of us in these busy days before her Feast and can’t be spared for you and yours, and likewise begging your pardon that we aren’t at your side risking all, while the rest of the Dale celebrates a New Spring, and what a celebration we’ll enjoy, Mum, and drink your health, and your children’s and theirs! And to Chrysalis too!

  Eight banners we’ve made, and eight banners infused with the Prayer of Safest Hearth. They work best when they can enclose an area, the more and the closer the better if you catch my meaning. At any rate, if you hang them all on the same structure, it should protect as large a building as you’re likely to find left standing at Thistle Ridge.

  Best of luck, Mum!

  Yondalla’s Smile,

  Dotty B.

The banners are all quilted in different fashions, though your colours and heraldry are consistent. They range in size from 4’-6’ wide, and 10’-20’ long, and have been expertly crafted with a variety of images ranging from the pastoral to the martial, each beautiful in its own rustic way. They can produce the effect of a Sanctuary spell when 3 or more are used to enclose an area, the effective DC of the spell varying depending on how many are hoisted over what size of area. The banners weigh 120 lbs all together.
