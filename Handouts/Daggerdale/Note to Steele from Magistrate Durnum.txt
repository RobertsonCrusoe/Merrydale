Mr. Steele;

I am told by many that you are a man of questionable morals, and that I should not get mixed up with you. But I know that you did good work for Force Grey in the past, and that the Blackstaff himself reccommends you for a capable operator.

And so, I extend a formal request to meet with you tomorrow evening at 7 bells. I have a matter of great importance to discuss with you that I cannot here commit to paper, as I am sure you will understand.

If you would be kind enough to meet with me outside the West Gate tomorrow evening, I would be in your debt. I am carrying out the duties of my Office in this matter, but I must confess that this is an affair that requires gentle handling, and so I thank you in advance for your discretion in this matter.

Yours in Justice,

Court Magistrate Durnum
