:—Robert E. Howard, The Castle of the Devil

Alystair is a big man standing 6'4”, though he remains lithe and hard-looking. He wears his travel-and-bloodstained cloak with as much honour as would any knight. A huge sword is strapped across his back. His leather clothes, bracers and boots look as though parts of them have been literally soaked in blood. To complete this grisly spectiacle, a bloodstained leather half-mask hangs below his chin. Those who know him are careful of that mask; when it goes on, things start dying.
