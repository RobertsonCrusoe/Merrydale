The very capable leader of the Sun Blades at Dwimmerholme Farm.  Oakdale was cooperative with Alystair Dretch during his investigations of 1479, helping to round up the villagers that had converted to Shar.  In Marpenoth of 1481 DR, he assisted the White Cloaks Bravo Team as well as he could, going so far as to send men into the field with them against standing orders.
