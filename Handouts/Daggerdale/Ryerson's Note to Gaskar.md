A note from Ryerson to Gaskar, dated 27 Mirtul 1371, discovered in the Neverwinter office of Emuldan's Carters.

Gaskar

You are to be in Dekanter by no later than Kythorn 20.

Please load up lots 22, 23, 24, 25 and 42. You will meet up with a man named Gildon, with whom you will negotiate a trade.

Do not trade for less than 100 gp/unit.

— Ryerson}}
