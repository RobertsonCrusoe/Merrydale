A threatening letter from an unknown party known as 'The Hunabar Slayer' to Haskar, patriarch of the Waterdhavian Noble House of Hunabar, delivered c. Harvestide, 1371 DR.

Hunabars,

I'm coming for you. I will not rest until every last one of your line's blood has been spilled. My blade hungers for your death. No Hunabar will escape my wrath. We had a truce Haskar. We had a truce until you maniacally attacked that Selûnite priest. The truce is now over.

I declare war on your kind. By the end of Harvestide, every citizen of Waterdeep, from Piergieron in his palace to the lowliest of beggars in Dock Ward will kwow the true nature of your family. And I will singlehandedly purge this city from the blot that are the Hunabars. I will personally have the honour of cutting out your heart. Fear not, immortaiity will be yours as your cold and mosntrous head, stuffed, will adorn my mantlepiece. I'm coming for you. I'm coming for you.
