Malvau is a wood-elf guardian who is a native of Cormanthor.  He and two of his tribe had been hunting after and harrying the bandit gang known as The Bloody Eye; an organized and experienced gang that preys on the roads of the Dales.  Unfortunately, the Blood Eyes managed to find the elven trio one night and ambush them.  Malvau's two friends were killed and he barely escaped with his life.

He continued to follow The Blood Eyes with a suicidal determination, when he saw the White Cloaks riding past him on the road; he had been in hiding, but he was inspired by the shimmering cloaks of the heroes, and shouted after them.  The White Cloaks quickly took the elf into their ranks, promising to help Malvau take care of the bandits, if he would help them on their mission to investigate a claim of werewolf problems at the Crossed Blades Farm.  The White Cloaks had just run into a scouting party for the Blood Eyes which nearly killed both Tuugan and Alystair, so the arangement seemed to benefit all.
