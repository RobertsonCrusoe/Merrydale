Belphegor was a pain devil (Excruciarch by the taxonomy of Abandon Hope, All Ye Who Enter Here) that was hired by Vetis to aid in the evil ritual known as Bel's Bargain.  Belphegor was able to torture the Fletcher family to such a degree that half of them gave up their souls, despite being the toughest men in the town.

Belphegor was destroyed at the ambush outside of Dwimmerholme Farm by Bravo Team.

The book Abandon Hope, All Ye Who Enter Here describes the Excruciarchs thusly;

Excruciarchs are relatively weak devils, but they are cruel and bloodthirsty foes. A pain devil’s scourge causes agony that briefly cripples its target. Pain devils are skillful and cruel torturers. They are common in the upper hells, especially Dis and Minauros. Sadistic mortals summon pain devils to the world to serve as mercenaries.  They are commonly purchased for periods of 111 days for the cost of 3 souls, though being mercenaries, this cost varies tremendously, depending on the hardship of the service being bartered for, and the party bargaining with them.
