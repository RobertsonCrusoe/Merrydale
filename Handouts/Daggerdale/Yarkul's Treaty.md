During his brief tenure as the Lord of Daggerdale, the Zhentarim mage Yarkul made the following overture towards the negotiation of a Treaty of Non-Aggression with the town of Shadowdale.

I, Yarkul, Lord of Daggerdale and ruler of Dagger Falls,

Do fully and whole heartedly offer a treaty of non-aggression with the people of Daggerdale.

For my part of this Treaty, I declare I will not hinder the travel of any citizen of Daggerdale, nor any other person or their property, traveling along the Trade Way, nor will I keep patrols on the Trade Way, nor will I keep patrols on the Trade Way beyond a distance of 10 mi. south of the Daggerdale Valley Border.

I will also free any citizen enslaved in my Dale and allow them to go or stay as it suits them, and make what reparations, in coin, that I can to return them to their former mode of life.

On the part of Shadowdale, its Lord and agents, we simply ask that its aggression towards Daggerdale cease.

I will hold up my end until such time as I get a rejection from the Lord of Shadowdale, or until aggressive action is taken against Daggerdale by the above mentioned parties.

I hope that we can make some arrangement to meet and discuss these affairs and begin to heal the wounds that my careless leadership has caused.

I hope, in the long run, to develop a mutually beneficial relationship between our two Dales, in which citizens will travel and trade freely.

These terms I offer truly and whole heartedly, along with an apology for the damage that my leadership caused in its budding year.

Please send a written reply with my man.
