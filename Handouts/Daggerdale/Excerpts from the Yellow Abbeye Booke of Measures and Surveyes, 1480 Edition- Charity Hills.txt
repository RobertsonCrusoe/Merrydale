==Charity Hills Farm==
Population: 1160
Non-Humans: 0

===Governance===  

It is of key importance to recognize that Charity Hills is a sovereign state within our borders, which exists under the Articles of Cooperation and Coexistence, 1401 DR.

The polity is ruled in the traditional fuedal system, imported from Westgate in the 14th Century.  Four noble families own most of the land, the patriarchs of which carry the title of Earl.  

The head of the entire polity is known as Chancellor, and this person is appointed on the Feast of the Moon by the bishop of the cathedral of Selune.  The Chancellor is picked from the 'peerage', or the Earls.  

There are three tiers of administration in Charity Hills.

The present Chancellor is John Ferguson.

Magistrate Ankger Willems (c.f. Yellow Abbeye Grande Accounting of the Clergy, 1480 Edition), aged 51, 3 clerks in Rotation

===Military===
7th Sun Blade Bright Spears, 3rd Platoon; bolstered Platoon of 5 Squads, under command of Lt. Kesh Oakdale, aged 30.

Militia under-strength; reported at only 35 men, 30 of which are retired from the Sun Blades.

===Real Holdings===
Class IV fortification, barracks for 150
  
Fortified holdings; 147 acres inside class VI perimeter, accounting for all inhabitants and ¾ of livestock  
 
Cultivated holdings; 664 acres, 1/3rd each in rotation of Wheat, Barley, fallow.  100 acres of private gardens with all known varieties of vegetable, fruits and herbs.    
 
===Holdings===    
Of Livestock; 46 dairy cows, 39 goats, 88 swine, 545 chickens, 140 (or so) rabbits, 43 sheep, 18 plough-oxen
  
Of Common Wealth; town hall furnishings worth 955 gold (itemized in Appendix M/S-D4), common market accoutrements worth 245 gold,   
 
Of Ecclesiastic Wealth; chapel furnishings and accoutrement worth 2045 gold (itemized in Appendix M/S-DA4).  
 
Of Military Stores; 200 long spears, 30 cross bows, 2000 bolts, 200 wooden shields, 40 pikes, 26 maces, 24 long swords, 2 large military hammers.
  
Of Mounts; 44 horses privately owned, 44  horses assigned to the garrison, 12 horses commonly owned.  
 
===Tax Revenue for 1479===  
 
Coin; 2980 gold to the State Coffers, 2980 to the Ecclesiastic Coffers.  

Kind; 10,000 measures each of Barley and Wheat, 4 dairy cows, 16 swine, 100 chickens, 35 rabbits, 5 sheep, in roughly equal measure to both coffers.
