This is a standing army of about 200+ dwarves that are in service to the Council of Silver.  They mainly hold ground that has been taken by the dwarves, and are cheifly tasked with guarding the East Gate and the Central Burroughs.

==Structure==

*Commander - Highest ranking Silver Guard, answers directly to the Silver Council - Currently, this is Dunkirk Kor-tes.

*Captain - Has command of a company (63 dwarven warriors in 3 platoons of 21).  There are 4 captains, and each has his own specific area or task.

*Lieutenant - Has command of a platoon (20 dwarves in 4 squads of 5).

*Sergeant - Hass command of a squad (4 other dwarves plus himself).
