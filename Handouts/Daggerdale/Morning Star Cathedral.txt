[[Image:Brotherhood Tower circa 1380 DR.jpg|frame|400px|Morning Star Tower, circa 1380 DR]]This building is the new centre-peice to the town of Daggerfalls, and is the very heart of the entire dale today.  It is the religious epicentre of the Church of Amaunator in the Dale, and the defacto political centre (replacing The Tower).

This building has a long and storied history, starting out as [[Brotherhood Tower]]; an evil building constructed during the Zhent rule of the dale in the 1370's.  It was converted to a temple to Lathander and Mystra after the [[Previous Adventures in Daggerdale|events of 1372 DR]], and renamed at that time Morning Star Tower, but was also often called the Tower of Freedom, especially by the many slaves who were freed from it who stayed in Dagger Falls after it's liberation.

In the late 1370's, when Mystra was slain and Lathander dissapeared, Morning Star Tower was abandoned, but only for a short time.  When [[Ardelusk Whitegate]] stepped forward to lead the poeple of his dale from the looming darkness, one of the first things he did was to clear out Morning Star Tower of the vagrants and few Sharites who had taken it over.  Soon after, the events of the [[Whitegate Epiphany]] followed, and the the worship of Amaunator began, as the [[Whitegate Family]] began it's climb to wealth and power.

The effect of the Whitegate wealth was felt immediately at Morning Star Tower.  With the revenue from the [[West Hill Mine]], and his love of fine stone architechture, Ardelusk began many ambitous projects, the crown of which was Morning Star Cathedral.  

Morning Star Cathedral is one of the best examples of human architechtural endeavour in Faerun today.  It has a soaring vaulted ceiling, clerestories with circular windows which are all stained glass, flying buttresses, spires and a dizzying amount of statuary, freizes and gargoyles.  

The cathedral layout follows the cross-design that has been popular for hundreds of years in Faerun, but the proportions are grander, and the details nearly unrivalled in human architecture.  The main cathedral itself took 40 years to finish, and since then, two large wings have been added.

[[Image:Morning Star painting.jpg|left|frame|400px|Morning Star Cathedral, circa 1475 DR]]The seminary wing, or south wing, is a large monastic school complete with dormitories for over 100 students and staff.  The administrative wing, or north wing, is where the ever-expanding bureaucracy of the [[Church of Amaunator|Church]] and the dale is now accomodated.  

There are always 20 [[Sun Blades]] on duty patrolling the grounds and gaurding the gates of the Cathedral, as well as a 4 knights of the [[Order of the Lambent Shield]] who are never far from the upper clergy.

The visitor to Morningstar Cathedral can expect to enter through the main doors to the grand entrance of the rear of the nave.  There, a host of clergy greets the visitor, asking about their business, and accompanies them wherever they might have to go.  The clergy there are polite and as helpful as they can be, but are unwavering in their duties.
