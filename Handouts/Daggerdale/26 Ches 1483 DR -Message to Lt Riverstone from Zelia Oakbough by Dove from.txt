26 Ches, 1483 DR -Message to Lt. Riverstone from Zelia Oakbough  by Dove from Loedegan Close 
Lt. Riverstone: 
Please share this with anyone you trust and value.  Im not sure I should be sending this based on the vague rules of this tournament, but it feels like the right thing, so I must. 
As you know, I did a brief scout of the ridge between Eastwatch and the ruin of Thistle Ridge Tower. The ridge is crawling with goblin spies from about two miles east of Gandrims Pass, all the way to the tower. 

Yellow Tooth Clan. There is a permanent garrison of them in the Tower, at least 10 by my count, but I didnt get too close. 
I caught a glimpse of the beast that leads them; a large hairy bugbear with a cruel looking longbow. I was very lucky to have seen it before it saw me.  I observed it laying a trap a few hundred feet east of the Tower.  The thing is cunning and I confess that I fear it. 
You will not be able to go further than the Moss Bend without being observed on the road, and I fear probably before that, though I speculate. 
Do not permit anyone, including my opponents in this contest, to travel the road without substantial guard. 
-Z. Oakbough
