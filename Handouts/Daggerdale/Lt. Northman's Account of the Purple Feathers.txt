From Lt. Northman;

\"We were deployed there on 27 Tarsakh.  There had been two attacks before that, and 3 masons were abducted, and 4 guards killed.  They came in small numbers, but they were smart and careful, unlike the hired guards at the tower.  When the masons refused to continue work on the new tower, the whole project was evacuated, and by the 27th we were there, like I said.

\"The next day, the 28th, I sent out my scouting unit, and we heard them butchered in the mountains nearby; they made some sport of it, too, and carted off one of them, dead or alive, I don’t’ know. He’s dead now, though, I don’t doubt.  Poor bastard.  I hope he’s dead.

\"When we rode out to render aid to our brothers, we found ourselves ambushed, and before we knew what had happened, 4 of our number were dead, full of purple-feathered arrows, and the orcs just slipped away into the mountains, abandoning the fight before it started.  That’s the problem I guess; they won’t stand and fight like regular orc, and we just didn’t have the tools for that particular job.

\"Still, orders came down from Captain Rainyerd on the 30th that we were to abandon the tower, taking anything of strategic value to deprive the enemy of it.  None of the boys were happy about it; we just wanted a straight fight and a chance to redeem ourselves and take vengeance, but orders are orders, and the Captain knows best.\"
