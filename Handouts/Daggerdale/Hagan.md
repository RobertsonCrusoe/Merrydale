[PC Attribution: Hagan (Les Blackwell)]
The Rooken Network's profile of this villain is based on notes compiled by Mourn Raventree before his death in the East.

*Murderer of Myelin Lathkule
*Hagan killed three Kormallis nobles and their consorts
*Loathes divination and would do everything to foil diviners
*Made several attempts at older Raventrees
*familiar named Roake (huge, smart spider in Dockward)
*Hagan has to kill once a month with every new moon
*Spends a great deal of time outside of the city
*Conjures a pyramid like Gregor
*Summoned werecreatures are his favourite lackeys
*Kills by close and personal encounters, or with a Spectral Hand that holds on
*Has a Necromantic “Seeking swarm” spell being sought by other mages
*Works alone, and has no known allies or friends
*Can turn invisible, fly
*2 days before or after the new moon, he kills
*Alias: Ravensblight
*Alias: Doomface
*Jameen Raventree died through life-drain by Ravensblight
*all manner of divination fails most of the time
*a conjurer of some kind
*spent too much time in the far east
*Curse on the three families
*Came back 4 years ago to finish the job

Possible members of a task force to find Hagan:
*Harkas Kormallis
*Rychel Voordan
*Steele
*Edgar Grimbold (drunken womanizer) at the Dripping Dagger
