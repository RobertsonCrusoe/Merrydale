An eladrin Marshall in the new forest empire of Cormanthor. In 1480, she was responsible for the sentence that exiled Ineluki and Eremus Elderbough, and Cirdan Vardamir, all wood elves, from Cormanthor for 120 years. This sad tale is told here.
