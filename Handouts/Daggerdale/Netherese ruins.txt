One of the most significant consequences to the receding of the sands of Anauroch is that many ancient Netherese ruins are being released from their centuries of entombment.  The Empire of Netheril is actively searching for these ruins, as they mark the borders of there quondam Empire, and many are full of lore and artifacts of great power.

The number of ruins and artifacts being produced by the receding sands is tremendous.  On the periphery of the old Empire, where the sands are just now vanishing, new buildings are being uncovered nearly every week, and certainly every month.  This has brought more and more agents of Netheril ever closer to Castle Radiance and Daggerdale, and has increased the level of conflict between the two polities.

The number of artifacts being recovered from the sands has brought Emissaries from Candlekeep to the Castle to catalogue as many as they can.  They have been known to offer bounties for suspected sites of newly-unearthed ruins.

The Castle has provided no end of adventurers to head out in the foothills and the new grasslands to investigate newly-revealed ruins, but it is often the case that they find  doing just the same.
