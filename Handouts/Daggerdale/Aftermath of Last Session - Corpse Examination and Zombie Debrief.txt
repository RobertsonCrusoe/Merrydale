I did have a question about Recall Knowledge in undead-related situations. ---

LES (3 Oct 2024, 11:50 AM):

The rule on Recall Knowledge is that each situation/spell/enemy has a main skill DC, and several secondaries.

By way of example, a typical zombie has Recall Knowledge: Religion DC of 13, and a Specific Lore DC of 11 (if you had the Undead Lore Skill). Occultism DC would be 15.
