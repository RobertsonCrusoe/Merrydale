Baron Drake was a cambion demon and follower of Iyachtu Xvim. He led a cult in Waterdeep that was able to manifest the Baneson in the sewers of Waterdeep, and later led the Brothers of the Black Fist in Dagger Falls.

*Status: Slain by Dorian and Maturin during the assault on Brotherhood Tower (the temple of Iyachtu Xvim) in Dagger Falls
