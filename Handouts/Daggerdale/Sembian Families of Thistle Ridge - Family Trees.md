ALAVANDOR FAMILY TREE

This family tree was composed by Magistrate Blaze Whitegate with the help of Clerk Daphne Dale, in late 1440 DR. Submitted to the Yellow Abbey Archives in 1441 DR after the tragedy at Thistle Ridge Farm.

* Alvund (b. in Sembia, 1349 DR, d. 1430 in Thistle Ridge of natural causes)
  - Senthia (nee Maeliticus – b. ? d. before coming to Daggerdale, first wife of Alvund, no known children)
  - Abrecia (nee Maeliticus – b. ? d. before coming to Daggerdale, second wife of Alvund, no known children)
  - Aisling (nee Maeliticus – b. ~1390, d. 1439 of self-murder at Alavandor Lodge, third wife)

    o Andarl (b. 1408 in TR Farm, d. 1439 in TR hinterland hunting) – Susserah (nee Clarandal, b. 1408 in TR Farm)
        - Andras (b. 1427 in TR Farm)
        - Lobelia (b. 1428, curious eyes – one blue one grey)
        - Dorian (b. 1428, d. 1439 in TR hinterland hunting)
        - Ridge (b. 1429)
        - Violet (f, b. 1431)
        - Lilac (f, b. 1432) [HIGHLIGHTED]
        - Rose (f, b. 1434)

    o Senthia (b. 1409 in TR Farm, married to Tergus Clarandal)

    o Abrecia (b. 1410 in TR Farm, married to Xerces Alavaxi, d. 1439 in TR Farm hinterland hunting)


CLARANDAL FAMILY TREE

This family tree was composed by Magistrate Blaze Whitegate with the help of Clerk Daphne Dale, in late 1440 DR. Submitted to the Yellow Abbey Archives in 1441 DR after the tragedy at Thistle Ridge Farm.

* Claudius (b. in Sembia, ~1345 DR)
  - Minerva (nee Maerimmon, b. ? d. before coming to Daggerdale, first wife of Claudius, no known children)
  - Sandara (nee Sakurth, b. ? d. before coming to Daggerdale, second wife of Claudius, no known children)
  - Saralla (nee Sakurth, b. ~1391 – third wife)

    o Tergus (b. 1408 in TR Farm) – Senthia (nee Alavandor)
        - Rosemary (f, b. 1428)
        - Archer (b. 1429)
        - Azalea (f, b. 1430)
        - Zinnia (f, b. 1432)
        - Oakley (b. 1433)
        - Hunter (b. 1434)
        - Lily (f, b. 1435) [HIGHLIGHTED]

    o Claudius (b. 1410 in TR Farm) - Coelathara (nee Alavaxi, b. 1420 in TR Farm)
        - Claudius (b. 1436)
        - Daisy (f, b. 1437)
        - Carpenter (b. 1438)

---
Note: Lilac Alavandor and Lily Clarandal are highlighted in the original document — the ghost-girls of Thistle Ridge.

Note: Susserah is described as "nee Clarandal" in the Alavandor tree (as Andarl's wife), but does NOT appear as a child in the Clarandal tree. The Clarandal tree lists only Tergus and Claudius Jr. as children of Claudius and Saralla. This omission is present in the original PDF — either an error in the source document, or a deliberate absence from the parish records compiled by Whitegate and Dale.
