Sent by Agamemnon to Beljuril, dated 9 Mirtul, 1372

Brother Beljuril;

If you are reading this, I am likely in trouble or worse. I have had the porter at the Tower of Order deliver this message to your honorable shrine because I am overdue from an errand of a dangerous nature. A similar letter has likewise been sent to Mourn Raventree and Rufus of Selûne.

This afternoon (this being the 9th, as I write this letter) I was approached by Steele, whom I am sure you know. He is a somewhat trusted ally, as he is part of a semi-secret organization called Force Grey (I tell this to you in confidence) which works for the safety of the City. Steele has arranged a meeting for myself and Altooth Mzoul (a.k.a. Kendal Thorne, operator of the Co. Aldebarran). I suspect Mzoul is the head of the Zhentarim organization, here in the City. Although I should trust Steele, I am wary of trouble wherever I tread in this ‘City of Splendours’, and I am taking the only two other allies we have. These two men you have not yet met, and I shall spare their names.

At any rate, something doesn’t seem right about this meeting, and something is certainly askew in the strange Elf’s brain. Thus, I am somewhat worried of trouble. Now that you are reading this, my doubts proved valid.

Know you this, Brother Beljuril of Azuth; As you read this, you may be the last of our organization in this part of the Heartlands. There may still be some in hiding, but we have been discovered and are now hunted. The Zhents are getting smarter and richer, which is to say more powerful and dangerous. You must be clever and ever wary, for certainly a price has been put upon your head. I have just learned that Grimwald’s Rangers have been hired to hunt down a bunch of men, some of which fit the descriptions of yourself, Rufus and Master Raventree. I have had but a single run-in with this murderer, and I can tell you he is a man not to be trifled with. He and his Rangers are perhaps the most dangerous Bounty Hunters in the Realms, and they may be after you. There is only one good way to keep them off your trail, and that is to put an end to the source of the bounty. This, I am working towards here in the City.

I am leaving you some of my notes, as you may be required to pick up where I seem to have left off. Please continue the excellent work you have begun, but be careful. Give no shadow your back, and trust no one. The Zhents have allies in many places in this City, so you must be wary, even, of the Law.

Azuth Bless Thee and Keep Thee.
