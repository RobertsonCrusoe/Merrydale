From Seth Forester

\"I know the area along Paladin’s road pretty good, and that pass between Black Death Mountain and The Old Man’s Tooth is nasty.  It’s called ‘Paladin’s Road’ because the Order spent most of ’66 and ’67 clearing  out of that canyon, and they say that there’s a dead Knight for every 100 yards of road that got built there.

\"But that’s not the real problem; the real problem is The Chute; it’s a natural stair on the other side of Bald Skull Lake that heads right up to the top of the Tooth, and no one who wants to keep people off the top of that mountain can fail.  There’s a few other ways up it, for sure, but none of those can be used to march an army up.  That’s why we’ll never take those damned  and  off’er.  We been sorta hoping they would take each other off it, but we been waiting two decades for that, now.\"
