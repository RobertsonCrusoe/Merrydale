TODD'S SETUP (27 Mar 2024):

Ah, spring! That splendid time when nature decides to put on a show, with flowers blooming like they're in a competition, birds chirping as if auditioning for the lead role in the dawn chorus, and streams flowing as if they've suddenly remembered they have places to be. It's a season brimming with excitement and bristling with life anew.

But hold on, what's this? Another springtime phenomenon is on the horizon. Ah, yes, dear reader—prepare yourself for the thrilling saga of young love.

Young love, young love, young love. A phenomenon so potent, it could probably be bottled and sold at a high price in certain markets. Innocent, pure, and fleeting. Is there anything more tantalizing?

Let's dive into the heart of this tale of amorous adventure.

* * * * *

Imagine, if you will, the Myth Drannor Embassy, a place where spring doesn't just visit, it takes up residence, encouraged by elven mythals so potent, they could probably make your grandma's rose bush win awards. The place is buzzing with the energy of patrons from far and wide—adventurers seeking quests, travelers in search of stories, and maybe a pilgrim or two, all mashed together in a cocktail of excitement.

In the midst of this, we find our heroes.

Meriodoc Burgundy, known as Merry to friends, has been orchestrating the setup of this bustling hub with the skill of a maestro. Sheets for walls—because who needs privacy when you're young and on an adventure?

Enter Fiona Scarlette, a lady recently unshackled from the dreary chains of servitude, now breathing the free air of a new beginning. She's been working side by side with Merry, not because she's told to, but because it's a joy. Unlike the orders barked by Madame Medea, Merry's requests are more like invitations to a dance.

Exhausted yet exhilarated, Fiona yearns for nothing more than a bath. A secret nook on the third floor beckons, where Merry has conjured a little oasis with a copper tub, hidden within the sheets. No hot water? No problem. Fiona's faith in Sune promises the luxury of a warmth bath, a small miracle for the faithful.

Cue the stealthy tiptoe, the furtive glances, and the dramatic reveal as she pulls back the curtain...

* * * * *

....only to discover Jasper, our second protagonist, already submerged in thought and water, contemplating his whirlwind life transformation from farmboy to fledgling hero. His story isn't one of fearless heroics but rather the exhilarating reality of facing fears head-on. And now, seeking solace in the very secret nook Fiona had set her sights on.

This secret nook, discovered through a comedic ballet of stumbles and head-bongs, now serves as his think tank (quite literally). The lack of heated water? Merely a triviality for someone who can conjure warmth with a flick of magic. Jasper's resourcefulness shines, a beacon of hope and heated baths.

* * * * *

And so, dear reader, we find our characters at a crossroads, brought together by fate and a shared need for reflection (and perhaps, a bath). Fiona, radiant and reborn from the ashes of servitude, stands on the brink of a new chapter. Jasper, a humble hero in the making, wrestles with the weight of his new-found fame.

What will happen when these two worlds collide in the confines of a clandestine copper tub?

Let's leave it to fate, or rather, a roll of the dice.

Roll a charisma check.

GUS'S RESPONSE (29 Mar 2024):

Jasper sits contentedly in the bath.  The stack of papers Jeska had given him to read lie strewn about and forgotten beside the tub; except for the one he's fashioned into a small boat for Mortimer.  The boat bops precariously along on the water forecasting imminent disaster; while the squirrel familiar scowls at the young wizard from the window sill, safe from aquatic danger.

The curtains gently part, and the very naked Fiona looks inside.  At the sight of the young woman Mortimer squeals and leaps out the window.

"What…?! Where are you going?" Jasper calls out to the squirrel, still oblivious of the girl's presence.

As he reaches down to grab another sheet of paper to fashion into a sailor's hat, Jasper's eyes finally notice the pale skin of Fiona, half-hidden by the sheer sheets.

"Yargh!!!" He leaps out of the bathtub in great shock and surprise, landing heavily on the papers, many of them sticking to his own naked body.  He awkwardly tries to get to his feet, trying his best to avert his eyes away from Fiona, stammering nonsensically all the while.

"Well, yes!  I mean… err… no!  That's… sorry?  Sorry!  Err… NO! I mean… goodbye!"

And with that, holding some of the wet parchments to afford him some cover of modesty over his genitals, the wizard squeezes past Fiona and runs out the door.

Screams of surprise can be heard as the hostel guests are treated to the sight of a naked Jasper running through the halls.

"I'm sorry!  Err… the thing is… well… I don't remember which one's my room! And well…. I left all my clothes behind…"
