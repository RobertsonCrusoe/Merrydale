Rediscovered in 1480 by the White Cloaks, this gate is found in the depths of Gorimdun Mine.  The original passage and entrance to the surface remains unfound.  It is clear that in the 14th century, the Zhentarim tunnelled through to huge hall containing this gate.

Presently, the gate has been retaken, and is always guarded by a platoon of Silver Guard.  Some of the finest crafters of the Tethyamar are working on restoring the friezes on the walls of the hall and the bronze inlay of the doors themselves.

 
The grand doors are 30' high, solid stone with decorative bronze inlay.  They can only be opened from the inside, and to date, the secret of how to open the doors (and how they were opened in the first place, after re-discovery) has been kept secure.
