This is the longsword of Ardelusk Whitegate.  This magical blade was carried by Ardelusk for most of his career, though when and where he got it remains a mystery.  The blade was taken from the tomb of Ardelusk, the 'White Saint' in 1479, and given to the 'Pilgrim', Banitt Harbaugh to carry out the will of Amaunator.

During the climactic Battle at Liberty Hill, in 1480, Banitt carried the blade into battle and faced off against the villain known as Belladonna Liberty, who wielded the Black Hand of Gaebril Tremalkin.

Of course, Banitt and the White Cloaks were victorious.

After the battle, Banitt took the position of Magistrate of Liberty Hill.  He kept the blade, and now has it on display in the Cathedral of Liberty hill, where it draws many pilgrims yearly.
