===Excerpt from Lord's Auction, 1481 DR===

This man is well known to many of the people in the Hall. He does about one third of the evaluations and histories of artefacts that are brought to their tower. He is genuinely excited about his craft and the objects he represents. His specialty is pre-plague mundane objects, of which his knowledge seems inexhaustible. He will be auctioning off all of these items, which accounts for nearly half of the collection today.
