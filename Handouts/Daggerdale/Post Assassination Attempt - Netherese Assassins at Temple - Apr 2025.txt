This is a nice narrative. Couple of thoughts from me:

But he didn't stop him and at least Ambershield is decent and not a total nutcase like Whitegate and Invictus, et al.

Jasper is equally frustrated at finding nothing here. I don't know what we were going to find, but finding nothing was not it.

• regarding the loot: we can't sell the netherese kit, and we can't use it either, so fuck it.

Once he's learned what he can he's happy to hand it over to whoever wants to use it. Aside from that, he has no interest or use in any of that gear.

Except IF the netherese gear has special properties, he can take some to study to see how that works and if he can apply that to other weapons or gear to help us fight off the shadows.
