A manuscript penned by an unknown hand discovered tucked into a list of invoices pertaining to the purchase of Phaulkonmere. 
------

Harvestide 1371, the Year of the Unstrung Harp.  Dark days. 

:It was then that the Zhent Soltan, known as Jafar, teetered on the razor's edge of damnation, between light and shadow. 

:It was then that good Tomas Quoyle was unwritten from the Book of Life, his soul devoured by Evil. 

:It was then that the elf known as Esperel betrayed his only friends and withdrew to the alleyways of his twisted mind. 

:It was then that young Garth Shanks lost his faith in men, and saw the Company of the Red Herring broken. 

:It was then that a desperate rogue named Dorian killed the elf Gilthanas, blinded by hunger and vengeance. 

:It was then that Hob Farwood was taken by Malkor the Mad, and sold east, to the slave mines of the Zhentarim. 

:It was then that the child-mage Beljuril saw the beholder staves stolen, wrested from Azuth's stewardship. 

:It was then that Rychel the ranger was cast in stone, his resolve made brittle, his heart turned heavy. 
:Blood flowed thickly, and clotted in city streets, villas and sewers ... 
:And grim Bane grew fat in the blasphemous womb of his grim child, Iyachtu Xvim... 
:And the brood of Vhaeraun withdrew carefully to the Shadow, waiting... 
:And wolf-children of a green-eyed god stalked Waterdeep... 
:And Those Who Harp were hunted and slaughtered... 
:And the Dalelands were sundered by war... 
:And devils walked the earth... 
:And a hard winter came.
