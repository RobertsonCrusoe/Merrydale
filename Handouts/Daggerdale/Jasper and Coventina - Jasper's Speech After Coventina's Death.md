Jasper and Coventina - Jasper's Speech After Coventina's Death
by Gus Miranda (as Jasper Hawkford)
Email thread: "Jasper and Coventina", 13 May 2025

An impassioned in-character speech by Jasper Hawkford, defending the memory of Coventina Cassimar after her gruesome death at Loki Mourvanesh's trap. Jasper pushes back against the party's (and Daggerdale's) tendency to blame the victims of the war rather than the perpetrators, and vows to avenge every last one of them.

---

Jasper speaks, his voice clear, free of the hesitation and tics that it often carries. It trembles in what you can tell is anger:

"She wasn't the evil Sharran you think she was. Yes, she was rich, from a fine family and beautiful, but she was also sad, scared and all alone in this world. Loki took advantage of her, abused her and killed her. If you want to blame the dead victims of this war instead of the real evil bastards who are doing the killing, you go right ahead and do that. They have no one to speak up and defend them, so you go right ahead and blame them.

Poppy, Lily, Lilac and Alaventa are the victims of Palus, and yet the "tragedy" of this ruin is laid at their feet. No one defends them or fights for them. They're just some evil that haunts this place and stand in the way of Daggerdale's progress. And we're here to make things right by purging them with Amaunator's blessed holy light of righteousness, so we can all grow fat and old as wealthy landlords, right?

I came to Daggerdale to fight for the victims of this war, not to play house in a ruin. And there, right there in mutilated pieces, lies another victim of this war.

So yes, she was a scared girl who turned to Shar, and yes, she was a spy for Loki, and you can accuse her of any other crimes you want - she can't defend herself now, so go ahead - and yet, after what we just saw, you expected her to fight off Loki's influence and win? You think she ever stood a chance? This is how you judge her?

I'm not absolving her of her crimes, but there she is: dead. You don't think she paid a high enough price? What more do you want? Should I animate her corpse for you so you can stick your weapon through her flesh and feel your own justice done?

You think me just a lovesick yokel. Go right ahead and think that, I don't care. But I will see her, like all the other victims, given the proper respect. I will avenge them all. Every last one.

And if you think that trap that quartered Coventina was gruesome, I guarantee you, Loki Mourvanesh will get no such gentle mercy from me."

---

Key lore points:
- Coventina Cassimar was killed by a trap set by Loki Mourvanesh — quartered
- Jasper defends her as a victim rather than a villain, despite her being a spy for Loki and having turned to Shar
- Jasper names Poppy, Lily, Lilac, and Alaventa as victims of Palus whose hauntings are blamed on them rather than their killer
- Jasper declares his mission: to fight for the victims of the war, not to become a landlord
- Jasper vows no mercy for Loki Mourvanesh
