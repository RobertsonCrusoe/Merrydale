Aegus Perigrinus;

I am sending you Troia, Ganolen Thendolain, and Alen Reks to assist you in your research.  It is no longer safe for so few of you to go about in the Dale. Something terrible is going to happen.  I can always see it now, just a little beyond the veil...just a little.

Please be careful.

Stay out of Quick Axes.  That room that Alystair has secured is no longer safe.

I will tell you something in confidence; my grandfather has sold all the contents of Loedegan Close, our estate in town.  He and my great uncle think things are going poorly in the west, and they are trying to raise as much capital for soldiers and supplies as they can.  They have discussed selling the Close, stating it is a luxury that we no longer need.  It is troubling to see them worrying about things in the finest of details.  I have never been privy to this, and I am forced to wonder if it is the times or just my time.

I have enclosed a key to the Close with this letter, and have sent a letter to my cousin, Hasting Blackmane, telling her to expect you.  She alone now dwells in that cheerless place, but I have given her orders to make the place hospitable for your team.  Know this; she is the daughter of Mayor Blackmane, of Tesh Valley, but they are estranged, and she is unlikely to pay you deference for any of your qualities.

Troia and the others will meet you at the Close as soon as you can get there.

There is one other thing, and I presently doubt myself for saying it, but it must be said; I think Iolus is right about Magistrate Hoetmer; they are keeping him tucked away, and do not really know, and do not really use, his extraordinary talents.  We should start to consider having him relocated.  Do not act rashly, but rather, think on it, and check in on him often.  I do not yet fully understand why he is tied so closely to us, but he is.

Please be careful.

Yours in Faith and unshakable friendship,
— Lumina}}
