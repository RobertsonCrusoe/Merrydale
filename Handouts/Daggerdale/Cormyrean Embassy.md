The Cormyreans have had no end of trouble with the expanding empire of Netheril, and as such have sent a small embassy to the Castle to keep track of their enemy's movements in the east.  The Embassy has a small detachment of purple knights, and a small party of adventurers that take bounties, but also gather intelligence in the field.  They lease the largest tower on the outer wall for 100 gold/month, and Lord Whitegate is happy to have them; they have been able defenders of the Castle who have proven their value more than once in the 4 years that they  have been here.
