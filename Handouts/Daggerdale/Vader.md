Magistrate Vader enters the story in the summer of 1371. He was working for Thagdul at the time, and bailed out the Company of the Red Herrings on more than one occasion. By 1373, he was working for Company Aldebarran (the latest incarnation of Drurginz), but since the scouring of Irlingstar Villa, he has vanished.  Rychel found out that he asked around about the company ledger for half a day, and is now likely aware that it is in Garth’s possession, and that he is compromised.  It is likely that Vader left the City, or is hiding out.

*Status: His whereabouts are presently unknown.
