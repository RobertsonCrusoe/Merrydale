At about three bells on the morning of Greengrass, Banitt was summoned to the tomb of Ardelusk Whitegate. He was escorted there by Lumina and four waiting Knights of the Order of the Lambent Shield.

In the tomb, he is surprised to see that Argent and Luminous Whitegate are already there, along with another four knights, who are just removing the great stone lid from the sarcophagus of Ardelusk Whitegate as he comes in. As the stale air rushes from the old tomb, and strikes Banitt, he falls to his knees and starts to pray. The others just watch and wait for him to finish. Finally, Argent, the Zenith Lord of the dominating faith of this region puts a hand on the Pilgrim's shoulder, and asks him to rise.

\"We have work for you, Pilgrim\" Argent says. \"When you came here, you were the first Pilgrim to come to the tomb of our forefather. We thought you merely an eccentric, if flawlessly devout man. This morning, this tomb will be flooded with pilgrims from across the realms; the first of thousands that we expect this season. We do not know if saints draw out pilgrims, of if pilgrims draw out saints, but that doesn't matter. You are here, and he\" motioning to the open tomb of Ardelusk \"is here too.\"

Argent then looks to his older brother, Luminous, the Lord of Castle Radiance. Luminous gives a short nod. Argent continues.

\"We are living in remarkable times, Pilgrim. Remarkable and dangerous. Just as your arrival has heralded the beatification of our ancestor, so too is there a Black Saint in our midst. We have heard rumours of the Black Hand of Gaebril Tremalkin all winter. Many are calling him 'Saint Gaebril'. We have our own saint, now. A White Saint to counter the Black, and if there are relics of that most unholy villain at large working against us, we must also battle with relics.\"

With this, Argent reaches into the tomb and draws out the glimmering blade of his ancestor. The longsword illuminates the room with a light like early morning. Banitt drops to his knees again, and the Zenith Lord approaches him with the blade, and touches both his shoulders with it.

\"I anoint you as Guardian of the Tomb, Banitt Harbaugh, First Pilgrim, and I charge you with the ferreting out of the Black Saint, and the destruction of his followers and allies. If you will take this title and this charge, you may likewise take this blade to help you in your duties.\"

Banitt drops to his knees and holds out his hands to receive the blade.

“I accept, and shall strive to be worthy of such a gift.”

He lowers his head in prayer.
