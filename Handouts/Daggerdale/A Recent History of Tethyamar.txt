A book discovered in the main tent of Tezster Gellinson by Durandal.  

It was written by Valusian Deep Anvil, and was finished in Ches, 1481 DR, and the ink was barely dry when Durandal found it.

The book is mostly a collection of stories and songs that Valusian and his kin remembered, and it was given as a gift to Tezster for his dedication to the search for Tethyamar.
