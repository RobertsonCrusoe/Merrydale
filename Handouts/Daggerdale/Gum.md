Gum was a hapless rank-and-file member of a group of would-be Company Aldebarran assassins that ambushed the Heroes at Roark's Tavern at midnight on Greengrass, 1373. 

Gum was wearing black half-plate armour under a disguise of ragged clothing, and carried masterwork weapons. His interrogation by Garth revealed that he had been hired by Clavius at the rate of 10gp per week (he carried 20gp on his person), and was based out of Dockward in Waterdeep. Clavius' organization was comprised of multiple cells of 16 men, with no contact between the various cells, but individual 'gang colours' that were recognizable to all cells.

Gum was turned over to Magistrate Richter in Waterdeep upon the Heroes' return on 1 Mirtul, 1373.

*Status: Presumably rotting in a cell somewhere.
