Beliard's Favourite Things - A Song from Castle Radiance
by Stephen Robertson (as Beliard Kathklan Emmarask)
Email thread: "Journal of Brother Vessuvian - Part 1", 27-29 Sep 2024

In the email thread discussing the first installment of Brother Vessuvian's journal, Stephen composed this song in character as Beliard Emmarask writing from Castle Radiance, summarising the events of the Thistle Ridge Campaign to the tune of "My Favourite Things" from The Sound of Music. Gus confessed he was singing it to the tune of "The Wreck of the Edmund Fitzgerald" instead.

---

BELIARD'S FAVOURITE THINGS

Sworn band of strangers who take up the gauntlet
Charity Hills lends a hand as their sponsor,
Alliances forged with complex legal strings,
These are a few of our favourite things!

Bawdy spring revels, new-budding romances,
Brawls with the locals and nights filled with dances,
Political currents with intrigue galore,
Secrets and scandals behind every door!

White Cloaks defeated, a rivalry strong,
Redjacks so brutal, the fights last so long,
Free Defense falters and Chrysalis wins,
These are a few of our favourite things!

When the curse bites,
When the witch stings,
When we're feeling sad,
We simply remember our favourite things,
And then we don't feel so bad!

Seances with spirits and grand mayor's balls,
Hinterland battles with wasps ten feet tall,
Fungus-fey creatures and goblin-filled raids,
These are a few of the quests we have made!

Purple worm sieges and gargoyle incursions,
Netheril's plots and Loki's diversions,
Melegaunt's schemes and the chaos he brings,
These are a few of our favourite things!

When the worm strikes,
When the wasp stings,
When we're feeling mad,
We simply remember our favourite things,
And then we don't feel so bad!

---

Beliard concludes: "It's still no 'The Munk!'"
