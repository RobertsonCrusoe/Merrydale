DDC – 1481 – 15: Issue of 'The Loyalist' 
21 Marpenoth, 1481 
GFD Notes: 
I would love to have spent more time in Charity. This place is an anomaly in Daggerdale, as it is a miniature replica of the great city of Westgate. It has its own weekly publication which is distributed to all 'Middlemen' and 'Landmen' of the town (these being the middle and upper-class men). 

This particular broadsheet can still be found plastered on the walls of some of the taverns (they have more than you would find in other Daggerdale settlements) of the town and are not hard to come by.
