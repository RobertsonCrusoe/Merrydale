TODD (19 Dec 2023):

Hey Dixon. I'm sending you an email because I'm starting to get overly excited about the DnD. I have been thinking about a bunch of things we can do, but really, Lucan is the man who should be coming up with the schemes. I just wanted to give you some points.

1. There is a lot of information Blacky has hidden in the PHP he made us. Giving that a read, perhaps with a highlighter, would be a great basis for an investigation.

2. In particular, there is tax revenue information at 4,250gp per year + 2,200gp for the church. That can shed some light on the value of the Ferguson deal.

3. As discussed on Sunday, we will need some plan to raise money. I think selling bonds or some other type of fundraising would be great. This again seems to be right in Lucan's wheelhouse.

4. Honestly, incorporating a city and starting it is something I'm not entirely sure about, but I believe it will be a big part of the story.

Also, just looking at your character:

5. I think you should strongly consider some sort of crafting skill. Blacky has said crafting will be part of the campaign, and you are an Int-based character. Wouldn't it be cool if you were good at forgery but used that skill more broadly to create the bonds we need to raise money for Thistle Ridge? Perhaps your skill could trickle into contracts in such a way that we can get more out of deals with a skill role? Just a thought. Maybe there are other useful crafting skills that would be a fit for your characters.

6. You should read through the various charisma skills and see if you might want to take one. Each of those skills has a list of actions that tell you what they can do. For example, information gathering is done via diplomacy in PF2e. Mari has good diplomacy, and your class should allow you to gather information via investigation. Still, there might be another social skill that will really help your character in role-playing situations.

Anyway, these are just some thoughts. I don't want to come across as telling you how to run your character. I just figured it might be nice to throw Int-based stuff your way so that I can concentrate on Wis/Cha-based stuff with Mari.
