A Call for Bravery, Endurance and Honour! 
Alturiak 30, 1483 DR. 

By Order of the Governors' Assembly, at its Seventh Sitting, Alturiak 21-29, 1483 DR, the establishment of the Thistle Ridge Reclamation and the creation of an Adjudicating Committee with authorization to oversee the Reclamation is hereby announced to all citizens of Daggerdale, and all her allies notified forthwith. 

It is the will of the Assembly that a Company of suitable capability and virtue be Chartered to Reclaim the settlement known as Thistle Ridge Farm, to secure its walls and other properties, to hold and defend those properties from enemies current and future, and to see to the growth and prosperity of that settlement. 

The Adjudicating Committee, which is composed of i) Bishop Whitegate, ii) High Cultivar Oakdale, iii) Mayor Cassimar, iv) General Thunderstorm and v) Ambassador Whitmore (Charity), will receive applications from organizations with a Charter Militant, and those seeking a Temporary Charter Militant for the purposes of this endeavour. They will select the five most suitable Companies and pit them in competition to test all the skills needed to carry out the Reclamation. 

The successful team will be given a Temporary Settlement Charter, which will give them authority for the following; 
 To clear all hostile creatures from Thistle Ridge Farm and its surroundings. 

 Establish the infrastructure and means to host a company of Sunblades in accordance with the Provisions for the Common Defense as laid out in the Daggerdale Concordat. 

 Establish the infrastructure and means to develop and maintain a suitable militia in accordance with the Provisions for the Common Defense as laid out in the Daggerdale Concordat. 

If the holders of the Temporary Settlement Charter accomplish these goals by the Feast of the Moon, 1485 DR, they will be granted a Permanent Settlement Charter, which will enshrine Thistle Ridge Farm as a member of the Daggerdale Concordat, complete with a seat on the Governors' Assembly. 

Application to the Competition can be submitted to Clerk Marlee Willow in Governors' Hall. 

The successful company will be expected to manage nearly all the affairs of the Reclamation and growth of the Farm under their own strength of arms, with their own coin and personnel. The Adjudicating Committee is authorized to lend the following support, which it will fully fund until the Feast of the Moon, 1485 DR; 

 One Platoon of Sunblades, to be formed apurpose, to be Recommissioned as 7th Sun Blade Bright Spears, 4th Platoon, under the command of Lt. Forge Farmountain and such junior officers as he sees fit. 

 One Notary to attend the Farm at such time as security allows and the disposition of assets between the Company and other Parties requires. 

 10 minutes of Standing at the Governors' Assembly for one Representative from the Company every Sitting. 

For clarity, the Temporary Settlement Charter shall also give the following Powers and Jurisdictions to the Company within the Farm and Surroundings, so long as they don't contravene the Daggerdale Concordat in any way: 

 To own the entirety of the Farm and Surroundings and proclaim or document such ownership in a ledger that can be shared with the Yellow Abbey. 

 To enter into trade agreements with neighbours and principled merchants that conduct regular business in the dale in accordance with the Daggerdale Concordat. 

 To establish, if needed or desired, institutions, laws and regulations for governance, education, trade standards, security, and all other matters normally administered by eldermen and other such bodies in Farms in the Dale. 

Congratulations, Adventurers!
