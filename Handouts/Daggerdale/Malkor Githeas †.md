A [[Zhentarim]] diviner based in Neverwinter, and follower of Shar.

The man had long curly red hair, blue eyes and a face only a mother could love. His deep-set eyes are rimmed by dark bags, and are themselves somewhat reddish. He seems sturdy of build, and is dressed in red breeches and chemise, with a grey cloak, broad hat and gloves.
