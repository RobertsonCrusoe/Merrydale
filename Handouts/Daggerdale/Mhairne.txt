In 1371, Mhairne was a 15 year-old orphan girl who was Thagdul’s servant at his home in Trades Ward. That house is currently empty.

In 1373, the girl found employment with Company Aldebarran, but was kidnapped by Quevin Dalael as a gambit during the negotiation of territorial control in Waterdeep with the director of Aldebarran, Kendal Thorne.
