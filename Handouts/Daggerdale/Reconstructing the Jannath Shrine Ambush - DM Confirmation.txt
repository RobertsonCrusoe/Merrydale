Reconstructing the Jannath Shrine Ambush — DM Confirmation
Stephen Robertson (as Lucan) and Les Blackwell (DM)

Stephen reconstructs from memory the sequence of events that led the party into the ambush at the Jannath Shrine. Les confirms the reconstruction is accurate and reveals a key detail about Coventina's eavesdropping: she did not name who she overheard the second time. A short but lore-significant exchange for establishing the chain of events and identifying Coventina's role.

---

STEPHEN (1 Jun 2025):

Hey Blacky

Todd and I were talking Daggerdale last night and trying to remember the sequence of events that led us into the ambush at the Jannath Shrine. Can you check over this reconstruction and tell me if I've got anything out of order? I asked Gus but he couldn't remember who Coventina said she overheard talking about Dretch.

Here's what I remember:

1. Jasper received a letter from Coventina about the overheard conversation among Whitmore, Cassimar, and Oakdale.
2. We speak to the group, with Blaze Whitegate present, I think.
3. Privately, Lucan lets slip to Oakdale that the Alavandors and Clarandals weren't totally on the up and up, which prompted her to stop backing Invictus's plan to raze the shrine.
4. Invictus later pays us a visit at the Embassy.
5. We agree to sanctify the temple, but not to destroy it. He was fine with that. He gave us the ritual paraphernalia for the sanctification ritual.
6. Jasper met with Coventina at the Hooded Lantern.
7. Coventina says she overheard... Invictus? talking to who? ... with the takeaway that Alystair Dretch is gunning for the temple to retrieve an artefact, and that we have to leave RIGHT NOW to stop him.
8. Lucan tries to verify the Dretch story with Administrator Reks, who dodges the question.
9. As he is leaving, Lucan encounters Alexandus Ward, who says that Dretch is on his way to Tethyamar.
10. We leave anyway, grab Ambershields on the way, and then walk into an ambush.

Does that sound right to you?


LES (2 Jun 2025):

Yep that's it exactly.


STEPHEN (2 Jun 2025):

LES (2 Jun 2025):

Just those three you mentioned the first time she was dropping eaves. She didn't say who she overheard the second time.

KEY LORE POINTS:

- The full sequence of events leading to the Jannath Shrine ambush is confirmed by the DM as accurate
- Coventina's first eavesdropping: she overheard Whitmore, Cassimar, and Oakdale
- Coventina's second eavesdropping (the urgent "Dretch is coming" intelligence): she did NOT say who she overheard — this is suspicious and potentially significant
- Lucan's disclosure to Oakdale about the Alavandors/Clarandals changed her position on razing the shrine
- Administrator Reks dodged Lucan's question about Dretch
- Alexandus Ward provided potentially misleading intelligence (Dretch heading to Tethyamar, not the shrine)
- Les's "You could ask her..." implies Coventina may have more to reveal — or that the question itself would be a productive in-game move
