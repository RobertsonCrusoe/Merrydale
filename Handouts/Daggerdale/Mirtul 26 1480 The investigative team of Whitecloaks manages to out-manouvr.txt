Mirtul 26, 1480: The investigative team of Whitecloaks manages to out-manouvre the Soul of Corruption, only to discover the horrible truth about Liberty Hill. The entire farm is cloaked in illusion, under the Ritual of Obfuscation; this ritual was also made possible by the powerful influence of the Imperiosus Orbis. The town and the people appeared to be normal, even though in truth the streets were littered with bodies and debris, and the pale faces and empty black eyes that looked down at them from a hundred windows were likewise masked in a visage of innocence. 
The White Cloaks were being led into a trap by Lt. Hardigan and Priestess/Mayor 
Goering. Unaware of the horrifying truth of the place, they followed the evil Sharrans, and would have been led directly to their deaths, if not for the intervention of Phin. Phin was following the White Cloaks in his Aetherial Dream, and the illusory magic of that terrible ritual did not extend to the Aether. He called out to them, and Alistair and Troia heard him, before they were led into the keep, where a score of undead and shadow-kind assassins waited. Once Alistair and Troia saw through the illusion, a desperate battle and flight took place. The entire town mobilized against the five White Cloaks, and they barely escaped with their lives. Tuugan will not even recount the tale to his friends; the man's character has been forever changed by this experience.
