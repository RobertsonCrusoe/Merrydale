Remainder of Ches 20 - Evening Activities and Cherry Business
by Les Blackwell (DM) and Scott Mclaren (Jeska)
Email thread: "Remainder of Ches 20", 12 Mar 2024

A short gameplay thread covering the evening of 20 Ches 1483 DR, after the first day of the Thistle Ridge Competition. Les asks what the party wants to do; Scott outlines Jeska's actions; Les provides DM responses including the Garadin Gorehammer hearing invitation and the Elvish Cherry trade deal.

---

LES (DM):

Guys;

Let me know what else you want to accomplish for the remainder of Ches 20. Jasper is pretty much done. The rest of you have from 6 PM onward to act if you wish.

---

SCOTT (JESKA):

Jeska stays at the Embassy. She asks Lucan to work with Merry and sell off 500gp worth of Elvish goods (needs money to support the hostel). She is also waiting for Lucan to bring back information regarding Semk.

That is until she hears of the Captains Meeting.. which she will attend (assuming she is invited).

---

LES (DM):

Yep, I was getting to that. A runner does come to the Embassy with an invitation to attend a hearing in the morning at Governors' Hall regarding Garadin Gorehammer and what happened to Bree. Only the captains of each team are invited.

More to come...

---

LES (DM):

Jeska and Merry negotiate an agreement with the bakery down the street to buy as many of the cherries as they can every morning. They can be harvested for the next 4-5 months, and should yield 7-10 sp/day in product (this is really lucrative for such a small harvest, but they are a delicacy, especially this time of year). The bakery is making what it calls Chrysalis Cakes (small cakes with Elvish Cherries), which are likely to be popular soon...

Merry has cautioned against selling anything substantial from the Embassy. He seems a little afraid of getting on the wrong side of the Miratars. "I've had a good living and provided dutiful service for many years here, Madame Elf, and I hope to continue that. I'm at your service, and will do anything instructed, but I think Lady Miratar will be upset to hear that we've sold the furniture..."
