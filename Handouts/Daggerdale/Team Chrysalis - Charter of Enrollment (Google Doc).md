# **Charter of Enrollment**

Team “Chrysalis”

Re: Competition for & Reclamation of Thistle Ridge

Sponsor and Governess: Barrister Jeska Monarch  
4 Team members to be selected

Accommodations:  
Room and Board will be provided at the Myth Drannor Embassy in Dagger Falls

Chrysalis Coffers:  
All amassed wealth will be split into 8 shares  
Each member will receive 1 share at each disbursement  
1 member to receive 1 additional share as voted by members  
The remaining shares will be added to Chrysalis' coffers  
Rare items will be distributed at the discretion of the Governess

Member Roles:  
Each member will be assigned to 1 of the following duties  
**Quarter Master**  
*(Travel, Mundane gear, Embassy upkeep, non-military/mercantile staff)*  
***Master Scribe***  
*(Records, Information gathering, networking)*  
**Mercantile Master**  
*(Trade, Sales, Procurement, Fencing, Mercantile staff)*  
**Master at Arms**  
*(Frontline leader, military staff, defenses)*

Term of Contract:  
1 Year or Successful Reclamation of Thistle Ridge
