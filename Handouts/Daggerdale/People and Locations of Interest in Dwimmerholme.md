==Locations and People of Interest in Dwimmerholme==


===Locations===

*The Old Stones – ½ mile south east of the village, just inside the forest at the edge of the fields.  A place of Chauntean worship
*The Cathedral – 60’ x 100’ stone building; centre of town, extending to eastern wall (access from eastern edge of square)
*The Castle – 150’ x 150’ stone building, 2 stories, 3 story towers at northern edge which extends to the northern  wall (access from the northern edge of the square)
*The Amber Hart Inn – See map details; access from western edge of the square
*The Market – barn-like building 60’ x 100’ on the southern edge of the square; many businesses inside
*Bronze Hammers – small smithy
*Daily Bread – large bakery operated by part of the Weaver family
*Butcher’s – busy, clean, unremarkable butcher run by a family with the same name.
*Barns – western wall is lined with 3 large barns where livestock are brought in most nights.

===People===

*Mayor Darius Winters – Retired veteran of the 4th Company Sun Blades – Foresters.  Young, capable.
*Magistrate Renfrew – new, young magistrate.
*Andrea Stuard – proprietor of the Amber Hart Inn
*Cultivar Marissa Dale – priestess of Chauntea, and aunt to some missing villagers
*Lt. Kesh Oakdale – Lt of the local platoon of Sun Blades.  Young, capable, wracked with guilt/grief.
*Roger Dunland – Son of the last mayor, and one of the ring leaders of a failed cult of Shar.  Reformed but now solitary.}}
