Pauldron is Knight Commander of one of 3 Rides in Master Knight Derfel's Brotherhood.  His Ride is smaller than most, having the least number of Supplicants in the entire Chapter.  This is because he is not known to be the most rigid adherent to the rules and orders of the organization.  However, this makes him and his Ride suitable for tasks that require flexibility and impromptu planning.

He is presently stationed in the Dagger Hills at the Shadow Dam.
