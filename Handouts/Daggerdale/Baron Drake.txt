# Baron Drake †
# Source: The Sieges Perilous wiki (backup dated 2010-02-21)

Baron Drake was a cambion demon and follower of Iyachtu Xvim. He led a cult in Waterdeep that was able to manifest the Baneson in the sewers of Waterdeep, and later let the Brothers of the Black Fist in Dagger Falls.

• Status: Slain by Dorian and Maturin during the assault on Brotherhood Tower (the temple of Iyachtu Xvim) in Dagger Falls
