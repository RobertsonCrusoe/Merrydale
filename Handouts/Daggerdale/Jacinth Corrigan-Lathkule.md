Jacinth Corrigan-Lathkule One of the victims of Hagan's rampage that uncovered the Secret Order of Witches. She married and divorced a commoner (who has since died). The progeny of this union, Adonai Lathkule was rescued from the slave pens of the Dark Market by the Company of the Red Herring, along with Loadin and Sarahn Raventree.
