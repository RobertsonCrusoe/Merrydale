The Council of Silver is a collection of 12 dwarven elders that are the ruling body in Tethyamar. Given the nature of the conflict between the two Gellinson brothers over who should be King in Tethyamar, the Council has taken command of the city, such as it is, until such time as one brother or the other manages to seat himself on the throne.  

There were 14 major clans of Shield Dwarves in their deep history. The Fourteenth are the traitorous Duergar that now hold the Southern Burroughs against the forces of Tezster Gellinson. The remaining 13 have survived the long dwindling of their people, but all have sent representatives to Tethyamar to aid in it's recovery.    

The Council itself does not have a sitting member from Clan Iron House, as the elders fo that clan are the brothers that have caused so much trouble. The remaining 12 clans are all represented.  

The Council has a standing army called the Silver Guard which is used to hold ground in Tethyamar.

The Council and their guard have taken on the responsability of securing the Central Burroughs of Tethyamar, though this is mostly a slow and defensive endeavour for them.
