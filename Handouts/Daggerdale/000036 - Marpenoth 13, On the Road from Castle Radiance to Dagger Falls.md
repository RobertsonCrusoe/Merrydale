[2012-07-04 02:41:41] DM (OP)
PC's Present: Beliard and Zancanelli

The morning is cool and clear; about as fine a morning as you could ask for in the autumn up in the mountains.  The pair of you are saddled and ready to go by about 10 bells; a little behind schedule as far as Zancanelli is concerned, but right on schedule for the diplomat.

What you know about each other is a bit limited;

Beliard, "Mr. Secretary" as he is called by his boss, Ambassador Tunbridge, is a gregarious man of the clever variety that makes quiet men uncomfortable.

Zancanelli, a tight-lipped Sembian attache to the Embassy is the sort of quiet man that makes greagarious, clever men uncomfortable.  He has been with the Embassy for nearly a year, and does jobs for the Embassy that cannot be done by someone wearing a purple tabbard. 

Today he is on one of those missions.  Zancanelli has been assigned to Beliard as his 'attendant', though the job description for this role has been left purposefully vague.  In fact, even the remuneration for this role hasn't been decided yet.

It is a journey of 2 days for two fast riders from the Castle to the Falls.  3 for slow riders.

The sun is shining, the sky is clear...
