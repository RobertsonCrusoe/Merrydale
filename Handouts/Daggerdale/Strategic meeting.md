The first thing that happens every morning, a half hour before sunrise, is the morning service called Matins; this begins with the ringing of the bells in the cathedral, and the migration of many of the faithful of Amuanator to the Fortress Cathedral of the Mount.  Here, hundreds of the castle inhabitants witness the rising of the sun, and listen to the morning prayer, which is usually delivered by Zenith Lord Argent Whitegate.

After this prayer, Lord Whitegate convenes his council in the war room in Madigan's Hall.  This meeting is usually attended by the following;

*Lord Luminous Whitegate
*Zenith Lord Argent Whitegate
*General Thunderstorm – highest ranking commander in the Sun Blades
*Captain Rainyerd – Captain of the 2nd Company Sun Blades; the 'Mountaineers’
*1-5 Lieutenants from the Sun Blade platoons that are in-castle
*1-5 Platoon chaplains
*1-3 Master Knights from the Order of the Lambent Shield, depending on how many are in-castle
*3-9 Knight-Commanders from the Order, accompanying their Master Knight
*1-3 Brotherhood Chaplains
*Magistrate Borus Helmhold – Magistrate of Castle Radiance
*0-5 adventuring company captains

==The War Room==

This is a large room at the back of Madigan’s Hall which is like a completely circular amphitheatre, 80’ in diameter.  One enters the room from one of 4 doors which are aligned with the points of the compass.  There are 4 steep sets of steps leading down from the doors to the floor of the room, and shooting off from each side of the stairs are three tiers for spectators.  The tiers each run the circumference of the room, and have round wooden tables which follow the arc of their tier.  The tables are deep enough for the participants of the strategic meeting to place their maps upon for updating.  There are no chairs; all meeting participants are forced to stand.

In the middle of the room is a huge, 10’ diameter circular table on which a huge map of the area is clearly displayed.  The ceiling of the room is a perfect spherical vault with a glowing iris at its zenith.  This light illuminates the floor very well, but leaves the spectator tiers in partial shadow.

==Proceedings==

The strategic meeting begins with a quick prayer from Argent Whitegate.  Argent then takes a place on the first tier behind his desk, and Lord Whitegate then takes command of the meeting.  He will introduce any new faces, or ask for introductions, and then move along hastily afterwards.

The main business of these meetings is update the master map with intelligence from the men and women attending the meeting.  The locations of recent sightings or encounters with the Netherese, orcs, bandits and other monsters or enemies are all discussed and marked on the map.

Towards the end of the meeting (which can go anywhere from 10 minutes to 2 hours), Lord Whitegate will ask for speculations about possible threats that are implied by the data, which might be significant.  This is usually the interesting part of the meetings, and the part where some arguments and lively discussions are prone to erupt.

Finally, new bounties will be announced, and Whitegate asks any bounty seekers to announce their names and and declare any bounties they are on.

The meeting breaks up of a sudden, with the Whitegates making their way hurriedly out of the war room, but many smaller groups tend to form and stay behind, discussing the smaller matters and details of the recent intelligence.
