A woman in her 40’s, and a recent widow.  She is the proprietor of The Amber Hart Inn, and is a gatherer of intelligence for both Alystair and Alen Reks.  There isn’t much that happens in town that she doesn’t hear about.  Especially because her friend and powerful ally, Cultivar Dale, holds court in her taproom.  Andrea is actually a follower of Amaunator, but respects Chauntea and the locals.  She is originally from Cormyr, and her accent and manners make her something of a local paragon.  She is very matriarchal in her dealings with all people, but she is not distant, and she is a good listener.  She knows about the dreams some of the children are having, but she keeps this to herself, letting the Cultivar deal with it.
