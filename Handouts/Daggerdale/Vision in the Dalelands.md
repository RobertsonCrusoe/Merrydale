A dream from the summer of 1371, when Maturin accompanied Oliver, Maevri, Hanibal and Falcon on their  to Waterdeep.

----

On the night before we rode into Voonlar, Maturin dreamed a strange vision. In this dream, he was hanging up on the tree again, in the same mountain pass in the Galenas where he’d been crucified before. This time, however, he was barely able to withstand the pain, and felt his faith waning. He honestly believed that he was to die on the tree this time.

A leper crouched at his feet, tears streaming from his eyes. Maturin tried to free himself to help the leper, but was unable. The leper looked up at him, meeting his eyes.

Suddenly, the leper stands and takes a step back. He grows in proportion until his face is level with the Paladin’s. His piercing blue eyes, filled with sorrow and suffering, look at him straight on.

The leper speaks with a quivering, but commanding voice:
14
:And beginning in the Year of the Unstrung Harp, when the Dark City lies crumbling, and the Ancient Ones rebel, the Lord of Darkness shall rise up in the Heartlands.
:And the Son’s Shadow shall manifest itself in the Jewel by the Water
:And the Father shall rise from his grave. And the Father shall regain his throne. And he will bring blood and ruin to the Cradle.
:And the Father shall be victorious. He shall rule his realm once more.
:And the Son shall become jealous. And the Prince shall become angered.
:And the Son shall overthrow the Father. And the Prince shall duel with the Son.
:And then shall the Lord of Darkness understand.
:And the Lord of Darkness shall repent.
:And the Lord of Darkness shall bring forth the Warrior. And the Warrior shall bring forth the Enduring. And the Enduring shall weep for the Cradle. And the Warrior shall bring forth the Dutiful and the Protector. And the Enduring shall unite the Dutiful and the Protector.
:And together all four shall bring the Even-Handed.
:And then shall the Father fall for the last time. And then shall the Son be banished and then shall the Prince sit upon his rightful throne.
:And so the Lord of Darkness shall find his eternal rest.”

With the last word, Maturin awoke in a cold sweat, shivering. He remembered every word and detail of the dream perfectly. It was something he will never forget. He knew that he had been visited by Ilmater, and his faith was stronger than it had ever been before.
