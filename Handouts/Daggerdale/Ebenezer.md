The progenitor of the  clan of wererats was a servitor of the Umbrusk family who served Gentletooth Umbrusk himself. The Gentletooth Clan maintains that Ebenezer was infected by Gentletooth. The Ebenezer Clan maintains that he was a true-born who chose to help the ancient progenitor of the Gentletooth Clan and House Umbrusk.
