Captain Griswold Horn holds his position on the Waterdeep City Watch, and was on the Ersgul payroll. Had a crew of eight watch that were loyal to him and his agenda. Following the dissipation of Ersgul, Griswold's loyalties were apparently available to the highest bidder, and he made the mistake of allying himself with House Dalael.

*Status: Deceased. Horn was killed during a battle at the shipping offices of the Margaster family in 1372.
