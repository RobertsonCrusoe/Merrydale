Mr. Shanks;

I wish to meet with you tomorrow at 7 bells in the evening, regarding something of great importance to both of us. I apologize that I must be cryptic at this time about the nature of my business, and own that I am ashamed to have to carry out the duties of my Office in such a manner, but there is too much at stake to make mention of it here.

If you would grace me with your presence tomorrow evening outside the West Gate, I shoudl be most appreciative. Please forgive me the imprudence of such short notice, the selection of venue and the withholding of further information. When you come alone tomorrow evening, you will learn why such secrecy is requried.

Yours in Justice,

Court Magistrate Durnum

PS; Please bring this letter with you as proof of your identity, if you could be so kind.}}
