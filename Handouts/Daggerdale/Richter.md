Magistrate Richter was the first corrupt Magistrate to enter the story. Company Dalael employed him in the summer of 1368. As such he had many run-ins with the Warders of Waterdeep (whose significant members still involved in this story include Steele, Rychel, and Gaebril). After the Warders (temporarily) defeated Dalael, Richter behaved himself for a time. However, in the summer of 1370, he once again got involved in the underworld of the City, aiding the Company Ersgul and Yarkul.

Richter has long been suspected by some City Inhabitants of being on the payrolls of a couple Co.’s (first Dalael, then Ersgul). He is heading up the investigation at the Wavesilver Villa, and taking his time about it. He has 100 City Guard there around the clock, accompanied by several Watch Wizards.

Today, Richter is still around, and there can be no doubt that the is still working with the Dalael, especially after trying to take custody of Thorne. Richter always travels with a group of elite Watch, at least 10 strong.
