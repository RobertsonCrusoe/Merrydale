Speculation by Brother Darred and others has it that these orbs are in the basement of Vaer’Lith Katahr, having been unearthed by Curator Loki Mouvranesh.  The Candlekeep Emissary informed Lumina Whitegate that these are powerful artifacts from Ancient Netheril that are used to greatly amplify any sort of magical effect or spell.  To date, the only suspected use of the orbs recently has been to completely blow away the remaining sands of Anauroch around the fortress in which they were hidden for centuries.
