Source: Gmail thread 196091119fcc5bad

Immediately upon hearing Ovee's report Jeska draws Jas and Sanduskin aside.
After a brief chat one of the wizards nods and waves his hand. (Jeska asked
for the spell that increases overland movement speed).
You all hear her say to them... "I'll be back soon".. and she runs out of
the camp heading toward the Tower (I think as fast as a horse.. spd 50...
and she is booking it with no caution taken).

Some few hours later she returns to the Farm and calls you together. With
Calix, Ambersheilds and Boxfray.
"I have been to the Tower and informed the Sunblades of the pending
threats. They have already sent missives to both General Thunderstorm In
Dagger Falls and the 1st Lt. at Gribbons. They have requested from the 1st
Lt. that the 10th company, patrolling the road head out as far as Thistle
Ridge to cover a flank. From Thunderstorm they are requesting a Part of the
18th Reserve company be sent to Greenwood. This would free the FDL to join
the defense of the tower."
She pauses briefly.
"The Sunblades are fairly confident they can get the requested 10th, they
are not so sure about the 18th reserves. Lucan, we need you to write to The
General, and anyone else in Dagger falls we trust to put pressure on him to
give us part of his reserve force. This will likely cost us... *what can we
offer the Thunderstorm as payment?\"*
Her shoulders sag slightly.. it is obvious she is tired from her run.
"The attacks come on the morrow... so we haven't time for much bandying of
words.. we need soldiers moving now."
Jeska *smiles*
"It is my intention that Chrysalis use this attack as cover and head for
Thistle Ridge. We will give our scarves to the FDL, who will use them to
portray us at the Tower in the open so all think we are there."
She looks to each of those gathered in turn.
*"If anyone can suggest an alternate strategy, or advise on other aid we
could call upon please have your say now". *
