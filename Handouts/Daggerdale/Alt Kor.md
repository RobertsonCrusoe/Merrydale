Alt Kor, son of Korak, of Dark Slate Pass, was a short, and short-lived, dwarven priest of an obscure dwarvish god named \"Rakkar\" (or perhaps Rakkur - the records are difficult to interpret). He accompanied those who would later become the Heroes of Neverwinter on their initial investigations into the Zhentarim plot in the North.
