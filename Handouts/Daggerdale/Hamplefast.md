The quartermaster for the Redjacks. Hamplefast appears to be the best at what he does; the logistics of the Redjacks are expertly handled by this man, and they are never short on supplies.  

During the Lord's Auction, 1481 DR, Hamplefast purposefully up-bid many items that the White Cloaks tried to buy, costing them a tremendous amount of gold.
