DDC – 1479 – 1: Report from Alystair Dretch to Cpt. Whitegate 
Wherein he describes the discovery of the Dark Garden, and the coven-leader named Lotus 
GFD Notes: 
In this report, the Avenger Alystair Dretch reports on a mission he ran with Tuugan Swift, wherein they foiled the creation of a powerful cult of Shar at Dwimmerholme farm (if only that was the end of trouble for that poor farm) 

This report as well as DDC – 1480 – 1 were both graciously provided by Administrator Reks. 

We hear of how expertly Alystair manages to gather intelligence and sends it up the proper channels quickly. He is wrong about a couple of details (such as the nexus of the Dark Garden, which turned out to be Liberty Hill, and the fact that Lotus was a Kenku; she was rather a shape-shifting dark druid that favoured the form of a raven as all 3 members of the coven did), but for the most part, he seems to have the entire thing figured out in Marpenoth. It's hard to say why it took another eight months and hundreds of lives to root out the other two covens. 

Although they didn't know it yet, their foe's name in this was the black druid Lotus, who would be killed nearly a year later at Liberty Hill. They had foiled her for now. 
-GFD 
6 Marpenoth, 1479 DR. 

Captain Whitegate; 
Tuugan and I have recently uncovered a most devious plot unfolding within the region. Its roots are tied to our dreams, the Dark Garden is flowering. 

Tuugan and I began our investigation here in Dagger Falls a few days prior to the Harvestide festival. 
*Note: I have secured a room for 1 year at Quick Axes for Whitecloak member use. 
We investigated a shady dealing involving a caravan master of the Moon Sea Collective named Tzunam, his Mercenary troupe leader named Yarlick Mean, and a woman named Lotus, and the Dwimmerholme farm. The investigation was aided in small way by Magistrate Invictus and would end with Tuugan and I departing for Dwimmerholme farm the next day. 

Dwimmerholme was besieged by foul works. Wolves acting unnaturally, acres of land being burnt, and a succession of Orcish raids were most prevalent. 

With the aid of Magistrate Hoetmer, and a Netherese Prayer Book in his keeping, we were able to root out and stop a Ritual called the Ritual of Founding from occurring. This ritual would be used to create a new Sect of Shar at Dwiommerholme. The dream was also accurate in its foretelling. 
Ritual of Founding 

Three scarifices are required 
The first is followed by a boon 
The second is preceded by a boon (sacrifice greater than first) The third sacrifice comes before the boon (greatest sacrifice) 

The three sacrifices are timed to be one moon apart. 
A high-ranking individual in the Cult of Shar must be present at the sacrifices. A Relic must be present at the third sacrifice 

The most important supplicants time the final sacrifice with the new moon, as well as other celestial events. 

Although Tuugan and I managed to prevent the Ritual at Dwimmerholme, during the second Sacrfice, I fear a greater evil was carried out that same night (Harvestide) in Dagger Falls. 

I believe the ceremony of the third sacrifice was completed, a new Sect of the Cult being established in Dagger Falls. 

My reasoning is thus; Harvestide is a great celestial event, and the most prevalent event this time of year. It would fit that the third sacrifice be carried out that night. However, the ceremony Tuugan and I interrupted was only the second. It fits that a more important Supplicant was established elsewhere, and by the dream the Dark Garden center is Dagger Falls. 

It is also my belief that the Artefact being utilized is The Black Hand of Saint Gaebril. Since it must be present at the third sacrifice, no two Sects can be established the same night. 

Although late in this report, I must now speak of the woman named Lotus. She was the high-ranking Sharan at the ceremony Tuugan and I interrupted. 

She is I believe a Kenku, and possibly a shape shifter as well, I know she can take the form of a Raven as I have borne witness to it. She commanded a group of Krinth, which Tuugan and I dispatched. 

She is commonly seen as woman of height approximately 5'4", she has short black hair and birdlike features. 
Two more women are in league with her. 
One is tall and stunning, with dark hair and very light skin. 
The other is less well kept, with shoulder length curly dark hair. It is said she smells of the woods. 

I believe these three to be the ring leaders, and high priestesses of Shar. Lotus was at the Dwimmerholme ceremony, someone else had to preside over the Dagger falls ceremony. 

I need not say that we must make every effort to see these women brought to justice. As a last note, I fear I have more bad news. 

During the time of the supplication of Roger Dunland in Dwimmerholme, all messages to and from Dagger Falls failed to reach their destinations. 

I hope this report finds you well, I will send another after Tuugan and I reach Dagger Falls. Yours in Faith, 
Alystair
