Recorded by Hrogan Goringrimm, Fifth of the Monastery of Sundered Faiths, in 1451 DR, The Year of the Ageless One.

thumb

On the morning of the 12th of Khythorn, 1451 DR, Saint Maturin Horgath shifted from the prone position he had been resting in upon a stone for seventy years and sat up. The glowing light that had once surrounded his body had coalesced into an odd looking, glowing right hand.  During his battle with the sharran demon, Horgath had his right hand and forearm severed at the elbow and it seems that although it was not his original flesh, the lower arm and hand composed of blue and silver light seemed to function perfectly. Saint Maturin sat up smoothly, opened his eyes and with a light, confident tone spoke the following words: \"I...I need to go home.\"

Saint Maturin would not be delayed with questions or protocol. he took no food or drink with him, and chose to wait in isolation while his armour and weapon was retrieved from the secured vault. He chose to wait in the chamber where he and Tremalkin’s remains had laid still for the last seventy years, praying quietly and peacefully over the remains of Tremalkin.

When his gear was made available to him, he simply left the monastery, refusing the company of the gathered Ilmatari as well as a few oghman priests that were fascinated by Saint Maturin’s resurrection and wished to study him.

It is postulated that Saint Maturin’s desire to \"go home\" is reference to the Duchy of Arcata in western Damara.  Saint Maturin’s insistence on traveling alone and an inability to scry his location or any information about him means that there is no guarantee that Saint Maturin actually made it to Damara or even out of the Graypeaks.}}
