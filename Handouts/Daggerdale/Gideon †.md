Gideon was an Honourable Member in the Fellowship of Salters, Packers and Joiners. He had his own shipping house and 8 other Guild Members under him. He was a high-ranking guild member with connections to the Waterdhavian noble houses of Anteos and Wavesilver.

*Status: Deceased. Like Ersgul Mlshool, this fellow was also found dead just prior to Greengrass of 1372. His death may have something to do with the deaths of the Drow and Mlshool. He was found hanged in his own warehouse just before Greengrass, his testicles in his mouth and a symbol branded into his forehead. His eight men were never found, but many bodies had clearly been dragged from the scene.
