From Out of the Mists - Wiki Recovery, the Durinbold Gambit, and the Warders of Waterdeep
by Stephen Robertson and Les Blackwell
Email thread: "From out of the mists...", 25-26 Feb 2026

A significant meta-thread documenting Stephen's recovery of old campaign data using AI tools. Stephen took the database backup of the old Vanilla forum board, fed it to Claude, and it generated an archive document. He also recovered the "Robertsonian" wiki content (several thousand text files) and used Claude to reconstruct the story of the Warders of Waterdeep, including a retrospective by a wizard named Sandoval. Les confirmed the Warders document contained "the first accurate accounting ever put to print of the Durinbold Gambit." Les then proposed assembling a songbook from the group's collected songs over the years. Stephen also shared the first draft of "Mule Company Part One," an AI-reconstructed campaign history.
