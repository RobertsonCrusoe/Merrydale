[PC Attribution: Job of Grumbar (Casey Bauman-Wike)]
Job, a Waterdhavian from Dock Ward, was a priest of the earth-god Grumbar. He was rescued from the Dark Market by members of the Company of the Red Herring during a mission they ran in Skullport. His rescue, such as it was, was financed his childhood acquaintance, one Tomas Quoyle, who paid 850 gp (of his own personal money) at Misker's auction for the priest. In truth, Tomas had always felt that Job was too bookish and serious a fellow, since despite his imposing physique, he never had anyone's back in a scrap. Nevertheless, the good-hearted fisherman felt an obligation to the fine folk of Dock Ward, and couldn't let the poor blighter be sold to slavers.

Job disappeared without a word of thanks soon after this incident.
