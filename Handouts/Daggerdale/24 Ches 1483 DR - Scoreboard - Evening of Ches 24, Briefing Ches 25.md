CHES 24, WHITE CLOAKS v. STANDING STONE LEAGUE

In the afternoon, the White Cloaks faced the Standing Stone League in the Arena, and beat them 2 - 1.  The SSL was still down one member, having not replaced Bree, but Tarra Hilltop sat all three matches, leaving them even strength.

The White Cloaks lost the third round, when the audience turned against the Cormyrean War Wizard, Alexandus Ward, for doing nothing but counter-spelling his opponent, the wizard Velemore Emphar.  Whether the crowd didn't like the stifling of the show, or the poor sportsmanship of the Cormyrean, they soon began chanting 'Sod off Alex' over and over again, before the relevant and rousing verse from 'the Munk' was sung by over 1/3 of the audience;

The monkey, quick with jest and jape, caught the war mage unawares,
Its arms were strong, its aim was true, it flung dung into his hair.
Cormyr's son, a wizard proud, was thus by a rustic mageling bested,
His pride was scarred by the monkey's jibe, his temper sorely tested.

Losing his focus to the crowd, he was eventually bested by Vellemore, and the round was soon won by the SSL.

---

MORNING BRIEFING - CHES 25, 07h00

In the Chauntean Chambre of Governors' Hall at 07h00, the usual group of captains of the teams with their organizational leaders are assembled with the five members of the Adjudicating Committee.  The meeting starts by Bargle Battlemaster resigning from the competition with these words;

"Yesterday, we were bested in the arena by the new and capable Chrysalis, who fought with such skill and honour as to make clear their advantage over my company."  Saluting Jeska, he continues "Ambassador Monarch, your team and your leadership are a new spark of hope in the Dales, and we wish you nothing but success."  Again addressing the Committee, he says "The Free Defense League is grateful and humbled by the chance to compete in this noble competition, among and against such incredible fighters.  However, it is clear given the standings that my organization is not fit for the purpose ahead, and we remove ourselves from the competition presently, and again thank you for the chance to have competed."

After a bit of deliberation, the resignation is accepted, and after a bit of heated debate about what to do about the two matches that they would forfeit, it is decided to award 3 points to their competitors for each match (already reflected on the scoreboard).  Many aren't happy with the decision, but it stands.

Finally, General Thunderstorm reads a small report that arrived by messenger dove earlier in the morning.

For General Thunderstorm, Bishop Whitegate and Mayor Cassimar

Plains – First clash between the 3rd Co and MLGT's forces WNW 25mi from the Rockline.  4 killed in action, but company-strength recon element repelled.

Greyhame Mtn – Suspicion of gargoyle clan confirmed.  Scouts estimate at least 50 combat-capable members of the tribe actively drilling in the mountains.  No contact yet.

Maw of Gruumish – Low-intensity skirmishes with probing parties of orcs continue.  Some casualties but none KIA.

Theory – Unchanged from last report.

Suggestions – Double the garrison at Gribons' Farm, end the contest and the campaign for Thistle Ridge.  Harry the denizens of the Ridge to stifle growth, but engage after our Western Borders are secure.

-24 Ches, 1483, Vespers

-LW
