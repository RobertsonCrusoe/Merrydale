Jasper and Lucan at the Campfire - Greenwood Farm
Date: 22 April 2025
From: Gus Miranda (responding to Stephen Robertson's Palus dossier)
To: Stephen Robertson, Les Blackwell, Scott Mclaren, Todd Burry

Evening.

Lucan sits on an upturned stump of a log, staring at the small campfire near the shore while the din of constant labour in Greenwood fades into the background.

He is uncharacteristically silent: brooding and staring at the fire.

Jasper stands at the water's edge, a few metres away, looking up at the countless stars above on this clear spring night.

"I agree with everything you've said," says the wizard.

"We've probably got the shape of the thing now. If these people weren't your family, what happened to your real family? Is that whole business about heading to Chessenta true, then?"

Jasper pauses to contemplate a Lucan doppelganger-cousin strutting about some pyramids complaining about the heat.

"In any case, what I'd like to know is when did Palus cross with the Netherese? If the chapel dates from the early 1400s, her plan must've been in place for quite some time. Was she the one that lured them here? Was she in league with the shades already? Or maybe her plan came about later and she hijacked the image and worship of Janath for her own plans?"

Jasper looks down from the stars and toward the eastern horizon where Thistle Ridge waits.

"I think it's time we get some real answers."
