This is rumoured to be the head of the various groups of villains inhabiting the depths of Tethyamar.  It is speculated that he may be a beholder, a dragon, an aboleth, an illithid, an...

He is reported to be in command of the Halls of Moradin, wherein the throne of Tethyamar is found.

 
He has a number of agents called 'Consuls' under him, each of which commands a borough of the dwarven city.  These consuls are known to be of various species; illithid, undead, and others.
