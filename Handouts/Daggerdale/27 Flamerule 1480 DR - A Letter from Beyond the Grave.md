This note was found tucked into a book that was delivered to Roger Dunland by Magistrate Phineas Hoetmer.  The book was delivered in a package on the 30th of Flamerule, 1480 DR.

Master Dunland;

I am not sure why I am sending you this, but I feel you will need it more than I.  Maybe not right away, but you will have cause to use it, or to put it to use sometime in the future.

It is a diabolical looking thing, I know, and the title is a little frightening.  But, rest assured this is a work of disciplined academia.  This is not a magical book in any way, but one that explains a terrible magic, and some other foul things besides.

Put it aside, and let none know that you have it.  You will know when it is time to put it to use.

Until we meet again,

— Phin Hoetmer

27 Flamerule, 1480 DR}}
