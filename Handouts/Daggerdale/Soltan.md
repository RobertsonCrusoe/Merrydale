This perfidious villain claimed to be a Calishite, but was soon revealed to be from the Moonsea area, likely Zhentil Keep. He fell under the sway of the wizard Thagdul and the beholder Zantiid, ultimately betraying his erstwhile allies, and bringing misfortune to Waterdeep. He likes fire.

A somewhat biased account of his character:

A case study on the psychology of wizardry. The man known as Jafar was a humble tinkerer, come to Waterdeep to find his fortune and make a new life after the horrors of Zhentil Keep. By pure happenstance, he fell in with the wrong crowd (the Red Herrings), who promptly decided it would be a good idea for him to fireball anything that moved.  Like a recovering alcoholic given the keys to the cellars of Sembia, he was lost. A poor, simple, humble mage viciously corrupted by that monstrously insidious and villainous boy-child of Azuth.
