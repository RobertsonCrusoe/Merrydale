The visitor to Arcadium's will enter first a front-shoppe that is a small portion of the ground level.  It is a neat and orderly business that lacks the usual chaos and character of a typical alchemist's lab.  The retail room of the business has many orderly shelves with clearly labelled vails and bottles of all sorts.  There is a long desk behind which a young lady named Valora sees to the needs of the customers.

A door behind the desk leads to the actual labs where Arcadium and his apprentices busily work at producing the most in-demand potions that the Castle seems to be consuming.

Most potions and other alchemical concoctions level 10 and under are readily available here.
