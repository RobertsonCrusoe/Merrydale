Tuugan is a Daggerdale native who grew up on _______ farm.  He is a ranger and exemplary tracker.  He favours the use of the Daleland Great Bow, which hunters have been making famous in this region for generations.  He is a skilled archer and swordsman, though he cannot claim to be master of either.

Tuugan actually worships Chauntea, rather than Amaunator, though he respects the church of Amaunator in the dale, and acknowledges its primacy.  He is one of the founding members of the White Cloaks.
