In late 1372, months after the Battle of Anathar's Dell, Dalak the Dwarf began to act decidedly strangely... 
----

Dalak has recently been having nightmares where he is in the battle with the Baron. In each dream Dalak takes the blow from the Baron that he thinks should be fatal. He opens his eyes for a brief moment to see a mysterious robed figure before he falls unconscious. At this point Dalak awakens in his bed trembling for his immortal soul. 

After the dreams have been going on for a while Dalak begins to see what he thinks is this robed figure around town. He usually just glances at the figure through his periferal vision only to see nothing there when he turns his attention. 

One time Dalak goes to take a customer's order at his tavern only to have the customer turn into this figure and whisper, \"You should be dead.\" A frightened and crazed Dalak yells out to the figure, \"What did you say!?\" only to have a terrified customer yelp back \"I said I wanted a pot of stew, please sir I mean not to have any trouble!\" This of course has Balak running out and restraining his brother with both force and comforting words. Dalak is kept away from the customers after this. 

Recently Dalak has been capturing more of a glance of this figure in his dreams. Each time he sees the figure now it says the same thing: \"You should be dead. I'm coming for you.\" 

Dalak is now sure that he somehow cheated the hands of fate that day at Anathar's Dell and that his punishment will be much worse than death. He must now seek the help of his old friends or face the consequence.
