29 Tarsakh, 1483 DR — Letter from Captain Jeska Monarch to General Dauntless Thunderstorm

[Source: Daggerdale - All Chapter 7 Content - june 11 revised.docx, extracted 2026-04-09. Drafted at Greenwood Farm on the eve of the Greengrass battles; carried by Ambassador's hand.]

From: Company Chrysalis, Stewards of Greenwood Farm and Thistle Ridge
To: General Dauntless Thunderstorm, Commander of the Sunblades

General Thunderstorm,

I write with grave urgency concerning an imminent and substantial threat to our eastern holdings. Intelligence gathered by Sanduskin Vale's familiar, Ovetchkin, confirms that a force of approximately 200 Torn Cheek goblins, accompanied by several troll-thralls and at least one siege engine, will attack Thistle Ridge Tower tomorrow, on Greengrass.

This warband has been assembled by a powerful witch (likely allied with the Shadow) dwelling in the Sorrow Bog. This witch commands many of the goblin forces in the region. They pose a severe threat to your battalion stationed at the Tower. While we could relocate our forces from Greenwood Farm to support them, our scouts have also identified a secondary force of 40 mounted goblins positioned just three miles northeast of Greenwood, leaving us unable to defend both positions simultaneously.

We urgently request two forms of immediate aid:

First, the deployment of the 18th Reserves to Greenwood Farm. Second, fast riders carrying alchemical supplies to the Tower. The presence of trolls makes fire and acid-based weapons essential — our previous encounters confirm that these creatures regenerate quickly unless wounded with fire or acid.

Of particular concern are the alchemical weapons employed by the Torn Cheek goblins. In our previous encounter at the Shrine, they used caustic compounds that burned through armor and flesh, as well as disorienting smoke bombs that caused temporary blindness and respiratory distress. We expect they will deploy similar weapons from their siege engines. Accordingly, we recommend sending supplies of neutralizing agents (particularly alkali solutions for acid burns), healing draughts, and eye-cleansing tinctures.

I understand this exceeds our contractual arrangements, particularly as the Sunblades are committed to your crucial campaign against Netheril in the West. However, should the eastern territories fall, it would create a strategic vulnerability that eventually threatens Dagger Falls itself. The swift response of your Reserves could mean the difference between holding our position and losing everything we've gained.

As payment for this extraordinary aid, I offer access to Myth Drannor's ancient knowledge: one hour per month, during which you may ask up to ten questions to assist in your Western campaign. Where you have decades of experience with your enemies, Myth Drannor possesses more than a thousand years of wisdom that could prove invaluable against Netheril. This arrangement would continue for as long as our mutual defense requires.

Our settlements now house over a hundred civilians, including Cormyrean pilgrims building a monastery at Greenwood Farm. These people have placed their trust in us, and by extension, in the protection offered by the Sunblades.

Time is of the essence, General. The fate of the eastern territories rests in your hands.

With respect and urgency,

Ambassador Jeska Monarch,
By the hands of Lucan Alavandor and Jasper Hawkford
