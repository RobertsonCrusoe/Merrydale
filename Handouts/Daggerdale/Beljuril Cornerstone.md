Beljuril Cornerstone is the son two Waterdhavian adventurers who disappeared into Undermountain during his childhood. His mother was a talented half-elven mage involved with the  and the City Watch, while his father was a ranking Grumbarryn cornerstone monk, from whom his patronym was derived.

The fate of his parents remains a mystery, and Beljuril was never able to learn the circumstances that led them into the Undermountain alone. Before their descent into Halaster's dungeons, the two left their son with an Azuthan order in Waterdeep, who quickly identified him as a prodigy at an early age, and raised him to be a member of their order.
