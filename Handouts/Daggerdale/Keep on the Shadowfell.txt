==An Experiment in on-line 4ed DND==
thumb|Cover Art from the Module

In early autumn of 2010, some of the guys started asking me (Blacky) about running an online campaign, set in Daggerdale.  In the course of 4 days I recieved 3 seperate emails from Stephen, Gustavo and {{Catlink|Steve|Hicks}}, all inquiring about the feasability of running an online adventure.

Before long, people were making characters, and we were starting to look for software for a gaming platform.

Gus volunteered to take up DMing a new campaign to test things out.

===Software===
*In November this year, we started using [http://www.rptools.net/index.php?page=downloads#MapTool MapTool].  This is a good platform, and can be improved with a number of resources, including;
*[http://www.houseofgenius.com/files/mtfw/v500/rfw-v5.html (Framework)]; this plugin for the MapTool program scripts most of what you need to plug characters right into the game, and governs many 4ed rules details
*[http://www.skype.com/intl/en-us/home (Skype)]; We tried out Ventrillo, but there is no reason on Earth to choose 'Vent' over Skype.  Skype is crystal clear and instant.  There is no feedback, it supports 3-way+ calling, and it is free.
