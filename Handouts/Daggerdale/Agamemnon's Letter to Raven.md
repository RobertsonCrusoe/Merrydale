A letter from Agamemnon to Raven, dated Mirtul 9, 1372.

Master Raventree,

If you are reading this letter, I am either in deep trouble or worse.

Tonight, I have been summoned to a meeting with Kendal Thorne, via my associate Steele.

Know this: Steele strikes me as an odd fellow, and I regret that I don’t have more reliable allies.

At any rate, I am in trouble and know I can trust you.  Similar letters have also gone to the Chosen of Selûne and Beljuril of Azuth.

I have been working at something I will need you to pick up on now that you are back in the city.

There is another cell of the drow in the city that aren’t allied with the Dalael.  They have similar interests, but are at odds.  This is mostly because of the head of this cell, a female drow named Briza Uloavae.

I have been trying to do her in, before they come to an agreement, with Dalael.

Furthermore, I have learned that the drow are at odds with the beholders, and that they have hired the services of Jannaxil Serpentil and Elaith “the Serpent” Craulnober to recover a book named Encyclopedae Ocularis Indomitus.  They want to get this book before the beholders.

We must get this book.

But I am short on time.

My work is important, but I have generated few leads for either of these tasks.

Know you this: above all else, this book must be retrieved,
