This map was captured from the Estawolde Fort excavation site by Grim during a pitched battle with the Netherese agents defending it.

It was later translated and interpreted by Ambassador Miritar.

The map is difficult to read at all without a special crystal which the Netherese call a Looking Stone.  This is a large clear gem which one holds over the map to make sense of the swirling and chaotic markings.

This particular map gives the locations of dozens of ancient ruins that the Netherese, and Loki Mouvranesh in particular are looking for.

The map was translated by Miritar as a favour to Grim.  Miritar has a looking stone with which he can read the map, and the language skills neccessary beyond that, but he would not relinquish his stone.  He said that a direct copy of the map would be difficult to impossible to render.  

The Netherese Archaeological Cult does not make maps like you or I might.  They are...different; layers of information, including feelings and other things we don't map, and they leave out some things we might, such as hard coordinates.  You cannot make a direct copy of this map without the right tools or the right mind.-Ambassador Miritar

This map is presently in the maproom of White Cloak Tower.
