# Bloody Eye
# Source: The Sieges Perilous wiki (backup dated 2010-02-21)

The Bloody Eye is a warband made up mostly of half-orcs and other monstrous humanoids that raid the roads all over the Dales.  Their success over a 10 year career has owed laregly to their changing of hunting grounds every couple of months.  Recently, they have been setting up ambushes on the roads of Daggerdale, mostly along the two main roads.

They are lead by a cruel and cunning half-orc named Kraken who recently took over leadership of the bandit band.

They likely number between 20-40 members, with a good many capable rangers and scouts, and favour the use of ranged attacks, and have some very skilled archers, including a few that use great bows.  

The Bloody Eye does not stay long in one spot, and they are adept at packing up their camp quickly and moving when they suspect they are compromised.

Recently, they have been causing trouble in the northern reaches of Cormanthor, and have crossed paths with the Fewelglayde Clan of green elves.  The druid Malveau has been pursuing them for several weeks, and recently had his two companions slain by their quarry.  It was then that he found the White Cloaks on the road, who had just recently been hit by a probe of the Bloody Eye which nearly killed two members.

Malveau gives an account of his experiences with the Bloody Eye.
