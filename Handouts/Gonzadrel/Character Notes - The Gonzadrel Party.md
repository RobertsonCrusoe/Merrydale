Character Notes: The Gonzadrel Party
Campaign: Gonzadrel / The Fringe
Source: Email threads, February-March 2025
Authors: Gus Miranda, Stephen Robertson, Les Blackwell

---

PLAYER CHARACTERS

Liltariel the Elf (Stephen Robertson) - DECEASED
Wood Elf, spontaneous fey-magic caster. Exiled from the Fickwood for a century and a day after a human child drowned at a magical spring, leading to a confrontation between her kin and a human "posse" in which more died. Nihilistic, disdainful of humans, ethereally beautiful but with terrible personality (CHA 8). Killed during the Chaos Keep adventure. Her body was carried aboard the longboat through the Underdark.

Friar Mungo (Stephen Robertson) - DECEASED
Follower of Dalt, the god of portals, doors, enclosures, locks, and keys. Killed by Yellow Mold in the first adventure ("In Search of the Unknown"). Stephen: "Naught but cosmic justice meted out by Dalt to an apostate who smashed a lock with a mace without begging his Lord's forgiveness beforehand."

Brother Crispin "the Calamitous" (Stephen Robertson)
Fortunist Initiate of Lady Luck. Sole survivor of three separate party wipes (piercers, gargoyles, sentient fungus). Believes he is blessed; the party suspects he is cursed. Weak, timid, religious. Sir Pellias loathes him. Fishook tolerates him because he can heal.

Muttonchop (Stephen Robertson)
Halfling. Affable, claims to be a great big-game hunter. Rides a pony named Nimrod. In a relationship with Thissy, a she-halfling (who is married to Dunny - not a big taboo among halflings).

Fishook the Hazardous (Les Blackwell)
Magic-user/wizard. Decadent, world-weary, amoral but pragmatic. Smokes a pipe of amber and opium. Views the antics of "rustics" as entertainment. From the Old World. Has a calculating, scientific worldview that clashes with superstition.

Hurl the Manservant/Bondsman (Les Blackwell)
Fighter. Fishook's bondsman/servant. Practical, solid. Wants freedom for slaves. Takes orders from Fishook but has his own moral compass. Pellias views him as a useful subordinate.

Sir Pellias (Gus Miranda)
Knight. Landless, penniless, desperate for gold to buy land and oppress peasants. Subscribes to "might is right" philosophy. Believes in inherent superiority of the noble class. Conceived as an Arthurian knight - but the kind from the actual stories: "gigantic fucking assholes... always raping and pillaging, murdering whole swaths of peasants for no good reason." Irrationally hates Crispin. Thinks Owin is a blowhard. Respects Fishook as a near-peer.

Owin the Bard (Gus Miranda)
Celtic-style bard - a priest, not a minstrel. Tells stories to please the gods, not for entertainment. Travels to gather experience for new tales. Cursed by the Melusine to wander forever in search of his lost home. Open-minded, believes everyone has a story worth telling. Appeals to Manannán (god of waterways) and Aodh (god of the underworld). Fascinated by Muttonchop. Has patience for Crispin. Finds Pellias's ignorance tiresome.

CAMPAIGN DETAILS

System: Old School Essentials (OSE) / Original D&D
Setting: "The Fringe" - an unnamed world with OSR sword-and-sorcery flavor
Key Locations: Gonzadrel (town), Barnuck (dwarven mining outpost), The Goblin Market (underground), The Chaos Keep (ruins on the moor), The Fickwood (elven forest), Maggot Villa, The Duchy
Tone: Gonzo old-school, Howard/Leiber/Moorcock influences, deadly, dark humor
