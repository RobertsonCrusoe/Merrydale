RULINGS AND RAMBLINGS

Good Work: I want to give an attaboy to your tactical choices, especially during your fight up the side of the cliff to your ship. There were a lot of things that could have turned that combat sideways, but you were able to fend them off with sound tactics. Using missile weapons on nasty creatures before they reach you is smart!

Continual Light vs Darkness: I think I will rule that continual light dispels darkness, but darkness has a 50% chance of turning continual light into a regular light spell before it goes.

Crossbows: Remember that crossbows are slow. If you intend to fire one, equip it on your sheet so you know to go last. Fighters should not feel ashamed of using a longbow instead.

Poison, Level Drain, and Other Nastiness: I just want to give a fair warning that nasty things become more common at higher levels. A few tips:
- Anything that looks like it might be poisonous probably is, and the poison is probably deadly.
- Higher level undead level drain, usually permanently.
- You have a bard that has a chance to know about the nastiness of a creature you encounter, but he must ask.
