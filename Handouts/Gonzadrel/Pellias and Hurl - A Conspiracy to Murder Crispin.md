Pellias and Hurl: A Conspiracy to Murder Crispin
Campaign: Gonzadrel / The Fringe
Source: Email thread "Gonzo-drel", February 15, 2025
Author: Gus Miranda (player-written narrative)
Characters: Sir Pellias (Gus), Hurl (Les), Brother Crispin (Stephen)

---

Later, as the rest of the party and passengers are engaged in the mundane tasks of passing the time aboard, Pellias motions for Hurl to join him at the stern of the longboat for a quiet word:

"You're keeping watch tonight," says the knight to Fishook's bondsman. "About an hour before everyone else wakes, I'm going to get up and come over, and then you and me will toss him overboard, alright? Before he gets us all killed."

Hurl narrows his eyes as he looks at Pellias, unsure about this plan.

"It's fine. I'll talk to Fishook, and he'll be in agreement," says the knight having no intention at all to talk to Hurl's wizardly master.

Pellias walks off, leaving Hurl to contemplate the plan on his own.

Later still, the appointed time comes, and Pellias creeps up and taps Hurl on the shoulder, and gives the bondsman a silent conspiratorial nod.

Hurl, slumps his shoulders with some reluctance and acceptance of the deed they are about to do.

The two men creep up to where Brother Crispin lay asleep, alone and softly snoring. Hurl positions himself by the man's feet, while Pellias hovers over the head and torso. In silence, the knight counts off with his fingers: one… two… thr-

"Light! Light-ho! I see a light up ahead," calls out Muttonchop from his watch atop the dragonhead on the bow of the boat.

Crispin, along with the rest of the company, is startled awake. He sees the shapes of the two warriors hovering above him, and Hurl reaches out to help him up to his feet, locking eyes with Pellias as he does so.

Pellias simply scowls and follows everyone towards the bow.
