Ilyeo is the youngest of the four Ossur brothers, a paladin of Kelemvor and one of the two that played a key role in the rescue of The Ghosts and the Miritars. While his brother Irst battled with and drove back the Nightweaver, Ilyeo slayed her minions and rescued those that he could.
