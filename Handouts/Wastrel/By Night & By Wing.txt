This book contains all of Barthas’ research, learnings and musings of vampirism, containing a detailed history of the fall of Merrydale as well as a great deal of information about vampires he had learned of and from. He has an extensive list of different types of vampirism as well. The book seems to be the least elaborately crafted of the three, with a crude binding of silk attaching two well-crafted sheets of oak. The pages within are crafted from a much more sinister material, as investigation of it will show that the pages are made of skin; of the female, elven variety. The entire tome is written in a common tongue from Thay.
Status of tome - Original reviewed by Kethoth Sepret
