One by one, then many by many the ships begin to leave through the calm water, some veering off north, some south, while still others continue on east. You and many other ships have left Freeport’s shores today and cannot return for another season. You have left Freeport’s shores today and may not return ever. Why is it worth sailing at all? That is the riddle of the sea. Is there an answer to this riddle? That I cannot tell for I am just the narrator. But do sail my friends. Sail where the wind takes you and sail where it guards against.
