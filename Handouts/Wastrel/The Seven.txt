An opportunistic group of adventurers that might not always total seven.  Directed and generally led by a trio; [[Kethoth Sepret]], [[Mattrick Lychaeus]] and [[Baird of Kelemvor]], the party formed in [[Loudwater]] in early Mirtul in the year 1479.

All of them had interest in journeying to the ruins of [[Spellgard]] and seek out [[Lady Saharel]] in hopes to have whatever question they could imagine answered.

Although they have never outwardly referred to themselves as The Seven, they have achieved some renown as well as infamy since they have banded together and are referred to as The Seven by a number of interested and concerned parties.

Founding members of The Seven are:

*[[Kethoth Sepret]]
*[[Baird of Kelemvor]]
*[[Mattrick Lychaeus]]
*[[Quintus Lucretius Cato]]
*[[Ender]]
