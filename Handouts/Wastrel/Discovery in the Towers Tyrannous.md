Tarukaja was infamous for running a number of antiquity shops between 1075 DR and 1120 DR. A few years prior to 1120 DR, it was revealed that the good and benevolent wizard that provided useful items to adventurers in Waterdeep was also a trafficker of ancient artefacts and slaver. It was revealed that he had a number of ties to disreputable people in Skull Port and significant dealing with neogi and other aberrant creatures from the far realm. Some time after 1120 DR and Tarukaja was being hunted for his crimes, he achieved lichdom. Although a number of adventuring companies claimed to destroy Tarukaja, the lich would return each time earning him the name, \"The Iron Soul\".
