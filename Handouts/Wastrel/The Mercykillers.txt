A group of individuals from Waterdeep that seem fixated on locating and putting to use eight Netherese, [[alabaster obelisks]].  The group can be recognized by their black coloured armour and clothing that is adorned with light blue coloured banding around the shoulders and down the sides of their torsos.  They seem to be a well-disciplined and well-equipped group of individuals led by a [[Ithrax Mal Turik|stern dragonborn that wields a pole-axe]].
