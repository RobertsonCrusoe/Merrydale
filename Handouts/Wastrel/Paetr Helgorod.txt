AKA Ol'Mossy.

A dour, dwarven warrior that has spent over fifty years stationed at the Graypeak Mountain outpost known as Falcon's Roost

He was assigned, but closer to being asked by the current captain of Falcon's Roost, Maeghuen Grimbold to accompany the adventuring party known as The Seven and protect her son, Tyriol Grimbold.

Paetr died while in the Towers Tyrannous, believed to be sometime around 1124 DR.  Paetr was climbing down into a two-hundred foot deep pit to provide support for Jalael Do'riden who was doing battle with a devil.  While climbing down, Paetr slipped and fell.  Shortly after striking the ground, Paetr's incapacitated or possibly dead body was used as a shield by the devil that was battling The Seven.  An arrow that was shot by Tyriol Grimbold ended up striking Paetr due to the infernal magicks possessed by the devil they were battling.

It is unknown at this time whether it was the fall or Tyriol's arrow that killed Paetr Helgorod but by the time the battle with the devil was done, Paetr was dead and beyond saving.
