Morgahn Redhand is the sergeant of the south-eastern  patrol of the Loudwater Wardens.  Morgahn is the most seasoned of all  the sergeants and he ranges far to the east of Loudwater, well past  Llorkh and sometimes even as far as The Smear.

Morgahn is wed to Syllinallyah, a wood elf from the Woodsinger clan (Taervor'Linnod) in the South Wood.

Morgahn has a son by the name of Arlyn Redhand who left town over a year ago to find adventure in the ruins of Spellgard.

Morgahn  also has a daughter of nineteen years, a half-elven, rare beauty by the  name of Mirabel that serves food and drink at The Green Tankard and  entertains with her singing.

Morgahn played an integral role in  the defense of Loudwater during the Iron Jackjal siege.  It is said that  Morgahn is still furious with members of The Ghosts over keeping the  captivity and location of his daughter, Mirabel a secret from him.   Shepherd Malaric has sought to engage him a number of times over the  last three months and Morgahn has simply turned from him and walked  away.
