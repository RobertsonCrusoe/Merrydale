But let me return to the tomb, for a moment.  At the end of the fight with Maskil, I was nearly ruined.  I had been drained of all but my last bit of life, and was in no condition to stop the horrifying ruin and destruction that the fool Tempusson visited upon the library and laboratory of the Baron.  For in two chambres branching from the Baron

s resting chambre, we did find two such rooms; an ancient and intriguing laboratory, and a full library.  If there is anything more in the world that makes my mouth water than a library in a tomb, I have not imagined it yet.  I must have had a smile from ear to ear when I entered that library and began poking around.

But then it happened; Tempusson entered, a flaming brand in his hand, and began to torch it all!  I was furious, and was made even more so when May and Brenean sided with the moronic cleric!  Were I in any shape to stand up against the cleric, I would have.  But I had no power to resist even a gentle breeze after the focussed assault of the Baron, and so I watched it all burn, while hatred burned inside of me.  And still does.

The worst was yet to come.  Knowing the full extent of my injuries from the Baron, the damnable cleric actually offered me a scroll of restoration, which certainly I needed to make a full recovery.  I was loathe to accept his help, but had no other options.  If I did not get help by midnight, I would be in bad shape indeed.  I went to the Inn, while the others went to the castle to get praise from the unpraisworthy.  I drank and drank, looking for an answer to my dilemma.  After a bottle of cheap and sour wine, I decided that I needed the scroll more than my dignity, and was subsequently cured of the damage done by the Baron.

This single act of generosity by Tempusson, unmercifully, I cannot erase from my head.  Were I able to forget his gift of the scroll, I could, in good conscious, hunt him down and make him to suffer for days.  But I think I will spare this man.  I shall always loathe him, as I loathe all the simple folk of that backward island, but his will be the last body on the heap when Chadrazzan Kossur returns to the Moonshaes.
