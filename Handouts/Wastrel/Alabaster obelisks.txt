It is currently known that eight of these obelisks exist as hinted at in The Analects of Kuryon.  Arya Nightweaver; armed with a keen eye and a profound understanding of arcane energies obtained from decades spent growing up in the Feywild, managed to recognize multiple layers of enchantments woven into and around the pillars.

It was later discovered by Kethoth Sepret; armed with information provided by Arya Nightweaver that the pillars, upon having gathered all eight could possibly be used to ensnare or imprison magical creatures of entities.

Each pillar is identical to the next.

They are Netherese in origin with a number of Netherese runes inscribed on each facing.

They are exactly eight feet in length with an eighteen inch base that tapers up to the top where it is eight inches wide from one facing to the other.  Each obelisk has eight sides and weighs roughly one thousand pounds.

Known Locations of Obelisks

As of 1st Khythorn, 1479 DR, the eight alabaster obelisks have been submerged in the small, westbound river just to the south of Kohr'Murron Stables.  The eight obelisks are sunk in twenty-five feet of water and difficult to spot unless specifically looking for them.
