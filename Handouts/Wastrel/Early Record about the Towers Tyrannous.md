Information collected by Loremaster Malfus Broca in 1197 DR, The Year of the Sundered Shields

thumb

There are scattered references to this location at best but from stories that I have gathered and from an antiquated map I have studied, it suggests that The Towers Tyrannous in the northern region of Thay; Surthay.  The Towers Tyrannous became a location of note in the Realms in 1142 DR; The Year of the Sword’s Oath.  During that year, an Aglarondan wizard, Zamomel Stass, was rumoured to have been killed by an ambitious Red Wizard of Thay by the name of Szass Tam. What is now legend amongst those in Thay and Aglarond, the battle in the ruins of The Towers Tyrannous lasted for five days and five nights until a smoking and charred, but breathing Szass Tam emerged from the ruins.

The Towers Tyrannous is believed to have been a temple of worship for followers of Bane.  The towers that gave the ruins it’s name were destroyed by an ancient green dragon by the name of Gaulhgratharinorh in 1030 DR; The Year of Warlords. After that, it appears that the ruins became infested with all manner of demonic entities and undead and although the towers were ruined, rumours persisted of an extensive structure under the ground that survived the assault from Gaulhgratharinorh.}}
