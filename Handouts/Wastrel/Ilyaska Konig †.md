In her twenty-first year, Ilyaska is the lovely, but quite hard matriarch of Fairbreen Farm. It was her parents that were killed by the Xeod'Roth years ago and it is through her knowledge and arcane dealings that seem to keep the Xeod'Roth at bay. Ilyaska is commonly recognized as one of the more educated and skilled healers in the Delimbiyr Vale.

After the Iron Jackals assaulted Fairbreen Farm, Ilyaska was brought to Loudwater after having been rescued. She was put to the task of tending to the dying Captain Maeghuen Grimbold.

Shortly before sunrise on Tarsakh 16, Ilyaska was killed by a number of undead that attacked Maeghuen Grimbold`s house. Her body was found, covered in deep slashes, laying overtop of Maeghuen Grimbold`s unconscious form.
