Calla is a middle-aged, round-cheeked, and round-bottomed  proprietor of the general good store in Loudwater.  She is a friendly,  if no-nonsense woman that goes out of her way to have a well-stocked  store despite the challenges she encounters from the current isolation  that Loudwater is suffering from.  Calla is also the ward and what she  perceives to be the adopted mother of a young boy by the name of  Raumadar. 

Raumadar was found by a caravan on the way to Loudwater  fifteen years ago on the shores of Highstar Lake.

Calla has  recently lost her family farm to Iron Jackals.  When she took it upon  herself to ask an individual by the name of Cirrato for assistance, he  regrettably declined but did secure three months worth of lodging at The  Green Tankard for her and her \"son\", Raumadar.

Calla and her  son, Raumadar were single-handedly rescued from the burning of the Green  Tankard by Shepherd, a battlepriest of Torm and resident of Waterdeep.

After  the death of Brother Griffon, Calla took up the charge of tending to  Maeghuen Grimbold.  A week after the death of Maeghuen Grimbold, Calla  began exhibiting traits of the illness that Maeghuen Grimbold succumbed  to.  Calla is currently locked away in the Grimbold house with only her  son Raumadar caring for her.
