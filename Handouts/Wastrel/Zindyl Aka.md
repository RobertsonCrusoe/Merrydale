Zindyl is a dark-skinned beauty hailing from Turmish. She is a worshipper of a little known goddess by the name of Sharess and although Brother Griffon quickly condemns her as a seed of ill within Loudwater she has become quite...quite popular with regulars of the Green Tankard. Zindyl is known for providing companionship for a price if she takes a shine to that person. She seems to favour both males and females of all races.

Zindyl prefers to listen, rather than speak and the few individuals that have been allowed to “know her” refuse to share anything about the experience but always, always speak of her in a reverent tone.

When Zindyl first arrived eight months ago she made her way to the Fisher Friend's Tavern. There she was accosted by two dock workers that refused to be calmed by her propositions, dragged her behind the tavern and raped her.

Zindyl refuses to speak of the event but the two that committed the deed were more than happy to share it with anyone that happened to pass by them within shouting distance.

Two days later, the two men were found staked and tied to the docks at sun rise. One had both arms and both legs shattered beyond any manner of healing available and was rambling in a high-pitched, terrified language that no one present was able to understand. He died a two weeks later, in pain and delerious and the only person that showed up to tend to the man was Zindyl, saying it was a shame that things had turned out so badly.

The other that was staked to the dock had both of his eyes burst with what seemed to be something quite hot, his genitalia cut off and stuffed into his mouth. It appeared that the man died from blood loss but that it took all night for him to bleed out while he laid there in the darkness.

Of course everyone believed that Zindyl was the one responsible for the retaliation but Zindyl had exercised her good will the night the two men were attacked and she had retired early, escorting Grandus Hammerfel to his bed and staying with him for the entire night.

The individual that committed the assault and murder of the two dock workers has yet to be identified.

Zindyl played an integral role in the psychological healing of Meghana Nistral. While speaking to Zindyl and Meghana, a member of The Ghosts happened to recognize a symbol of Hoar around the neck of Zindyl.

On the 9th of Tarsakh, Zindyl found Artagan while he was staying at the newly formed `hostel`for the displaced farmers of the Gray Vale. Zindyl seduced Artagan and while doing so, also converted him to the faithful of Hoar.

On the 18th of Tarsakh, Zindyl was found, seated on the ground outside of Maeghuen Grimbold's home, cradling the upper-half of Nomen Ryne's body.

Despite the death of Grandus Hammerfel and that Zindyl lived in his residence while alive and Magistrate Brannikar took up residence there, Zindyl continued to live in the Magistrate's new home.

Zindyl was seen from time to time over the next few months, traveling in her usual circles and was reported to have left through the west gate on Kythorn 30 in the late evening by members of the Shields.
