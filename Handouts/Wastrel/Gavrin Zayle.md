Gavrin Zayle is a very average looking, average sized  man.  In fact much about Gavrin is average save the reputation he has  made for himself.  Apparently a close friend with Garrus Cal’Botran;  many of Loudwater know Gavrin Zayle simply as “The Gatekeeper”.  Those  that wish to travel west, beyond Llorkh and stay on the relative safety  of the road invariably meet up with Gavrin.  For a relatively fair  price, Gavrin will offer to be their escort through Llorkh and if the  travelers keep to their word and pay the agreed upon price then no harm  will come to them.  It has happened enough with adventuring parties  headed to the Fallen Lands that this is not legend or rumor.  Those that  deal fairly and honestly with Gavrin Zayle keep their breath in their  chest and their head on their shoulders.

It is rumoured that only  a few have tried to lie, trick or actually fight Gavrin Zayle and those  people tend to end up in a bad way, with their corpses dumped in the  Gray Flow and their women and children slapped into steel collars.

Gavrin  Zayle approached the adventurers on the 13th of Tarsakh and attempted  to bargain with the adventurers and make an offer.  He would give them  more information about the Iron Jackals if they sought out Garrus  Cal'Botran.
