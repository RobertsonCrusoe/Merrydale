Information collected by Loremaster Kennyth Thames while studying in The Monastery of Sundered Faith in 1381 DR, The Year of the Starving

Sifting through the limited references to Hanahlaos, it has been discovered  that she resides in a tower that has been dubbed, “The Spire”. It is rumoured that she is a sorceress of no small power and oddly enough the Spire is possibly situated in the Eastern edge of the Graypeaks.Of particular note is the fact that the name “Hanahlaos” is mentioned briefly from time to time in documents that comefrom the following dates. 1096 DR, 1214 DR, 1215 DR, 1237 DR, 1291 DR and 1338 DR. That is certainly a broad range of years for one individual, even with arcane assistance.  Although it is possible that I have come across different persons of a similar name, the context of the references to “Hanahlaos” causes me to believe that Hanahlaos is some manner of steward or keeper for this secretive location dubbed “The Spire” and she/he/it has gained some way to extend life. Certainly not common in the Realms but certainly not impossible. Perhaps it is this area of the Realms and Hanahlaos is akin to the rumoured ghost/lich, Lady Saharel south of here in Saharelgard?}}
