== Noteworthy Swag ==

=== Salazar's spellbook ===
Salazar obtained his spell book through mysterious means, but he has had it in his possession since before his tour on the S.S. Shylock. The book is eleven inches wide by fourteen inches long by three inches thick. It contains seventy six pages with several pages having been obviously torn out in the middle after the last writings at the beginning. The book contains no front cover and no back cover, both having been removed previously. The spine of the book contains fine silk like stitching, but is not otherwise protected. The book fits nicely into a companion carrying case that is latched by a tightly fitted belt. It has a sturdy thick strap that is long enough to be thrown over an opposite shoulder. The strap is made of cheaper Owlbear leather died black while the case is made of Nightmare leather which is naturally black. The book and the case are both fairly plain other than this: The case has a small \"D\" embossed on its bottom right front side with a similar \"D\" on the cover page of the book. Both of the letters are written with the Draconic alphabet in a distinct calligraphy. The cover page also includes a paragraph written in some unknown alphabet.
