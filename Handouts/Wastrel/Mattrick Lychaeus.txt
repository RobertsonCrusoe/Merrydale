Priest of Kelemvor, Sergeant of the 2nd Regiment of the Watch Wall and ward of Baird of Kelemvor.

== Manner of Presumed Death ==

On the twenty-eighth of Mirtul, 1479; Mattrick Lychaeus, while under the arcane influence of the vampire known as Barthas; hurled himself off the edge of a cliff within a cave in the southern foothills of the Graypeak mountains.  After the fight with Barthas had concluded, investigation of the crevice that Mattrick had thrown himself into proved that the crevice was quite deep.  Dropping a light source down the crevice resulted in the light dwindling to nothing even before it seemed to hit the bottom, furthermore, listening for an impact showed that either the crevice was too deep for any negligible sound to be heard if to be heard at all.

\"If having Mattrick back also meant having Barthas back, I would concede such without a moment's regret.  Losing Mattrick hurts us all...\" , Kethoth Sepret upon concluding the final fight with Barthas.
