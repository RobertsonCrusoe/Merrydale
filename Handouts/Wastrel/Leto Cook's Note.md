A note accompanying the Wastrel when it was purchased during Freeport's Raidfest festivities.

If you are a true sea dog then my boat is now in good hands. If you are not then may Umberlee swallow you whole dammit! I suspect however that this boat has now found a worthy owner as I have always trusted in fate and it has never failed me before.

Yes, I am Leto Cook and this is my ship. I was the first and only chosen of Valkur. He chose me and built me this ship so that I could answer the riddle of the sea for him. I sailed all of the world over but could not find an answer. Because of this Valkur has never chosen another. Because of this I sank the ship myself.

Let me tell you this though: the things I saw and the adventures I had made it all worth it. Because of this I have left the means to follow in my wake.

The figurehead in this compartment is meant for this ship and this ship only. With it beneath the bowsprit the ship will never sink as long as you are sailing by the sailor’s code.

I also leave you with my log which contains most of my findings and that of other captains. I have not put everything in there for sailors should oft live by their wits alone.

Go and see what the world may hold for you. Go and find the magic of the sea. Farwell my hearty, and may your sails always be full.

— Leto}}
