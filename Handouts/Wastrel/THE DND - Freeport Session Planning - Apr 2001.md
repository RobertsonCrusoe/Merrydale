PART 1: TODD — SESSION OPTIONS — TODD BURRY (2 Apr 2001)
---------------------------------------------------------
Source: Message e59f8a80b530000
From: tburry@dymaxium.com

"I can definitely DM on Wednesday. We can't go right into the next major
adventure though because you guys have about a month to kill first (and you
will most certainly die if you do not train up). So we will be doing a
short (hopefully) interlude before things start getting heavy again. What I
want to know is this: What do you guys want to do? Here are your options:

1. Do you want to see what is going on on the Bloody Vengeance. You suspect
now that Scarbelly did not have anything to do with the Unspeakable one and
he certainly didn't kidnap Luscious. However, they were hiding something
and there is nothing wrong with looting a bunch of salt-seared Orcs. They
can't have come by any of there swag honourably. This might bring you one
step closer to being a Pirate (with a capital 'P') Zech.

2. I have another short adventure that even you guys can't waste time on.
It will definitely have the 'interlude' feeling.

Please email me your votes and please all go along with the majority. I
hope you can all play on Wednesday. Also, I will not let you lot use that
+2 ring of protection unless everyone is at the table to decide who gets
it, but thanks for the understanding about the magic mix-up.

FYI:
Potion of Cure Light Wounds: 50 GP (two available each)
Potion of Cure Moderate Wounds: 200 GP (one available each)"


PART 2: LES/SALAZAR — LOOT AND WENCHING — LES BLACKWELL (3 Apr 2001)
---------------------------------------------------------------------
Source: Message e5a337e10ee26d1
From: theblackcobb@hotmail.com

"BLACKWELL HERE;

I AM VERY HAPPY THAT WE WILL GET TO PLAY ON WED. I AM SURE THAT I WON'T
GET TO SEE THE FINNISH OF THAT SESSION (AS IS USUAL) BUT WHAT CAN YOU DO?
MAYBE WE COULD START AT AROUND 18H00? THAT WOULD BE GREAT. ALSO, I THINK

SALAZAR HERE;

I SAY LET'S DO BOTH, BUT WE WILL GO AFTER THE BLOODLY VENGEANCE FIRST (AS
THEY MAY LEAVE PORT ANYTIME SOON).

ALSO, I WOULD LIKE AT LEAST ABOUT 10 DAYS IN THE GAME TO PROCURE SOME EQ,
SCRIBE SOME SCROLLS AND GO WENCHING.

AS FAR AS THE RING GOES, I SAY WE DICE FOR IT (THEY HAVE DICE IN FREEPORT,
DON'T THEY). THE WINNER GETS IT, AND IT IS HIS PERSONAL POSSESSION AND
THAT'S THAT.

I DON'T WANT ANY NOTION OF AN ADVENTURING COMPANY OR ANY COMPANY. I DON'T
THINK WE SHOULD HAVE COMPANY RESOURCES; EVERYTHING SHOULD BE SPLIT UP
RIGHT AWAY. THAT GOES FOR POTIONS TOO. THAT ONE HEALING POTION WE HAVE IS
WORTH 50 GP, AND IT SHOULD BE TAKEN AS A SHARE OF LOOT. IT SEEMS WE HAVE
POTIONS-A-PLENTY TO PURCHASE, SO PEOPLE SHOULD ACQUIRE THEIR OWN POTIONS
AND LOOK AFTER THEMSELVES. 'CAUSE THERE'S ONLY ONE PERSON I TRUST. ONE
PERSON I RELY UPON...ME.

-SALAZAR.

BLACKWELL SAYS; TODD; YOU ARE DOING A GOOD JOB DM'ING. I AM REALLY
ENJOYING YOUR CAMPAIGN."
