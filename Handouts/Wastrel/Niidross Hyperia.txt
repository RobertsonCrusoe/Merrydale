Ambassador Hyperia is a learned and well-spoken eladrin hailing from Myth Drannor. He was sent by Coronal Ilsevele Miritar in the hopes to find the trail of her wayward cousin, Aryaluystrinne Miritar; a princess of the royal court of Mithrendain. 

Ambassador Hyperia seems out of sorts in the Gray Vale. It seems that for being an ambassador, his ability to speak with individuals is limited despite his ability to speak more languages than most folk have fingers. Although on a mission for Coronal Miritar, it seems the excitement and pride over being given such a task has waned and now, with limited options and opportunities to complete the mission given him, he is often melancholy and without much hope.

Being taken on as Lady Moonfire's most recent lover, although initially reason to keep his spirits bouyed has become more of a curse since the Iron Jackals absconded with Lady Moonfire. Considered to be the consort of Lady Moonfire, a significant portion of the citizenry are now looking to Ambassador Hyperia for guidance and direction; something that he seems to struggle with greatly.

Ambassador Hyperia was conspicuous by his absence during the battle against the Iron Jackals although two Wardens could attest to the fact that the Ambassador had met with two different groups of what could be identified as adventurers a few days prior to the battle.

As of Flamerule 1480, the Ambassador appears to be dealing with only one group of adventurers that has been seen visiting the Ambassador over the last three months.
