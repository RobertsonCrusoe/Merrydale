**Loudwater Notes - Pathfinder Campaign - Session 1 (February 19, 2012)**

**Players/Characters**

Blacky - Dr. Heath Malmotte, Harrigan Farshore
Scunny - Tarr Whuton, Caldwell "Huntmaster" Harrington
Gussy - Ford Grayson, Khel Quarrie

**Elesias 1, 1480 DR**

* Start off talking with Captain Tyriol Grimbold in the Green Tankard.  He offers us 7gp each in supplies to go and check out Ravenblight Farm.
* 4 members of the Ravenfall Family returned to the farm months ago, after the siege lifted, and the nasty business at the farm was concluded by the Ghosts
* No one had heard from anyone at the farm for over a month
* Dire Patrol went to check out the farm, and were long overdue
* 2 days ago, a squad of Bracers (Felden Angrist \- Animal Speaker Bard and Sento Andros \- Warden Ranger) were sent to check things out.
* We have been sent to check up on the entire lot, with the only possibility of payment being whatever loot we can haul off; Grimbold is only interested in information
* Harrigan spends the day fishing with Barnabas (catches 6 rations' worth)
* Team decides to appoint Malmotte to quartermaster
* Malmotte goes to Garwan Glimmerstone's shoppe for special provisions
* Glimmerstone gives us a map of the area, and sells us 3 potions of *Cure Light Wounds* ; there is much haggling over the price of the potions.  In the end, he settles on 50g for the three, and wants first crack at purchasing cards from the *Deck of Many Things,* which apparently have been cropping up in these parts
* In the evening, we feast in the Green Tankard, and Malmotte tries to pick up Ninda

**Elesias 2**

* Set out early in the morning for Everblight
* Approaching a bridge crossing a small river, our lead scouts (Harrigan and Huntmaster) hear laughing up ahead.  The two of them plus Ford approach under stealth, and a battle soon ensues.
* We kill three bandits and take 3 captive, all without taking a scratch ourselves.
* The bandits are the remnants of a larger outfit of Jackals that deserted after the siege; they have been ambushing small caravans for a few months
* We take the captives and the bodies back to Loudwater, where we surrender them to Sergeant Erdric Broan of The Blades.  He gives us 180gp for the lot of them, and promises to pay 50gp/head for any further living criminals we catch
* There is some discussion about how Shepherd would want some form of formal, lengthy trial, but as Branikar is the nominal ruler of the town in any case, we decide it is well enough to hand them over (for pay) to his men
* Malmotte and Broan agree to maintain this relationship, but to kept things discrete
* We have a pile of kit from the bandits, and hire safe storage from Laval for 15 sp/day (see list of gear below)
* Each of the 6 party members is paid out 100g out of the loot from the bandits and the payout from Broan
* Malmotte negotiates for 20 days worth of room/board at the Tankard for 55 gold for the 6 of us, as well as the storage and horse.
* Malmotte insists on having 'room service' that night; Ninda comes up, and has to be *charmed* by Malmotte in order to get into bed with him

**Elesias 3**

* Head out for Everblight again
* Before nightfall, we see a campfire, and approach.  We are discovered by the Bracers, who bring us to this camp
* The Camp is 1 mile south east of the farm.  There are a host of wounded wardens, the pair of Bracers, and remnants of the Ravenfall family.
* The farmers look emaciated, while the wardens look physically beaten up.  We ask what happened, and Cento and the sergeant offer to show us; they bring us to the edge of the farm, where we see a spectacle; there are odd green lights hanging on some of the walls, and we see a warden acting quite creepy, who moves unnaturally, and climbs up a wall like a squirrel.
* We are told they don't go far from the farm.
* Arcana, spellcraft and planeslore reveal that the creature we are watching is not demonic or necromantic in nature.  Probably other-worldly.
* We are told that who ever dies in Everblight remains there to try and kill who ever therein lives.
* We go back to the camp, and proceed on the Pro Boards

**Session 2 - (February 21, 2012)**

**Elesias 3 Continued...**

* At the camp, we talk with the wardens and wounded.
* Malmotte is able to get a couple of the farmers ready for travel, but needs another full day to get enough of them ready to travel so the group can all leave together
* Tending to the matriarch of the Ravenfall family, Rentia, Malmotte asks what is wrong with the farm.  He is told by her "It is anchored in the past. Look for the Script!"

**Elesias 4**

*  After much discussion about what we should do, we decide to raid the farm in order to see if we can find a way to cure the sick Ravenfalls.
* We have some of the able-bodied wardens create a distraction for us at the south end of the farm, while we go in through the north.
* We are immediately set upon by 'creepers' (the reanimated warden husks); they hit like a tonne of bricks, and spray foul poisonous ichor once killed.
* Harrigan absorbs the brunt of the enemy's blows, while the ranged strikers obliterate the enemy from range.
* Ford does a great job of fighting up close, and gets Harrigan's hide out of the fire.
* Khel uses an illusion to great effect, distracting the enemy for a full round.
* Tarr storms the house with Malmotte behind him.  Malmotte is able to put many of the denizens (evil looking spiders that the DM described as "three spiders all stapled together"), and Tarr takes care of the rest.
* Proceed on Proboard.
* 700 XP awarded
* No loot at the present time
