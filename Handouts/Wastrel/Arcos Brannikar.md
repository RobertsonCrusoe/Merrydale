Arcos is a warrior hailing from Waterdeep that spent a number of years seeking out Lady Saharel in Spellgard. Although unlucky when it came to finding her, he did manage to cart back a significant amount of treasure, saying that he took it all from a vampire he had managed to destroy. 

With that treasure, he bought a farm.

Brannikar is quick to tell a tale of his own if he feels that someone else might have a tale that is better than his own. Although a capable warrior in his time, that time has long since passed. He still keeps his armour, weapons and warhorse in a perpetually readied state in case he needs to defend his farm or the nearby area but Brannikar's time has clearly passed. Whether he fought a vampire or not, he came back with a terrible limp and is not what he once was.

He still has a keen eye for skill however and he has formed a well-trained, reasonably disciplined and well-geared militia of sorts at his farm.

Arcos Brannikar, twenty mounted warriors and forty foot soldiers arrived at Loudwater alongside Grandus Hammerfell on Tarsakh 18, 1480. Their timely arrival allowed Loudwater to keep it's western entrance as well as sweep throughout the town and provide assistance to other battles where needed.

The battle for Loudwater ended shortly before high noon on Tarsakh 18 and Arcos Branikar kept himself busy throughout the rest of the day, gathering up and apprehending Iron Jackal prisoners as well directing where the remaining Loudwater Wardens should be stationed and what they should be doing.

A day later, after the murder of Grandus Hammerfell, Arcos Brannikar took up the mantle of leadership for the town of Loudwater.As of Flamerule 1, 1480 Arcos Brannikar has established himself as the Magistrate of Loudwater. 

Work continues on Castle Hammerfel to the southeast of Loudwater and Brannikar has invested heavily in a group of warriors called The Blades that he uses as he sees fit throughout the Grey Vale.

Brannikar also keeps a personal guard and for the last three months, no one can recall a time where they have seen Brannikar without them. Two men, both large and severe-looking as well as a slender man that some speculate is a wizard.
