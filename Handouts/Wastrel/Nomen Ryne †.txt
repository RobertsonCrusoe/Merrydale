Nomen is is a pale-scaled dragonborn hailing from  Tymanther.  It is unknown when he arrived in the Gray Vale but it has  been some time in the past year.  He was hired by Arcos Branikar to be  the “captain of the guard” for the the Over-Flow that Branikar and those  at his farm have taken upon themselves to tend to , protect and bully  where needed.

Nomen is a soft-spoken dragonborn that carries a  steel-shod flail. 

Although soft-spoken, he radiates menace and at times  and keeps all of those under his command in line.  A sell-sword hoped  to test Nomen when Branikar first made him “captain of the guard” in  attempt to wrest control for himself.

Although not often spoke of  there are tales that the sell-sword is still unable to walk and is now  paid by Branikar to make arrow heads while permanently bound to a stool.

==Death==
Nomen was torn in two by an unknown assailant while defending Maeghuen Grimbold early in the morning of Tarsakh 18, 1480.
