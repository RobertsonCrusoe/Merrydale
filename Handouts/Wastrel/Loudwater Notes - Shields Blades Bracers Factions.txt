However, over the last three months (much more detail to follow on the proboard) there is an organized group of warriors run by the Tormtar, Sheppard and they fall into the following categories.

"Shields" \- The "Shields" are the "hearthguard".  They are stationed ONLY in Loudwater.  They are NEVER sent outside of Loudwater.

"Blades" \- The "Blades" are a smaller group than the Shields.  A number of them are capable of mounted warfare and they tend to have a significant tie to the current leader of Loudwater, Arcos Brannikar.

"Bracers" \- The Bracers are run by Adamo Rei, a survivor of the Iron Jackal seige and trained in spellcraft and swordplay by the deceased Synsyrillion Miritar.  Adamo Rei is a local with a clear Netherese heritage.  He doesn't particularly answer to Brannikar BUT...he is a "loyal son of Loudwater".  The Bracers are a highly specialized group of men and women that tend to investigate the things that are above and beyond what the Loudwater Wardens would investigate.

Including Rei, there are six "Bracers".
