Zamomel Stass’s death in the ruins of the Towers Tyrannous at the hands of Szass Tam are clearly incorrect. Although reported to have been killed in 1142 DR, Zamomel Stass appeared at the side of the Witches of Rashemen in the battle that involved the destruction of Szass Tam’s mortal form in 1159 DR. Zamomel Stass certainly was not young when he first battled Szass Tam in the Towers Tyrannous. Although a birthdate for Stass cannot be confirmed, it is speculated that he was close to a century old when assisting the Witches in the destruction of Tam.}}
