Curuvar is a wizard that has been living in the Loudwater  region longer than any living resident currently in Loudwater and more  often than not, can be found sitting in his favourite corner at the  Green Tankard, drinking ale and smoking his pipe.

Curuvar is  known to be on the stand offish side with nearly everyone in Loudwater  save a very select few residents; one of them being Maeghuen Grimbold.   He is generally intolerant of most and is quick to judge someone as a  “bumbler”.  For nearly the last year, Curuvar has not been seen around  Loudwater often.  He did arrive  half a day after Maeghuen Grimbold was  returned to Loudwater in a near-death state.

Since the 8th of  Tarsakh, Curuvar has not been seen in Loudwater.  It was revealed by  Ilyaska Konig that Curuvar informed her that he needed to investigate a  \"matter of great import\" and then he would be contact The Ghosts  directly.

On the 10th of Tarsakh, it was revealed by Anaiya Ossur  that Curuvar was seen heading into the abandoned ruins of Castle  Loudwater (on the 8th of Tarsakh).  Curuvar offered her a handful of  sweets to keep her from telling people about where he was headed.

On  Tarsakh 16, 1480 an alarphon of the College of War Wizards, accompanied  by a Purple Dragon, both hailing from Cormyr seeking to speak with one,  Padreous Curuvarin.  It is currently the local wizard Curuvar and this  Padreous Curuvarin are the same individual.

As of Tarsakh 13, it  seems the last person to speak with Curuvar was Ciratto.  Curuvar's  current location is unknown and an animal messenger ritual failed to  result in contact with the wizard.

There is speculation that  Curuvar The Brazen might also be a once-War Wizard of Cormyr that was  known as Padreous Curuvarin.  Padreous Curuvarin was at one time, 2nd  Magister & First Assistant to the Chairman Emperius Ganrahast.
