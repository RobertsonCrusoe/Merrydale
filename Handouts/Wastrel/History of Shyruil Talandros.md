Shyruil Talandros, a half-elf born in Waterdeep in the year of 1228 DR.  Shyruil became a priestess of Selûne in her sixteenth year and in 1301 DR, she became the High Priestess for the Selûnite temple in Waterdeep.

Shyruil was and is still considered to be one of the more unremarkable High Priestesses that has controlled the Selûnites of Waterdeep save three exceptions that she is notoriously known for.
#She would spend significant amounts of time in the archives, pouring over ancient scrolls and tomes to the extent that she would miss prayers as well as ceremonies where she was expected to be present.
#She was responsible for accidentally burning down nearly a third of the archive, countless tomes and artifacts were destroyed as well as all records involving purchases, and expenditures since the temple had been built in Waterdeep.
#Although one of the longest living High Priestesses and still of her wits as well as bodily functions at a late age, Shyruil Talandros was found murdered in her private chambers in 1338 DR.  Much later, it was revealed that her body was found flayed of all its skin and the features of what was left of her face was contorted in a way that suggested she died in pain.  Rumors persist; although denied by the Selûnites of Waterdeep that Shyruil’s private chambers are cloaked in darkness and no manner of spell or any other solution has been able to dispel the darkness.  It is rumoured that her private chambers were walled away and the fact that all High Priests or Priestesses of the Waterdhavian order now reside in an entirely different tower.}}
