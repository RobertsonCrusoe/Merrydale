Immediately after Cedric was felled by his assassin, Mr. Spedicut found himself engaged with two of the bastards, and he had a chance to show his worth. Though the two assassins engaged the man expertly, William showed that he was much more than just a man of words, and put his hanger to expert use in defending himself.

After all the villains were slain and Cedric stabilized, I had a moment to talk with Mr. Spedicut, commend him on his swordplay and cool head, and ask his assistance in stripping our attackers of their valuables. It was then that we had a good chance to look at the faces of our attackers... For our attackers had the look of men from my homeland. At any rate, we also found that these men had masks concealed in their vestments, which are supposedly used as calling cards to mark their victims. These are the markings of the organization known as the Night Masks.
