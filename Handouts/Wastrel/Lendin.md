[PC Attribution: Lendin (Casey Bauman-Wike)]
The mysterious Lendin is said to be the last of the psychic warriors. One of the original Heroes of Freeport, Lendin later joined the crew of the Wastrel, and sailed as far as Amn before he decided to retire from adventuring, claiming that he was intent on openng a mercanary/bodyguard agency in a large port city there.
