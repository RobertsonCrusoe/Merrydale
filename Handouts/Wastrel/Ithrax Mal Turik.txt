A warlord of dragonborn descent, hailing from Returned Abeir.

Killed by The Seven during a skirmish in the ruins of Spellgard on the 21st of Mirtul.

Ithrax was put to sleep by an enchantment of Kethoth Sepret's. While enchanted and helpless, Mattrick Lychaeus and Quintus Lucretius Cato assaulted Ithrax and killed him.
