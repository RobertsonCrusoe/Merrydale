* AKA \"Starra\"

The proprietor of Starra’s Knives, Deanna “Starra” Star   hails from Tethyr.  She has long, black hair; very slender with a severe  edge to her beauty.  She often wears a relatively unknown form of make  up around her eyes that looks to be coal.  Although unusual, it does  make her blue-violet eyes appear to be that much more vivid and some of  the younger girls in town have tried to mimic “Starra” with generally  disastrous results.

Starra prefers to talk about knives.  All  types, how they’re made, how they feel and how one uses them.  Her  second topic of choice being all other weapons in general.  She is  generally friendly but one gets the feeling she is doing so simply  because she is speaking to a customer as opposed to actually being  friendly.

From time to time, Starra has been known to have a few  magical items and weapons for sale in her shop, something that irritates  Garwan Glimmerstone to no end.

Early in the morning of Tarsakh  18, it was revealed by a captured Indo Morningrock that Deanna “Starra”  Star was also a member of the Iron Jackals.  Upon visiting Starra's  store and place of residence, it was revealed that all of the store  items were gone and appeared as if Starra had left town.
