Unuruhl is a wilden that carries a large stone club across it's back. Krianolyrhh and Synsyrillio refer to Unuruhl as “he” or “him” so what citizens of Loudwater that have caught sight of the strange creature. It is unusual to see Unuruhl within the walls of Loudwater but whenever Ambassdor Hyperia leaves the settlement, the wilden appears within moments of the ambassador walking through the gate.

Unuruhl accompanied his companions, Krianolyrhh, Synsyrillio and The Ghosts into the ruins of Castle Loudwater to seek out information regarding Arya`lustrinne Miritar.

Unuruhl died while trying to hold back half-a-score of undead monsters while his beleaguered companions battled with Arya Nightweaver. His body was left down there.
