Little is known about the human that has claimed ownership of the Fisher Friend's Pub. Kyos took over the pub a little over two decades ago and he keeps to himself. Kyos is known to mutter to himself at times when he believes himself to be alone. When muttering to himself, it is generally in a language that no one seems to be able to understand. Kyos generally wears robes that are unkempt and stained with ale and food.

Kyos was killed during the battle for Loudwater. The adventurers found his headless corpse in the back of the Fisher Friend's Pub, having been killed by a unique group of Iron Jackal mercenaries only a short time before the adventurers arrived.
