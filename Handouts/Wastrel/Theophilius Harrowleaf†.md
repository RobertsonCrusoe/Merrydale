Captain Theophilius Harrowleaf, was appointed captain of  the Loudwater wardens and patrols by Lady  Illustryel Moonfire.   Although perceived by some as a bumbler, particularly in comparison to  Maeghuen Grimbold, Captain Harrowlead died in the defense of Maran's  Farm nearly two months ago.  Captain Grimbold said during his funeral  rites that it was a tragedy to lose him, and that Loudwater is worse off  without him.  There were more Woodsingers from the Southwood at Captain  Harrwoleaf's funeral than had ever been seen in Loudwater in over three  decades.
