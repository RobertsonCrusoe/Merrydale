A warlock of human descent, hailing from Daggerdale.

Killed by The Seven during a skirmish in the ruins of Spellgard on the 21st of Mirtul.

Nuryiel played a critical role initially in the skirmish as she had attacked Mattrick Lychaeus with the assistance of Stringer and managed to critically injure the priest of Kelemvor.

Later in the battle, Nuryiel was blasted into ruin by a barrage or arcane missiles originating from Kethoth Sepret.
