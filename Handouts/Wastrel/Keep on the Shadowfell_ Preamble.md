==A Start in Gmail==

From Gussy;

Your characters know each other and have decided to travel together in search of adventure and booty.  How, wy, etc is up to you to decide amongst yourselves.

The important part is that you now find yourselves traveling nor'easterly up the Winterhaven Road.

It is a few days after Midsummer, and you have been traveling long days and resting short nights.  It is now getting on into the late afternoon and early evening, but there are still plenty of daylight hours left and you've not given thought to stopping for the night just yet.

As you're walking along, you come across a young girl of no more than 15 years, crying and scared, hiding in a gully near the road.  After a little effort to calm her down and prove you mean her no harm, she explains that she became lost and separated from her traveling companions.  They were returning to the village of Winterhaven after a successful market day in the larger town of Fallcrest.  As her and her companions were traveling along on what has always been a safe road, they were surprised and attacked by monstrous bandits and she fled into the surrounding woods.  After almost the entire day lost in the woods, she managed to find her way to this road, where you now find her, all alone, desperate and upset.

right|450px|Thumb|A girl...It is always a girl...

Spying the weapons and armour you're carrying, she pleads with you to help her find her companions and deal with the bandits, and then excort her back to the town.

And that's where we begin.

Oh yeah, girl's name is Kaily and her picture is attached.
