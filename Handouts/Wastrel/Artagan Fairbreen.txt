Ranger, and local inhabitant of Loudwater area. One of two surviving members of the Fairbreen Family.

Follower of Malar, and Assuran in the last hours of his life.


==Death==
10th of Tarsakh, 1480.  Perished with many others in the secret crypt beneath an old ruined temple of Tymora.  Was slain by the vampire known as The Nightweaver.

==Regrets==
Artagan was at odds with himself in the last 24 hours of his life. After taking (and delivering) a beating in the warehouse that would become the Loudwater Hostel, he was approached by the enigmatic and stunning Zindyl Aka in his convalescence.  She offered herself and her faith (Assuran) to Artagan, and he accepted both.  Artagan made a plea to keep his medallion of Malar, but Zindyl would not let him keep it.  Never able to stand up to the women in his life, he abandoned his faith in Malar, and accepted Zindyl's offer.  This decision weighed heavy on his soul until the very last breath he took, 24 hours later.

After his faith-changing night with Zindyl, Artagan and the Ghosts met to discuss a plan of action.  Artagan made a case for rescuing Mayor Moonfire from the Iron Jackals.  However, Otra and Azalia both insisted upon investigating the ruins by Ossur's Farm.  Otra was acting to aid the search for Arya with the Eladrin who had come looking for her, and Azalia wished to investigate a missing patrol of wardens.  In the end, Artagan again bowed to the women that ruled his fate.
