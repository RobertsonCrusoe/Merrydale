The gregarious mayor of Loudwater, and close friend of Kethoth.
 
The half-elven mayor of Loudwater. Illustryel is a star-pact warlock but it is not something that she is keen to put on display for the residents of Loudwater. Illustryel is known for her ability to throw a party, and to dote over adventurers using Loudwater as a way point in their journey rather than being known for her strengths at public policy and governing the settlement of Loudwater. It is widely believed that were it not for Maeghuen Grimbold, Loudwater would be pounded into the soil by the enemies arrayed it against it. Illustryel is the mayor of Loudwater because no one else wants the task.

Illustryel had taken a tiefling mage by the name of Kethoth Sepret for a lover and there was much speculation regarding who was using the other one to their own advantage more in the coupling. Six months ago, upon the arrival of Ambassador Niidross Hyperia, it took precisely a fortnight for Illustryel to lure him into her bed and they have been a couple since.

Recently, Loudwater was invaded and during the chaos, Lady Moonfire was kidnapped by brigands of the Iron Jackals.

On Tarsakh 19, 1480, the Ghosts of Loudwater journeyed to Everblight Farm on the information obtained through the interrogation of Indo Morningrock. Indo revealed that Everblight Farm was a desgnated spot for the Iron Jackals to regroup if they needed to retreat.

After a significant battle with surviving Iron Jackals, Lady Illustryel was found in an upstairs bed room. She was naked and bound to a bed but had clearly been taken over by some manner of fell and alien entity. She had become bonded to a terrifying looking, amorphous and tentacled blob and attacked her rescuers. The large, and alien-like creature nearly defeated the Ghosts of Loudwater but in the end, the foul entity that Lady Illustryel had bonded with was destroyed, killing Lady Illustryel with it.

Adminster Crane speculated that a month of torture and rape at the hands of the Iron Jackals had caused Lady Illustryel to reach out to some manner of aberrant patron not of this realm in order to find some manner of help. The help she received might not have been the help she desired.
