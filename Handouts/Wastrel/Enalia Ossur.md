At four years old, she is the youngest daughter of  Irst Ossur.  She carries the same traits as all the Ossur girls.  Quiet,  with lustrous brown hair and pale blue eyes. 

She has a solemn and  serious air about her although she will crack a smile from time to time  if she knows her father, uncles, brothers or cousins are not around.She seems to have taken a shine to Edward.
