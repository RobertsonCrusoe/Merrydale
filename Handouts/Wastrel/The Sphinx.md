\"Why do they call him the Sphinx?  Well...I s'pose it's 'cuz you don't always know what'cher gonna get with'im. A sphinx is a lion, a bird and a person and above all you're never really sure what it's intentions are.  He's like that. He's a bit o'this, and a bit o'that.  He's just as likely to shoot fire from his hands as he is to stab you through the heart with a knife.  He's bold enough to walk right up to the open Lord of Waterdeep and cut out his heart, but he might just wait in the shadows instead, invisible and plotting.  Make no mistake, whatever he is...whoever he is...he's dangerous.  Maybe not so much to the likes o'you n'me but watch where you tread. He don't strike me as the type that takes kindly to those that step on his boots.\" , Gamling Brodar - 2nd Lieutentant, Falcon's Roost - as told to Volund Heimdal and Enderion Gateward.

The infamous leader and founder of The Mercykillers.  

Brought to light by the now deceased, Sandoval Luchra, Leirran Grennirath, better known as Steele, is rumored to be The Sphinx.
