Information collected by Loremaster Kennyth Thames while studying in The Monastery of Sundered Faith in 1381 DR, The Year of the Starving

thumb

I have not been able to gather information from anyone that has ever visited The Spire, nor seen it for that matter.  Many would believe there is little reason to believe The Spire is little more than a fanciful name for an unusual outcropping of rocks in the Graypeaks but I see more.  Be it tenuous and wispy at best, there are enough smoky tendrils of information to make believe that there is a fire burning somewhere and I simply need to look in the right place.  I believe The Spire does exist and I believe it is some manner of staging point for expeditions to venture into Anauroch.

I believe that The Spire is controlled and funded by the temple of Selûne in Waterdeep and has been around since roughly 1150 DR.  I would like to postulate that Selûnites built The Spire at the eastern edge of the Graypeak mountains in order to observe the ruins of ancient Netheril located in Anauroch.

Mention of The Moonstars suggests that was the title given to the Selûnites that were sent on expeditions in the name of Selûne.  There is extremely little references to these Moonstars or what they managed to accomplish for that matter but from what I have been able to gather, the Moonstars were sent where situations were extremely dire and they were able to travel to their destinations in a manner that suggests it was extremely quick, if not instantaneous.

The realms are a strange and wondrous place and although rare, the existence of portals, gateways and other phenomena exist that allows one to travel a great distance in a short period of time.  It is possible that The Spire has one of these portals to facilitate a quick transfer of the Moonstars to where they are needed.
References to The Spire are sparse at best but as of 1338 DR, any and all references to The Spire ceased to exist.}}
