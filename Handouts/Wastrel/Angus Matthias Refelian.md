Angus Matthias took over the High Road Metal Works at the young age of 20, after his apprenticeship was completed. Old Waldorf, well into his venerable years, stepped into semi-retirement by founding the sister business, the High Road Silver Company. As Waldorf is fond of saying, \"Tin is the business, silver is the art.\" Waldorf has made a new name for himself by returning to his roots as a silversmith, and crafting exceptional quality, custom made, luxury silver goods for the pampered classes of Waterdeep. Refelian High Road Silver has become the standard measure of quality in the city. Meanwhile, in Angus Matthias's care, High Road Metal Works continues its strong dominance in its chosen market.

In fact, the success of High Road Metal Works has entered a new chapter under Angus Matthias, for he was the inventor, and developer, of what has come to be known as the Refelian Can. These ingenious cylindrical containers of varying sizes, vaccum sealed with cork, have begun to revolutionise the exportation of food stuffs and other perishables throughout the Sword Coast. With the Refelian Cans, it has become possible to export various fruits, meats, vegetables and liquids over great distances without resorting to expensive magical means. But the initial success of the cans came from the adventuring quarter. Potions, long since the literal life-blood of many a sellsword, could now be carried in extremely inexpensive and durable containers. Although scarcely a year old, the tin cans are quickly replacing the vulnerable and heavy glass bottles, as well as the expensive silver and steel vials, that had been a staple of the Waterdhavian adventurer. The widespread promise of the Refelian Can alone is sure to secure the fortunes of this family for generations to come.
