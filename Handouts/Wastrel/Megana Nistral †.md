Megana is the tall, broad-shoulders and “big-boned” blacksmith of Loudwater. Her father, Albus Nistral was killed by dark creepers and ettercaps nearly a year ago at the Nistral family farm. Megana had earned herself a reputation of having a soft spot for brawny warriors and had also earned herself a reputation of being spurned by brawny warriors that she showed her interest in. That all changed about a year ago when a holy warrior of Kelemvor showed her kindness beyond measure (according to Megana), and he gave her a sense of peace with the violent death of her father. Since then, she has kept to the smithy and never hesitates to remind people that her heart belongs to Baird of Kelemvor, a man with a heart as kind and strong as he is far from his homeland.  Something that causes the residents of Loudwater to remind each other that Megana is indeed a blacksmith and not a bard.

Recently, Megana has been angered by the Warden's inability or lack of concern regarding the fact that her farm south of Loudwater has been taken by Iron Jackals. Megana managed to recruit and bully five local men (dockworkers) to ride with her to take back her farm. It ended poorly. Three of the men were killed, and Megana was captured.

The farm was liberated and Megana freed from captivity a day later by The Ghosts. Megana was found in her barn, a yoke around her neck, and broad shoulders, bound to the wall and stripped naked.

Megana had been beaten and violated in a number of awful ways and was in a terrible state of shock when rescued.

Upon returning to Loudwater, Zindyl Aka played a significant role in Megana's rapid recovery who now seems incredibly focused on wreaking vengeance on the Iron Jackals.

Megana was killed during the battle for Loudwater on the 16th of Tarsakh. She was caught up in a fight that far exceeded her skill as a warrior and although she played a role in the rescuing of Sergeant Magnus Zane, Megana perished after taking a crossbow bolt through her throat.
