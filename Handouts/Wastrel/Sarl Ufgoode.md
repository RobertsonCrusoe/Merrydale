Rivermaster Ufgoode is a human in his forties with more grey hair than brown. Although thin and small, the man never seems to slow down and there seems to be some manner of romantic connection between him and Calla Maran. 

Rivermaster Ufgoode runs the docks of Loudwater and keeps everything running orderly depsite the quality and disposition of the workers.

Ufgoode is a childhood friend of the itinerant warrior Harrigan Farshore.
