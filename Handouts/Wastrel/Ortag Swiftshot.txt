A ranger of orcish descent, hailing from north of Luskan.

Killed by The Seven during a skirmish in the ruins of Spellgard on the 21st of Mirtul.

Ortag was injured early on in the fight with The Seven and while trying to recover in the rain-soaked ruins where the battle was taking place, she was assaulted by Enderion Greenward. Their battle ended with the halfling slitting Ortag's throat while she lay incapacitated on the ground.
