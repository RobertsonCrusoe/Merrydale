Ightas was the second youngest of the four Ossur brothers. Ightas`role was one of a scout and information gatherer in the Vale.

Up until sunrise of the 16th of Tarsakh, Ightas had never been seen by members of The Ghosts. Ightas was seen with his brother Issen that morning, standing between Loudwater and a horde of fel monsters, beasts and humanoids. The host of monsters was arrayed to the south of Loudwater and when they charged to attack, Ightas and Issen opened up the ground beneath the host and left them to be attacked by a horde of undead within the large hole.

Ightas was last seen, standing on the edge of the hole, fighting undead with his two handed sword before slipping and falling into the hole. It is assumed that Ightas is dead, killed by the undead, the Iron Jackal monsters or a combination thereof.
