Magnus Zane has just celebrated his twentieth year but is currently the sergeant in charge of the North-West region patrol, commonly referred to as the Grey Patrol.

Not particularly the most creative of names but given due to the time the patrol spends amidst the ruins of Castle Loudwater.

Magnus' father, Ignatius Zane was the former sergeant of the Grey Patrol. There was some doubt initially regarding Magnus' ability to command the Grey Patrol at such a young age but he shows skill far beyond his years and has done an exceptional job of commanding for the past two years.

Magnus, and his father Ignatius are two of a small group of people in Loudwater that speak highly of the Ossur family and see them as more than just pale-faced, creepy worshippers of Kelemvor.

Around highsun on the 9th of Tarsakh, Magnus Zane was found bleeding and near death at the edge of Loudwater. Brought into the town proper by refugees that had been living outside of the town. Magnus was brought back from the brink of death by the skilled hands of the odd woman by the name of Azalea Winter. While being carried to Maeghuen Grimbold`s house so that Magnus could be tended to by Ilyaska Konig, Magnus was heard murmuring, `...Nightweaver`. This was overheard by Synsyrillio Miritar whom immediately left after hearing this.

Magnus Zane was killed by Santos Dane and Iron Jackals on Tarsakh 18, 1480.
