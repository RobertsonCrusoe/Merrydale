After the brief battle in the torture chambre, Kethoth gathers all the party about him, once the convalescence of the priestesses has begun.

\"I wanted to have a talk with the whole lot of you about how we are going to go about proceeding into the Monastery. After interrogating that orc, I perceive that we are about to embark upon our biggest challenge yet. We need to shape up our tactics and behaviour before we can proceed.

\"The first thing we have to do is pace ourselves. We will not have the benefit of rest for quite sometime, I am afraid; this place is crawling with hundreds of orcs, and we will not have time to recuperate between battles as we have become accustomed to.

\"Lucretius or I will use signals in combat to indicate how hard we must fight; If we yell 'Paced Battle' then make sure that you do indeed pace yourselves. This means no running into the fray and fighting like it is the last fight of your life, or expending any other party resources that we might need later.

\"If we yell 'Fight Hard' then it is time to do just that. I doubt very much you will hear me yell this until we see the likes of Mordja, Rahnok, or even Gokar 'The Blue'. We need to pace ourselves until these battles because we must now use speed to our advantage, and hit this place hard before we are found out.

\"We will also focus our fire, especially our ranged fighters, against a target of my choosing. Once I choose a target, we will attack it mercilessly until it is dead or until I nominate another target. There will be no more questioning of my orders in battle or otherwise. We must now leave behind whatever personal goals or quests for glory that one might yet retain. We will not survive otherwise.

\"There will be no more bickering over shares of loot. We have until now always entrusted the divvying of spoils to me, and all of you have grown wealthy as a result, and there is the lion's share yet to come. I know you all well, and will share out useful gear to the greatest benefit to the party, not the individual.

\"We will drive towards a common goal, and fight until it's conclusion. That goal is this; the liberation of the Monastery of Sundered Faith and the enslaved and tortured residents herein. After that, we will make our inquiry about the horns of Eltab, and thereafter we will discuss new goals and rostering

\"This may sound harsh and dictatorial to some of you, our newer members especially. However, this endeavour has always been mine, and this is not a democracy. We must work as one body, with one mind; my mind, my command.

\"I have always welcomed your input and respected your personal goals, and I shall continue to do so. In fact, Tyriol and Ender will continue to act as the eyes of this unified body, and others as the hands and feet. I will remain the head. 

\"At this very moment, I will give democracy it's only breath in this endeavour; Any of you who don't wish to follow me until the conclusion of this noble liberation of good men and women from the clutches of villainy, say so now and be free to go as you wish. 

\"Any of you that will call me 'Captain', and respect what I have just said, and work towards this liberation, pledge so now, and let us set about flushing these green-skinned devils from this most Holy of Houses.\"
