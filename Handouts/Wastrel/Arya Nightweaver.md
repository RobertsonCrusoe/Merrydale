== House Miritar ==
'''Aryaluystrinne Miritar''' was born and raised in Mithrendain within her home realm of the Feywild. There, she was a pampered member of the Royal Court within Mithrendain with much of her family's notoriety coming from her uncle [[Seiveril Miritar]]. Seiveril led the retaking of Myth Drannor in 1374 DR and purged the once great but ruined city of the demons and monsters that had called it their home for centuries. He was killed during the later stages of the reclamation of Myth Drannor and for the last century, Myth Drannor and the Eladrin force now occupying Myth Drannor is led by Seiveril's daughter whom has achieved the impressive rank of Coronal in the Eladrin empire. [[Ilsevele Miritar]] is an intelligent, intolerant, ferocious leader that keeps a tight reign on the newly reformed Myth Drannor and when not battling evil that crawls out of the depths of the city and surrounding forest even after nearly a century and a half of "purging" ; she is kept busy with reforging relatioships with the Dalelands and Cormyr as well as keeping a close eye on the ambitious and undisputedly evil realm of Netheril.

== Apprenticeship ==
This was a world that Aryaluystrinne Miritar had heard about from when she was quite small and at one time when she was in her fourteenth year, she met and spoke with her esteemed cousin Ilsevele. She was enamoured with the tales that Ilsevele had shared with her and the very next day Aryaluystrinne insisted upon learning the ways of the Sword Mage in the hopes that in time she would be allowed to join her cousin and take up the fight in Myth Drannor and the strange but assuredly exciting world that it was a part of.

Her parents showed no small amount of reluctance when it came to allowing her to learn the ways of combat. They had already begun to take steps to keep their lovely daughter in the Royal Court of Mithrendain and train her in the ways of courtly politics and the managing of kingdoms as opposed to the stabbing and burning of demons and mortal creatures that were little more than a pox upon the land that they tread.

Aryaluystrinne took to learning the ways of the Sword Mage in secret and although following the direction of her parents, she continued to long for the opportunity to leave the Feywild and experience the realm that she had so often thought about since her early years.

== A Name for Herself ==
In her forty-fifth year, although considered by some, she was barely out of her "teens" in the Feywild. She had spent years planning and plotting. Having grown weary of the royal courts and the sedentary lifestyle expected of her she had learned of a portal that would bring her close to the forests of Cormanthor and Myth Drannor but allow her time to create a name for herself. By proving her value and skill, her cousin would never insist on sending her back to the courts and her family in Mithrendain.

She had already settled on a "name" that would be more "suitable" in the realm of Toril and had dreams of being known as Arya Nightweaver, a name that struck fear in the hearts of the enemies of the realms and the Myth Drannor empire. In time she would be even greater known than her cousin and her uncle. The changing of her name would also allow herself some anonymity incase someone she encountered knew of the name Miritar and the history that went along with it.

== Beyond the Portal ==

In her forty-sixth year, on the day after the celebration of such, she packed what gear she had accumulated and spirited away into the wilderness outside of Mithrendain. It was a radical change from what she had previously experienced and far more different and challenging that she had planned.

The portal she had planned on using was no where to be found after half a day's journey and she was afraid that she was being followed. The silhouette in the distance that she would spot from time to time did not seem like someone sent from the royal court to find her and seemed far, far more sinister. Anxiety and fear weighed on her more heavily than the supplies and gear she carried on her slender shoulders and it was with no small amount of relief and joy when she found the portal she had been seeking and boldly stepped through it into the world she craved to be a part of.

Aryaluystrinne had found the wrong portal however and she emerged into a secluded grove hundreds and hundreds of miles to the west. She was in the Gray Vale and directly north of her was a wall of dark, twisted trees that caused her nature-tuned Eladrin soul to tie into knots so in desperation, she headed south.

The harrowing trip in the Feywild seemed like a lark compared to the fear she felt as she traveled alone in a forest; in a realm that she knew nothing about and she gripped her sword, white-knuckled for the entirety of the day that she traveled south. Upon reaching the forest's edge, she spied what she perceived to be a crude and primitive settlement further to the south.

Having battled with her fear, irritated with herself for being afraid and coming to grips with such, she headed for the unknown settlement of [[Loudwater]] in order to better understand where she was and further understand her place in this new world.
