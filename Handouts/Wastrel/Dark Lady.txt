Revealed to two separate members of The Ghosts on two  separate occasions.  The Dark Lady was mentioned to Ciratto by Calla  Maran.  She revealed that henchmen of \"The Dark Lady\" would come to her  place of business and try to extort coin from her as payment for  \"protection\" from bandits of Llorkh.

She was also mentioned to  Biggs by Kyos, the owner of the Fisher Friend's Pub and henchmen of hers  approached him, much the same as they had approached Calla Maran.

Time  spent questioning dock workers and ne'er-do-wells in Loudwater revealed  that The Dark Lady was involved with a number of what might be  considered petty crimes but also was a competitor to the bandits of  Llorkh when it came to the slave market.

The Dark Lady was not  heard of until a little more than twelve months ago.  The Ghosts with  help from the poor and downtrodden of Loudwater have managed to locate  the warehouse on the docks where it is suspected The Dark Lady operates  out of.

It has been revealed that The Dark Lady most likely hails  from the Shadowfel.  Her and her slaving ring was done away with by The  Ghosts on the 9th of Tarsakh.  While the slaving ring was definitely  defeated, The Dark Lady did put up a ferocious fight and in the end,  escaped the justice that The Ghosts were looking to dispense.

While  The Ghosts were doing battle with Zanin Redthorn; The Fist (Captain) of  the Iron Jackals and the Cormyrean knight known as Palomides, The Dark  Lady appeared a number of times, slashing at the backs and throats of  the Iron Jackals.  No attempt was made by The Ghosts to do battle with,  or apprehend The Dark Lady.
