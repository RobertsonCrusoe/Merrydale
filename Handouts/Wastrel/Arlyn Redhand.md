Half-elf and best friend of Tyriol Grimbold.  Where Tyriol had his speed and wits, Arlyn had plenty of brawn and the two of them managed to get into no small amount of trouble when they were younger...or the past week for that matter.

Arlyn's brawn and ability with a broadsword had caught the eye of a charming mercenary by the name of Devlin.  Where Tyriol was under strict command from his mother to never leave Loudwater with an adventuring company, Arlyn was under no such obligations.

Devlin hired an eager Arlyn to join the Free Company of the Crescent and head east to the ruins of Spellgard to find the fabled spirit there that grants wishes and answers any question directed to it.

This filled Tyriol with no small amount of jealousy and a fortnight after Arlyn had left for Spellgard, Tyriol had an opportunity to take part in a journey with tiefling that had been in Loudwater for close to a month by the name of Kethoth Sepret.  Tyriol jumped at the chance and this began the creation of the adventuring party known eventually as The Seven.

Arlyn did not have a great deal of good fortune with the Free Company of the Crescent.  Arlyn and his companions encountered a vampire by the name of Barthas.  The vampire fell upon the group while they were setting up camp within the ruins of Spellgard late one evening.  The vampire appeared to be enraged and mercilessly killed the group of six adventurers in a matter of a few heartbeats.

A few days later, Tyriol and his companions encountered and were attacked by foul, undead creations that consisted of the the skins of humanoids that appeared to slither and shift across whatever surface they were moving across.

After defeating the abominations, Tyriol discovered that one of these creatures was the skin of his friend Arlyn, peeled from him and animated through the dark energies of some vile ritual or arcane science.

While much of the party was recovering, or disturbed by the fight with the foul undead creations, Kethoth Sepret gathered the skins of the destroyed abominations and stored them in a trunk.

Over time, Tyriol grew more and more disturbed by the thought that his friend was \"rolled up and stored away\" in a wizard's trunk.  In a quiet moment during the siege of Kohr'Murron, Tyriol; with the assistance of one of the surviving wardens of Loudwater broke into Kethoth's trunk and absconded with the remains of Arlyn Redhand.
