Indo is a Halfling hailing from Zelbross and most recently an escaped slave from Llorkh. Indo is generally sullen as far as Halflings go but considering his past, many of Loudwater are willing to give him some leeway if he’s not the most friendly. He sleeps in the cellar of the Green Tankard and is now the main cook for the Tankard. Despite the grim atmosphere in Loudwater of late, Indo’s cooking has ensured that the taproom of the Tankard is filled with far more people than usual.

It was believed by Ciratto of The Ghosts that Indo was the individual responsible for having attempted to pick the lock of Cirrato's chest while staying at the Green Tankard. Ciratto had trapped his chest with poison and the next day, Indo took up ill; much to Marsh Laval's disappointment.

Ciratto gave Marsh the antidote to the poison and told Marsh to give him the antidote hoping to confirm his suspicions that it was indeed Indo that had tried to open his chest.

Ciratto became busy and checked in with Marsh two days later. He met Marsh who was frustrated and angry over the fact that Indo has completely disappeared.

After searching Indo's sleeping quarters in the basement of the Green Tankard, Ciratto surmised that it looked exactly what his own quarters would look like if he was living somewhere and had to \"leave in a hurry\".

Indo Morningrock was captured by The Ghosts early in the morning on Tarsakh 16. Indo had played a role with Easak Dromm in lighting the hostel on fire and was attempting to escape when apprehended.

Indo was questioned by The Ghosts after having been threatened violently by Merrick Brash. Indo revealed what the Iron Jackals had planned for their attack that morning, as well as revealed that Deana Star was an Iron Jackal.

Indo is currently alive and imprisoned in Loudwater.
