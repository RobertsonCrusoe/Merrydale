Adamo Rei is a member of the Loudwater Wardens and up until the recent death of Synsirillio Miritar, was learning to become a swordmage and out of the three mentoring under the eladrin; Adamo showed the most promise.Adamo is one-quarter Bedine although his parents were born and raised in Loudwater. Adamo's father, Andai was also a Warden and died while on duty a little over a decade ago. His mother, Lienn passed away only recently due to illness.Adamo; although many a warrior would still consider him \"green\", carried himself well during the battle against the Iron Jackals.On Tarsakh 18, 1480 it was Adamo that discovered some manner of arcane circle involved in a ritual close to Maeghuen Grimbold's house. Unfortunately, further information to be gleaned from the circle was beyond Adamo's skill.On Mirtul 10, 1480; Adamo established a sub-group of Loudwater Wardens that were called The Bracers.
