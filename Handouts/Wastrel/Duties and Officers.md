== Ranks aboard the Wastrel ==

COMMAND 
*Captain: Commands the ship; responsible for sailing, discipline, and battle command 
*1st Lieutenant: Captain's assistant; commands the ship in Captain's absence

WARRANT SEA OFFICERS 
*Master: Navigates and pilots close to the coastline & on rivers 
*Boatswain: Responsible for rigging, sails, anchor, and ropes 
*Carpenter: Repairs 
*Surgeon: Healing 
*Purser: Supplies

LIEUTENANTS 
*All of these people assist the Captain with orders

INFERIOR WARRANT OFFICERS 
*Chaplain: Create water, purify food, cure, everyone will love you 
*Cook: The first hobbit on board 
*Schoolmaster: Teach the barbarian to read 
*Surgeon's mates: help the surgeon 
*Armorer: All metalwork 
*Sailmaker: Also the tailor I guess 
*entering or leaving port

PETTY OFFICERS 
*Steward: In charge of the money 
*Yeomen of the sheets: Kept the sails in order 
*Trumpeter: Bin 
*Quartermaster: Supplies, also takes over the helm when the ship is 
*Coxwain: Looks after the boats 
*All of the mates: Assist other duties

IDLERS 
*Steward's mate, Carpenter's crew, Sailmaker's crew

SEAMEN 
*These men sail the ship and are organized into watches

MARINES 
*These are the fighting men
