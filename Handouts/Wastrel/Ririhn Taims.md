Although Ririhn is clearly of Netherese descent, the Taims family has been in the Loudwater area since before the Spellplague.

Ririhn is the sergeant of the South-West patrol. Despite the fact that the South-West patrol is the least dangerous of the patrols and often referred to as \"Easy March\", Ririhn is a respected warden and runs a very tight group of wardens.
