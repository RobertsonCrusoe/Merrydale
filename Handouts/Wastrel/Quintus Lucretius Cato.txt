Stalwart and steadfast, dragonborn tactician of The Seven.  While travelling with The Seven and upon returning from Spellgard, Lucretius suffered a devastating head injury during the battle with The Red Hand at Kohr'Murron Stables.  While battling with hobgoblins and a hill giant, holding the line at the main gate of the stables, a large stone tower collapsed upon Lucretius.  Pulled free and somehow able to battle on afterwards, his injuries caught up with him shortly after the first battle.  Unwilling to rest and insisting on assisting Mattrick Lychaeus in the digging of Baird's grave, Lucretius fell unconcious and slipped into a coma shortly after his brother-in-arms has been buried and funerary rites performed.  His injuries were far beyond what Mattrick or the local priest was capable of tending to and Lucretius was left resting in a secluded stone tower in a coma while the battle continued in and around Kohr'Murron Stables.

Having been waylaid by a terrible head injury and unable to participate in the destruction of Barthas, with the loss of Arya and Mattrick, the party returned to find Lucretius lucid and on the mend once more.

Lucretius accompanied the party to the Monastery of Sundered Faith.  Upon completing their research their and setting out for the next stage of the journey to seek out the Horns of Eltab, Lucretius declined to travel any further, stating that he wished to stay at the monastery and assist in the rebuilding and protection of the monastery.
