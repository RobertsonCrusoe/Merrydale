Maeghuen Grimbold is a human residing in Loudwater in her thirty-ninth year. She is a retired adventurer, the \"true\" Captain of the Loudwater wardens and the mother of Tyriol Grimbold. She rarely spends time at the small but well-furnished home and is often in the company of Loudwater scouts as they patrol the surrounding area.          

She is a proud woman and ferociously protective of her son who seems to be a bit of a free-spirit; something that she was also rumored to be yet Tyriol nor anyone recently has ever seen evidence of such.

She never speaks of Tyriol's father, having only shared small amounts of information with her son in rare moments of weakness. She has shared the following with Tyriol.        

* Tyriol's father is indeed an elf, not only an elf but one of wood elf descent.

* Tyriol's father's name is \"Leirran\"

* Tyriol's father is a man that Tyriol should avoid at all costs.

Maeghuen was born in the Loudwater area to her father, Mollahn Bremen and her mother Cyriph (Seer-iff) Grimbold. Mollahn was a hardworking stone mason who rarely saw eye to eye with his strong-willed and beautiful daughter while Cyriph doted on her daughter. Cyriph would regularly tell Maeghuen tales of her adventurous great-great-grandfather, Edgar Grimbold and how he fought dragons and evil mages all while taking the time to instruct her daughter, Maeghuen how to use a bow. Mollahn and Cyriph were killed by orc raiders from the Graypeaks when she was fifteen and she raised herself from that point on. When Maeghuen was sixteen and being pursued by a number of suitors, she disappeared. She was last seen in the company of two elves at the Green Tankard tavern; one that was bald with a face that was frighteningly scarred and the other with eyes as pale blue and cold as the sky in Hammer, and with long, pure white hair. Maeghuen returned when she was nineteen years of age with a newborn in her arms by the name of Tyriol. Maeghuen never seems to want for coin and prefers to pay for things with small, expertly cut gemstones and run an account until there's no credit left and then simply uses another gemstone. Tyriol always had food and clothing when he needed it and although they lived in a small house, it was always well-furnished and well-tended to. Rumors in town persist that Maeghuen had been whisked away to be made an elven bride in the Feywild but had a falling out with her elven husband and the gems with which she pays for everything are gems sent to her by her husband who wishes for her to return to his side once more. What's twenty years to an elf in the Feywild right? It's like yesterday to them.                          

==1480==
Maeghuen Grimbold; now in her 40th year is beginning to show her age. Still a great beauty, and still as skilled at woodcraft, archery and the leading of men, it seems that she has lost a step or two in the past year. Upon returning to Loudwater nine months ago she was dismayed to find out that Captain Harrowlead had been slain by well-organizedbandits in a battle at Everblight Farm. Maeghuen returned to find well over half of her Loudwater Wardens killed over the last twelve months and began taking greater and greater risks to secure the land around Loudwater from the forces that appear to be gathering around it.

Two weeks ago, while attempting to reclaim Everblight Farm and rescue the mayor of Loudwater from the bandits responsible for it, Captain Grimbold was struck down by nearly a dozen arrows. The Wardens that managed to survive, returned to Loudwater with Captain Grimbold near death.

Currently, Captain Grimbold is still in a state near death. She is being tended to by Brother Griffon, and Curuvar the Brazen but the healing needed is beyond either of their skill. A request for help has been sent to Secomber as well as Falcon`s Roost in the hopes that a healer of skill can be found there.

Recently; Ilya Konig, a young but skilled healer had been brought to Maeghuen's bedside by the group known as The Ghosts. Ilya has been able to stabilize Maeghuen for the moment but has expressed concerns that there is still something terribly wrong with Maeghuen and that a possible solution to Captain Grimbold's current state would be to retrieve wood from a tree that can be found in the Dire Wood called \"Heart Wood\".

On the 9th of Tarsakh, Ilyaska Konig revealed to her cousin Artagan that there was something `wrong`with Maeghuen Grimbold. Her health had been stabilized but since that time, she seems to be deteriorating once again.

==Death==
Early on the morning of Tarsakh 18, 1480 Maeghuen's home was assaulted by what was reported to be undead. It was unknown how they managed to get into the town but some speculate it had something to do with the arcane circle that Warden Adamo Rei had discovered. The reason for the attack is unknown but the undead killed everyone guarding her and then left her there in her home.

 
Nineteen days later, on Mirtul 7, 1480 Maeghuen Grimbold died from the strange affliction she was suffering from. She was cremated on a bier of wood provided by Woodsinger elves on Mirtul 10, 1480.
