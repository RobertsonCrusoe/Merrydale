Irst is the second oldest brother on the Ossur farm, a paladin of Kelemvor and the father of Anaiya and Enalia Ossur.

It was Irst and the youngest brother Ilyeo Ossur that came to the rescue of The Ghosts and the Miritars. Irst did battle with the Nightweaver and her undead minions, driving her away while his brother dragged those that hadn`t been killed or eaten to safety.
