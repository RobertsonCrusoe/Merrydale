KREE-ANNO-LEER)

A male, eladrin seeker that escorted Ambassador Hyperia on his mission. Krianolyrhh is generally quiet, and keeps away from the rest of the Loudwater citzenry. He has closely-cropped, seemingly silver-coloured hair. He wears no armour to speak of and moves seemingly without sound earning him the name, “The Ghost” from people within Loudwater. Krianolyrhh is often difficult to find within the walls of Loudwater and it has been said that he has been seen on foot, heading north-west in the direction of the Dire Wood.

On the 10th of Tarskah, Krianolyrhh accompanied his companions; Synsyrillio Miritar, the wilden known as Unuruhl and The Ghosts into the ruins of Castle Loudwater, hoping to find some information regarding their missing cousin, Arya'lustrinne Miritar. It was agreed that if The Ghosts helped Krianolyrrh with finding information about their cousin, the Miritar`s would assist with the defense of Loudwater and provide assistance for a future expedition into the Dire Wood by The Ghosts.

Krianolyrrh was one of the four survivors that came out the ruins after encountering and doing battle with Arya Nightweaver. 

Krianolyrrh played a key role in the defense of Loudwater against the Iron Jackals. On the second and final day of the seige, he was responsible for leading the Woodsinger elves in the defense of the East Gate. The Woodsingers did battle with a large group of hobgoblins from the Hand of Doom clan. The battle was going in the favour of the Woodsingers when a terrible construct was released, later to be identified as the Glass Assassin. Krianonlyrrh lost the Eastern Gate and his life while encountering the Glass Assassin.
