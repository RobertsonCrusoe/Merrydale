Information collected by Gritta Roaringhorne, priest of Oghma while studying in Candlekeep in 1314 DR, The Year of the Shadowtop

thumb

Hanahlaos; wizard or possible sorceress that worshipped Selûne sent a scroll to Waterdeep detailing a list of supplies that would be necessary for an expedition to The Towers Tyrannous.  The scroll was sent to High Priestess Shyruil Talandros; a priestess of Selûne. It is important to note that the scroll was written in an older form of Chondathan that has roots from at its earliest, five centuries old, possibly older.  The list consisted of very little in the way of food or supplies that most would consider for an expedition.  Instead it primarily consisted of obscure and expensive spell components.  At one point, Hanahlaos stated, “The journey east will feel expeditious to those being sent, but the components will be essential for the ritual to be effective and ensure that they have a way to return to us should their efforts prove successful.  If it is indeed a necromancer of power that resides in the Towers Tyrannous or even if it is a Netherese agent seeking to attain a foothold in Thay, our Moonstars will prove their worth and you will be in a better position to work our necessary agenda in the City of Splendours.”}}
