Garwan is a wizened, white-bearded dwarf that passes his  time running the “Curiosity Shop” in Loudwater.  He is on close terms  with Curuvar and it is rumored that Garwan was the one responsible for  finding the pipe that Curuvar is so often seen smoking in the Green  Tankard.
Glimmerstone and Heath Malmotte have an arrangement for preferential trade.
