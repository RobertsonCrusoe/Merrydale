We had spent a couple of nights trying to work out some connection bugs (I should make a post about how to port-forward your computer and all the other tech nonsense, but I won't), but eventually we all managed to get connected, and had a night to spend a few hours testing out the system.

Brash, Shepherd, Bramble and Mandelbrot escorted Kaily along the road leading to Winterhaven, and were ambushed by kobolds!

600px|right

This first session was exciting.  It is really fun to be in control of 1st level characters again, and although the format made for a bit of a disconnect from what we were used to, the session was really fun.

MapTool was a little clunky out of the box, but we all quickly identified that this program had nothing but possibilities.  I (Blacky) was able make a few macros on-the-fly which calculated my dice rolls and damage all at once.  By the end, it was actually moving along pretty well.

The only drawback to the entire session was Vent, which we have not used since.

In the end, we handilly beat the entire group of kobolds without one of us even becoming bloodied.  Gus would see to it that we wouldn't have as easy a time again...
