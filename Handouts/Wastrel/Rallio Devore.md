A tiefling.

Kethoth's commentary on Mr. Devore;

This is the worst sort of tiefling.  Mr. Devore is a self-loathing creature who is looking to find an answer to the question \"Who could love such a monster as I?\"  Well, I'll have to answer for Lady Saharel, as she has been absconded with to some nether-realm where I most certainly hope no ghost can be tortured and raped.  \"The answer to your question, Mr. Devore\" I would offer to this despicable creature \"Is that you must force others to love you.  Do so with your wits if you can, or with violent force otherwise.  

\"In fact, forced love is the only variety with any currency.  What is the value of something freely given?  Nothing!  You cannot go asking spirits for validation any more than you can go on bended knee to a woman and beg for love.\"

Thoran had the right idea; he was going to enslave the spirit and force her to do his bidding.  I may do just that yet; I have everything he required to do the job, and Kossur knows I have the time.

And as regards those women still in flesh, sweet sweet woman-flesh, Kethoth would instruct the snivelling Mr. Devore to find love by way of forcing himself upon her.  It is only when a lover resists with all her might, and that each screaming inch is a tooth-and-nail victory, that love is worth having.

Devore, Rallio
