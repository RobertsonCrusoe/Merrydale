This is all fantastic, boys!

I now regret not giving you the chance to receive your poem at the fair, but perhaps it is for the best since I doubt Adelard would've taken too kindly to your words. I think he probably would've been more upset at being referred to as Lardy, than having his poisonous plot revealed for all to hear.

I wanted to add a couple of quick things:

1. Turnip, with an Intelligence of 14 also learned the Remove Fear spell. Attached are your spells for easy reference.

I'm still thinking about whether I will allow Boffo's Purify Food and Drink to also be reversed into something like Spoil Food and Drink. I'd like to, just because I don't see why this spell can't also be reversed, but I also think that it would be anathema for a hobbit to wilfully spoil food. We'll have to come to it when we come to it, and see under what circumstances Boffo does it.

I will say that since there are no Clerics, I have shuffled all spells into just one big list. So you've learned some spells that are not wholly Arcane, but I don't really care for that distinction.


2. Attached is your character class. I tried to make it as legible as possible using the info from OSE books.

As 1st level characters you will roll another d6 for Hit Points next session. You get to keep your starting 6 hit points as a base. Because there is only 3 of you, and the game is deadly, I wanted to give you that little bit of extra survivability as a starting bonus since you will not have access to a cleric.

We're using the Expertise rules for the Thief Skills. These are official OSE optional rules which change rolling percentile dice and replace it with rolling a d6. It also lets you improve the skills you want to improve as you level up, but I will say that you have to actually have used the skill you want to improve in order to improve it. I'm not going to let you improve Pick Pockets if you've never picked a pocket, for example.

The same Expertise rule brings Read Languages up to a 1st level skill (from 4th) and can also be improved. However, I am going to leave Read Languages as a 4th level skill gain, because you've not had any real exposure to other languages yet. I like leaving that as an acquired skill.

3. I'm still debating on whether to continue using THAC0 or switch to the modern Ascending AC rules.
