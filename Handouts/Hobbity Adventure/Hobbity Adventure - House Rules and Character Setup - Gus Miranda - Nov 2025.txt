A Hobbity Adventure - House Rules and Character Setup
Compiled from campaign emails by Gus Miranda
23-26 November 2025
Source: Email thread 19ab2d030f860382

[Note: This compiles the house rules and character setup emails from the start of the Hobbity Adventure campaign. The game uses OSE Classic Rules Tome as primary, with Carcass Crawler magazine and Advanced Rules for optional material. Players: Stephen (Turnip), Todd (Boffo), Les (Wedge). Gus is the DM.]

---

INITIAL HOUSE RULES (23 Nov 2025):

Rules Priority: OSE Classic Rules Tome first, then Carcass Crawler magazine and Advanced Rules for optional stuff.

Healing (no clerics in the party):
- Any character can use a "Bind Wounds" action on another character outside of combat to give them 1d4hp. Once per day per character, requires healing supplies.

Death at 0hp:
- If knocked to 0hp or below, Save vs Death. Success: unconscious and bleeding out for 1d4 rounds, then dead. Failure: dead immediately.

Combat: Using the Swords & Wizardry version of the combat round.

Thief Skills: Using the d6 for Thief Skills rules, plus the Expertise option.

Encumbrance: Item-Based Encumbrance rules.

Economy: GP standard. (Previously used SP standard to make Buford's wealth seem extravagant.)

XP: Primarily GP based.

TURNIP'S CHARACTER SHEET (Levelled Up) (25 Nov 2025):

HP: Roll 1d6 and add to existing total.

Attack Bonus (Ascending AC):
  Melee: 0
  Missiles: 0 (-1 for low Dex, +1 for being a Halfling)

Starting AC: 10 (base AC11, -1 for DEX)

Saving Throws:
  Death/Poison: 8
  Wands: 9
  Paralysis/Petrification: 10
  Breath Attacks: 13
  Spells/Rods/Staves: 12

Thief Skills:
  Climb Surfaces: 1
  Find/Remove Traps: 1
  Hear Noise: 2
  Hide: 2 (special 5 in the wilderness)
  Move Silently: 1
  Open Locks: 1
  Pick Pockets: 1
  +4 discretionary points to distribute (max 5 per skill)
  Roll 1d6 under skill number to succeed; rolling 6 always fails.
  Hobbits have automatic 5-in-6 chance hiding in wilderness (must be motionless).

---

UPDATED HOUSE RULES (26 Nov 2025):

Healing (revised):
- Replacing "Bind Wounds" with the Mage class healing from OSE Carcass Crawler Issue 1:
  "Once per day, a mage may spend 1 turn to heal an ally. This either cures 1hp per level or allows another save against a negative effect (e.g. a curse or poison)."
- Used as a class ability for the party. Can be used in combat.

Death at 0hp (revised):
- Replacing custom rule with OSE Dolmenwood "Death's Door" rules:
  - At 0hp: unconscious, lingering at death's door (not immediately dead).
  - Each Round: 2-in-6 chance of dying.
  - Aid: Instantaneous healing saves the character, restoring to 1hp. Characters brought back from death's door suffer permanent loss of 1 point from a random Ability Score.

Backstab: Removed from Hobbits. "I don't think backstabbing is a very Hobbity thing to do."

Spellcasting: Reverting to Classic Rules method of learning spells (not Advanced). "This will limit your spell casting quite a bit."
