⚔ OTHER MILITARY FORCES ⚔

Local Militia & Wonderbringer Warsmiths of Daggerdale — Summary & Roster

About This Document

This document covers the two remaining military categories in Daggerdale that fall outside the Standing Army, Holy Orders, and Chartered Companies: the local farm militias and the Wonderbringer Warsmiths. Both are structural elements of Daggerdale’s defence rather than narrative-rich organizations with extensive rosters. They are combined here because the source material for each is thin.
I. Local Militia

Every fortified farm community in Daggerdale maintains its own militia of fighting-aged people. Under the Concordat, one-fifth of eligible persons are enrolled at any time, serving four-year terms with a minimum of two hours per tenday of training. The Concordat also requires common militia-readiness drills four times per year across all farms.

Militia captains are paid by their own settlements, not by the central government. The militias serve as the first line of local defence; the Sun Blades patrol the wider region while militias hold their own walls. Each community also maintains a permanent temple to Amaunator.

II. Wonderbringer Warsmiths

Engineers and artificers devoted to Gond, based at the Wonderbringer Warforge at Fort Luminous. They are not part of the Sun Blades chain of command and operate independently, developing and maintaining experimental weapons and magical equipment for the dale’s defence.

Wonderbringer Warforge
Fort Luminous — junction of Mountain Road and Whitegate Mine Road
One day’s ride from Castle Radiance, two days from Dagger Falls. Serves as a horse-change station for urgent packages. Workshop for magical item creation and repair. Home to the great bronze gun. Includes gnomish specialists. Connected to Gond worshippers throughout the region.
