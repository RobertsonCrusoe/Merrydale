⚔ THE REDJACKS ⚔

Chartered Company of Daggerdale — Summary & Roster, c. 1479–1483 DR

About This Document

The Redjacks are a large mercenary company operating in Daggerdale. Source material for this organization is thin compared to the White Cloaks or the Free Defence League, and this document reflects what is currently known. Under the Daggerdale Concordat of Elesias 1482, the Captain of the Redjacks holds a seat on the Governors’ Assembly.
I. Summary

The Redjacks are a “storied outfit” of more than 60 veteran soldiers and adventurers, described as “very capable, if somewhat self-interested.” They are led by Captain Hastle Blackwater (also rendered “Redwater” in one Chronicle passage—a discrepancy that may be a transcription error or an alias). Their footmen are paid two gold coins per day, an exceptionally high rate for 1479 DR, indicating the Redjacks are well-funded.

The company has a history of combat experience in Scardale and of fighting on both sides of the Sembia conflict. They have a contentious relationship with the Free Defence League—in 1478, they were banned from Castle Radiance for interfering with a Gryphon Company bounty. By 1479 they were permitted to return under the scrutiny of clerk Jonas Millstone. That summer, the Redjacks were stationed at Fort Last Sun for western front operations against Netheril.
